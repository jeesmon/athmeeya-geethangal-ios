• Vandanam ..vandanam van-da-nam naadhaa
    Ninte rakshackkaay-vandanam
  1. Unnathathil ninnu enne prathi
    Mannil vanna naadhane!-vandanam
  2. Nandiyode innu ninte daasan
    Vandikkunnu mannane!-vandanam
  3. Shathruvaaya enneyorthu ninte
    Puthranaakki theerthathaal-vandanam
  4. Ente naamam jeeva pusthakathil
    Cherthathineyorthitha-vandanam
  5. Enthu modam ente antharange
    Santhatham vilasidunnu-vandanam. P.V.T
