• Nin mahaa snehameshuve!
    enmanassinn-agaadhame
    Ennil nin snehakaaranam
    ennarivinn atheethame
  1. Thaarakangalkku meetheyum
    thaavaka snehamunnatham
    Aazhiyilum ninsnehathinn
    aazhamagaadhamen priyaa!
  2. Doshiyaamenne thediyo
    krooshuvareyum thaanu nee!
    Praananum nalki snehippaan
    paapiyil kandenthu nee
  3. Maranamo jeevano pinne
    uyaramo aazhamoyenne
    Ninthiru snehathil ninnum
    pinthirikkilla yaathonnum
  4. Nithyathayil nin sannidhi
    ethi njaan vishramikkave
    Nin mukhakaanthiyil sadaa
    nirvruthi nedum njaan paraa. M.E.C
