1. Dukhathinte paanapaathram karthaavente kaiyyil thannaal
    Santhoshathodathu vaangi halleluyya paadidum njaan
  2. Doshamaayitt-ennodonnum ente thaathan cheykayilla
    Enne-yavanadichaalum avenenne snehikkunnu
  3. Kashta-nashta-meri vannaal bhaagyvanaay theerunnu njaan
    Kastmetta karthaavodu koottaaliyaay theerunnu njaan
  4. Loka saukhyam-enthu tharum? aatmaklesham-athin phalam
    Saubhaagyamull-aatma-jeevan kashtathayil vardhikkunnu
  5. Jeevanathin vambu vendaa kaazhchayude shobha vendaa
    Koodaarathin mudi pole krooshin niram maathram mathi
  6. Ullilenikkenthu sukham thejasserum kerubukal
    Koodaarathinn-akathunde shekkeenaayum-undavide
  7. Bhakthanmaaraam sahodarar vilakku pol koode unde
    Praarthanayin dhoopam unde meshamelenn appam unde
  8. Praakaarathil-ente mumbil yeshuvine kaanunnu njaan
    Yaaga peetdam-avanathre ennum ente rakshay avan
  9. Dinam thorum puthukkunna shakthi-ennil pakaruvaan
    Swatccha jalam vechittulla pichala thottiyum-unde
  10. Lokathe njaan-orkkunnilla kashta-nashtam-orkkunnilla
    Eppolente karthaavine onnu kaanaamenneyullu. M.K
