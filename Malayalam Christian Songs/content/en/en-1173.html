• Rakshanya suvishesham kettu kolvin
    Rakshakaneshuve ettu kolvin
  1. Paaril vannu praanan thannu
    Paapikalkkaay marichu paran- krooshil
    Paathakane pol marichu paran-
  2. Mruthiye vennu uyirthezhunnettu
    Mahathwathileshu jeevikkunnu- innu
    Rakshakanaayeshu jeevikkunnu-
  3. Sathya vazhiyum moksha vaathilum
    Nithya jeevanu yeshu maathram- avan
    Ellaavarkkum rakshaa daayakanaam-
  4. Aathma rakshaye thalli kalanjaal
    Agni narakathil chennidume- theeyil
    Allum pakalum erinjidume-
  5. Anthya vidhiyil nonthidaathe
    Swanthamaayeshuve sweekarippin- ennaal
    Anthyam shubhamaay theernnidume. G.P
