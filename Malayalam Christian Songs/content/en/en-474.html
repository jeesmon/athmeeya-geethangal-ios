• Aanandam anandame- en jeevitham
    Aanandam aanandame
  1. Lamkhanam kshamicheshu paapam marachu thannu
    Sankadamilliniyum enikku nithyam
    Than kazhalaanabhayam
  2. Nalloridayanaayi vallabhaneshuvunde
    Nallamechil dinavum arulumathaa-lillenikkoru kuravum-
  3. Seeyon nagaram nokki pokunna sanchaari njaan
    Naashamillaa nagaram avideyethre ente pithrubhavanam-
  4. Lokavanaantharathil pokumbozhennakathil
    Jeevaamrutha vachanam pozhikkumathaa- nennumenikkashanam
  5. Koorirul vazhiyilum koodeyenneshuvunde
    Vairikalin naduvil virunnorukki
    dairyamaayi nadathum
  6. Nanmayum krupayum ennaayurantham vareyum
    Unmayil pinthudarum naadhanodothu
    vaanidum njaan anantham. M.E.C
