1. Jeevante uravidame ente aasraya sankethame!
    Jeevante puthukkathil njaan
    Dinam nadakkuvaan krupa tharika
    • Krupa tharika kurpa tharika
      Dinam thorum thanniduka
      Ee ghora bhoovil yaathra cheyvaan
      Daiva krupa tharika-
  2. Shaashwatha parvvathavum ente
    Aashwaasa sankethavum
    Kannukaluyarthikkonde
    Priyaa ninne njaan nokkidunnu-
  3. Kashtabhaarangalum ente
    Jeevithathe alachaal
    Patharaathe munneruvaan
    Ee maruvil nin krupa tharika-
  4. Jeevitha paathakalil ente
    Kaalukal vazhuthidaathe
    Anthyam vare gamippaan
    Ninte amithabalam tharika. O.C
