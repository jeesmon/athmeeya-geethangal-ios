1. Swarggeya pithaave nin thiruhitham
    Swarggathileppole bhoovilaakkane
    Nin hitham cheythonaam nin suthaneppole
    Innu njaan varunne nin hitham cheyvaan
    • En daivame ninnistam cheyvaan
      Vannidunne njaaninnu modamaay
      Ente ishtamonnum vendaa priyane
      Ninte ishtam ennil poornnamaakkane
  2. Nanmayum poornna prasaadamulla
    Nin hithamenthennu njaanariyuvaan
    En manam puthukki maaridunnu nithyam
    Nindyamaanenikkee loka lavanyam-
  3. Njaan avanullam kayyilirikkayaal
    Aarumillenikku dosham cheyyuvaan
    Innenikku vannu neridunnathellam
    Than hithamaanennu njaanariyunnu-
  4. En thalayile mudikalumellaam
    Nirnnayamavanenni ariyunnu
    Onnathil nilathu veenidenamenkil
    Unnathanarinje saadhyamaayidu-
  5. Yeshu kristhuvin sareera yaagathaal
    Ullayishtathil njaan shudhanaayitheernnu
    Daiva ishtam cheythu vaagnatham praapippaan
    Poornna sahishnatha ekane priyaa-
  6. Aarumariyaatha shreshta bhojanam
    Njaan bhujichu nithyam jeevichidunnu
    Ente rakshakante ishtamellaam cheythu
    Vela thikackunnathente aahaaram-
  7. Ente kashtangal daivam tharunnathaam
    Ente praanane njaan yaagamaakkunnu
    Enne thakarthidaan thaathanistamenkil
    Than hithamennil sampoornnamaakatte. P.P.M
