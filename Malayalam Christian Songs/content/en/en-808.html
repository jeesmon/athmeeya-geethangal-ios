• Aashritha vatsalaneshu maheshane!
    Shaashwathame thirunaamam
  1. Ninmukha kaanthi ennil nee chinthi
    Kanmasham-aake akattiyen naayakaa!
    Nanma valarthanam-ennum
  2. Paavana hrudayam ekuka sadayam
    Kevalam loka sukhangal vedinju njaan
    Thaavaka thruppaadam cheraan-
  3. Apakadam nirayum jeevitha maruvil
    Aakulamilla nin nanmayezhumarikil
    Agathikalkk-aashrayam tharikil-
  4. Kshanikamaan ulakin mahimakalarikil
    Anudinam nin padathaarina thirayukil
    Anantha santhoshamund-oduvil-
  5. Varunnu njaan thaniye-enikku nee mathiye
    Karunayin kaathale vediyaruth agathiye
    Thiru krupa tharanamen pathiye. M.E.C
