• Nin sannidhiyil daivame! poornna-
    Santhosham poornna bhaagyame
    Lokathin impangalaal shokam vardhikkunnithaa
    Raagangal dehathilum rogangal aathmaavilum
  1. Maan vellathe kaamkshikkumpol-ninne
    Vaanjichichidunnen aathmaavu
    Thaana hrudayamathin aanandamaakunna nee
    Vaanukondirikka en praana naadhane! ennil
  2. Srushtiyilallen swasthatha-ente srushtaavaam ninnil maathrame
    Drushtanallaatha neeyarishta hrudayathil nin
    Nishkalanka santhosham ishtampol pakarunnu
  3. Sathyamaay ekadaivamaam-ninnil
    Mandhyasthanaam kristhuvinaal
    Bhruthyanu paripoornna sathya samaadhaanavum
    Mruthyuvine vizhungum nithya jeevanumunde
  4. Puthrathwathinte saakshikkaay-enne
    Mudrayittaavi moolam nee
    Ethra pakacho ninne athra nee snehichenne
    Mithravumaakki nin vichithramaam vaatsalyathil
  5. Anchindriyangal vazhiyaay-ee
    prapancham nin snehathil ninnu
    Vanjchippaan enne oro thancham anweshichaal nin
    Nenchil marackkayennu kenchi yaachichidunnu
  6. Ninnil en vaasam aakenam-illa
    Ennil veroru kaamshayum
    Vinnil neeyente vinnum mannil neeyente ponnum
    Minnumen rathnakkallum innum ennekkum aamen. M.V
