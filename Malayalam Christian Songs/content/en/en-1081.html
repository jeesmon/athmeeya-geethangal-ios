    suvishesham(2)
  1. Paapathe verukkunnu-
    daivam paapiye snehikkunnu
    Nalla sandesham -nammude desham
    engume ariyikka naam
  2. Daivathin mahatsneham-
    eka jaathane thanna sneham
    Kalmanam maattum-nanmakaloottum
    krupayude yuravidamaam
  3. Lajjitharaakenda-
    lava leshavum bhayam vendaa
    Daivika shakthi-paapikku mukthi
    nalkunnu suvishesham
  4. Veroru suvishesham-
    illa paarithil innashesham
    Paapikal swarggam-pookunna maarggam
    mattillenn ariyikka naam. M.E.C
