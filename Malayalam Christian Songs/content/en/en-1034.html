1. Kaanthane kaanuvaanaarthi valarunne
    Illa prathyaasha mattonnilum
    Kandaalum vegam njaan vanneedaamennura
    Cheytha priyan varum nischayam-
  2. Paazh marubhoomiyil klesham sahikkukil
    Nithya thuramukhathethum njaan
    Vishramichidum njaan nithya kottaarathil
    Nisthulyamaaya prathaapathil-
  3. Thammil thammil kaanum shudhanmaar vaanathil
    Kodi kodi ganam thejassil
    Sarvvaanga sundaran aakumen priyane
    Kaanaamathin mandhye ezhayum-
  4. Njaan ninakkullaval neeyenikkullavan
    Innaleyum innum ennekkum
    Kandaal mathivaraa sundara roopane
    Koodi kaanmaan vaanchayerunne. C.S.M
