• Rajaathmaja virunnathin vivaram
    ee upamayo mahaasaaram
    Rajaathmaja virunnathin vivaram
  1. Raajasuthan veliyonnu kazhichithu pandu
    Raajyathulla pauranmaare kshanichathukondu
    Bhojanathin naalananjenn arachanum kandu
    Aa janathe vilichudan aalayachumkondu-
  2. Venda vibhavangalellaam cherthu njaan virunnu
    Vendum vidham chamackkayaal ningalippol vannu
    Venduvolam bhujikkuvin thruptharaavin ennu
    Vendinaar kshanam labhicha maanujarodannu-
  3. Ozhikazhivorotharam paranjaaranneram
    Oruthanu nilathilaay manassil vichaaram
    Nija sthreeyil laikkayaal oruthana saaram
    Mudakkunde kaalakalaal aparannu bhaaram-
  4. Vanniduvin virunninenn ivarariyichu-
    Kondathu marukkumulam arachan kopichu
    Annagaram nashippippaan udan kolli vechu
    Venthu porinjagni thannil avar vilapichu-
  5. Kaaryamevam bhavikkayaal bhoopathi thirinju
    Daasarodu paranjithu ningalo viranju
    Velikal vazhiyarikenn ivvidam thiranju
    Saadhujanangaleyaake sambharikkarinju-
  6. Kittiya janangalaale shaalaye nirachu
    Chattamivarudethonnu nokkuvaanurachu
    Pettennarachan varave thannudal marachu
    Kottililirunna neecha veshanaalarachu-
  7. Raajano vilichu chonnu shubhra vasthram vitto?
    Raaja virunnil kadakka pathiventhu matto?
    Namme-ivan nindikkayaal iruttinkalitto
    Nalla shiksha kodukkenam theekkadalil chutto-
  8. Bhruthyarivane varinju ketti vairam thedi
    Pattanathinu purathe mathilinkalkkoodi
    Ottumadi kaanaathulla kooriruttil pedippettu
    narakathil thaazhaan thalliyum vechodi. K.V.S
