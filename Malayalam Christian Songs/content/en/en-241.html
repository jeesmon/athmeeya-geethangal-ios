• Ethrayo nallavaneshu ethra dayaaparanennum
    Ithratholam cheytha nanmakalorkkumbol
    Ethra stuthichaalum poraa-
  1. Veezhaathe enneyum kaathu
    Thaazhaathe enne pidichu
    Ezhayaamenneyum kaathupaalichathaam
    Sneham njaan orthu paadum-
  2. Kuppayil ninnangu uyarthi oppamiruthi than koode
    Appaa nin snehathe eppozhum orthu njaan
    Thruppaadam kumbidunne-
  3. Ennu njaan vannangu cherum?
    Annente khinnatha theerum
    Annaalil priyante ponmukham kandidum
    Ennum njaan aaraadikkum. M.J.P
