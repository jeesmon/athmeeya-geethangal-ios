• Aashrayam enikkini yeshuvilennum
    Aakayaalillini aakulamonnum
  1. Paaridathil pala shodhana varikil
    Paadidum njaan puthu gaanamen hrudiyil
    Allalin alakal nere vannidukil
    Halleluyya paadi aashwasichidum njaan-
  2. Simha-vaayadachum thee balam keduthum
    Samhaara doothan than kaikale thaduthum
    Allilum pakalilum than bhuja balathaal
    Nallapol kaathavan nadathidum krupayaal-
  3. Vaanile paravaye pularthidum daivam
    Vaasana-malarkale viriyikkum daivam
    Maruvil than janathe nadathidum daivam
    Marannidaath-enneyum karuthidumennum-
  4. Than-mozhi kettum thanmukham kandum
    Than-paada seva cheythum njaan paarkkum
    Paarile naalukal theernnuyen priyane
    Neril njaan kaanumbol theerumen khedam. G.P
