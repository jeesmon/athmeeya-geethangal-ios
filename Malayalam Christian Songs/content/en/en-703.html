1. Aashwaasame enikkere thingidunne
    Vishwaasakkannaal njaan nokkidumbol
    Snehameridumen rakshakan sannidhau
    Aananda-kkoottare kaanunnallo
  2. Aamodathaal thingi aashcharyamodavar
    Chuttum ninnu sthuthi cheythidunnu
    Thanka thirumukham kaanmaan kothichavar
    Ullaasamodithaa nokkidunnu
  3. Thanmakkalin kannu-neerellaam thaathan thaan
    Ennekkumaayi-thudachithallo
    Pon veenakal dharich-aamoda-poornnaraay
    Karthaavine sthuthi cheyyunnavar
  4. Kunjaadinte raktham thannil thangal anki
    Nannaay veluppicha koottaravar
    Poornna vishudharaay theernnavar yeshuvin
    Thanka rudhirathin shakthiyaale
  5. Thanka kkireedangal thangal shirassin mel
    Ven nilayanki dharichoravar
    Kaiyyil kuruthola enthittavar sthuthi-
    Paadeett-aananda-modaarthidunnu
  6. Chernnidume vegam njaanuma-kkoottathil
    Shudharod-onnichang aanandippaan
    Lokam venda enikkonnum venda-ente
    Naadhante sannidhau chernnaal mathi. M.V
