• Sneha charadukalaalenne
    yeshu cherthu bendhichu
    Than kurishodenneyonnichu
    Njaanellaam thannilarppichu
  1. Thinmayerum vazhikalil njaan nadannakannallo
    En kaalkal idari veenallo
    Thedivannu jeevan thannu kandeduthallo
    Enne thaan veendeduthallo-
  2. Panni thinnum thavidupolum ulakam thannilla
    Thunackkaay aarum vannilla
    Daivamallaathithra nalloraarumeyilla
    Sahaayam nalkuvorilla-
  3. Thante divya sannidhaanam tharum samaadhaanam
    Mattellaam bheethiyin sthaanam
    Alavumilla athirumilla anthavumilla
    Santhosham kristhuvilunde-
  4. Ulakame neeyuyarcha nalkiyupacharikkenda
    Enne nee aakarshikkenda
    Kurisheduthen guruvin pinpe
    pokanamallaa-Thenikkinn aasha verilla-
  5. Avannadima anubhavikkum swaanthryam pole
    Verilla swaathenthryam ethum
    Anthyashwaasam pomvarethan vela cheyyum njaan
    Thruppaada seva cheyyum njaan. M.E.C
