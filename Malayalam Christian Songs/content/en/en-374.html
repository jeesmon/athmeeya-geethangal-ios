1. vannidenam yeshunaada! Inneeyoga mandhye nee
    Thannarulka nin varangal nin stuthi kondaaduvaan
  2. Mannidathil vanna naada! Ponnu thirumeniye!
    Nandiyoditha nin daasar vannu ninne kumbidaan
  3. Aaniyetta paadukale kaanichinnee daasare
    Naanikkaatha saakshikalaay theerkkanam enneshuve
  4. Thaathanaamen daivame shreeyeshunaamam moolame
    Thannidenam aatma daanam praarthana cheythiduvaan
  5. Vairiyaam jadam pishaachu lokamennivayithaa
    Khora senayodu vannu porinaay muthirnna ho-
  6. Ithra valya senayode theerppathinu njangalil
    Thraaniyethumillaa karthaa paadathil veenidunnen-
  7. Vikkanaakum moshaye nee vaakkilettam dheeranaay
    Aakkiyallo ninkrupa varangalaalen daivame-
  8. Aadukalkkidayane nin ponnunaadam kettithaa
    Naadu vittu veedu vittu koottam vittirangiye
  9. Israyelin gothrangalku mun nadanna meghame
    Innee daasar mun nadannu kaattidename vazhi-
  10. Halleluyya yeshuvinum thaathanaatmanennume
    Halleluyya nithyakaalam nithyagaanam paadum njaan-
