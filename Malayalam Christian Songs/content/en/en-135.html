• Naam vimukthanmaar-daiva krupa labhichor - yaahil-
    Santhoshich-aandichaarkkuka naam
  1. Yaahile santhosham balam namukke
    Evarum than thiru sannidhiyil
    Aathmapoornamaay dinavum-naam
    Paadi vaazthi sthuthichiduka-
  2. Naalthorum thaan cheytha nanmakalkkaay
    Nammude bhaarangal vahichathinaal
    Vandyanaam pithaavine -naam
    paadi vaazhththi sthuthichchiduka-
  3. Naanaavidha parishodhanakal
    Naalu paadum namme moodiyappol
    Thaangi than krupaakarathaal-naam
    Nandiyode sthuthichiduka-
  4. Thankrupayaal raksha praapicha naam,
    Vankrupayil namme sookshikkunnu
    Krupa krupayennaarthu kondu
    Krupaasanathodaduthiduka-
  5. Inneyolam priyan than krupayil
    Kanmani pol namme kaathathinaal
    Ponnunaamamuyarthi modaal-naam
    Nandiyode sthuthichiduka. C.K
