• Ethrayo valiyavan bahu dhanikanum jagadadhipanum
    Kristhu naadhanaho!
  1. Ithrayum mahaan-oruthan-idharayilundo?
    Marthya raksha cheyyuvaan- marthya raksha cheyyuvaan
    Mahithalamithil manujanaay vanna devanivan-
  2. Mruthyuve jaichuyirtha mattoruthanundo?
    Nithya jeevanekuvaan-nithya jeevanekuvaa-
    Nidharayithil itharanillethrayum sudrundam-
  3. Vaana dootha senayum vanangumee mahaanne
    Maanavum mahathwavum-maanavum mahathwavum
    Dhanavum balavum sthuthiyum naam dinavum nalkidenam
