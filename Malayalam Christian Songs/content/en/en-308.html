• Paadum njaan yeshuvinte snehathe
    Keerthikkum than naamathe santhoshamaay
  1. Kuppayil kidannayenne veendeduthallo
    Shrestaraam prabhukkanmaarodoppamiruthi
    Nandiyaalen manam nirayunne-
  2. Nithyamaam narakathinu yogyanaamenne
    Swantha puthranaakki theerthathenthoratbhutham!
    Nandiyaalen manam nirayunne-
  3. Paapathinte shambalam maranamennathaam
    Daiva neethi puthranmel chorinja snehame
    Nandiyaalen manam nirayunne-
  4. Veendum vannu cherthu kollumenna vaagnatham
    Nalki name veededutha nalla rakshakan
    Nandiyaalen manam nirayunne. P.V.B
