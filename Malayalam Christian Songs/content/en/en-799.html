• Yeshu nallavan en yeshu nallavan- athe
    Samshayamill-ethumente yeshu nallavan
  1. Than karunayo - ennekkumullathaam -nithyam
    Than kazhalenikkabhayam yeshu nallavan
  2. Nalla idayan - illa chanjchalam-thante
    Chollenikku maadhuryamen yeshu nallavan
  3. Illa thanikku - thullyanaarume-bhoovi
    Vallabhan maheshanente yeshu nallavan
  4. Innaleminnum - ennekkumavan thaan-thanne
    Onnupol-irikkunnavan yeshu nallavan
  5. Kashtathakalil - aashwasaam ekuvaan-eni-
    Kkettam adutha thunayaam yeshu nallavan
  6. Yeshu nallavan - ennaaswadichavar thannil
    Aashrayikkum melkkumelen yeshu nallavan
  7. Nandiyodu njaan - ennaalumavane -abhi-
    Vandhanam cheyyumanantham yeshu nallavan. P.V.T
