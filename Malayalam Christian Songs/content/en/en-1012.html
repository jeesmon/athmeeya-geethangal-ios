• On the resurrection morning
  1. Cherumuyirppin prabhaatha deham dehikal thammil
    Khedam rogam mruthyuvilla haa! melil
  2. Dehamalpaneram maathram mannilurangidenam
    Kaahalam dhwanikkuvolam vishraamam
  3. Kallarakalannu nalkum sarvva mruthareyum haa!
    Thaathan maathaa makkal veendum chernnidum
  4. Dehadehikal chernnonnaay kristhan swanthacchaayayil
    Thejassodunarnnu vaazhum thruptharaay. E.I.J
