• tUNE: Devajana samaajame
  1. Deva devaneshuvine sthuthikkanam naam
    Davakalinnaraadhyane
    Devaadhidevan swargga deshe vasichirunnon
    Deharoopamaay dhare narare thedi vannu-
  2. Aarum vanangidumathi shreshtamaam naama-
    Dhaariyaam karthane nammal
    Ekamaay vangenam eki sarvva mahathwam
    Ethoru naavumettu chollunna karthaavaakum-
  3. Andhamaay-aaraadhanayil bendhitharaaya
    Anthamillaa manujare
    Bendhuvaameshu devan bendhanam neekki namme
    Sathyaathm aaraadhanackkaay sathyamaay verthiricha
  4. Vishudha mandirathil pra-veshanamennum
    Assaadhyamaaya marthyarkkaay
    Deva suthanaameshu deha thirasheelaye
    Cheenthi praveshanavum krupaasanthin thanna-
  5. Paapa parihaaram varuthiyennekkumaay
    Ekamaam yaagam moolamaay
    Melini yaagam vyarthdam paapaparihaarthdam
    Aathmeeya yaagamarthdam aayathennathre vyaktham
  6. Divya rudhiram vilayaay thaaneki, vaangi
    Thannaalayamaay nammeyum
    Divyaathma vaasamaaya devaalayathil ninnum
    Sthothra yaaga dhwanikal ennennumuyarthi naam. T.C.V
