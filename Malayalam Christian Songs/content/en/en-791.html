• Ee daivam ennumen daivam
    Jeevaanthyatholam vazhi nadathum
  1. Shaashwatha bhujangal keezhilunde
    Yeshu eppozhum koodeyunde
    Kaangalil vahikkum kannuneer thudackkum
    Karuthiyenne nadathum-
  2. Ullam thakarnnu neeridumbol
    Uttavarakannu maaridumbol
    Undenikkarikil unnathaneshu
    Uthama nal sakhiyaay-
  3. Eri varum dukha bhaarangalo
    Ekanaay theerum nerangalo
    Enthu vannaalum mannilennaalum
    Enikkiniem yeshuvunde-
  4. Aanandamaayoru jeevithamaam
    Aavathallenikkathu varnnikkuvaan
    Aarthu njaan paadum keerthanam cheyyum
    Aayussin naalkalellaam. C.J
