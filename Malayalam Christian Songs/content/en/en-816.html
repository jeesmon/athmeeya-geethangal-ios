• Ente yeshu naayakan Enikku nalla snehithan
  1. Vinnin sukham vedinjee mannithil enneyum than-makanaakkuvaan
    Vannu jeevan thanna naadhan ennum-ananyanaam-
  2. Aarum sahaayamillaathe njaan paaram valanjidum velayil
    Arumayodannarikil vanna aatma-naadhaneshuvaam-
  3. Ellaam-enikkente nanmackkaayi swarlloka naadhan tharunnathaal
    Illa bheethiyullil-ente nalla naadhan avanallo. G.K
