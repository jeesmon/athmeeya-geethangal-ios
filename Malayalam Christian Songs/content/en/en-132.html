• Oo..... rakshakaneshuvine - paadi sthuthchiduka
  1. Paapiyethedi paarithil vannu paadu sahichu paran
    Paapikalkkaay marichu moonnam dinamuyirthu-
  2. Mannavaneshu vanmahima vittu mannithil vannenikkaay
    Vedaneyettadhikam yaagamaay theernnenikkaay-
  3. Paapiyaamenne veendeduthonum thanmakanaakkiyonum
    Paavananeshuvallo paarithin naadhanavan
  4. Paarithil palathaam kashtathayerukil thellume bhayam venda
    Rakshakaneshuvunde santhatham thaangiduvaan-
  5. Vegam varaamennuracha naadhan vegam vannidumallo
    Thaamasamadhikamilla naadhanavan varuvaan. P.V
