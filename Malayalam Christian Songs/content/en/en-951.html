• Sweetly I am resting in Jesus
  1. Karthaavu thaan varum vegam than vachanam sathyame
    Kaathirikkunna than samkham thannodu cherume
    Marthya shareeram maarume nithyashareeram labhyame
    Nithya santhosham vrutharkkekuvaan
    Karthaavu thaan varum vegam
    • Vegam vegam vegam thaan varume
      Nithyasanthosham vrutharkkekuvaan
      Karthaavu thaan varum vegam
  2. Karthaavu thaan varum vegam than raajyam sthaapikkume
    Neethimaanmaar bhoomau vaazhum thannodu koodave
    Kannuneer thaan thudackkume annavar aanandikkume
    Nithya santhosham vrutharkkekuvaan
    Karthaavu thaan varum vegam
  3. Karthaavu thaan varum vegam andhakaaram maarume
    Chanthamerum sooryakaanthi chinthum bhoovengume
    Santhaapam neengippokume
    Santhosham poornnamaakume
    Nithya santhosham vrutharkkekuvaan
    Karthaavu thaan varum vegam
  4. Karthaavu thaan varum vegam samshayam illottume
    Than varavin kaalalakshyam kaanunnund engume
    Athi thalirthidunnithaa venaladuthupoyi haa!
    Nithya santhosham vrutharkkekuvaan
    Karthaavu thaan varum vegam. E.I.J
