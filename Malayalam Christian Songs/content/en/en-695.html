• Njaan yehovaye ellaa naalum
    Vaazhthidum stuthi paadidum
    Ente naavil than stuthigeetham
    Ennum ennum irunnidum
  1. Ente sankada yaachanackkavan
    Utharam thannanugrahichu
    Njaan bhayappedum neramennude
    Chaare vannu viduvichu-
  2. Maanasam thakarnnidum velayil
    Chaare vannavan thaangidum
    Thante bhaktharin praananeyavan
    Kaathidum paripaalikkum
  3. Yaahine bhayamulla bhaktharin
    Kannuneeravan kandidum
    Than vachanamayachu bhakthare
    Thalkshanam viduvichidum. K.V.H
