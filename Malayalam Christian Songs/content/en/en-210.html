• Krooshum vahicha kunnin meethe pokuvathaaro!
    Klesham sahichor agathiyeppole chaakuvathaaro!
  1. Sarvveshwaraneka suthano? saldootha vandithano!
    Suraloke ninnum nammethedi vanna snehithano?
  2. Nee vaakkaal cheythorulakil ninkai rachichorkkarikil
    Nee vanna neram bahumathi-aayavar thannathu kurisho!
  3. Ennaadhiyakattaan thaniye krooshedutha devasuthaa!
    Pinnaale njaanen krooshumeduthu varunnithaa krupa thaa
  4. En jeevitha kaalam muzhuvan nin sneha maadhuryam
    Paadipukazhthaan naadha! tharika naavinu chaathuryam. M.E.C
