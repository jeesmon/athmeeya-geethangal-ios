• Yauvanam mohanam sundaram
    Maadhuryam yuvathee-yuvaakkale
    Yauvanam vegam theerum aarogyam kuranjidum
    Yauvanathil nin srushti
    karthaavin paadam chernniduka
  1. Yauvanakaalam mohanamallo
    Aarogyam daivathin daanamallo
    Ee nalla kaalam nee vyarthamaakkalle
    Daivathin paithalaay jeevichiduka-
  2. Yauvanathil nee ninnishtam pole
    Ninnude naalukal theerkkukayo?
    Daivathin mumbil oru naal nilkkenam
    Daivathin paithalaay jeevichiduka-
  3. Innu nee kaanum sarvvavum maaya
    Venthupom choola polee-yulakam
    Loka saubhaagyathinn-anthyam santhaapam
    Daivathin paithangalkk-annumaanandam. A.V
