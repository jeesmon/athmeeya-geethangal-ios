• Wonderful, wonderful Lamb of Calvary
  1. Golgothaayile kunjaade Athbhuthamullor kunjaade,
    Paapashaanthi neeyathre Golgothaayile kunjaade
  2. Golgothaayile mruthyuve Athbhuthamullor mruthyuve
    Jeevavaathil neeyathre Golgothaayile mruthyuve
  3. Golgothaayile rakthame Athbhuthamullor rakthame
    Envishudhi neeyathre Golgothaayile rakthame
  4. Golgothaayile neethiye Athbhuthamullor neethiye
    En prashamsa neeyathre Golgothaayile neethiye
  5. Golgothaayile thaazhmaye Athbhuthamullor thaazhmaye
    En uyarcha neeyathre Golgothaayile thaazhmaye
  6. Golgothaayile snehame Athbhuthamullor snehame
    En kireedam neeyathre Golgothaayile snehame
  7. Golgothaayile Jayame Athbhuthamullor jayame
    Ente shakthi neeyathre Golgothaayile jayame
  8. Golgothaayile kunjaade Athbhuthamullor kunjaade
    En sangeetham neeyathre Golgothaayile kunjaade. V.N
