• Stothram yeshu devaa stothram jeevanaada
    Stothram innumennum doothanmaar
    Raappakal vaazhthidunna devaa
  1. Paapikale thedi paaril vanna devaa
    Paavana ninam paapikalkekiya
    Prana naayakane
  2. Nee marichuyirthu haa jayam varichu
    Vaaniluyarnnu thaathan
    Vala bhaagathinnu vaazhunnone-
  3. Aayirangalil nee aarilum sundaranaam
    Vaanilum bhoovilaake
    Mahonnathaneshu rakshakane-
  4. Vaazhthidunnithaa njaan loka rakshithaave
    Vaazhthidunnennum sarvva
    Lokaadipaneshu naayakane. G.P
