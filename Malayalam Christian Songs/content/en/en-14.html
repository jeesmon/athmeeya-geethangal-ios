• Maname pukazhthidu nee
    mahonnathan than mahima
  1. Marakurishathil marippaanaay nara janma meduthu
    Vanna than naamam manoharam-Aha!
    Than naamam monoharam
    mahaatbhutham than premam-
  2. Bahu vipathukalezhum bhoovil-
    sneha kkaikal neettiyenne
    Thaangum than naamam manoharam-Aha!
    Than naamam manoharam
    mahaatbhutham than premam-
  3. Pala kuravukal vannaalum-
    enne thallaathe krupayaal
    Kaakkum than naamam manoharam-Aha!
    Than naamam manoharam
    mahaatbhutham than premam-
  4. Oru nimishavum thalaraathe-
    thirumarvil vishraamam
    Thedu than naamam manoharam-Aha!
    Than namaam manoharam
    mahaatbhutham than premam. M.E.C
