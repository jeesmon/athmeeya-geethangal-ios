• Daiva roopaththil irunna yeshudevan
    Thante daivathvam murruke pidikkaathe
    Daasaroopam eduththu manushyanaaya
    Mahaa sneham nammukku marrakkaamo?
  1. Thannaththaan ozhichch manushyanaay vilangngi
    Thannaththaan thaazhththi maranaththolam
    Krooshile maranaththolam thanne
    Anusaranamulavanaay theernnu thaan--
  2. Adaamya janmapapam neekanay
    Pazhaya paampinte thalaye thakarkanay
    Dasaropam eduththu manushyanaya
    Mahaa sneham nammukku marrakkaamo?
  3. Daivavum yeshuvine etavum uyirththi
    Sakala naamaththinnum mel naamam nalki
    Yeshu naamaththingkal monnu lokarudeyum
    Muzhangkal madakki thalathazhththi kondu--
  4. Yeshukristhu karththaav ennu annetuparrayum
    Pithaavaaya daivaththin mahathvaththinaay
    Thathen valabhage nammukkayi jeevikuna
    Vegam namme cherkan meghathil vannidunna
    Manavalanay inum orungi nilkkam. M.P
