• Nandi chollidaam ennum modaal
    Thaathan cheytha nanmakal oronnorthidaam (2)
  1. Aavashyangal oronnum nalla thaathan arinju
    Kshemamaay dinam thorum pottidunnathaal-
  2. Aakulangal enthinu daivathinte paithal nee
    Ninte bhaavi avanil bhadramallayo-
  3. Pakshikale nokkuvin vithilla vithayilla
    Ennaalum avayellaam jeevikkunnathaal
  4. Chanthamulloraambalum shaaronin panineerum
    Neythidaathavayellaam ethra mohanam-
  5. Thuchhamaayorivaye ithramel karuthunnon
    Anpulla thanmakkale marannidumo. B.J
