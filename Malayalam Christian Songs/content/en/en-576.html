• Karuna niranja kadale
    Kaalaakaala-mellaam enne kaathidane
    Karthaave kaividaathe-
    karuna niranja kadale
  1. Olangal mala polu-yarnnidunna
    velayilum van vinakalilum
    Chirakadiyil thava nizhalil
    abhayam tharum nee-yenikke
  2. Maaruthen-ethiraay veeshidilum
    maarayinn-anubhava-meridilum
    Manassalinju maravidathil
    mannava enne kaathidane-
  3. Vairikal naduvil virunnorukki
    dhairyamaayenne vazhi nadathum
    Parama naadhaa! nee niyatham
    piriyaath-arikil paarkkaname
  4. Kaalvary kurishathil enikkaayi
    vaavittu karanju marichavanaay
    Erinju theernnu dharayil ninnum
    pirinju povathenn-aagrahamaam. G.P
