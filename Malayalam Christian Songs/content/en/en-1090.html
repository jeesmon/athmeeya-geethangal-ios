• Sowing in the morning
  1. Vithachiduka naam swarggathinte vithaam
    Kristhan suvishesham hrudayangalil
    Aatmamaari peyyum daivam krupa cheyyum
    Tharum koythineyum thakka kaalathil
    • Koythu kaalathil, naam santhoshichum
      Kattakal chumannum kondu vannidum
  2. Vithachiduka naam, snehathin adhwaanam
    Oru naalum vyarthdam alla aakayaal
    Ennum praarthdichidin velayil ninnidin
    Vithu nanachidin kannuneerinaal-
  3. Vithachiduka naam, vardhanaye daivam
    Nalkum sarvva neram than van shakthiyaal
    Venalkkaalam, varsham, kattu, sheetham, ushnam
    Cheyyum daivam ishtam bhoomi nilkkum naal-
  4. Vithachiduka naam, thadassam anekam
    Saathaan kondennaalum than vairaagyathaal
    Thalarnnu pokaathe snehavum vidaathe
    Nilkka ksheenikkaathe kristhan shakthiyil-
  5. Vithachiduka naam, vithackkunna kaalam
    Avasaanichidum ethra vegathil
    Ippol vithackkaathe irunnaal koyyaathe
    Rakshakan mumbaake nilkkum lajjayil-
  6. Vithachiduka naam, divya samaadhaanam
    Mulachiduvolam shoonya deshathil
    Marubhoomi kaadum utsavam kondaadum
    Parvvathangal paadum daiva thejassil. V.N
