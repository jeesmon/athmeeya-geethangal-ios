• Paapiyil kaniyum paavana devaa!
    paadam paninjidunnen
    Paapiyamenne snehicho nee
    paarilenne thedi vanno!
  1. Dushtanaranaay dooshanam cheythu
    dooramaayirunnen
    Thediyo nee enneyum van-
    chettilninnuyarthiyo nee!-
  2. En paapam theerppaan paralokam cherppaan
    heena naranaay nee
    Enthu njaanithineedu nalkidum?
    ennum ninnadimayaam njaan-
  3. Vinnin mahima vedinju neeyenne
    vinnil cherthiduvaan
    Nirnnayam nin sevayenye
    onnumilliniyen modam-
  4. Paapathin phalamaam maranthin bhayathe j
    aichavan neeyoruvan
    Jeevanum samaadhanaavum en
    sarvvavum neeye nirantham-
  5. Maayayam ulakin veshavishesham
    veruthen njaanakhilam
    Nisthulam nin snehamen manam
    Athrayum kavarunnu naadhaa. M.E.C
