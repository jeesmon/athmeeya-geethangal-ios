• Yeshuvente naayakan yeshuvente daayakan
    Yehuvente paalakan ente sarvvavum
  1. Koorirulin paathayil deepam kaatidum
    Agniyil naduvilen chare vanneedum-
  2. Kaalvary kurishathil yaagamaayavan
    Paapavum shaapavum neekki thannavan-
  3. Pachayaaya pulppurangal kattidunnavan
    Swatchamaam nadikkarike nadathidunnavan-
  4. Vaana dootha senayumaayi vannidunnavan
    Vegamenne cherthidum
    than sannidhaanathil. T.M.K
