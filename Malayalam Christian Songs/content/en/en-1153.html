    Yeshu ninne vilikkunnu
    Kaalvariyil uyir thannavanaam
    Yeshu ninne vilikkunnu
  1. Paapathinte bhaarathinaal
    paaram kenu valanjidunno?
    Paraneshuvil ninte paapathinte
    parihaaram kandiduvaan-
  2. Daahamerum jalamalla
    daanamaayi jeevajalam
    Devanandanan tharum samthrupthamaam
    Divyamoda puthuhrudayam-
  3. Marthya jeevitham kshanikam
    mruthyu vannidum orunaal
    Marakkaathe ninnudeyaathma raksha
    Munname nee praapikkuvaan-
  4. Ennidam varunnavare
    onninaalum kaividilla
    Enna vaagdatham thanna jeevanaadhan
    Innu ninne vilikkunnu. C.J
