1. Kristhuvin imbagaanam ennume
    Ennude jeevavaakyam ennume
    Thannude jeevamozhiy ennume
    Ennude jeevannaadhaarame
    • Njaan pinthudarnnidum njaan pingamichidum
      Ennude jeevithayaathrayil
      Ee maruvil choodathelkkumbol
      Than chirkenikku vishramam
  2. Kristhuvin divyayishtam ennume
    Ennude jeevithathin aashaye
    Naalennum kroosheduthu njaan
    Thaathan ishtam niravettume
  3. Kristhuvin ninda njaan vahikkume
    Ennude bhushanam athennume
    Naalennum athennum en nikshepamaay
    Thejassaayenikku labhyame
  4. Kristhuvin shabdam njaan shravikkume
    Ennude paathaye athennume
    Naalennum athil nadannu njaan
    Thejassin theerethethume
  5. Kristhuvin mukham njaan darshikkume
    Ee ghoramaam samudrathin naduvilaay
    Ananthatha vidurave njaan kaanumbol
    Aa ponmukham prathyaashayin uravidam
