• Varuvin yeshuvin chaare varuvin anuthaapathode
    Varuvin ningal varuvin daivam nalkum
    Raksha krupayaal - vishwaasathaal-
  1. Kristhu naadhan kaalvariyil krooshil
    Raktham chinthi marichuyir poondu
    Shikshaavidhi neengi nithya rakshaa maarggam
    Vilambaram cheythu bhoovil-
  2. Paapabhaarathaalulakil vaazhum
    Maanavare ningal vanniduvin
    Paapa bhaaram neekkum shaapamellaam
    pokkum Parama saubhaagyamekum-
  3. Loka shukham nodineram maathram
    Shokamallaath-enthaanathin nettam
    Nithya samaadhaanam kristhuvinte
    daanam Niyatham santhoshamallo. G.P
