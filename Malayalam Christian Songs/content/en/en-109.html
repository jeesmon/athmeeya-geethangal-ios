• Jeevodanamaaya yeshu-devaa! vandanam
    Devaadheeshaa! neeyozhinjing aarullaashrayam
  1. Chaavine vennuyirthaaviyin jeevanie-
    Bhoovil nararkkarulaan vaanil ninnirangiya-
  2. Manmayanaam aadaamin vazhiyaay vanna
    Mrunmaya shareerathe mannilninn uyarthunna-
  3. Lamkhanathaal naramsamkham asheshavum
    Samkramicha marana shankayozhikkum divya-
  4. Paavanamaam thiruchorayinaalenne
    Devasavidham thannil sevippaan niyamicha-
  5. Nin vadanam thannil ninnu thookum krupa
    En vivashathayellaam onnaayakattidunnu. K.V.S
