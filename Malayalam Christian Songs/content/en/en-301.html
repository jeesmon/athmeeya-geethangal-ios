1. Thedi vanno doshiyam enneyum enneyum naadha
    Ithramam shneem uyir koduthenikkay
    Mannava varnnippan eluthalle enikku
  2. Kshoneethale ksheenam bhavicheeda thenneyum naadha
    Aanippazhuthulla paanikalale
    Preenichanugrahi cheeduka nithyam
  3. Poshippikka padtya vachanamaam ksheerathaal enne
    Nirmala thoyam nithyam kudippichu
    Pachappul shayyayil kidatheedukenne
  4. Nirtheeduka kalangamatteshuve karayillathenne
    Palunku kadal theerath irunnu njaan ente
    Madhura gaana radham athileri gamippan
  5. Kunjadinte koode gamichavar paadume modal
    Seeyon malayil seemayattandandam
    ennineem labhikkumo malpraana naathhaa
