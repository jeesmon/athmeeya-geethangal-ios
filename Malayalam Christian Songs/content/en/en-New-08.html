• Tune: Praarththhanayil nal nerame
    (Sweet hour of prayer)
  1. Krupa, karuna niranja
    Maratha vishvastha! thaathaa
    Deerghakshama mahaadayaal
    Nammedinam nadaththunnon
    Varunu nin makkal chaare,
    Nandiyal ullam nirranju
    Swargasenayodu chernnu
    Inum vazhthi sthuthikkunu-
  2. Papachetil nithyam veenu
    Thanu poya adiyare
    Sapa maranam sahichchu
    Vendedutha sneha nidhe!
    Nirmalamam jeevaraktham
    Paapa pariharam chinthi
    Paripoornarakkiyathal
    Inum vazhthi sthuthikkunu-
  3. Gaththsamane adiyaarin
    Paapam mutum etetduthu
    Raktham viyarkkum vedana
    Sahichu nammalkkevarkkum
    Paapaththinte shabalamaam
    Nithya maranavum etu
    Nithyaviduthal thannathaal
    Inum vazhthi sthuthikkunu-
  4. Munaam nalil maranathe
    Sathane nithyam jayichu
    Kezhadakki vallabhan than
    Uyirththinnum jeevicheedum
    Thathen mumbake nammale
    Shudharayi nirutheduvanayi
    Vegam varum kaanthaa ange
    Inum vazhthi sthuthikkunu- M.P
