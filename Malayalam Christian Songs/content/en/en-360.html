• Vegam varenam prabho-bhavaan yogamithiladhyakshanaay
    Ekaantha chinthayode-
    njangalevarum kaathirikkunne
  1. Oottamaay kaattilaki kada-
    lettavum irambidumbol
    Muttum bhayam theerkkuvaan mana-
    muttarikilethum vibho
  2. Ninneppirinjirippaan guro-
    njangalaal kazhikayille
    Sooryane koodaathundo pakal-
    chandranenye raathri nanno?
  3. Gandham pirinjidukil-
    pookkalenthin upayogamaakum?
    Chanthamakannidume njangal-
    kkanthike neeyillayennaal
  4. Ninnaadam kelppikkane ninte

    Vanmaaripolaashishameki
    Nanma valarththidename

    puramaarnna ninphalangal
