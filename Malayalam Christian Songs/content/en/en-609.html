• Tune: Sthothram sthothram nin naama


    Paaduvin sthuthi geetham naam parannu
    Hrudi cherumathi modamode-
  1. Paapam pinipashi theerum khedamellaam ozhinjidum
    Paavanaathma niravil naam gaanam paadenam
    Aadiveda naadamaakum-eshu naadha sthuthi geetha
    naadabhangi lokamengum kaathukalil muzhangatte
  2. Naasha-kkuzhi-yathil-ninnum daivam namme kkarayetti
    Kaalukale urapperum paaramel nirthi
    Naavil thannu puthu gaanam daivathinu sthuthi-thaanaa Gaanashruthi neramaarum yaahe rekshithaavaay-kkaanum-
  3. Mosheyude veena gaanam velippaadil kelkkum geetham
    Reksha dhaivathinte daanam-ennathu thanne
    Shakthi, behumaanam, sthuthi nithya thaathan puthranaathmaa-
    Vinnum nithyam bhavikkatte halleluyya aamen sthothram. K.J.G
