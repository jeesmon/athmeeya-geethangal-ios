1. Nithya jayageetham paadaan naavuyurunne kandu
    Karthane stuthippaanenikkaashayerunne
    Ethrayumijipthinnadhikaaram moodilum oru
    Maathrayaam veendeduppin naalkkilla thaamasam
  2. Theeram kaanaathodidunna kappalaalukal para-
    nnidum pakkshikalekkandu dairyamaarnnapol
    maaridakkurikal kandutherum varavo thellum
    maattamillaatheduthennu nischayichidaam
  3. Thittamaay vaanasainyam lakshanam kuri-athi
    Shrestamaamee sangathikku saakshikalothi
    Dustalokathakramangal mattuvitta ho! Engum
    Kaattum kuri varavinu thakka kuriyaam
  4. Bhoomiyilo deerkhanaalaay shaapam vaazhunnu engum
    Kemamaam romayin ketta keliyaadunnu
    Kshaamavum pakarcha vyaadi porivayellaam-varum
    Shreemanuvel varavinu saakshikalallo
  5. Kaadukalin varalchayo neengi varunnu-chori
    Njeedum maariyettu nilamullasikkunnu
    Eedezhumolivu malamel choodidum paadam-kaanmaan
    Naaduthorumorukkangal koodivarunnu-
  6. Yoodarude veendeduppin naalanayunnu pura-
    Jaathikalin kaalam thikayaanadukkunnu
    Shreeyerusalemil kudiyeri varunnu-janam
    Raajashakthikkethir balam koodivarunnu
  7. Bhakthiketta paapalokam paanjilakave-varum
    Parishudhimaanmaar paran paadam paniyum
    Nithyamaayippaadulakil ninnu marayum peyin
    Neenda shathruthayodungum nindayozhiyum
  8. Aayathinaal daivamekum munkurikalaamiva
    Avan varavenye veroraashwaasamilla
    Ee jagathe yudharicha yeshuparanaam jala
    Thaarakamozhinjenikkillaashrayamennum. K.V.S
