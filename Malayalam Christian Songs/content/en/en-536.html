• Enthubhaagyam jeevithathil!
    Enthu modam ennullathil!
    En paapa bhaaramellaam
    Neengippoyi halleluyya!
  1. Paapathin kuzhiyil kidannu
    Paaram kenu valanjavan njaan
    Parama santhoshamullil
    Pakarnninnu puthugeetham paadidunnu
  2. Aadyamenneyavan snehichu
    Aadiyugangalkku munname njaan
    Agathiyaamenne daivam
    Anugrahich avakaashi yaakkiyallo
  3. Than ninam moolamaay mochanam thannu
    Than swanthamaakkiyenne
    Avaniniyennilum njaan
    Avanilumaakayaal bhaagyavaanaam
  4. Aayiram pathinaayirathil
    Athisundaranen manuvel
    Avanennum maadhuryavaan
    Aarumill enikkithupoloruvan. C.J
