• Anugamichidum njaanen parane
    Parane- kurishil marichuyirtha naadhane
    Innum ennum maattamillaatha vallabhane
  1. Mama kodum paapam theerkkuvaan thaan kaninjenno!
    Vimalajen jeevan tharuvathinum thuninjenno!
  2. Shodhana palathum mediniyithil undennaalum
    Vedanayekum velakalere vannaalum-
  3. Vanditha paada sevayathenn abhilaasham
    Nindithanaayi theeruvathaan abhimaanam
  4. Ksheenithanaayi kshoniyil njaan thalarumbol
    Aanikaletta paanikalaalavan thaangum-
  5. Koorirul vazhiyil koottinnu koode varum thaan
    Vairikal naduvil nalla virunnu tharum thaan-
  6. Nanmayum krupayum pinthudarum enne yennum
    Vinmaya veettil nithyatha muzhuvan njaan vaazhum M.E.C
