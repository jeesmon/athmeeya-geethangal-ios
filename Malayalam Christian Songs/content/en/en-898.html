• Ephesos sabhayodu Rev. 2:1-7



    Kaanthe! nee kelkka kaamineemaule!
  1. Than valankaramathil thaarakelezhum thaangikondanisham
    Thankavilakkuka-lezhinum naduve-thankunnen vachanam
  2. Ninte pravruthiyum yatnavum dushtare vittakalum pathivum
    Ninte sahishnuthaa sheelavuminnu njaan kandirikkunnu priye
  3. Kallayapposthalar vellavesham dharichulloru velayil nee
    Kallarennaayavar thammeyarinjudan thalliyathum sukrutham-
  4. Aarumilakidumaarath-ighoramaay-aanjadikkum pakayinna
    Aapperunkaattilen naamavumenthi nee ninnathu vandhyatharam-
  5. Enkilumonnu neeyorkkukaye ninakk-aadiyil-aadivasam
    Kondirunnulloru sneha-mathe vidham undo innu thava?-
  6. Perinanusari chullorayavu the vannathil ninniniyum
    Vegamunarnnu ninnaadya-nadapadikkothu nadannukolka-
  7. Alleyannaakil njaan vannu nina-kkippozhulla prakaashamathum
    Allupolaakkidum nin vilakkingu njaan pinvalichidumudan
  8. Ente janangale koymakalennapol kaalkkeezh thaazhthivarum
    Shuntdikalaakiya nikkolaviyare nee verukkunnu shari-
  9. Njaanumaho! pakackkunnavare jayaalikalkkokkeyum njaan
    Devaparudeesin jeeva-vrukshaphalam thinmaanaay kodukkum-
  10. Yeshuvinnaaviyinneevidham chonnidunn ephesos sabhaye!
    Aarkku cheviyundo aayavanee vachass-aadaraal kettidatte. K.V.S
