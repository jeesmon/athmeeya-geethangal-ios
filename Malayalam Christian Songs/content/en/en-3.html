• Yeshuvinte thirunaamathinu
    Ennumennum sthuthi sthothrame
  1. Vaanilum bhoovilum melaaya naamam,
    Vandhitha vallabha naamamathu
    Doothar vaazhthi pukazhthidum naamamathu
  2. Paapathil jeevikkum paapiye rakshippan
    Paarithil vannoru naamamathu
    Paralokathil cherkkum naamamathu
  3. Uthama bhakthanmaar vaazhthi pukazhthidum
    Unnathamaam daiva naamamathu
    Ulakengum dhwanikkunna naamamathu
  4. Sankadom chanchalam shodhana velayil
    Thaangi nadathidum naamamathu
    Bhayam muttumakattidum naamamathu. M.E.C
