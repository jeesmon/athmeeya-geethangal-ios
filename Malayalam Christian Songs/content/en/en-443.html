• Madhuratharam thiruvedam
    maanasa modavikaasam
  1. Tharumithu nithyam parichayichidil
    Niravadhi nanmakalundaam
    parama dhanamithil kandaal
  2. Vaanoli neengi irulumanneram
    Bhaanuvin deepthipol ninnu
    bhaass arulidumithennum
  3. Bahuvidha kashtamaam kaippukal moolam
    Madhuram asheshavum poke
    madhuvithu nalkidum chaale
  4. Niswatha ninne nikrunaakkumbol
    Ratna vyaapaaritha thanne
    prathna dhaniyaakkum ninne
  5. Ajnjanu jnjaanam andhanu nayanam
    Nalikidum eeshwara vachanam
    pulkidunnu vijnjarithine. K.V.S
