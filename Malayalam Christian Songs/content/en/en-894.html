• Enthu kaala thaamasam vibho- ennethirilennu kandidum prabho?
  1. Eluthallorkkuvathinee kashtam velivaay sahippaan kazhivumill
    Eliyavarkk nin thunayallaathenthu thelivaayundinganyadhaa
  2. Peruthaayulla kashta kheda durithamivayil ninnu vegaal
    Cheruthaam thannude sabhaye cherppaan varumennennu cholkidilum
  3. Thiru shareeram baliyaayente durithashamanam varuththuvaanaay
    Kurishil maranamathinnozhinjoru paramasneham albhutham
  4. Sathathavum manathilangettam chithamallaathulla ninavumoolam
    Adhikamaay kalangidumeeyalavil hithamurachu kaakkuvaan
  5. Thiru bharanam bhooviyurappicharikulangale haranam cheythu
    Parama salem puri vasicheedharayilennum vaazhuvaan. K.V.S
