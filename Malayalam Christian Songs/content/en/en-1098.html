1. Padam padam urachu naam krooshumenthi pokanam
    Jeevanenkil jeevan vechu bhaaratham nedidanam
  2. Paapathanthrya badharaam bhaaratheeya sodarare!
    Paapa bhaaram neekkidum yeshuvinkal odi vaa!
  3. Poruvin yuvaakkale! cheruvin bhraathaakkale!
    Kristhunaadhan porinnaay odi odi kooduvin!
  4. Swaathanthryamaarkkum labhyamaam kristhuvinte krooshathil
    Swathanthraminnu khoshikkaam bhaarathathilengume
  5. Jeevane thyajichathaam raktha saakshi samkhathil
    Aayudhathe-yenthi naamaayodhanam cheythidanam
  6. Peedakal naduvilum paalanam cheythidume
    Paavanaatma daayakan paarileshu naayakan
  7. Snehathinnirippidam thyaagathin vilanilam
    Rakshayin sankethavum yeshu raajan thanneyaam.
