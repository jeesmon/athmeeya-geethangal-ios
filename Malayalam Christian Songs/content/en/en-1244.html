• Yeshu kristhu jeevikkunnu halleluyya!
    Khoshippin jayam! jayam! halleluyya!
  1. Jeevante naayakanaay vaazhuvaanaay
    Chaavine jaichonu halleluyya!
    Daivathin puthranavanennarivaan
    Evanum kazhivippol halleluyya!-
  2. Thaazhuvaan kurisholam vannavan thaan
    Vaazhunnu hrudayathil halleluyya!
    Paazhaakum ullathil thaan ezhunnallumbol
    Maarunnu dukhamellaam halleluyya!
  3. Kevalam paapathinu chathavaraay
    Mevuka yeshuvil naam halleluyya!
    Daivathin ishtathinnu jeevikka naam
    Jeevante puthukkathil halleluyya!
  4. Yeshuvin uyirppinaal jeevanullor
    Aashayin amshikal naam halleluyya!
    Naashavum maalinyavum than raajyathil
    Leshavum kaankayilla halleluyya!
  5. Edenil jeevavruksham poyathinaal
    Khedikka venda thellum halleluyya!
    Chetham ennennuka ellaatteyum naam
    Neduvaan yeshuvine halleluyya!
  6. Innum ennekkum yeshu maattam vinaa
    Minnum neethiyin sooryan halleluyya!
    Vinnum ee mannum than thruppaadathinkal
    Vandikkum bhakthiyode halleluyya! V.N
