• Raajaadi raajanaam yeshuvine
    Arraadikkunnathu nallathe
    • En maname nee stothram paadu
      Unnathane nee vaazhthippaadu
      Ninne rakshicha daivamavan (2)
  1. Devaadi devanaam yeshu devan
    Devaattin kuttiyaay krooshileri
    En maname nin shaapam maari
    Nin manabhaaram theere maari
    Ninne daivathin paithalaaki (2)
  2. Illillithupol oruvanum nin
    Praanente rakshackkaay praanen thannon
    En maname nee aaraadikku aatmaavilum sathyathilum
    Ninte jeevante jeevanavan (2)
  3. Raajaavaay paaril pirannavannaay
    Shreeyeshu allaatheyaarumilla
    En maname nin jeevakaalam
    Than mahatwam nee ghoshikkuka
    Ninte jeevante udayavan thaan (2)
