• Halleluiah He is risen
  1. ayam jayam yeshuvinnu divya rakshakan ithaa!
    Chaavin kallarayil ninnu uyirthu halleluyya!
    • Jayam jayam halleluyya vaazhka jeevanaayaka!
      Jayam jayam halleluyya vaazhka jeevadaayaka!
  2. Chatha karmmangalil ninnu yeshu namme rakshichu
    Nammil jeevikkunnathinnu thannethaan prathishtdichu
  3. Mruthyuvin bhayankarangal neengi than uyirppinaal
    Nithyajeevante imbangal vannu suvisheshathaal
  4. Manmayamaam ee shareeram aathma mayamaakuvaan
    Kaahalam dhwanikkunneram kalppichidum rakshakan
  5. Neduveerppum kannuneerum dukhavum vilaapavum
    Nodineram kondu theerum pinneyillor shaapavum
  6. Jeevanulla rakshithaavin koode naamum jeevikkum
    Ennennekkum than pithaavin raajyathil aanandikkum. V.N
