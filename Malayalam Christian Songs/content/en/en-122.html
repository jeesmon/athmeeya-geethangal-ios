• Sthuthichu paadam yeshuvine
    Stuthikalil vasikkumunnathane
    Sthothravum sthuthiyum maanam mahathwavum
    Arppichu vaazhthi sthuthichidaam
  1. Paapam niranjoree dharaniyilannu
    Paapiyaamennethedi nee vannu;
    Nithymaam jeevane daanamay ekiya
    Kristheshu naadhane sthuthichiduvin-
  2. Anavadhi krupakal anubhavichidaan
    Anuvadikkunnenne yanudinavum
    Anthyatholamen krooshumeduthini
    Anugamikkum njaaneemaru yaathrayil-
  3. Karthanin nanmakal keerthikkum njaanennum
    Maanavaril cheythor athbuthangal
    Nandiyaalennum ninne pukazthidum
    Nashwaramaamee paaridathil-
  4. Kandidumoru naalen priya kaanthane
    Shudharodonnichang-aaraadhikkum
    Maranavum shaapavum maariyathorthennum
    Paadidaam jaya jaya geethangal. I.M
