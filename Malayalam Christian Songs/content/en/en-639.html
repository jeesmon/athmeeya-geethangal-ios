1. Jeevanulla devane varoo Jeeva vaakyam othuvaan varoo
    Paapathe veruthu jeevippaan Papa bodham njangalil tharoo
    • Yeshuve nee valiyavan Yeshuve nee parishudhan
      Yeshuve nee nallavan, Yeshuve nee vallabhan
  2. Maanasam kaninjiduvanaay gaanamaalyam ekiduvanaay
    Aavasikka ente dehiyil nee vasikka ente jeevanil
  3. Vaagnathangal cheytha karthane vaakkumaaraathunmayullone
    Vaagnathangalkkaayi varunnu vallabhamaatmamaari nalkane
  4. Nyaayavidhi naal varunnithaa priyan varaan kaalmaayallo
    Lokathil nashichu pokunna lokare nee rakshichidane.
