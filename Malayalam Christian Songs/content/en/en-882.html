• Enneshuve neeyaasrayam
    Ee bhoovathil neeyaasrayam
  1. Marthyarilaasrayam enikkilla
    Prabhukkanmaarilumillenikke
    Ente aasrayam yaah thanne
    Shaashwathamaayoru paara thanne-
  2. Dhanathilaasraymenikkilla
    Saundaryathilumillenikke
    Parama sambannan en maanuvel
    Atheeva sundaren mal praanen thaan-
  3. Lokathilen manam chaayukayilla
    Sambathilen manam veezhukilla
    Ennavakaashavum ohariyum
    Ennude pankellaam thaathan thanne-
  4. Orikkal vannidum enne cherppaan
    Nin maarodananjidum ezhayaam njaan
    Aanikaletta paani njaan kande
    Sundaravadanathil muthidum njaan. A.N
