• tUNE: Yeshu nallavan enikku yeshu
  1. Mannil vannavan namukku jeevan thannavan
    Moonnaam naaluyarthezhunnu vinnil chennavan
    Veendum varumennarulicheytha divya rakshakan
    Vegam vannidum - vegam vannidum
  2. Mruthyuvinn adimayaayirunna nammale
    Raktham chinthi veendeduthu kristhu naayakan
    Nithyathayil namukku vendi veedorukki thaan
    Vegam vannidum - vegam vannidum
  3. Vithu chumannu kannuneeril naam vithackkukil
    Katta chumannu kondu nammalaarthu koshikkum
    Karthan kaikal kannuneer thudackkum naalithaa-
    Vegam vannidum - vegam vannidum
  4. Vinnil kaahaladhwani muzhangum nerathil
    Mannil maranja shudharumuyirthu naamumaay
    Vinnil chernnu priyan paadam chumbichidum naal
    Vegam vannidum - vegam vannidum
  5. Nithya nirvruthi shirassil peri vaazhumaa-
    Prathyaashayaal sthothra geetham paadi vaazhthaam naam
    Nithyathayilethi nammal vishramikkum naal
    Vegam vannidum - vegam vannidum
