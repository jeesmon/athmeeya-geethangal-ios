• Ethra stuthichuvennaalum ethra nandi cholliyaalum
    Karthaavu cheytha nanmackku prathyupakaaramaakumo?
  1. Mruthyuvin hasthathil ninnum
    Shathruvin asthrathil ninnum
    Bhruthyanaamente praanane kaatha than dayayenthatbhutham-
  2. Kastanastangal vanaalum dustaloka pakachaalum
    Karthaavil njaan santhoshikkum
    Kastangalil prashamsikkum-
  3. Chitham kalangumanneram karthaneyullil dyaanichu
    Dukham marannu paadum njaan
    Uchathil stothra geethangal-
  4. Theeraa vishaadangal thernnu
    Thoraatha kannuneer thornnu
    Karthaavin paadam chernnu njaan nithyayugangal vaanidum. G.P
