• Seeyone nee unarnn-ezhunnelkkuka
    Shaalem raajenithaa varuvaaraay
    Sheelagunamulla snehaswaroopan
    Aakaasha-meghathil ezhunnalli varume
  1. Anthya-samayangal aduthupoye priyare!
    Swantham-enneshuve saakshikkuvin
    Andhakaara prabhu velippedum mumbe
    Santhosha raajyathil chernnidume naam-
  2. Bhoo mandhalangal ulanjilakidunnu
    Vaanangal aakave nadungidunnu
    Premamattulla nin saadhukkal karalil
    Khedam poondangingu veenuzhalunnu-
  3. Sainyathin shakthiyaal raajyangal aakave
    Thakarnnudanjidunnu dinam-dinamaay
    Sainyathin shakthiyaal onnilumalle
    Aatmabalathaal jayam neduka naam-
  4. Thiru-sabhaye! nin deepangalennume
    Suryaprabhapol vilangidatte
    Mahithan than thejassil ezhunnalli varumbol
    Udalode priyane eithirelppaan pokaam-
