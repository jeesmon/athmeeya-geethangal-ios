• Kaanunnu njaan yaahil
    Enikkaasrayamaayoru shashwatha paara
  1. Susthira maanasanavanumen yaahil
    Aasrayam vackkil anudinam naadan
    Kaathidumavane nal swasthathayode
    Paarthivanavan thirukarangalilennum-
  2. Kunnukalakalum van parvvatha nirayum
    Thannidam vittu pinmaariyennaalum
    Neengukillavan daya ennil ninnathupol
    Nilanilkkum samaadaana niyamavum nithyam-
  3. Jwalikkilumavan kopam kshananeram maathram
    Nilanilkkum prasaadamo jeevanthyatholam
    Vasikkilum nilavili raavilenkoode
    Udikkume ushassathil aanandaghosham-
  4. Cheyvathillavan nammal paapathinothapol
    Prathiphalamarulunnilla kruthyangal ganichum
    Vaanamee bhoovilninnuyarnnirippathu pol
    Paran daya bhakthanmel valiyathu thanne-
  5. Odikkukillavanettam chathanjathaam oda
    Keduthukillavan thiri puka vamikkukilum
    Nadathum than vidhi jayam labhikkum naalvareyum
    Thalaraathe avan bhoovil sthaapikkum nyaayam-
  6. Vazhuthidaathavanenne karangalil kaathu
    Nirthum than mahimayin savidhathil naadan
    Kalankamattaananda poornnathayode
    Bhavikkatte mahathwamangavanennum amen. E.V.V
