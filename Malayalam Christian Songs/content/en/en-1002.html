• Mannavanaam mashihaaye kaanaanji-ttente
    Kannukalum paaramithaa thalarunnu
  1. Bhoothalathe suvishesha naadathaal-moda-
    Poorithamaakkidendavar nila thetti
    Khora rana-sannadharaayidunnu chithil
    Oruvathupolumaakaa gunasindho!-
  2. Loka nanma yulavaakum shaasthrathe-yippol
    Loka-nasheekaranathin yanthrangal
    Aakruthippeduthuvaanaay sarvvesha! paapa-
    Poorithanmaar thuniyunnu-paarthaayo?-
  3. Yudha sannadharaayayyo! kristhyanmaar-khora-
    Yudhamathinirangunnu dayaneeyam;
    Bombukalum taankukalum bhuvanathe theere-
    Chaambalaakki kkalayunnu-paarthaayo?-
  4. Lokanaadhaa! ninte divyakaarunya-nethram
    Shok asamudwignaraaya njangal mel
    Nee thirichu bhayankara baadhakale bhoovin-
    Meethil ninnu neekkuka nee vegathil-
  5. Yaathoru nimishavum nee kalayaathe-
    mekhavaahanathilezhunnalli viravode
    Thaavakamaam chirakadi thannil nee kaathu
    Paalanam arulvaan bhoovil vannaalum-
  6. Yudhabheri ninaadamall adiyangal-kkiniyuthamamaam
    thirunaama madhurathaal
    Chithamathu kulirthidaan arulenam saathaanidharaye
    pilarkkumitharunathil-
  7. Agni choornnamathin gandham gaganathil ninnu
    Nishkramichu pushpagandha mathu nalkum
    Kunda kusumaadikalaal charchitha vaayumandhalamarulka
    njangalkkakhileshaa! K.V.S
