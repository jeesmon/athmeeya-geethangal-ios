1. Paapa bhaarakkadalilaandu valayuvoree lokare
    Thaapamaatti kkarayanachoreshu devaa! vandanam
  2. Neethiyenthenn arivathinnu praapthiyillaa njangalil
    Neethimathwam karuna moolam nalkiyone! vandanam
  3. Galamodichu kalayavendum kazhuthakalkku thullyaraay
    Veliyil ninnoreliya njangal nee nimitham muktharaay
  4. Kallilullezhuthu maachu njangal thannullangalil
    Nalla ninvazhi thelichu thanna naadhaa vandanam
  5. Theere-yeliyoradiyaar cheyyumeeyaaraadhanam Paaramaam karuna poondu sweekarikka saadaram
  6. Keerthanangal paadi njangal nithyakaalam ninnude
    Sathamamaam sannidhiyil vaazhumaaraakename. K.V.S
