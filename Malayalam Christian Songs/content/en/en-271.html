• Nin rakthathaal
    nin rakthathaalenne shudhanaakki
    Nin neethiyaal yehovaye
  1. Kaalvary maamalamukal
    eriya daiva kunjaade
    Kaikaal virichokrooshinmel
    krooramaam mulmudi choodiyo?
  2. Paapathilaandu jeevicha
    nindithanaamen perkkaayi
    Paapamenthann ariyaathone
    nin rudhiramkrooshil chinthiyo?
  3. Krooranaam saathaan than thala
    krooshil chathacha veerane!
    Shaapathin keezhil ninnenneyum
    Shaashwatha maarggathilaakki nee-
  4. Nin thirunaamam vaazhthuvaan
    nin puthugaanam paaduvaan
    Shaapam niranja en naavine
    shakthippeduthi ninrakthathaal. P.A.J
