• Innum-ennum enn-aashrayamaay ingini yeshu mathiyaam
    Ennaadhiyellaam onnaayakannu pokunnu than chaare varumbol
  1. Njaanaashrayikkum en daivam-enne anaadhanaay kaividumo?
    Kanneer thudachum kaikal pidichum kaathidum kanmanipole-
  2. Jeevan vedinja enjeeva naadhan jeevikkunn-athyunnathanaay
    Avan-und-enikk ellaamaay-ennum avaniyil karuthuvaanaay-
  3. Kashtathakalil maaraatha nalla karthaav-enikkullathinaal
    Kalangaathe-ulakil pularunnu dinavum krupayaale ha halleluyya! C.J
