• Tune: Naam vimukthanmaar


    Ente priyano avan enikkullavan
    Avan ennekkumaayenne veendeduthavan
  1. Ithramahal snehamaarilum njaan
    Idharayilengum kaanunnilla
    Enne swanthamaakkiduvaan
    Enperkkaay raktham chorinjavan thaan-
  2. Lokathin sthdaapanam muthalenikkaay
    Arukkappetta daiva kunjaadavan
    Paapaashaapam neekkiduvaan
    Priyan shaapamaayi kroosh-athinmel -
  3. Aayirathil pathinaayirathil
    Sarvvaanga sundaranenpriyan thaan
    Praanathullyam snehichathaal
    Thanjeevan nalki maruvilayaay -
  4. Nandiyaalennullam niranjidunne
    Sthothrangal paadi njaan vaazhthidume
    Jeevanulla kaalamellaam haa!
    Nandiyode sthuthichidume. T.V.S
