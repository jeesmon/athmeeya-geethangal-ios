• Snehichidum ninne njaan- kristheshuve
    Sevichidum ninne njaan
    Sevikkum njaanente-jeevakaalamellaam
    Jeevan vedinjenne-snehicha naadhane
  1. Innu muthal njaanumen-
    Kudumbavum ninne maathram sevikkum
    Ninne sevikkumbol neridum kashtangal
    Ennum kshamayode -ellaam sahikkum njaan-
  2. Bendhu janangalenne vedinjaalum
    Santhatham sevikkum njaan
    Bendhuve poolenne -sandhaaranam cheyyum
    Thanthayaa meshuve-santhoshamode njaan-
  3. Lokam dweshichidilum-oru naalum shoka-ppedukayilla
    Lokathil ninnenne-nithya muyarthi swar-
    Lokathil cherkkum shreeyeshu rakshakane-
  4. Sarvva lokangalekkaal-valiyavan urvviyil neeyenikku
    Sarvva janangalum-enne vedinjaalum
    Sarvvadaa njaan ninne-sevichidum mudaa-
  5. Vakrathayullavarin mandhye-
    Dinam salkramamaay jeevippaan
    Ee krumikkethum kazhivilla devaa, nin
    Ulkkrushtamaam krupayere nalkidenam. K.V.C
