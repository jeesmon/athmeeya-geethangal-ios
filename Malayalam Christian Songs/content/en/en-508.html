• En jeevanaayaka! enneshuve!
    Innum ennum onnupole kaathidum vibho!
    Nee nallavan- nee vallabhan enneshuve- en rakshaka
  1. Marthyaraam sakhaakkal evarum pirinjupom
    Mruthyuvinte naalathil pirinjidaathe nee
    Nithyavum nadathidunna snehamorthu njaan
    Kristhunaayaka ninakku paadidunnithaa-
  2. Paaridam kulungiyaakave maranjidum
    Neravum kulungidaathe ninte maaridam
    Charidaan orukkidunna nalla naayakan
    Maaridaatha snehithan neeyonnu maathrame-
  3. Nalla paalakan enikkinn ullathaalini
    Allalilla thellume neeyethra vallabhan!
    Allilum pakalilum nadathidaamidam
    Cholliya nin vaakkenikk angundinn-aashrayam. G.K
