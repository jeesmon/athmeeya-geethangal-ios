• Nithyamaam raksha danamaay thannavane
    Ninne nithyam sthuthichidunnen
    Nin ninam chorinju
    veendeduthathinaal
    Nandiyode njaan paadidume

  1. Pharavonnadimayaay theernnathaam
    enne nin suthanaakkiduvaan
    Vedinju swargga mahimayakhilam
    Thaanuvannu mannithil nee
    thannu jeevan pakaramaay

  2. Kaalithozhuthathil heenanaay
    kaalvaryathil ekanaay
    Daahajalathinu kenavan nee
    praananeki yaagamathaay
    chinthi raktham maruvilayay

  3. Unnathanaamen daivame!
    prapanchathin paripaalakane
    Athbhuthame paapiyil nin sneham
    Avarnnaneeyam agaadhame
    apprameyam athishayame

  4. Ee mahal snehathe orkkumbol
    enthu njaanithinn ekidume
    Ponnu paadam thannilananju
    veenuvanangi namichidunne
    Vaazhthippaadi sthuthichidunne. T.V.S
