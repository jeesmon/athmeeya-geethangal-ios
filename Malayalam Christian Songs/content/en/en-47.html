    Sthuthichiduva-thenthaanandam!-
    shree yeshudevane
    Sthuthichiduva-thenthaanandam!-
  1. Modamode naamevarum thiruppaadam thannilathiyaadaram
    Veenu vandanam cheythukondu
    Santhosha sangeetham chernnu paadi-
  2. Kaadu keriyoraadu pole
    njaan-aadaloduzhanneedave
    Thedi vannenikkennum-aashrayam
    nedi maarilanacha devane-
  3. Aayiram pathinaayirangali-
    laarilumathi sundaran
    Aayidu makhilaanda naayaka-
    naashritheshwaraneshu devane-
  4. Periya khedavum peedanangalum-
    peruki vannidum velayil
    Thiruchirakathi-nullilaashraya maruli
    Raksha tharunna devane-
  5. Loka jaathikalengu mevarumaake-
    yaakularengilum
    Aashritharkka mithaarthi theerthidu-
    Maartha naadhanaameshu devane. M.E.C
