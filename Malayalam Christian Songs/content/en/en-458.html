    Per cholli vilichu mumbe nadakkum
    Njaanathu shravichu pimbe gamikkum

  2. Pachamechilinkal enne kidathi
    Swatcha vellathinkal enne nadathi
    Thirichennaathmaave thante naamathaal
    Neethiyin vazhiye nadathunnathaal
  3. Chaavin nizhalinte thaazhvarayil njaan

    Thankolum vadiyum aashwasippikkum-
  4. Shathrukkal mumbaake meshayorukki
    Thannen shirassaake pushunnenna nee
    Ente paanapaathram nirayunnithaa
    Ninakku njaan sthothram cheyyum sarvvada-
  5. Aayushkkaalamaake nanma kaarunyam
    Pinthudarumenne athen laavanyam
