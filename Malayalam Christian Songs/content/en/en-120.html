    Athyunnathan suthane-Aarambha kaaranane
    Angaye sthuthichidum-Aaraadhi chaanandikkum
    Sri YesuKristhuRaaja!
  1. Kaalvary maamalayil kaarirumbaanikalaal
    Krooshithanaay ente paapangal pokkiyathaal
    Nandiyaay kumbidunnu--
  2. Kashtathilaashrayam nee dukhathil saanthwanavum
    Shathru kaiyyil ninnum maamaka mochanavum
    Nin krupayonnu maathram--
  3. Jaathi vamshangal madhye sathyathin saakshiyaayi
    Iruliloru deepam pol ninnil njaan shobhichidaan
    Arulenam, ninkrupakal--
  4. Aatmaavam daivame nin shakthiyil njaan nadappaan
    Paavanahrudayam ennil pakarnnidum nee
    Paarilen naalkalellaam--E.P.J
