• Parameshajaathaa! vandanam sadaa ninakku
    Parameshajaathaa! vandanam
  1. Parama ninmahimayee dharaniyil theliyippaan
    Naranaay-janicha-suranaa-yakanaam--
  2. Irulin bharanamellaa nararilum parannathaal
    Arule-kiyana-ratham kaa-thidunna--
  3. Duritha phalamaayulla marana mathinkal ninnu
    Thiru vee-ndeduppaal-viduvi-chu bhavaan--
  4. Sharanamenikku bhavaan paramoru gathiyilla
    Paramaa-thmaja ni-nnadikoo-ppidunnen--
  5. Thirusareera menikkaay kurishathil baliyaakki
    Thirunee-thi phali-ppathinnaay shramicha--
  6. Marichu thirike jeevi-cha marthyanaay bhavichathinaal
    Maranathe neekkaanadhikaaramaarnna. K.V.S
