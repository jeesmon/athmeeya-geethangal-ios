1. Enne rakshichunathan
    Than kodenum parkuvan
    Thante sahajeevitham danam cheythitha
    Mmanidam chamachavan manide charichavan
    Enne enum nadathunath enthorathbhutham
  2. Thante kroshil kanuna snehathinte purnatha
    Ente rakshayathil thaan nivarthichu
    Bandhanastham cheythu than anthyamaya yachana
    Swanthana jeevithathe bandhamay nalki
  3. Ethum bhethiyeniye thathanodu kode naam
    Modamay jeevikkunu thanteyaviyal
    Ethanarthham kasheangal sadhuvinundayenal
    Adhiyudan nekidu men rajarajan than
  4. Ente raksha daname enumull ashwasame
    Onnum cheyithittale njaan thanude krupa
    Manidathin kroshathil ninumuyarnuyarnu
    Unathamaam swargathilen vaasavum nalki
  5. Bhoovil swargajeevitham arambhichedunitha
    Aviyude vasamo jeeva sawrabhyam
    Neethi samadhanavum athma santhoshamathum
    Daivarajyam buvathil pinne swargavum.
