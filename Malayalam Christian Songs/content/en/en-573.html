• Savior like a Shepherd
  1. Rakshaka! nin aadukalin
    mumbil nadakkename
    Lokathil ee saadhukkalin
    sharanam nee maathrame
    Nalla mechil thannidenam
    dinamthorum yeshuve!
  2. Ninte janathin mel
    paapam ottum bharikkaruthe
    Seenaay malayude shaapam
    aayathil pedaruthe
    Krupayil nee kaathukolka
    nee veendeduthavare
  3. Daiva bhakthiyude vesham
    lokar dharichidunnu
    Athin shakthiyo ashesham
    thalli-kkalanjidunnu
    Sathyathil nee kaathu-kolka
    njangal njerangidunnu
  4. Sathyathilum aatmaavilum
    daivathinnu vandanam
    Cheyvaan ellaa nerathilum
    aatma daanam tharenam
    Yeshuve! nee mandhyathinkal
    ellaayppozhum varenam
  5. Saathaan velichathin doothen
    enna-pole aduthaal
    Ninne ariyaatha mooddan
    njangal-kkethirthu ninnaal
    Veezhaathe nee kaathu-kolka
    nin vishudharude kaal
  6. Agniyilum vellathilum koodi
    nee kadathumbol
    Udan nin tholukalilum
    njangale chummannu-kol
    Bhayam vendaa njaanaakunnu
    ennu kanivodu chol
  7. Nohayude praavinodu
    thullyaraakum njangalkku
    Kaiye neetti preethiyodu
    vaasam thaa nin akathu
    Yeshuve! nee olippikka
    njangale nin maaraathu
  8. Snehathinte agnijwaala
    ullil jwalippikkuke
    Aatma phalangalin maala
    bhangikkaay kettename
    Anthyatholam kaathu-kolka
    ninte aattin koottathe. V.N
