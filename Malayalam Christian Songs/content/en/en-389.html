• Mannayil varnnanamaa-moru kada
    Chinthanam cheythiduvin
    Unnathanaam daivam vinnulakil ninnu
    Thanjanathinu koduthoru daanyamaam

  1. Ettam cheriyathallo-bhujippathi-
    Nnettoru daanyamithe
    Uttam peruthoru naadanaam mannayi-
    Ngettam cheriyavanaayirunnillayo-
  2. Illamuna cheruthum-urundoru nallavadivithinu
    Nallavaneshuvum koorppiyalaavidham
    Thanneyathre bhoovil jeevanam cheythathe-
  3. Thaan tharayil mannakidannupol
    Thaanuvannente naadan
    Thenoli vaakkukal chonnaanathokkeyum
    Swaadulla mannayil kaanunnu njaanithaa-
  4. Manjilulpaadithamaay- manna
    Vishudhaaviyil yeshu thaanum
    Kastamidipodiyetta mannaayente
    Istanaam naadane orkkumaaraakkunnu-
  5. Aarkkumeduthukolva-neluppamaay
    Veettumuchathu mannaa
    Pattil kidakkunnu naadanumavannam
    Nokki vilippavarkkellaam sameepasthan-
  6. Aarkkum thikavarulum-mannayithu
    Theerkkum pashi, daahavum
    Swargga kanaannathir thannilethum vare
    Deerkha naal nilkkumi shaashwatha bhojanam. K.V.S
