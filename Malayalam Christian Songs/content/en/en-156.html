• Vandaname deva! Thava vandaname
    Vanningalaminnum krupa thannidukamannaa! Thava
  1. Ninnude sannidhi thannilanayumee
    Mandaraam daasare nokki krupa
    Thannadiyaarude khinnathayaakave
    Thallidenam sthiraraakki
    Chinneettiha nin rudhiram mannichu mahaa duritham
    Vinnil sthiramaam nagaram
    Thanningithilenthaparam-
  2. Sathyaatma sevakar chernnu nadathumee
    hrudya samaajamingennum-vala
    nnathyantha shobhayodidharayil prabhaa
    poorthiyaruluvaaninnum
    vardhichoru nin krupaya yardhithu; shamam hrudaye
    vardhippathinissamaye shradhikka suthaarthanaye-
  3. Anya sahaayamillinnararkkenniha
    Nannaayarnjeeduvaanum-bhoovi
    Thannidum geevitham ninnude sevayil
    Thane nayichiduvaanum-
    Nin salkrupa nalkidane virnnorkkani bhupamane!
    Thinmakkethiraayirane vanporiduvaan varane.
  4. Yisrael makkale seen marubhoomiyil
    Thangi nadathiya deva! Dukha
    Mishramaam geevitha neekiyenne kanaan
    Naattilayakkane yohaa!
    Yordhaan jada van nadiye maattiduka; salgathiye
    Chertheettah; durvidhiye theerthidu sabhaapathiye!- K.V.S
