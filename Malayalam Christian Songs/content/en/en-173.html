• (“Psalm 136”)
    Atbhuthavaane athishayavaane
    Aaraadikkunnu ninne njaanaaraadikkunnu
    Daivaadi daivathinnu stothram cheyyuvin
    Karthaadi karthaavine vaazhthi paaduvin
  1. Jnaanathode vaanathe nirmmichavane
    Bhoomiye vellathinmel virichavane
    Prabha choriyaan thaarakam nirmmichavane
    Aayussulla kaalamennum stuthi karettidaam-

  2. Chenkadalil vazhi thuranna daivam neeyallo
    Yisrayele vazhi nadathiya shakthan neeyallo
    Shathruve samharichu swantha janathe nee
    Poral lesham eshidaathe marukarayetti-
  3. Eri vailil marubhoovil mekha sthambhamaay
    Koorirul thaazhvarayil agni sthambhamaay
    Maaraye madhuramaakki anudinamenne
    Jayathode nathuvaan shakthanaayavane-
  4. Baashaane ogine seehoneyum nee
    Samharichu deshathe veendukondu nee
    Yisrayelinavakasham ekiyavan nee
    Ennennum vaazhthipaadaan yogyanum neeye-
  5. Thaazhchayil enneyorthu swayam vedinjavane
    Shathruvin kayyil ninnenne veendavane
    Annannu vendathaam manna thannavane
    Anthyamaam shwaasam vare aaraadikkum jnaan-
