• Daivamangorukkidum bhavanamundathil
    Thaamasam koodaathe njaanum chernnidume
    Doothasamgham aarthu paadum puthen paattukal
    Njaanum cherunnu halleluyya geetham paadum
  1. Innu njaan pokumee koorirul paathayil
    Ennumen yeshuve nokki nokki poyidum
    Theerumente bheethiyum maarumen anarthdavum
    En priyane kaanum nerathil-
  2. Ee maruyaathrayil veenidunna velayil
    Yeshuvinte ponkaram thaangidunnu nithyavum
    Maarumente ksheenavum theerumente kleshavum
    Swantha veettil cherum naalathil-
  3. Enthinee thaamasam mal-priyan vannidaan
    Ethra naalee bhoomiyil kaathukaathu paarkkanam
    Nerilonnu kandu ente kaanthanodu cheruvaan
    En manam kothichu kaakkunne. P.B.S
