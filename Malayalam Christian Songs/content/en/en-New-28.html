• Tune: Teri Duniya Se

    Srishtavam daivasuthan
    Namme sampanar akuvan
    Daridranaayi, dasanayi,
    Dushta bhovil vanu.
  1. Thanishta mohangngalil nadannu
    Daivaththil ninnetam akannu
    Dushta vikarangalil nashichu
    Daivakopam eta namude
    Shikshakal krooshiletu marichu
    Pakshavadam cheythinu jeevichu
    Rakshichu thankoodenum
    Iruthidan veendum varum.
  2. Yeshunathane arinjum
    Parishudhathmaval niranjum
    Yeshukristhuvil vasichum
    Athmaphalam adhikam niranjum
    Ulsukarayi pravarthichu
    Vishvastharakum sakshikalayidam
    Thalsamayam tharum than krupakal
  3. Ithramam krupaye maranum
    Karthan vazhikalil ninnu akanum
    Shathruvin valayil kudungi thathra
    Jnjanathal patharathe
    Puthrane thanathathen sneham
    Ethramathra menarinjidam
    Ethrayum vegam kanthan varunalo. M.P
