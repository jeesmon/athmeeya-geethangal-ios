• Thiruvadanam shobhippiche-nnirulakale pokkiduvaan
    Karunaavaaridhe! daivame-namichidunne
    Nirukaravum kooppithozhunnen
  1. Parimala thailathaal ninte-shiramabhishekam cheythoru
    Mariyayil athyantham kaarunyam- chorinja naadha
    Varamarulidenamivannu--
  2. Arivararin sainyam kandu paravashanaay theeraathe njaan
    Irupuravum ninnekkaanuvaan hrudayakankal
    Urukrupayaal thurakkename--
  3. Paramaguro! nin naamathe-kkaruthiyenikkundaakunna
    Karumanakal nithyam sahippaan arulka devaa!
    Kurishil marichullor eeshane!--
  4. Eravukazhinjathi mohanamaam arunan udichidum pozhuthum
    Tharani maranjidum neravum bhavalsmaranam
    Varnamenikkullil swairamay
  5. Aranimisham ninne vittaa-larikilenikkaarullayyo!
    Marimakane, nin sugandhamaam- shwaasavaayuven
    Karalinurappekunn eppozhum--
  6. Marana dinatholam ninte-charanayugam sevichaathma
    Sharanamathaay ninne praapichu-ninnil layippaanarulaname
    divyaashissukal. K.V.S
