• Bhoorasamaanasa maarnnidum- perggamos sabhe!
    Nee shravikkennude vachanam
    Aryamoorchayulliru dhaarayulla vaalvahikku-
    Nnorahitha kulanaashan yeshuvothunnarikithu
  1. Ninnude paarppidamevidam-ennu njaanariyunnu
    saathaante simhaasanam
    Ullidam avide eni-kkulla naamamathu ningal
    Thallidaathe pidichangu-ninnukollunnathum nannaam
  2. Ningalil puramaam vairith-nnidathanthippaavenna
    vishwasthanaam saakshi me-
    Durnnayar vadhikkamoolam chhinnagaathranaaya pothum
    Ennile vishwaasam ningal-kaivedinjillathum kollaam-
  3. Enkilum chilathundenikku-vigrahaarppitham
    Thinnuvaanum daivajanangal
    Durnnadapp-aacharippaanum kannivehca bileyaamin
    Bhinnatha pidichavarangundu nikkelovyarum the-
  4. Aakayaal manam thirika nee allaaykil vannen
    Vaalukondavarodettu njaan
    Poru cheyyunnathumoolam-aayavarin shavam vazhi
    Neelave kidakkumaarkkum naari veruppaakumavar-
  5. Perggamos yugathilulloru-poril jayikkum
    Marthyano maranja mannayin
    Bhakshyamekum shwethashila-pathramathilavan maathram Paarthariyum puthiya per
    cherthavannu koduthidum. K.V.S
