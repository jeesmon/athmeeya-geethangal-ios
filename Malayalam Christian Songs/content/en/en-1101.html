1. Israelin daivam yudhaveeranaam
    Yehudayin simham raaja raajanaam
    Mruthyuve jaichuyuirthatheshu naadhanaam
    Nammude naayakan kristhu maathramaam
    • O! poyidaam kristhuvin pinbe poyidaam
      Haa! paadidaam jayathin geetham paadidaam-
  2. Ghoranaaya saathaan namme muttilum
    Krooramaayethiruthu nilkkumenkilum
    Bheethiyenye poyidaam kurishin paathayil
    Neethi maarggamothidaam lokamengum naam-
  3. Andhakaaram mannil vyaaparikkayaal
    Andharaayi marthyar hantha! mevunnu
    Atbhutha prakaashathin deepamenthi naam
    Aatma naadhaneshuvin naamamuyarthidaam-
  4. Allalerum kaattum kolum kandu naam
    Thellum kalangendathillee yaathrayil
    Vallabhan yeshu thaan koodeyullathaal
    Halleluyya geetham naam paadi poyidaam. G.P
