1. kelkka kel or kaahalam mochanam svaadhandhryam
    daivathin vilabaram mochanam svaadhandhryam
    naasamaakum evarkkum bhaagya yovel valhsaram
    ghoshippin ellaadavum mochanam svaadhandhryam
  2. Cholluvin kaaraagrahe mochanam svaadhandhryam
    kelkkuvin he! baddhare! mochanam svaadhandhryam
    kroosin mele kaanuvin nidhyamaam uddhaaranam
    yesuvodu vaaguvin mochanam svaadhandhryam
  3. Dushttanmaare rakshippaan mochanam svaadhandhryam
    sishyaraakki theerkkuvaan mochanam svaadhandhryam
    daivasneham kaanuvin vaazthuvin than kaarunyam
    jeevan daanam praapippaan mochanam svaadhandhryam
  4. Paapa bhaaram maattuvaan mochanam svaathanthryam
    maayaseva theeruvaan mochanam svaathanthryam
    ha! saubhaagya vaarththaye engngane nishedhikkaam?
    ithra valliyor rakshaye mochanam svaathanthryam
  5. Swarggam saakshi puthranil mochanam svaathandhryam
    tharkkam vendaa bhoomiyil mochanam svaathandhryam
    visudhaathma mudrayaal undu poornna nischayam
    praapikkaam visvaasaththaal mochanam svaathandhryam
  6. Parvvadhangngal kelkkatte mochanam svaadhandhryam
    aazhangngal muzhagatte mochanam svaadhandhryam
    dveepum kettu chollatte yesu naadhhaa vandanam
    srushtiyellaam paadatte mochanam svaadhandhryam. V.N
