1. Vaazhthidum njanete rakshakene ennum
    Sthothram cheyyum thante van krupackkaay
  2. Shathruvin shakthikaleshaathe ennayee
    Aazhcha muzhuvanum kaathathinaal
  3. Veezhchakeleshaathe sookshichathorthinnu
    Kaazhcha veyckunnenne nin paadathil
  4. Aazhcha vattathinte onnaam dinam ninne
    Aadipithaakkanmar vaazhthiya pol
  5. Imbamodinnithaa nin makkalonnichu
    Thumbamenye ninne kumbidunnu
  6. Shudharadonnichu sthothram cheyvaanenne
    Paathramaakki yone vaazhthunnippol
  7. Naattam pidichu njaan chettil kidannappol
    Ettiya karthane vaazhthunnippol. K.V.S
