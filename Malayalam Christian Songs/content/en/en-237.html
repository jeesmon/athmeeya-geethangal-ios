• Aaraadhichidaam aatma naadane
    Aayussin naalellaam nandiyode
    Ithramaathram enne snehichidunnoru
    Rakshakan illa ikshithiyil (2)
  1. Simha kkuzhiyo, chenkadal choolayo
    Enthu vanneedilum bhaaramilla
    Enne vilichavan en koodeyundennum
    Enne nadathidum anthyam vare-
  2. Enthu njaan thannidum en kartha ninakkaay
    Enne njaan thannidunnu nin karathil
    Edukka enne nee nin hitham polennum
    Nadathidename yeshu naadaa-
  3. Aayiram aayiram devanmaarekkaal
    Ethrayo unnathan ente yeshu
    Avanoppam parayuvaan avaneppol aarume
    Illenikkennude jeevithathil-
  4. Naalukal oronnaay theernnoru naalil
    Nadanarikil njaan ananjidumbol
    Odi varum enne maarodanachidum
    En kannuneerellaam thudachidum thaan-S.K.B
