• Krupa mathi yeshuvin krupa mathiyaam
    Sankadathil ente sambhramathil
    Thuna mathi yeshuvin thana mathiyaam
    Kashtathayil ente vedanayil
  1. Thalayile oru cheru mudi polum
    Vilayillaa cheriyoru kuruvi polum
    Ente daivam sammathikkaathe
    Nilathu veenu nashikkukilla-
  2. Anarthangalanavadiyeridumbol
    Avashathayaalullam thalarnnidumbol
    Ente daivam eben ezer
    Anartha naalil kaividumo?
  3. Manam nonthu thirumumbin karayumbol
    Manassalinjaashwaasam pakarnnu tharum
    Ente daivam yehova yire!
    Karuthum kaakum paricharikkum-
  4. Maruvile maaraye madhuramaakki
    Urappulla paaraye jalamaakkum
    Manjil ninnum mana nalkum
    Maamaka daivam vallabhanaam-
  5. Varumini punaradi viravilavan
    Tharum puthu mahassezhumudelenikku
    Swargga naattil swantha naattil swantha veettil
    Santhatham vaazhum halleluyya. G.P
