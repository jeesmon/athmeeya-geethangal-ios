• Ennantharangavum en jeevanum
    Jeevanulla devane stuthichidunnithaa
    Nee nallavan nee vallabhan
    En rakshakaa mahaa prabho !

  1. Kannuneerin thaazhvarayil njaan nadannidum
    Neravum nin kaikalenne pinthudarnnidum
    Ninnaalaye vasikkuvanee ezhayenneyum
    Nee theerthathaal njaan bhaagyavaanaay
    Theernnu nischayam-
  2. Njaan vasikkumeeyoru dinam ninnanthike
    Aayiram dinangalekkaal shrestame thava
    Sannidhaanamente nithya aasrayam vibho !
    Sarvvavum tharunnithaa njaan nin karangalil. K.V.H
