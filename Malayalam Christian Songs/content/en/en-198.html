• To God be the glory
  1. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Than naamathin shreshtatha keerthippaanay
    Purohitha vamsham naam vishudharum
    Than nithya mahathwathin amshikalum
    • Vaazhthuvin paaduvin sarvva gothrangale
      Vazahthuvin paaduvin sthuthi sthothrangale
      Pithaavinte sannidhiyil varuvin
      Than puthran mukhaantharam vandichidin
  2. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Thaan lokathe snehicha snehathinnaay
    Ellaavarin paapathe neekkiduvaan
    Than maarvvile puthrane ayachu thaan
  3. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Than mudrayaam aathmaavin daanathinnaay
    Abhaa! en pithaave! ennurachidaan
    Ee heenare yogyanmaar aakkidinaan
  4. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Pishaachin pravruthikal azhippanaay
    Senaapathi namukking immaanuvel
    Naam jayam kondaadunnu shathruvinmel-
  5. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Than nithya sookshippin vaagdaanathinnaay
    Than aadukalonnum nashikkayilla
    Than kaiyyil ninnaarum parikkayilla
  6. Pithaavinu sthothram than vankrupackkaay
    Orungiya swarggeeya bhaagyathinnaay
    Thante thirumukham naam kaanum sadaa
    Prakaashathil vaazhum naam Halleluyya! V.N
