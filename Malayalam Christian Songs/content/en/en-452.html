• Alas and did my Savior
  1. Yehova ethra nallavan
    thaan aashritharkkellaam
    Maaraatha mahaa vishwasthan
    thaan nithya paarayaam
  2. Vishudhi than simhaasanam
    neethiyum than chenkol
    Thaan nithya preethi vaathssalyam
    mahaa samudram pol
  3. Kristheshuvil than niyamam
    nammodu sthdaapichu
    Sampoornna paapa mochanam
    nalkaan prasaadichu
  4. Thaan unnathathil vaazhunnu
    raajaadhi raajaavaay
    Paathaalatholam thaazhunnu
    than bhakthar rakshakkaay
  5. Nerullavarin rakshakan
    anaadharkkum pithaa
    Vishudhanmaarin snehithan
    ithathre yehova
  6. Dukhangalil aashwaasangal
    aarogyam rogathil
    Aathmaavil divyollaasangal
    undu yehovayil
  7. Thaan sarvvashakthan aakayaal
    thanmel naam chaaruka
    Thaan sathyavaanaay paarkkayaal
    thannil naam theruka
  8. Ee bhoomiyil samaadhaanam
    swarggeeya paurathwam
    Yehovayin krupaa daanam
    than bhakthar dhanyaraam
  9. Yehova naama maahaathmyam
    varnnippaan aavilla
    Thanikku nithya vandanam
    sthuthiyum sarvvada. V.N
