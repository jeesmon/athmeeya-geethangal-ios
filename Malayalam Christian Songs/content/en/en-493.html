• Vaagdatha naattilen
    vishramamaam
    Vaagdatham cheythavan
    vishwasthanaam
  1. Engum njaan pokunn ennariyaathe
    than vilikettu pokunnu njaan
    Inginiyethu khedam vannaalum
    pinthirikkilla pokunnu njaan
  2. Vittu njaan ponnath onnumeyorthi-
    nnottum madangi pokayilla
    Uthamamaaya nithya nikshepa-
    munnatha naattilund-enikkaay
  3. Paapathinn-imbabhogam vendaa njaan
    paadupedaam enneshuvinnaay
    Misrayim sambathethilumente
    kristhuvin nindayethra nannaam!
  4. Onnilume manam thalaraathee-
    mannil njaan yaathra cheythidunnu
    Annannu vendum mannayundennum
    anthikathil en naadhanunde
  5. Chenkadal yorddaan vattipomellaam
    vanyeriho mathil thakarum
    Vishwaasa yaathraye vilakkaan ee
    vishwathilonnum shakthamalla
  6. Kannuneer thorum nindakal theerum
    vinnilen veettil chennucherum
    Ennikkoodaatha shudharotheshu
    sannidhiyil njaan vaazhumennum. M.E.C
