• Jeevanenikkaay vedinja devakumaaraa!
    Devakumaaraa! sarvva paapa vidooraa!-
  1. Swargga mahimaasanavum nisthula prabhaa niravum
    Athryum vedinju bhoovilavatharichone!
    Avatharichone! thaazhma swayam varichone!-
  2. Anchuyava appavum meen randumangeduthu vaazhthi
    Anchu sahasram janathe poshippichone!
    Poshippichone, haa! santhoshippichone!-
  3. Kurirul niranjathaami ppaarinnu prakaasham nalki
    Bhoorisukham arulum mozhi arulicheythone!
    Arulicheythone! jeevavazhi thelichone!
  4. Paathakar naduvil mahaa paathakaneppol kurishil
    Nee thakarnnu mruthiyadanjath orkkunnayyo! njaan
    Orkkunnayyo! njaan athen perkkennarinjen. E.I.J
