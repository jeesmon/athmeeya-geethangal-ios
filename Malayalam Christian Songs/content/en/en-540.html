• Ennum santhoshikkumen karthaadhikarthanil njaan
    Ente paapabhaaram neengiyathu moolam
  1. Nithyathayilenne paran kandu,krooshil yaagamaay
    Mruthyuvinte kaikalil ninnenne raksha cheythu thaan
    Rakshippin geethangal uchathil paadidum
    Prathidinam thiruvadi paninjidum njaan-
  2. Ennumente prashamsayen rakshakantekrooshilaam
    Lokam tharum pukazhchakal khedamaan enn ennum njaan
    Krooshinte paathayil naadhante koode njaan
    Thirumozhi shravichennum nadannidume-
  3. Snehithanmaar dushichenne pakachaalum shaanthanaay
    Sneha naadhaneshuvinte maaridathil chaarum njaan
    Dukhangal maaridum kanneerum thornnidum
    Manassukhamodu dinam vasichidum njaan-
  4. Mannilente vaasarangal theerum vare naadhante
    Ponnu paadaseva cheythu modamode vaazhum njaan
    Pinneyenneshuvin sannidhi thannil njaan
    Chennu sthuthi cheythu nithyam vanangidume. G.P
