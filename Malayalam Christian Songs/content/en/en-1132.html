• Jeevitha yaathrakkaaraa! kaaladikalengotte?
    Naashathin paathayo, jeevante maarggamo?
    Lakshyam nin mumbilenthe?-
  1. Anpin roopi yeshunaadhan ninne vilikkunnille?
    Pokalle nee andhanaayi loka saubhaagyam thedi
    Ponnin chiraku ninakku meethe
    Karthan virichathu kaanunnille?
    Sooryanin thaapamo khoramaam maariyo
    Ninne alatta en ponmakane-
  2. Vaishamyamaam medukale engane nee kadakkum?
    Engane nee yorddaaninte akkare chennu cherum?
    Nin thoniyil karthan eshuvundo?
    Nin naavil praarthdanaa gaanamundo?
    Puthan gaanamathu paadi sthuthikkuvaan
    Hruthade swarggeeya shaanthiyundo?-
  3. Swargga pure nee kelkkunnille seeyonil gaana shabdam?
    Vendayo nin swanthamaayi swarggeeya santhoshangal?
    Vaanatheril meghaarudanaayi
    Vegam varunneshu raajanavan
    Cherkkuvaan ninneyum shudharin samkhathil
    Kanneerilla swarggavaasam athil
