    Mangalam devadevannu

    lengum niranju maruvum-paraaparannu--

    Lokarakshacku nalkiya-suradhipannu--
  4. Neethiyodiha loka-jeevitham kazhippathi-
    Nnaavithannanugrahikku-makhileshannu--
  5. Devanivaasamaayi- bhoovine shariyaakki
    Mevuvaan krupayarulum-sanaathanannu. K.V.S
