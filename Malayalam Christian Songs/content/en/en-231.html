• Daivam nallavan ennennum nallavan
    Njaan ruchicharinju paadum daivam nallavan
  1. Avangkalekku nokkiyor prasobhitharaayi
    Aarum nilanilkkukayillavarkkethiraayi
    Doothar bhakthar chutum nilkkum vanmathilaayi
    Dushdar pokum kaatupaatidum pathiraayi
  2. Vindhalam bhoomandhalavum nirmmikkum manne
    Undenikk anaadiyaayi daivamaay nanne
    Thalamurakal kkaashrayamaam nallavan thanne
    Marannidaathe kaathidunnu nithyavum namme-
  3. Kashtakal shodhanakal neridumbozhum
    Ishtaraayor vittakanne poyidumbozhum
    Nashtamallathonnum nithya daiva snehathaal
    Nanmackkennu vyaaparikkum enikkavayellaam-
  4. Enthumente jeevithathil sambhavichaalum
    Enthinennakam kalangi chodikkilla njaan
    Nonthozhukum kannuneer than ponnu paadathil
    Chinthi njaan thudarnnu paadum daivam nallavan. M.E.C
