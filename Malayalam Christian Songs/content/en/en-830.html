1. Eeshaneyen yeshunaadhaa! sthothram-ennekkum
    Sarvva-kleshavum krooshil vahicha naayakaa!vande!
  2. Vaanjchikkunnenn-antharangam ninneyeppozhum ipra-
    Pancha santhoshangalil samthrupthiyilla me
  3. Bhuthalithilaashrayam nee maathram-enikku-divya
    Modamennullil thannu nithyam kaaval cheyyunnu
  4. Roga-shokangal samastham neekki-aarogyam sneha-
    Saagaram kaninj-enikku nalkidunnathaal
  5. Raathriyen kidakkayil kristheshu naadhane! ninne
    Keerthanangal paadi vaazhthi vandichidum njaan
  6. Sarvvavum saamodam-arppikkunnu njaanippol-ente
    Sarvvavumaam vallabhaa! nin sannidhaanathil. E.P.V
