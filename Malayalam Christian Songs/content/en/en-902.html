• Tune: Manujanivaan bhaagyavaan


    Parameshannuyiril ninnuruvaakum sahodara-
    rorumichu vasippathu shubhavum bhangiyumathre
  1. Aharon than thalayile vileyerunnoru thailamathupole
    Seeyon manjinnatholamaam prabhapole
  2. Perike manjulamaami pparamaamaikya morkkumbol
    Orupole nararithil sthiramaay paarkkanam nithyam
  3. Avide sthaapanam cheythaanamalan thannanugraham
    Azhiyaathulloru jeevanavide-thanne-yarppichaan
  4. Sthuthiyennum paramesha suthanum thaathanum bhavy-
    Mathiyaamaatma rupikkum sthuthiyennum bhavikkatte. K.V.S
