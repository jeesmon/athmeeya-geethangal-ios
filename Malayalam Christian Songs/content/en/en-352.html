• Krupayulla yehove! devaa! mama nalla pithaave! devaa!
    Krupa krupayonninaal thava suthanaayi njaan-krupayulla
    • Krupa krupayonninaal thava suthanaayi njaan
      Krupayulla yehove! devaa!
  1. Doorave poyakannor enne nee orkkave orkkave
    Sweekarichithe vidham nee kanijathalbhutham albhutham
    Athu nithyamorthu njaan aayussellam paadidum
    Padam muthi paninjidum devaa!-
  2. Innu njaan ninnodothu panthiyil modamaay modamaay
    Vannu thinnu thrupthanaay ethrayo bhaagyavaan bhaagyavaan!
    Panni thinna bhojyavum annu njaan kothichathum
    Ennekkumaay marannupoy devaa!-
  3. Thaazhchayil enneyortha ninte maa snehame snehame
    Krooshileka jaathane konnathaam yaagame yaagame
    Akayaal innnezha njaan aakulamakannitha
    Aayi ninte sannidhiyil devaa!-
  4. Daivame nin padathil nandiyaay vandanam vandanam
    Cheyyumennathenniye enthu njaan thannidum thannidum
    Ennum ennum raappakal aaraadhichennaakilum
    Nin krupacku pakaramaay theeraa. T.K.S
