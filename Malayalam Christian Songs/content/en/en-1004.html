• Maraname vishamengu? ninte
    vijayavumevide? enneshu
    Maranathe jaichu thanikku
    sthuthi halleluyya!
  1. Than krooshil njaanum haa! marichu-
    nithyamaam Jeevan kaivarichu-thannil njaan sarvvavum layichu halleluyya-
  2. En jeevan kristhuvil bhadram- maname
    Paaduka sthothram ennabhayam
    thankrupa maathram halleluyya-
  3. Vrudaavilalla njaan cheyyum prayatnam
    Oduvil njaan koyyum thadayuvaa-
    nilloru kaiyyum halleluyya
  4. Prathyaasha attavareppol alla naam
    Kristhuvil marippor uyirkkum
    thaan varumappol halleluyya-
  5. Swarlloka kaahalam dhwanikkum-maricho-
    Rakshanamuyirkkum than thejass-
    akshayam dharikkum-halleluyya-
  6. En deham manmayamennaal iniyum
    Thaan varumannaal-vilangum
    thejassil nannaay halleluyya. M.E.C
