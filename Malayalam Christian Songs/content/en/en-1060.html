    Vaagnathanaadathil eithiduvaan kaalamaayi
    Halleluyya – halleluyya
    Halleluyya – halleluyya
  1. Kaanthan vanniduvaan kanthaye cherkkuvaan
    Kaalamaayi naalangaduthupoyi
    Orungiyirikkunnor orukkiya veettilum
    Cherum samayavum asannamaay-
  2. Kannuneer thornnidum nindakal maaridum
    Ennamillaa yugam vanidum naam
    Onnichu kooduvaan onnichu paaduvaan
    Onnichu vaazhuvaan kaalamaay-
  3. Puthanaam shaalemil puthen shareerathil
    Puthen geethangal naam aalapikkum
    Pon veenakal meetti vinmaya veettil naam
    Ponmukham kandennum aaraadhikkum. M.J.P
