    Vaanalokathezhunnellinaan
    Vaanalokathezhunnelli naanolivu malayil ninn
    Aandamuyarthi shishyar vaanil nokki ninnidave
  1. Vinnulakathil ninnirangi manujaathanaayi
    Vannu maa guruvaay vilangi
    Chonnu shishyarod upadesham nanma cheythu
    Nadannariyichu suvishesham
    Mannidathullorukku chora chinni marichu maranam
    Vennuyirthu naalppathaam naalinnilam vittu jayamaay
  2. Melkkisadekkinte kramathil purohitha velacku
    Thante swantha rakthathe
    Thrukkara thanka thalikayil -enthiyathinkal
    Mukkiya viralullavanaa-
    Yikkula papamokkackkum thakka parihaaram cheyvaan
    Swarggamaam vishudha sthalam
    Nokki mahaapurohithan
  3. Thannil vishwasikkunnorkkaayitt azhivillaatha
    Mandiram orukkuvanaayi
    Ennumavarod irippaanaay sathyaathmaave pa-
    Karnnavarkku koduppanaayi
    Unnathan valabhaagathinnu pakshavaadam cheythu
    Thannudayorkku mochanam thannu rakshippathinnaayi
  4. Senayin karthan parishudhan ennu swarggeeya
    Senakal sthuthichu paadave
