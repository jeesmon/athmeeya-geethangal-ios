• Prabhaathakaale vannadiyan nin
    Prabhaavathin munnil vanangidunne
    Prabho sampoornnamaay samarppikkunne nin
    Prabhayaal enne nirachiduke
  1. Karunaa saagarame nin karuthal
    Oro nimishavum anubhavippaan
    Thiruhithamaay theernnathinaal
    Thirumeniye stuthichidunne-
  2. Arunodayathil aruliduka nin
    Arumayaam thiruvachassin mozhikal
    Aanandame aashwaasame
    Aaramyame nin vachanam-
  3. Innen kriyakal vaagmanobhaavangal
    Mannavane nin hithamaakane
    Onnilum njaan thalarnnidaathe
    Ninniduvaan krupayarulke. G.V
