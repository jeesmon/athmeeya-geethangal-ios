• Lead me to the Rock
  1. Krupa krupa krupa thanne krupayude paithal njaan
    Krupayaalen hrudayethe kavarnnu rakshaakaran
  2. Prathikoolangale neekkie-ttathimodam hrudaye
    Sathatham thannidunnennil krupayaal athyunnathan
  3. Nithyanaaya daivathinte nithyamaayullanpinaal
    Chatha naayaamenneyoru puthranaakki theerthu thaan
  4. Nithyanaaya rakshakante rakthamathaal kazhuki
    Puthanaakki nithyajeevan maathrathorum tharunnu
  5. Halleluyya! halleluyya! daivamaam threeyekannu
    Halleluyya! halleluyya! halleluyya! vandanam! T.J.V
