• Tune: Praarthana kelkkaname


    Karthaaveyekaname ninte krupa
    nithyamee daasanu nee
  1. Jeevitha paathayil veenupokaathennum
    Ee loke kaakkaname krupaanidhe
    thaavaka daasaneyum
  2. Shodhana velayil aakulanaakaathe
    Naadhaa! karuthename-anudinam
    thaavaka daasaneyum
  3. Ksheenichidaatheyen ottam thikackkuvaan
    Thaaniyekidaname- daya paraa! daasanaameeyenikku
  4. Nalkunna bhaarangal thaangiduvaanaayi
    Nalla karuthu nalki-thaangename
    thaavaka daasaneyum
  5. En manobhaarangal neekkiduvaan balam
    En manassinu nalki-paalikkane
    thaavaka daasaneyum-
  6. Dukha samudrathil aazhthikkalanjidum
    Vankaattil ninnumennum-kaakkaname
    thaavaka daasaneyum. G.K
