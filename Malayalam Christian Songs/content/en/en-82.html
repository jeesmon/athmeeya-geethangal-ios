• Ennalum sthuthichiduvin-yeshu
    Rajaadhiraajavine nammal
    paadi pukazhthiduvin
  1. Aadiyumavanaam anthavumavanaam
    Atbhutha rakshakanaam-namme
    Thediyee dhaathriyil vannuthan geevane thannoru snehithanaam-
  2. Sambannanennaal namme karuthi nirdhananayi theernna-thante
    Nisthulyamaam krupa nithyavum dhyaanichu
    Vaazthistuthikkuka naam
  3. Vaanam bhuvanam ellaam chamachon
    Heena marakkurishil-namme
    Snehichu shaapa mruthyu varichu rakshichathum ninache-
  4. Nandiyaalullam nanne niranju
    vandanam cheythiduvin-daiva
    Nandanan cheytha nanmakalorthu
    keerthanam paadiduvin. G.P
