• The Great Physician
  1. Eppozhum njaan santhoshikkum en yeshu ente gaanam
    Ellaadavum aaghoshikkum en rakshakante daanam
    • Yeshuve nee swarggathil ente naamam ezhuthi
      Aarum edukkaatha ee bhaagyam en santhosham
  2. Nin raajyathinnoranyanaay bhooviyivan uzhannu
    Nee vannathaale dhanyanaay praveshanam nee thannu
  3. Mahathwamulla rakshakaa! nee thanne swargga vaathil
    Swargeeya geethangal ithaa dhwanikkunnente kaathil
  4. Ee lokathil or maanavum enikkillaaykilenthe?
    Swargeeyaperum sthaanavum tharum en divya bendhu
  5. En naamam maachukalavaan pishaachinaal assaadhyam
    En yeshu shakthan kaakkum thaan-than rakthathin sambaadyam
  6. Mruthyuvin naal sameepichaal ingilla klesham thaapam
    En jeevan kristhan aakayal marikkayaalum laabham
  7. Than nyaaya theerppu kelkkumbol anekar bhramichidum
    Enikko yeshuraajan chol- nithyaanandam nalkidum
  8. Santhoshame santhoshame en daivathinu sthothram!
    En jeevanaam en yeshuve neeyum sthuthikku paathram. V.N
