• Kaalamellaam kazhinjidaaraay Innu
    kaanmathellaam azhinjidaaraay
    Kaahalangal muzhangidaaraay ente
    Kaanthaneshu vannidaaraay-kaalamellaam
  1. Rakthaambaram pol chuvappaam nin paapam
    Thiru ninathaal kazhuki venmayaakku snehithaa nee
    Thiru krupa anubhavikku Varu nee krupayil
    Dinavum avan sannidhiyil Snehithaa nee varumo?
  2. Paapavum shaapavum kaalvary krooshil
    Avan ninakkaay vahichu orkkumo? nee snehithaa
    Aarum nalkaatha sneham Varu nee krupayil
    Dinavum avan sannidhiyil Snehithaa nee varumo? J.K.T
