    Stuthippinennaalum
    Kurishil jeevan thannu namme
    veendeduthone
  1. Lamkhanam kshamichavan thaan-
    Paapa bandhana mazhichavan thaan
    Santhaapam theerthennil than
    sthuthi sthothram
    Santhatham paaduvaan thannavan thaan--
  2. Ullathe kavarnnavan thaan-perum
    vellathil nadannavan thaan
    Kaattum van thirakalumadakkiya devan
    Muttum sankethamaay namukkullavan--
  3. Kallara thurannavan thaan-kodum
    vairye vennavan thaan
    Vinnil pithaavin sannidhi thannil
    Vaazhum pradhaana purohithan thaan
  4. Mahimayaninjavan thaan-sarvva
    maanava rakshakan thaan
    than thiru padathil aashrayichullor-
    kkevarkku maananda vallabhan thaan. G.P
