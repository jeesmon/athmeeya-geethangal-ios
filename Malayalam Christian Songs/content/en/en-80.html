• Koorirul niranja lokathil
    thajassaayi devaa nee vannu
    Sthuthi mahima karthaavinnu
  1. Nishkalanga yaagamay krooshilen paran
    Amoolya rakthameki mukthi maargamaay
    Enthu njaanithinnu badalaayekidum prabho!
    Sthuthi mahima karthaavinnu-
  2. Mruthyuve thakarthu haa! enthorathbhutham
    Bheethi pokki preethiyekiyullathil
    Thaathan chaare pakshavaadam cheyvathum neeye
    Sthuthi mahima karthaavinnu-
  3. Gaanam paadi vaazhthidum modamode njaan
    Van kadangal nin karangal theerthathaal
    Vaanil vegam vannidum ninnanthike cherppaan
    Sthuthi mahima karthaavinnu. K.D.W
