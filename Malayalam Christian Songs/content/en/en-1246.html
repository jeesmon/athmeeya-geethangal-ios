• TUNE: Sankadam samasthwavum

    Aashissekanam vadhuvararkkinnu nee-paraneshu
    naadhane! kaninju swarggeeyamaam-para-maashi
  1. Pandu nee galeelayile kaanaavinkal-chennukondavarkku
    vendi jalam draakshaarasa-maakkiyindalaakave
    akattiyennonami-nnum prasaadamodirangi
    vannu nalkename shubham-
  2. Sneha bendhanangalaale yojichavar oru
    Dehamaay vilangunnathinnekanam-varam
    Ekayaashrayam pravruthi sambhaashana-mivayaakave
    vishishtamaam vidham kaanuvaan-nithyam. E.I.J
