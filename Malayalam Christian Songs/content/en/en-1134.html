• Vanniduvin - ippol vanniduvin
    ninne yeshu vilikkunnithaa!
    Raksha thanniduvaan mokhsam nalkiduvaan
    Yeshu kristhu vilikkunnithaa!
  1. Vinnin mahima vittu
    mannilirangi khora-
    Krooshil marichuyirthu
    rakshakan jeevikkunnu-
  2. Adhwaani kkunnavarkkum
    bhaaram chumakkunnorkkum
    Aashwaasam nalkiduvaan
    yeshu vilikkunnithaa
  3. Swarggathin paathayum thaan
    sathyavum jeevanum thaan
    Jeevante appavum thaan
    jeevane thannavan thaan
  4. Mruthyu varunnathin mun
    kristhuvil aashrayippin
    Nithyamaam dandanathil
    nithya muzhannidaathe-
  5. Yeshuvin paade veenu
    paapangalettu chonnaal
    Paapa vimochanam thaan
    thannippol sweekarikkum. G.P
