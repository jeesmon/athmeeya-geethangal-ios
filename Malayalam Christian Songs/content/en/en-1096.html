1. Veendeduppin naaladuthupoye daiva janame
    Ksheenikkaathe vela cheythidaam
    Vinnin naadhan vannidaaray khinnathakal theernnidaaray
    Veettilethi vishramippaan kaalamithaa sameepamaay
  2. Atthi vruksham thalirthathinaal venaladuthu
    Ennu nammalariyunnaho! aarthiyode pravarthikkaam
    Raathri kaalam varum mumbe poorthiyakkaam nallapole
    Nalla naadhan vela nammal
  3. Innu nammal mindaathirunnaal naadhante vela
    Onnu polum cheyyaathirunnaal
    Vannu naadhan namme cherthu prathiphalam thannidumbol
    Onnumilla prayojanam annirunnu karanjittu
  4. Andhakaaramaaya bhoovithil ee loka janam
    Bendhitharum andharumallo
    Rakshakanaam yeshuve naam santhatham
    Velippeduthaam akshanathilazhinju pom
    Bendhanavum andhathayum. A.K.W
