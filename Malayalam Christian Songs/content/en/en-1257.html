• Tune:Maraname vishamengu

    Deveshaa, adhikamaay aashirvadikka-vadhuvararivare-maheshaa,
    Kuthukamaay onnaay vasippaan ithumuthal thudare
  1. Aadaamum havvayum chernnu-sammodam
    Edanilaarnnu athupol aadhikal theernnu-vasippaan
  2. Prathyaasha, sneham, vishwaasam ivayaal
    Nithyam aashwaasam-labhichu kristhuvil vaasam cheythidaan
  3. Yehove sevikkum njaanen-kudumba-
    Sahithamanyoonam-ennaruli yoshwa ennonam-vasippaan. T.K.S
