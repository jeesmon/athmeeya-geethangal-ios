• Varumeshu vaanilannaal
    avanodu chernnidum naam
    Priyaatheyennum vaazhum
    athimodamode nammal
  1. Naaminnu annyaraayi paarkkunnu paarilennaal
    Namukkaayorukkiedunnu athiramyamaaya geham
    Athileshuvodu koode anavadya danyaraayi
    Priyaatheyennum vaazhum athimodamode nammal-
  2. Karthaavilinnu nammal cheyyunna velakalkkaay
    Nalkum prathiphalangal doothagana sadassil
    Theera vinakal theernnu ullaasaraayi vinnil
    Priyaatheyennum vaazhum athimodamode nammal
  3. Azhiyumee marthyadeham aa divyakaanthiyaale
    Azhakullathaayi maarum avanodu thulyamaayi
    Thejassalannu nithya avakaasha bhaagya naattil
    Priyaatheyennum vaazhum athimodamode nammal. G.P
