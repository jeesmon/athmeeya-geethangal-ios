1. Pokuka pokuka sodarare!
    naam pokuka yudham cheyvathinnaay
    Pokuka krooshin padayaalikalaay
    pada poruthidaan poyidaam-
  2. Vachanam vaalaay kaikalilenthi
    sathyam nalloru parichayumaay
    Kavachamathaayi neethi dharichum vishwaasathin shirasthravumaay-
  3. Theduka neduka naadukal neele
    rakshayilekkinn aalukale
    Veedukal medukal kaadukal
    kayari velikkarikilumullavare-
  4. Pakalolimaadam naalu varunnu
    irulu parathum naalukalaam
    Parameshante vaalu dharichu
    poruthuka maranatholam naam. K.V.J
