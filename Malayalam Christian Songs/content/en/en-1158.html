• Vishwaasa samkhamey-unarnn-orungidaam nirannidaam
    Daivathin sarvvaayudha varggameduthu kolluvin-
  1. Swathanthraraam namukkihathil kleshamerumenkilum
    Akattidum paraaparan santhaapamaake doorave
    Namukkavan karuthanaam sahodaran naam chaarave
  2. Alasathayakatti vegam durddinathe ethiridaan
    Sarvvaayudham-aninjorungi nirannidaam sagarbhyare!
    Sarvvonnathan sadaa nammodu koodeyundu jayam tharaan
  3. Sathyamaam-arakkettum salneethiyenna kavachavum
    Avikala prashaanthiyekum suvisheshavumenthuka
    Athinnorukkam kaalukalkku nallarakshayaakkuka
  4. Dushtanaamaraathi nammil chorinjidum theeyambukal
    Keduthuvaaneduthu kol vishwaasa paricha sodaraa
    Rakshayaam shirasthravum-aatmaavin vaalumenthidaam
  5. Pragalbharaay, prabudharaay, samyuktharaay nirannidam
    Vijaya padahadhwani muzhakki neengidaam ranaankane!
    Namukku por nadathidaam pithaavinnaay vishwasthare! dheerare! T.O.C
