• Paathaalame! Maraname ninnude jayamevide
    Kunjaattin ninam kotta than bhaktharkke
    Samhaarakan kadannupoy
    • Jayathin ghosham ullaasaghosham
      Bhaktharin koodaarathil ennum puthugeetham
      Mahathwa raajanaay senayin veeranaay
      Abhayam thaanavarkkennume
  1. Bheekaramaam chenkadalum
    Misrayeem sainy nirayum
    Bheeshaniyaay mumbum pimbum
    Bheethippeduthidumbol-
  2. Shaktharaaya raajaakkalaam seehonum
    Ogum vannaal
    Shanka venda bheethi venda
    Shakthan nin naayakan thaan-
  3. Agni ninne dahippikkilla
    Nadi ninmel kaviyukayilla
    Agniyathil naalaaman thaan
    Aazhimel nadakondon thaan-
  4. Koorirul paathayil nee
    Nadannaal velichamaayavan ninakku
    Koottinu varum than kolum vadiyum
    Koodennum aaswasamaay-
  5. Bhoomiyum paniyum azhinjupokum
    Nila nilkkum thanvachanam
    Maranam maarum naam vaazhum jeevanil
    Thankoode yugaayugamaay-
