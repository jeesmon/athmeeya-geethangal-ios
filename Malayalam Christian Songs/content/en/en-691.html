• Vishwaasa naayakaneshuven
    Vishwaasa yaathrayilaasrayam (2)
  1. Bhaaram prayaasangaleriyaalum
    Bhaarappedaanilla karthaavunde
    Bhaarangalellaam vahikkunnon
    Bhaasuramaay vazhi nadathidum
  2. Aakula chithanaay theernnidumbol
    Aavashya bhaarathaal neeridumbol
    Aavalode than paade veezhil
    Aakulangalaake thulackunna-
  3. Ullam thakarunna nerathilum
    Undenikkaashwaasamaayi naadan
    Indelellaamakattidumbol
    Vendathellaamaayi undenikke-
  4. Medini vedana shodhanakal
    Shobhanamaakkuvaan shakthanavan
    Vedanayetta veda naadan
    Verpiriyaathente koodeyunde. T.C.V
