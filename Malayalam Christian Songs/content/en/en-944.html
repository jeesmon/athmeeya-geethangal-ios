• We shall stand before the King
  1. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam kandidum than saundaryam
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Doothanmaarkku thullyaray vazhum naam santhushtaraay
    Halleluyya -vegathil

  2. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam neekkum porin aayudham
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Theernnu yudhaabhyaasavum theernnallo prayaasavum
    Halleluyya vegathil
  3. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam praapikkum than vaagdatham
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Naam pithaavin raajyathil vaazhum nithya-thejassil
    Halleluyya vegathil
  4. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam shobhikkum nirantharam
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Theernnu balaheenatha thernnu ella ksheenatha
    Halleluyya vegathil
  5. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam praapikkum nithyaanandam
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Nithya-jeevan imbangal thejassin kireedangal
    Halleluyya vegathil
  6. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam cheyyum sneha samsarggam
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Ellaa daivamakkalum ennum onnaay vaanidum
    Halleluyya vegathil
  7. Raajan mumbil ninnu naam cheyyum nithya-vandanam
    Halleluyya -Halleluyya
    Naamum swargga-sainyavum iprakaaram paadidum
    Halleluyya vegathil. V.N
