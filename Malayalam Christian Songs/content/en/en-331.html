• Tune: Daivakarunayin


    Daivasutha darshanamenth aanandam -varnnikkaavatho
    Daivasutha darshanamenth aanandam!
  1. Pon nilavilakkukal ezhinum naduvil
    Ven nilayanki dharichu
    Maarathu ponkacha nibbandhichavanaayi
    Vilangidum sundaramaam roopam-
  2. Chikuravum shirassum venpanjisamaanam
    Dhavalamaam manjinodoppam
    Pesha layanamath agnijjwaalackothoreesha
    Suthan roopam kaanmeen-
  3. Charanangal ulayathil chuttu pazhuppicha-
    Chelezhum vellottin samamaam
    Peruvellathinnirachil polavan shabdam
    Alamalamiyalaatha roopam-
  4. Shakthamaay shobhikku minasamavadanan
    Valankaram thannilezhuthaaram
    Cherchayaay purappedunn iruvaaythalayulla vaal
    Avan mukhamarkkabimbam pole-
  5. Marichavanenkilum uyirthezhunnettu
    Marana paathaalengale vennu
    Nirupama thejassil vilangidum naadhanen
    Aruma manavaalenennum. T.O.C
