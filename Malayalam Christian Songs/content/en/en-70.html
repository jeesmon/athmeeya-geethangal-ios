• Njaanennum sthuthikkum
    enparane thiru manusuthane
    Aananda gaanangal paadi pukazhthi
  1. Paapathin shaapathil ninnum-
    ente praanane kaathavanennum
    Paaril than anpinu thullyamillonnum-
  2. Nalkiyavan rakshadaanam-
    thannilkandu njaan daivika njaanam
    Than pada sevayathannabhimaanam-
  3. Thanthiru sannidou veene-
    ente khinnatha theeru; neraane
    Than mozhiyen nenchil thookunna thene-
  4. Aayiram naavukalaalum-
    pathinaayiram vaakkukalaalum
    Haa! divya sneham avarnnya maraalum-
  5. Nithyatha thannil njaaanethum-
    thante sthuthyapadangal njan muthum
    Bhakthiyilaananda kkannuneer veezhthum. M.E.C
