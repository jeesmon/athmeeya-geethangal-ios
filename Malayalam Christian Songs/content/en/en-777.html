• Thaangum nin karangal enne-naadaa
    Thaangum nin karangal enne
    Thaathanum maathaavum snehithanum nee
    Paarilen ashwaasa daayakanum nee (2)
  1. Vedanayerum velakalakhilam
    Shodanayaanathe ninnil njaanurackkaan (2)
    Divyamaam aashwaasam ennullil nirackkaan (2)
    Nee orukkum vazhi ee vidham dinavum (2)
  2. Rogavum kleshavum eridumthorum
    Jeevitha bhaaram perukidunthorum (2)
    Praana priyaa nin snehathilaliyaan (2)
    Nee orukkunnenne eevidham dinavum (2)
  3. Aakulam vyaakulam aakave thingi
    Maanasam neeri erinjidumbozhum (2)
    Uruki thileinjennil ninroopam vilangaan (2)
    Nee orukkum moosha ee vidham dinavum (2)
  4. Vyaadiyaalen deham valanjidumbozhum
    Aadiyaalennullam kalangidumbozhum (2)
    Mahathwam deham swargeeyavaasam (2)
    Kaanunnenulkkannaal ee mannil dinavum(2) E.P
