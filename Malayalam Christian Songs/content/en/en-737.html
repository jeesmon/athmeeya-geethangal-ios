• Nearer my God to Thee
  1. Nin sannidhi mathi haa! yeshuve!
    nin prasaadam mathi ee enikku
    Van dukhangalilum nin sannidhi mathi
    Nin sannidhi mathi innum ennum
  2. Bhoomiyilakilum-maa samudram
    kopikkilum bhayam illenikku
    Annu nin kai mathi nin sannidhi mathi
    Nin sannidhi mathi innum ennum
  3. Lokathilekanaay theerukilum
    rogathaal baadhithanaayidilum
    Thrukkannenmel mathi nin sannidhi mathi
    Nin sannidhi mathi innum ennum
  4. Aayirmaayiram vairikalaal
    aavruthanaakilum njaan bhramikkaa
    Neeyen paksham mathi nin sannidhi mathi
    Nin sannidhi mathi innum ennum. E.I.J
