• Yeshu varum vegathil aashwaasame
    Yeshu varum vegathil-kristheshu--
  1. Megham than therum anekaraam dootharum
    Shekharippaan thannilekkellaa shuddhare-
  2. Daivathe sathyathil seva cheythavarkku
    Chaavine jayichu than jeevane koduppaan-
  3. Thanthiru-varavinnaay santhatham kaathavar
    Anthamillaathoru santhosham labhippaan-
  4. Bhruthyanmaar thaan cheytha sathya-prakaaram
    Nithya-mahathwathin raajyathil vaazhaan-
  5. Than-janathintellaa nindaye neekki
    Anpulla kaikonde kannuneer thudappaan-
  6. Thanthiru-mukhathe naam kankondu kandu
    Santhushtaraayennum than naamam sthuthippaan-
  7. Lokathin chinthakal pokatteyellaam
    Eka prathyaasha ingaaka en yeshu. V.N
