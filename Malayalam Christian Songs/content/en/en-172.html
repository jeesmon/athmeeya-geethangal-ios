• Tune: Sing praise to God who reigns above
  1. En maname vaazhthiduka nin daivathin vishudha naamam
    Thann-upaka-rangal onnum nin ayussil maranidalle
    Nin akruthyam mochichathum roga saukhyam ekiyathum
    En maname marannidalle
  2. Nin jeevane naashathil ninn enneckumay viduvichathum
    Than dayayum karunayathum nin shirassil aniyichathum
    Nin yavvanam azhukidathe kazhukane pol puthukkiyathum
    En maname marannidalle
  3. Peetdithanay-theernna neram nyayamonnay nadathiyathum
    Than vazhiyim pravarthikalum ninnodennum ariyichathum
    Nin lamghana paapangale than dayayaal kshamichennathum
    En maname marannidalle
  4. Prakruthiyellam arinjidunnon podiyaam ninne orkkunnathum
    Vayalile pullin samam nin aayussethra kshanikamathum
    Arinju ninte dinamoronnum vishudhiyil nee vasikkendathum
    En maname marannidalle. S.V
