• Vandanam ponneshu naadhaa!
    Ninte krupackkaay-ennume
  1. Innushassin prabha kaanmathinnaay
    thanna krupayorthithaa- vandanam
  2. Poya raavil enne kaaval cheytha
    naayakane nandiyaay - vandanam
  3. Innalekkaal innu ninnodettam
    chernnu jeevikkenam njaan- vandanam
  4. Innu ninte aatma shakthi moolam
    enne muttum kaakkuka- vandanam
  5. Ninmukhathilulla divya kaanthi
    enmel shobhikkename -vandanam
  6. Azhiyaatha jeeva shakthiyennil
    ozhiyaathe paarkkenam-vandanam. P.V.T
