• Aayiramaayiram stuthikalaal aaraalum aaraadyanaam
    Yeshu enne veendeduppaan chora chinthiyathaam
  1. Yaahen paarayam en asrayamavan
    Paapakkuzhiyil ninnenne karetti thannilaakki thaan
  2. Paapam pokkiyen shaapam neekki thaan
    Kalvary krooshile yaaga maranathaal mochinaayi njaan
  3. Impamekuvaan enikkaay thumpamettavan anpulla naamam
    Dyaaniche njaan ennum impamaay paadum-
  4. Jaiche vaazhunnon innunnathe thaathan valabhaage
    Thirichu vannidum shudhare cherkkuvaan
    Mekhe vegathil- K.V.I.
