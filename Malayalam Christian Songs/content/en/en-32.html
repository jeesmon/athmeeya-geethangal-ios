• Devakumaara! sarvva paapa vidoora! jaikka
  1. Kesham veluthavane! jwaalaagnilochanane!
    Uchakkathiravan polujjwaaladaanane-
  2. Anki dharichu maaril ponkacha kettiyone
    Thanka vilakkukalkkul thankunna dharmajane-
  3. Chuttupazhuthorottin thwittin madamashesham
    Thattikkalanjapaada thwittaarnna sadpadane-
  4. Kodijalattsarikaa-pathathinnothavidham
    Needaarnnorochayodu kudum sanaathanane!-
  5. Muttum prabhaavalaya mandhye thilangumoru
    Nakshathramaala kaiyyil chaarthunna naayakene-
  6. Vaaithala randinaalum shathrukkaleyarinju
    Veezhthunna khadgamonnu vaaikkullilenthiyoru-
  7. Nyaayaasanstha! ninte kaayapradarshanathaal
    Maayaa vimohamellaam bheeyaarnnu mandidume-
  8. Ekleesyayackku prema vigrahamaayavane
    Matcleshamaake neekki mukhy-aashissekaneme. K.V.S
