• Puthranil vishwasikkunn ellaavanum nithya jeevanunde
    Puthranil vishwasikkunn ellaavanum
    Sathyamaam daivathin vachanam kelkkuvin
    Marthyaraam ningal ellaavarum shradhippin!-
  1. Vishwasikkunnavan paapathin shaapathaal
    Agnikkadalil veenu nashichu pokaathennekkum jeevippaan
    Daivam snehichu lokathe
    Rakshippathinn aay than puthrane ayachu
    Shikshichu paapathe avante jadathil-
  2. Marana nizhalil kidakkum bhoomimel
    Oru velichavumoru karayum kandidaathulla maanushar
    Veendum aashwasippathinnaay paramadaathavu
    Jeevante velicham karunayaale-udippichu namukke
  3. Maraname ninne pedikkunnilla njaan, ninne jaicha yeshu
    Sharanam enikku, haranam ninakkum-avan enikkum jeevan
    Tharuvaan daivathin valathu bhaagathu
    Bharanam cheyyunnu sarvvadhikaariyaay. V.N
