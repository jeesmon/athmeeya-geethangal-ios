• Neeyen swanthame enthaanandame!
    Jeevan thannoru naadhaa ennume! athisundaran neeyen
    Nithyaanandame nithyaanandame!-
  1. Paapathin bhaaramaakave ninmel
    Kurishathil njaan kande-haa-kurishathil njaan kande!
    Aa dinam muthale-nnaadhikalakannu kaivannenikku bhaagyam
  2. Neeyen sanketham jeevante balavum
    Bhayamenthinneeyulakil-haa-bhayamenthinneeyulakil?
    Karumanakalilum karunayodarikil kaanunna thuna neeye haa!
  3. Ee divyasneham thannil ninnenne
    Verthirippaanaamo-ha-verthirippaanaamo
    Aapathum vaalum mruthiyum vannaalum aavathalloru naalum haa!-
  4. Nin thirunaamam ennaalumadiyan
    Paadippukazhthidume-haa-paadippukazhthidume
    Nin thirumukham njaan kandidumorunaal halleluyya aamen haa! T.K.S
