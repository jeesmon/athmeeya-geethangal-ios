• Maname sthuthika nee unnatha devane
    Thannude mahimakal orthu nirantharam
  1. Vaakkinaalulavaam sarvva charaacharam
    Ninnude mahimakal varnnikkumbol naadhaa
    Thrukkaikalaalulavaam njaanengane
    Thruppaadam thannil maunamaakum-
  2. Aakaasha bhoomikalaakave chamachavan
    Aayathin naduvilaayenikkay thoongi
    Kenenikkaayavan kevalam paapipol
    Kaarirumbaaniyil kaarunya naayakan-
  3. Aarilu munnathan yeshu maheshan
    Aanenikkunnathan viduthalinn udayam
    Aa thiruprabhayathen andhatha neekkiyen
    Anthare vaazhunnen aathmasakhiyavan-
  4. Chinthanam cheyyuka antharaathmaavinaal
    Nammude praananu valiyava cheythavan
    Aa thirusannidhow veenu vanangidaam
    Sarvva mahathwavum daivathinekidaam. T.C.V
