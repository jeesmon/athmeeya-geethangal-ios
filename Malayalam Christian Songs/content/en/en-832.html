1. Ente jeevanaam yeshuve! ninte thiru-ratham kondu
    Veendedutha ee nin vishwaasiye nee kaakkename
  2. Sishtamaam naalukalil nin-ishtathinu njaan jeevippaan
    Dushtanmaaril aalochanackkenne elppikkaruthe
  3. Lokam than vanmaayakalaal aakarshippaan adukkumbol
    Ekaagrathayodu en maanasam kaathidaname
  4. Jada balaheenathayil vidaruthe daasane nee
    Idavidaath-unarnnu praarthippaan thunackkaname
  5. Theey-ambukale ente-mel eyyumbol pishaachaayavan
    Neeyanpodu nin chirakin keezhil marackkaenne
  6. Ella aakula chinthayum vallabhanaam ninmelaakki
    Nalla por vishwaasathil poruthaan sahaayikkuke
  7. Paramaarthda mariyaatha nararenne njerukkumbol
    Sharanam nee aakeyen yeshuve! nin saadhuvinnu
  8. Swarnnam theeyil sphudam cheyyum-
    Vannam nee pareekshikkumbol
    Poornnamaam vishwaasavum kshamayum nalkidename
  9. Hrudayamaam aalayathil amruthamaam nin snehathin
    Mruduswaram karthaa sadaa enne kelppikkename
  10. Marubhoomiyil naalkku naal karunayin sambannane!
    Arulka nin mannayum vellavum en aatmaavinnu
  11. Maranam vare enikku tharanam nal vishwasthatha
    Sharanam appol adiyaanu thaa nin maarvvidathil. V.N
