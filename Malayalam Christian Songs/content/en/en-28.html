• Yeshu naayaka-vaazhka
    jeevadaayaka-nirantham
  1. Aadi muthel pithaavin shri thangidum madiyil
    Veethaamayamirunna chethoharaathmajane-
  2. Paapam pariharippaan paaril janichavane
    Bhoovinnashudhi neekki shaapam thakarthavane-
  3. Anchappavum cherumeen randumeduthu vaazhthi
    Ayyaayirathinnathi samthrupthiyekiyoru
  4. Odunna chorayodum vaadum mukhathinodum
    Paadettu kaivirichu krooshil kidannavane-
  5. En devadeveney-enthenne vedinjathennu
    Khinnaathmanaa vilichu-chonnu marichavane!-
  6. Aayaarilaayiranga-laayulla dootherodum
    Sweeya mahimayodum-thaane varunna para!-
  7. Haasyamaam mulmudiyu-mankiyum chernna meyyil
    Raaja saubhaagya mudra-shreekhram dharippavane-
  8. Saalempuram yadaartha-raaja nivaasamaakkaan
    Kaale varunna yooda-raajashikhaamaniye. K.V.S
