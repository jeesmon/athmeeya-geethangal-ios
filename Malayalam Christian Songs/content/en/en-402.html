• Jesus! I my cross have taken
  1. Naayaka! en kroosheduthu nin pinnaale varum njaan
    Nindyanaayi theernn ennalum nin mahathwam gho-shippaan
    • En karthaave njaan pinchellum thanraktham thaan chorinju
      Lokamenne kaivittaalum krupayaal njaan pinchellum
  2. Laukikaa-bhilaashamalla swarggathinte daivam thaan
    Ente divya pankannekkum njaan mahaa sau-bhaagyavaan
  3. Nin prasaadam en pramodam nin prakaasham jeevanaaam
    Nee thaan ente eka laakkum nee ellaatti-lum ellaam
  4. Shathru ettam krudhichaalum mithram hasichidilum
    Ninte mukha shobha moolam kleshamillo-rikkalum. A.S
