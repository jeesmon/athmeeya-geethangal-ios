• Tune: Manujanivan bhaagyavaan


    Palatharam hrudayangal palathilaasha veckunnu
    Mama laakkennude yeshu avanethreyenikkellaam
  1. Kanakamo nidhikalo, kalimela rasangalo,
    Udalin bhangiyo sukha-marulunnilleni kkottum
  2. Ulakavum athinnude bahalavum nashichidum
    Malarpolaayathu vaadum-kshanamaathram nilanilkkum
  3. Ithuthaan gopuramathi-nethiraarum varikilla
    Thiruraajyam nashikkumo? nilanilkkumoduvolam
  4. Adiyano paradeshi alayunnaakilumenne
    Suralokamahimayil- tharasaa cherthidum naadhan. K.V.S
