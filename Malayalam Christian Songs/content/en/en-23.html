1. Thirucharana sevacheyyum nararilathi premamaarnna
    Paramaguna! yeshunaadha! namaskaaram!
  2. Nija janaka sannidhiyum vibudharude vandanayum
    Vedinju vanna divya guro! namaskaaram!
  3. Pashukkudiyil jeernna vasthramathil pothinja roopamathu
    Shishu mashiha thanneyavo! namaskaaram!
  4. Krooshil thirudeham swayam yaagamaakki lokaraksha
    Saadhichoru dharma nidhe! namaskaaram!
  5. Pithru savidhamananju mama kuravukalkku shaanthi cheyvaan
    Maruvidunna maanymathe! namaskaaram!
  6. Niyutha ravi prabhayodiha punargamichu paapikalkku
    Niraya shiksha nalkum vibho! namaskaaram!
  7. Ulakinulla malinathakalakhilam pariharichu bhoovil
    Dasha shathaabdom vaazhuvone! namaskaaram! K.V.S
