• Yehova daivamaam vishudha jaathi naam
    Avan-avakaashamaam janam naam
    Paradeshikal naam bhaagyashaalikal
    Ithupoloru jaathiyundo!
  1. Aapathil nammude divya-sankethavum
    Balavum daivam oruvanathre
    Aakayaal paaridam aakeyilakilum
    Naamini bhayappedukayilla-
  2. Avaneethalathil apamaanam namu-
    Kkavakaasham ennorthidanam
    Avannaay kashtatha-yelkkukil thejassil
    Anantha yugam vaanidum naam-
  3. Nira nira nirayaay ani niranneeduvin
    Kurishin padayaalikale
    Jaya jaya jay kaahalm-ootheeduvin
    Aya veeranaam yeshuvinnu. M.E.C
