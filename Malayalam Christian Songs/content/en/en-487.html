1. Ente bhaaviyellaamente daivamariyunnuvennu
    Poornna samaadhaanaamode naal muzhuvan paadidum njan
  2. Munniloru chodu veyppaan maathramida kaanunnu njaan
    Aayathu mathiyenikku sheshamellaam daivahitham
  3. Lokayirul neengidumbol swarggamenmel shobhichidum
    Enne yanugamikkenna nerthaswaram kettidum njaan
  4. Adutha chodariyaathe irippathenth anugraham
    Thanichenne nadathaathe valathu kai pidikkum thaan
  5. Thalarnnoren manamenne kaninjitha kadaakshikkum
    Paramesha suthan thannil samaashwasich irunnidum
  6. Kaazhchayaal njaan nadakkukil enikkenthu prashamsippaan
    Vishwaasathaal nadakolvaan krupa nalkumen rakshakan
  7. Thanichu njaan velichathil nadappathil anugraham
    Irulilen maheshanod orumichu charippathaam
  8. Dinamprathi varunnoru vishamatha sahichu njaan
    Viruthinaay daivaseeyon nagariyod ananjidum. K.V.S
