• Yeshu raja varika vegam
    ee bhomiye nethiyil bharipan
    vegam varamenura cheytha
    karthadhikartha vegam varika
  1. eelokam paapaththal niranjum
    ashudhi dinam dinam uyarnum
    daiva bhayam leshamilatha
    thalamurakal varunnalo
    daivathe vendathoru valiya
    kuttathe ithaa naam kanunu
  2. kristhuvin namam dharichor
    thaneyathin shakthi ilathakidunu
    bhakthiyin vesham dharichu kristhu
    namathe dushikunor erunu
    sathanya shakthiye muzhuvan
    nithyamaay jayichu keezhadaki
    yeshuvin namam mathramenum
    ee bhovilengum uyaratte
  3. kroshin sneham nammilkodi
    lokam muzhuvanum inum ariyatte
    manasalivin hyradayam nammilum
    lokamengum kanatte
    aarum nashichu pokathiripan
    namukorumichu pravarthikam. M.P
