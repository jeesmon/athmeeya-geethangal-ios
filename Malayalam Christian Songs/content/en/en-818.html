• Njaan-enthinn-aakulan aayidunn-ulakil
    Kaavalinn und ulakaadhipathi
    Thaanente thaathanum njaanavan makanum
    Aakayaal bhaagyavaan njaan-
  1. Kaalvary-naadhan than vilayerum chenninamen vilayaay
    Nalkiyallo thaan veendeduthanne kaividumo pinne?-
  2. Sankadam thingi-ullam kalangi-yen manamurukidave
    Than karam moolam thaangi thalodum van kanivodeyavan
  3. Thaathenen chuttum kaavalinnaayi dootharin paalayavum
    Paathayil deepamaay thiru-mozhiyum nalki nadathumallo
  4. Shaashwatha bhujamen keezhil-ullathinaal
    Aashwasikkaam dinavum naasthithwathin-mel bhoomiye
    Thookkum eeshanenn-aashrayamaam. T.K.S
