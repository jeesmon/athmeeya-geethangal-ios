• Aakaashatherathil karthan vannidaaraay
    Aatma naadan chaare cheraan neramaay
    Aatma santhoshathaal pulakitharaay naam
    Aa sudinathinaay kaathirunnidaam

  1. Kshaamam mahaavyaadi bhookambam yudhangal
    Adikkadi bhuvil perukidumbol
    Namukkunarnnidaam balam dharichidaam
    Aa sudinathinaay kaathirunnidaam

  2. Bhinnatha vediyaam namukkonnaay koodaam
    Bhayabhakthiyode ottam odidaam
    Aarthu paadi stuthikkaam halleluyya paadaam
    Aa sudinathinaay kaathirunnidaam

  3. Kastathayin naalil shodana velayil
    Karthan krupa nalkum anudinavum
    Kastathakal theerum karthan chaare cherum
    Aa sudinathinaay kaathirunnidaam. V.M.J
