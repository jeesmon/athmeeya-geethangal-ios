1. Yaahenna daivam ennidayanaho!
    Yaathoru kuravumillenikkinium
    Pachappul-ppurathenne kidathunnavan
    Nischala jalam enne kudippikkunnu-
  2. Santhatham ennullam thanuppikkunnu
    Thanthiru-ppaathayil nadathunnenne
    Koorirul thaazhvarayathil nadannaal
    Saaramill-enikkoru bhayavumilla-
  3. Unnathan ennodu koodeyundu
    Thannidunn aashwaasam than vadiyaal
    Enikkoru virunnu nee orukkidunnu
    Ennude vairikalin naduvil-
  4. Shirassine akhilavum anudinavum
    Pooshunnu saurabhiya thailamathal
    Ennude paanapaathram dinavum
    Unnathan karunayaal kavinjidunnu-
  5. Nanmayum karunayum ennaayussil
    Unmayaay thudarnnidum dinavumaho!
    Swarggeeya aalayam thannilee njaan
    Deerkhakaalam vasikkum shubhamaay. E.V.V
