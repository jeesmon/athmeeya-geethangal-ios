• Tune: Immaanuvel than chankathil
  1. Nisthulya naadhan kristhuvil- vaasthavamaam modam
    Darshikkumethu vyakthiyum-krooshin prakaashathaal
  2. Avarnnyamaam than snehamo-agannya naamenne
    Rakshichu krooshin rakthathaal-shiksha vimukthanaay
  3. Saashtaamgam veenidunnu njaan-srushtaave, rakshakaa,
    Ninekken bahumathiyum-dhanam samayavum
  4. Sampoornnamaay nalkidunnu-anperum naadhane,
    En deham bhunikshepangal-nin ghoshanathinnaay
  5. En deham dehi maanasam nin sneha jwaalayaal
    Prashobhithamaay theeruvaan-naadha thunackkuka
  6. En jeevan saukhyam muttumaay-samjeevadaayakaa
    Arppikkunne nin kaikalil-cheyvaan nin ponnishtam
  7. Samasthavum nin paadathil-samarppikkunnaham
    Jaikkuka bhavaanennum-nayikka saadhuve. A.M.S
