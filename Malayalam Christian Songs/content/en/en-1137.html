    Paapikalkkaananda vishraamam
    Jaya! jaya! nirmmala suvishesham
    Kurishin nisthula sandesham

    Shaapam nirayumerinarakam
    Krupayaal daivam nalkuvatho
    Kristhuvil paapa vimochaname-

    Chirakaalam naam valayaathe
    Paragathi nammalkk arulaanaay
    Parama suthan vanniha naranaay-
  3. Aathma vishappinu virunnum van-
    Paapavishathinu marunnum thaan
    Theera vinakal theerkkumavan
    Dhaaralam krupa nalkumavan-

    nikarundoyini vere
    Thirunaamam poloru naamam
    Tharumo shaashwatha vishraamam?

    Ithopolaarini sevippaan?
    Anudinam namme paalippaan
    Aarundithupol vallabhanaay?
