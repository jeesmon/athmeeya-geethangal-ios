• Tune: Neeyallo njangalkkulla
  1. Yeshuvin veeranmaare dhairyathode nadappin
    Ashu numukkundu jayam vairiyodu poruthaan

    Yeshu thaan koodeyunde naayakanaay eppozhum-

    Rakshappetta janathinte dhairyamavar kaanuvaan

    Rakshakante vachanamaam aatmavaalum dharichu-

    Bhayappedaan adimayin aaviyalla namukke-
  10. Veeranmaarkkaavashyam nal dheerathayathallayo?
    Bheeruthwam namukkini theere venda nishchayam-
