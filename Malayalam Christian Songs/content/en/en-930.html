• Baalyakaalathile nin-srushtaavine
    Orthukolken priyane!
    Durdivasangal ninakkanayaatheyum
    Ishtamillaakkaalam vannu cheraatheyum
  1. Nalla velichamengu-makanu nee-
    Rulla kaarin nirayaal
    Ellaadavumirundu nabhassinaalallinu thulyamaayi
    Nallathaam yauvanashobhnilachudan
    Vallaatha vaardhakyamenthukil durkhadam
  2. Veettukaavalkku nilkkum-pradhaanikal
    Njetti virackkumannu
    Muttekkuninjupoku-moodal bala-
    Ppettavaren priyane!
    Muttanum saadhuvum bhedamenthenpathil
    Ittidathangu kidannu valanjupom
  3. Muppathum randumunde-yuvaavinu
    Muthinodotha dantham
    Naalppathaakum vayassil kozhinjava
    Mikkathum theernnu pokum
    Doctor patdichathum pattathum-neekkiyaal
    Shistamethaanumundottum phalam varaa
  4. Jaalakathiloode-nokkunnava-
    Raakeyandeebhavikkum
    Veedhiyin nereyulla-kathakuka-
    Kalaakeyadanjupokum-
    Bhakshymarackkunna shabdam kurayume
    Pakshikal than shabdathil vrudhanunarpom-
  5. Pattukaarokkeyayyo-thalarnnupom
    kettam bhayapradamaam
    Rottilavidavide bhayathinu hethuvum sambavikkum
    Badaam maram poothu vettukkiliyoru
    Bhaaramaay theernnidum deepanam naasthiyaam
  6. Marthyano bhoomi vittu-thanikkulla
    Nithya gruhathilekku
    Yaathra cheyyunnudane-maranathin
    Vaarthayum kondanekar
    Dukhagaanam paadi veedhiyilokkave
    Chutti nadakkunnu mattenthu saadhyamaam
  7. Vellicharadazhiyum-yadaarthdamaay
    ponnunkinnam thakarum
    Oottiludanjupokum kudam kinattil thakarunnu chakram
    Mannudan bhoomiyil cherumaatmaavathu
    Nalkiya naadante chaarathu poyidum
  8. Nallathum theeyathumaam pravruthikalkkulla kanakkavide
    Thannude nyaasabha-yathil kodutheedanam marthyanannu
    Nannaarahasyamaayulla kriyakalum
    Mannavan mumbil varaathe pokaa drundam
  9. Maayayennothidunnu-samasthavum
    Maayathaanenthu bhedam
    Nyaaya budhiyurachu-thakarkkuka Maayayin darshanathe
    Jnaana sooryanudichaananda rashmiye
    Maanase veeshanam maaya neengaan sakhe-
  10. Ellaattinum churukkam-urachidaam vallabhane dinavum
    Ullil bhayannu thante nideshangalellaamanusarichaal
    Nallathaam lokathilallalillaathe naam
    Ellaayppozhum vaanu thanne stuthichidaam. K.V.S
