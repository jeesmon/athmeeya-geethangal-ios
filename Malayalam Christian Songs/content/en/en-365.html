• Sthothram sthothram nin naamathinnu paraa!
    Enne kaathu paalicha pithaave!-
  1. Keerthimahathwam prakaasham
    Sathyam dayayum prabhaavam
    Poorthiyaay vilangunnoru karthane! deva
    Paathranellenkilum ente praarthana chevikkolluvaan
    Than krupaasanam vazhiye paartharulenamingippol
  2. Poornnachitham shakthiyodum nirnnayam salbhakthiyodum
    Ninne vandippaan sahaayam thannarulenam
    Ninnodulla preethi bhayam ennilonnichu vasippaan
    Innu ninnathmaavin agni ennil jwalippichidenam
  3. Innu shudharude yoge ennodezhunnallanam nee
    Ninne bhakthiyaay vandippaan nin thuna venam
    Anya chinthakalonnume-nnullil anayaathirippaan
    Ente nottam muzhuvan nee nintemel pathippikkenam
  4. Vyaadhi yaapathin nimitham modamaay aaraadhanackkum
    Praarthanackum varaan vikhnam aarthiyumulla
    Kristhusabhakkaarkke karthaa swastham aashwaasamarulka
    Praarthanakale shravichu poorthiyaayarul tharika
  5. Andhakaarathin prabhuvin bendhanathulloreyum nee
    Swantha bhaktharaakkidenam shakthimaan deva!
    Raajarum prajakalellaam poojitha deva! ninnude
    Raajaputhraraayiduvaan yojyathappeduthiduka
  6. Shakthanaam vishudhaathmaave bhakthi daanam cheyyunnone
    Mukthibodham nalkum nee chithathil varika
    Sathya suvisheshasaaram bodanam cheyka inneram
    Sathya vishwaasam salbhakthi datham cheyka ikshanathil
  7. Annavasthram saukhyam budhi thannarul nee innumennum
    Onnum aapathu varaathe ninnude mumbil
    Enne nadathippaalikka unnathaapithaa suthaathma!
    Ninnude naamathinennum vandanam vandanam amen
