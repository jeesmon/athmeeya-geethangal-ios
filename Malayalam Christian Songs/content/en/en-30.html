• Sthothram sadaa parane- thirunaamam
    Vaazhthipukazhthidum njaan
    Dhaathriyile marthya gothramasheshamaay
    Keerthikkum ninnude keerthiyezhum naamam
  1. Samkhyayillaa ganangal sadaa thava sannidhi thannil ninnu
    Pankamakanna nin thankanaamam vaazhthi
    Sankadamenye somseva cheyyunnavar-
  2. Jeevanattorulaka mithinnu nin jeevanaruliduvaan
    Dyovin manivilakkaayirunnulla nin
    Paavana soonuve bhuvilayechathaal-
  3. Vishwastha naayaka! nin- athynthama aishwarya kaarunyangal
    Kristhuvil vyaaparippichavannam nija-
    Dathaavakaashathin puthrarkkum nalki nee-
  4. Swargam bhuvanathala- mivayile vargamellaam pinneyum
    Kristhuvilaikyappeduthum vyavastha nin
    Shudhimaanmaarkk-ariyichu koduthu nee-
  5. Swarloka sambandhama- maashissukal ellaattinaalum bhavaan
    Chollezhum puthranil-aashirvadhicha nin-
    Nalla naamemennum cholly vaazhthiduvan. K.V.S
