• Tune: Ente daivam mahathvaththil
  1. Bhaktharil vaatsalyamulla daivame nin priyanaamee
    Marthyanil kaninju shakthi nalkanam- loka
    Shakthiye jaichu jeevichiduvaan
  2. Andhakaaraprabhu thante thanthramororo vidhathil
    Chanthamod ayachidunnu santhatham-ente
    Bendhuve chinthikkanam njaan nin hitham-
  3. Aarumen jeevithapaatha thuchamaakki daathavannam
    Kaaryamayen nila ninnil kaakkanam- ennil
    Saaramaam salgunangal vilanganam-
  4. Kristhuvin prathyakshatha naalethrayum egathilennan
    gorthu njaan nithyavum kaathirikkanam- nalla
    Shradhayoden jeevitham nayikkanam. E.I.J
