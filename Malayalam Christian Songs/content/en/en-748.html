1. Yeshu aarilum unnathanaam-ennaatma-sakhaav-avane
    Thaay marakkaam enkilum enne marakka snehithane
    Evarum enne kai vedinjidukil yeshu ennarikil kaanum
    Ethu khedavum theerum njaan thirumaaril chaaridumbol
  2. Enne thedi vin nagaram vittu mannil vannavanaam
    Ente paapa shaapamakattaan jeevan -thannavanaam
    Enthinum haa! thanthiru sneha -
    Paasha bendham azhikkuvaan kazhiya
    Ennum ennum njaanini-yavanilum avan-ini yennilumaam
  3. Maanasame! chaaruka dinavum ee divya snehithanil
    Dyaanam cheyyuka thanthiru sneha
    Madhurima santhathavum ethu khedam varikilum patharaa
    Yeshu thaan ninn aasrayamathinaal
    Anthyatholam poruthuka kurishinn uthamanaam bhadanaay. M.E.C
