• Psalms 34
  1. Vaazhthum njaan yahovaye
    sarvva kaalavum muda
    Sthothrameppozhum mama
    naavil ninnuyarnnidum
  2. Ennullam prashamsi
    chidunnennum yehovayil
    Mannileliyor-abhi
    nandi chidumaayathu
  3. Ennoadothu chernnu ma
    hathwam nalka devanu
    Onnaay chernnu naam thiru
    naamethe-yuyarthuka
  4. Njaan praarthichathinnavan
    nalki yuthara mudan
    En bhayangal sarvvavum
    pokki veendeduthu maam
  5. Thanne nokkiyorkku pra-
    kaasham vannavar mukham
    Onnilenkilumida
    vannathilla lejjippaan
  6. Saadhu njaan karanjathu
    kettu devenennude
    Baadhayokkayil ninnu-
    meki raksha poornnamaay
  7. Daiva doothaneppozhum
    bhakthanmaarude chuttum
    Kaaval ninnu ha! vidu-
    vichidunnu shakthiyaal
  8. Daivam nallavan-enna-
    thevarum ruchikkuvin
    Evan thanne nambumo
    noonam bhaagyavaanavan. E.I.J
