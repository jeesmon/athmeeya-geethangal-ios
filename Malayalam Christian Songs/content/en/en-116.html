• To Calvary Lord in Spirit
  1. Ennantharangame paadu-ponneshuvin geetham
    Enne veenda than snehathe- ennaalum keerthikkaam
  2. Aashayattitt aarthanaayi- kleshichu kenu njaan
    Eeshaa! neeyente paathakam-krooshil vahichu haa!
  3. Ennaadhikal envyaadhikal -ellaam vahippaanaay
    Ellaayppozhum chaareyullon-aashwaasadaayakan-
  4. Vinnilethum naalvareyum-kannuneer thaazhvara
    Mannile vaasamenkilum-poornnaanandam neeye!-
  5. Enperkku, pakshavaadamaay-anpezhumaachaaryen
    Unnathathil vaanidunnu- en poornna rakshakanaay. M.Varghese
