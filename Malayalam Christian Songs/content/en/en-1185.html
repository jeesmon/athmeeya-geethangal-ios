• tUNE: Yeshu ennadisthaanam


    Yeshumahonnathane! aashritha vathsalane!
    Aashishamekidanam-aashayil kenjchidunnen
  1. Kannuneer thingum paaril jeevithapaatha-paaram
    Bhadramaay kaathidenam kaarunyanaam eshuve!-
  2. Ksheenaraay theeraathengal maadhuryamerum-divya
    Nithya vachassin modam santhatham arulenam
  3. Aavashya bhaaravumaay vannithaa njangal munnil
    Yaachana nalkidenam jeeva dayaa parane-
  4. Aakula velakalil praarthana kelkkaname
    Shaashwatha shaanthiyekum karthaave! yeshu naadhaa! P.P.G
