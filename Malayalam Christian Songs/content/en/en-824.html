• Yeshu en rakshakan jeeva-
    naadhan aashwaasa daayakan
    Aashrayam than padamaan-
    enikking-aakulamillathaal
  1. Kaarirul moodumen jeevitha-
    paathayil deepamaay vannavan
    Thaapamezhum marubhoomiyil nal-
    thanal meghamaay ninnavan
    Naal-thorum nal-vazhithaarayil
    Kaividaa-thenne nadathum thaan
  2. Shodhana velayil kaikalaal
    thaangidum nalloru naayakan
    Sodarar maaridum nerathum
    maraathoruthama snehithan
    Annannu vendhunnathellaam thaan
    thannennum enne pularthume-
  3. Mannin dhanathilum maanavar-
    aarilum alleyannaashrayam
    Aakaasha bhoomikal srushticha devanilalloyenn-aashrayam
    Anpezhum than divya ponchirakin keezhi-lennumen vishramam-
  4. Enneshu vannidumannaadhi
    theernnidum kannuneer thornnidum
    En deham maaridum than roopamaayidum
    vinnil njaan chernnidum-
    Aa dinamenni njaaninnu paaridathil
    parkkunnullaasamaay. G.P
