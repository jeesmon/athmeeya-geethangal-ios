• Poornnahrudayaseva venam devajaathanu pari
    poornnanaakuvaan ithu vendathaanaho!
  1. paathimanassodekidunna devapoojaye
    paran sveekarichchidaa paramaaSissaay varaa
  2. kaayanseva palavidhaththil nyoonamaayiru
    nnathudoshahethuvaay perum saapamaayathu
  3. paakamaaya manassin theerchcha daivasevayil
    sthhirajeevanekume paranaayathelkkume
  4. nammudethennivideyothum svammilokkeyum
    varadharmmamaayath parannekanam sadaa
  5. deham, keerththi, jnjaanam, Sakthi, dravyamokkeyum
    parannaay kodukka naam sthhiraraayirikkanam
  6. koduththasesham thirichchedukkaan thudangngidolla naam
    phalamodukkamaay varum drudhamodukkamaamathu
  7. svarggathaathanennavannam poornnaraakuvaan
    paranaajnja thannaho! nirravetanamathu. K.V.S
