1. Yehovackkaay njaan kaathu kaathirunnu
    Avanennil chaanjen nilavili keettu
    Naashakaramaam kuzhiyinkalum van
    Cheettinkalum ninn avanenne kayatti
  2. Kaalukaleyoru paaramel niruthi-
    Yente gemanathe sthiramaakki theerthu
    Ennude vaayil puthiyoru paattum
    Thannavan nammude daivathinu sthothram
  3. Nigalavum kapadavum niranjavaraaya
    Loka sakhaakkale muzhuvanum veruthu
    Yehovayaam than devanil maathra-
    Maashrayamaay-avan bhaagyavaan ulakil
  4. Daivame! nee cheythor-athishaya kriyakalum
    Njangale prathiyulla chinthayum valare
    Ninnodu samanaay oruvanillulakil
    Varnnippaan kazhiyumo nin krupayarikil!
  5. Njaano eliyavan deridranennaakilum
    Nannaay karuthunnu karthaav enne
    Ente sahaayavum sarvvavum neeye
    Varuvaan naadhaa! thaamasikkaruthe. M.E.C
