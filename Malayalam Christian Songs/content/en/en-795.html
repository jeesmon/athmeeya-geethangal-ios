• Kaarunnya-kkadale - karalaliyaname
    Kaathu-kollaname-adiyane dinavum
  1. Kaikalaal thaangi nadathuk-enne nee
    Kai varum balamenikkaadhikal neengi-
  2. Ghora-pishaachin kruratha kalarum
    Paaril ninnudaya paadamen sharanam
  3. Oottamaayadikkum kaattilen padakil
    Ettavum sukhamaay yaathra cheythiduvaan-
  4. Ennum nin vachanam ennude-yashanam
    Aakki njaan-anudinam paarkkuvaan noonam-
  5. Aakula-chinthakal-eridum neram
    Aakave nin-mel-aakki vishramippaan-
  6. Ksheenathay-akannum nyoonatha nikannum
    Kshoniyil nin-janam kaanuvaanennum-
  7. Ee maru-bhoomiyil nee mathi sakhiyaay
    Aamayam neengi kshemamaay vasippaan. T.K.S
