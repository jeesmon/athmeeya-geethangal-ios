1. Neethimaanmaarin koodaarangalil
    Jayasangeethangal paadiduvin
    Yeshuvil vishwasicha arthiduvin-
    jaya santhoshame

    • Jaya santhoshame jaya santhoshame
      Yeshuvil vishwasicha ashrayichaal-
      Jaya santhoshame
  2. Vairiyin paashangal chedikkuvaan
    Swarggeeya thejassupekshichu thaan
    Marthyarkkudhaaranam nedidinaan-
    jaya santhoshame
  3. Nithyamaam neethikkaay marichu thaan
    Mruthyuvin bheethi samharichu thaan
    Nithya samaadhaanam varuthi thaan -
    jaya santhoshame
  4. Nyaaya pramaanathin shaapangalum
    Aayathaalulla vilaapangalum
    Poyaho kruishinmel muzhuvanum-
    jaya santhoshame
  5. Saujanya daanangal samasthavum
    Mochanam neethiyum vishudhiyum
    Krooshile rakthathaal ellaavanum-
    jaya santhoshame
  6. Khandhippaan veda virudhangalum
    Vandippaan aathmaavil sathyathilum
    Saadhyamippol ethu saadhuvinnum-
    jaya santhoshame
  7. Lokathin mohangal arackkuvaan
    Aakave paapathe verukkuvaan
    Shakthiyum bhakthiyum nalkunnu thaan-
    jaya santhoshame
  8. Bheeshani vairi prayogikkilum
    Eshani vyaajamaay chollukilum
    Klesham asheshamillaayathilum-
    jaya santhoshame
  9. Kurirul maari prakaashathinaay
    Durasthar nithyam sameepastharaay
    Swarggangal bhakthar nivaasangalaay-
    jaya santhoshame
  10. Thejassil vegathil vannidum thaan
    Vyaajamaam poojakal neekiduvaan
    Raajathwam asahritharkkekidum thaan-
    jaya santhoshame. V.N
