1. Thankaniramezhum thalayudayone!- devaa
    Ninkazhalinapanivor dhanyaraame
  2. Ninnudaya thirumukham paarthukonde -ninte
    Sannidhiyil ninnidumbol bhaagyavaanmaar
  3. Aayiram dinangalekkaal ninte mumbil-oru
    vaasaram kazhippathathathi modamallo
  4. Bhoothalam adiyaarkkoru paradesham-njangal
    Veedunokkiyodunnithaa priya naadhaa!
  5. Ninmukhathin velichathaal njangale nee-nithya
    Nanmayil nadathiduka yeshunaadhaa!
  6. Inpamerum thirumozhi kettu njangal-thellum
    Thunpamenye ninpaadam vanangidatte
  7. Thaamarakal vidarthunna ravipole-muzhuyaaminiyil
    vilangunna sashipole
  8. Paithalinu paal kodukkum amma pole-pari
    Shoshithamaam kulathinu mazhapole.
  9. Neeyirikka njangalkennum chaaru roopaa! ninte
    Thoomozhikal kelppikkuka gurunaadhaa! K.V.S
