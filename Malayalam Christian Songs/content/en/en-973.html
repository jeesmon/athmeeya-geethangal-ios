• Eka raajan yeshu naadhan bhoovil vaazhume
    Lokaadhipanmaar aakave thanmumbaake vanangidume
  1. ‘Mannavanoruvan neethiyil vaazhu’menna thiruvachanam
    Nirnnayamathupol nivarthikkumathinaal
    Navayugam kaivarume-
  2. Srushtikal daivamakkalin velippaadishtamaay kaathidunnu
    Pushtiyaay phalangal nalkiduvaan
    Santhushtiyod-aashikkunnu-
  3. Varanda nilam marubhoomiyum modam
    Poondakhilam vilayum
    Nirjjanadesham panineer kusumam puthumanam vitharum
  4. Daivathin mahathwam thejassum nithyam
    Urvviyellaam nirayum
    Garvvamakannu sarvvarumannu daivamonnennariyum
  5. Dukhavum neduveerppokkeyum neengi
    Saukhya samboornnaraayi
    Rakshitharevarum seeyon-
    puriyathil akshayaraay varume. T.K.S
