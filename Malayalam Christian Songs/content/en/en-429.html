• Snehichidum njaan ennaatma naadhane!
    Kaalvary krooshil jeevanum thannu
    snehichathenne nee-
  1. Swarggathin raajaavu nee bhoovil pulkkoottil jaathanaayi
    Kaadukal medukalil enne thedi nadannavan nee
    Kaikal virichaa kaalvary krooshil kandethiyenne nee-
  2. Enne vilichavan nee ennum vishwastha naayakanaam
    Neethiyin paathakalil enne nere nadathidum nee
    Koorirul vazhiyil shathrukkal naduvil kaividukilla nee-
  3. Nin sneha kkodikkeezhil maarum ennaakulangalellaam
    Nin mukha thejassinaal maayum ennaalasyangalellaam
    Inneleyu minnum ennum anannyanaam
    Nin maarvvil chaarum njaan. P.T
