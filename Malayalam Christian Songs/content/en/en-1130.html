• Innu nee manam thiriyanam nin vazhikale vediyanam
    Pinneyennu nee paranju thallukil
    Nannallaayathu peyin thanthramaam
  1. Hrudaya vaathil thurakkanam durnnayangale verukkanam
    Maa dayavodu jeevadaayakan vaathilil mutti vilikkunnu sakhe!
  2. Naaleyannath-assadhyamaam narakam ninte sambaadyamaam
    Valayittu thaan ennarinju nee vegam kristhuvin arikil vaa sakhe!
  3. Anthyakaalam-aduthupoye kristhu raajan varuvaaraay
    Enthiniyum ninnanthamennu nee chinthichu manam thiriyuka sakhe! M.E.C
