• Varunnithaa naadhan vaazhuvaan bhoomau
    Niranna than parivaara padavikalode
  1. Narannu vaazhvaan bhoomi-yorukkinaanaadiyil
    Naranude maranathaal nadannillathennaal-
  2. Nara suthanaayavan marichuyirthaakayaal
    Bharanamee bhoomel cheyvaan labhichavakaasham
  3. Pithrubharanaasana-mavannu devan nalku-
    Madhipanaamivanennu pravachanamunde-
  4. Jaricha bharanangal marichu neekki thante
    Varicha sathiyodothu bharichirunnidaan-
  5. Katdina niyamangal thadavu cheythengum thaan
    Kudilatha vittu dharmmam nadathuvathinnaay-
  6. Thannude perkkaayi mannilalanja than
    Ninditha janangade nilavili neekkaan-
  7. Thiru janangalkkettam durithamiyattiyo-
    Rarikulangalkku thakka prathikaaram cheyvaan-
  8. Parama pithaavinte thiruhithamothu bhoo-
    Yerushalem puri raaja-nagaramaakkidaan. K.V.S
