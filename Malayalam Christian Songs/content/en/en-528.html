• Kaarunya poorakkadale!
    Karalaliyuka dinamanu-
  1. Kaarananaaya paraaparaneyen
    Maaranakaari mahaasura sheersham
    Theereyudachu thakarppathinaayi-
    Dheerathayod avaniyil avatharichoru-
  2. Paapamathaam cheli poondudalaake
    Bheekaramaaya vidham malinathwam
    Chernnu viroopatha yaarnnori vannu
    Cheruvaan ninnarikathil bhaagyamundaayi-
  3. Nin valankai nivarthennethalodi
    Nin mukhathaalenne chumbanam cheythu
    Ninnudae enikkeki mothiram cherippu
    Aannum innumonnupole kaathupottunnenne-
  4. Pannikal thinnunna thavidu bhujicha
    Nindyamaam kaalangal marannupoy saadhu
    Mannavane thirumeshayil ninnu
    Swarnnagara bhojanam njaan thinnuvarunn innum-
  5. Aarkkumatheeva manoharamaam nin
    Swargga yerushalem maalikayil njaan
    Deergha yugam vasichaananda baashpam
    Veezhthiyaalum nin karunackkathu badalaamo?
  6. Jeeva parudeesinn-aanandakkuyile!
    Jeeva vasantharthuv-aarambichille?
    Jeeva vrukshakkombin meethilirunnu
    Jeevamozhi madhuramaay paaduka nee dinavum. K.V.S
