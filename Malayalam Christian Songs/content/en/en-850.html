• Ente aashryam yeshuvilaam
    Enikkennum ellaamavanaam
    Santhathavumen sankethamavan
  1. Enne nadathunnu than krupayil
    Ennum karuthunnu than karathil
    Innale, innum, ennum, ananyan
    Yeshu enikku ethra nallavan-
  2. Ullam thalarnnu thakarnnidumbol
    Utta sodarar akannidumbol
    Ettam adutha nalla thunayaay
    Muttum enikken yeshuvundallo
  3. Enne oru naalum kaividilla
    Enna vaagnathmundenikke
    Bhayappedilla bhramikkayilla
    Bhaavi orthu njaan bhaarappedilla-
  4. Ente naalukal theernnoduvil
    Ethum swarggeeya bhavanamathil
    Anthyam vareyum karthan vayalil
    Adhwaanikkum njaan vishramamenye. C.J
