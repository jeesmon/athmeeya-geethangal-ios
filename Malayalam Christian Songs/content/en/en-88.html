• Deva suthaa vandanam- sadaa thava
    Yeshu paraa vandanam
  1. Naashamakattuvaan maanuja roopiyaay
    Bhoomiyil vannavane! sadaa thava
  2. Neethiyin theeyathil ventherinjiduvaan
    Deham kodutha paraa! sadaa thava
  3. Oomaru madharu maadiyaayullavarkk
    Aamayam theertha vibho! sadaa thava
  4. Chanchala maanasa naakiya pethruvin
    Sankadam theerthavane! sadaa thava
  5. Maryathaam vara naarithan sevaye
    Sweekarichoru guro! sadaa thava
  6. Chillikal randinaal ninkrupa thediya
    Dhanyayin ponkudam nee sadaa thava
  7. Raajakumaarakaa! saadhujanaavana
    lolupaa! vandya vibho! sadaa thava
  8. Kaarirul neekkidum ninmukha chandrane
    Kaanuvaan aagrahame sadaa thava. K.V.S
