• Tune: Enthoraandamee kristheeya


    Enthorathbhuthame! kaalvary kurishathil
    Enikkaay marichen rakshaken
  1. Mahimakal vedinjanpilen perkkaay
    Marakkurishathil kaalkararm viricho
    Maranam vare marukkaatheyen
    Mahaa paathakamavan chumennozhichuvenno!-
  2. Ulakam muzhuvan ulavaakki vaakkaal
    Uyir nalkiyatho paapiyen perkkaay
    Doothavrundangal sthuthikkunnavan
    Mruthiye varicho enne snehichathaal-
  3. Mahathwa naayakan daahikkunnavanay
    Dustta marthyar nindikkunnavanay
    Ente daivame ente daivame
    Enne kaivittathenthenn alarukayo!-
  4. Pathinaayirathil shreshtanen naadhan
    Mruthiye vennavan unnathanennum
    Athisundaran bahuvandithan
    Sthuthigeethangal nalkuvaan yogyanavan. P.T
