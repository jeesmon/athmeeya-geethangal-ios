• Paapikalin rakshakan thaa-nivan paadam vanangidu nee
  1. Paapa shaapam theerppaan paaril janichoru
    Devasuthanaakumeshu naayakan-ivanarika-
  2. Aadi vachanamaay bhedamenye ninnu
    Mediniyod-akhilavum modamaay chamachathivan-
  3. Aadipithaakkalkkor-eka sharanamaay
    Saadaramaay ninna divya daana vasthuvaayathivan-
  4. Divya balikalkkoravyaaja shakthiyaay
    Bhavyamaayirunna nithya-havya vasthuvaayathivan
  5. Krooshil marichathaal naashamozhichu thannaashrayam
    Thelinjudan prathyaashayin vazhi thurannon
  6. Jeevanil pookenda vathilaam thanniludaavalaayaduppor
    Divya jeevanil kadannidunnu. K.V.S
