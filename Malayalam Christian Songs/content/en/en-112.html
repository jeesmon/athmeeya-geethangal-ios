• Bhupathimaar mudimane! vaazhka nee
    Paaril perutha paapam neenguvaaniha
    Yaagamaayoru naadhan nee
  1. Saadhuvaamivan puthujeevanil kadakkayaal
    Saadaram bhaval sthuthi cheyyume
    Jayam paadume sathatham prabho--
  2. Ninthiru krupayorthaal bandhutha labhikkilum
    Anthamattathi dosham cheythavan
    Phalam koythavan katdinan vibho--
  3. Neethiyin vidhikku mun bheethanaay bhavikkave
    Paathakanivan bahubhaagymaar nnathi-
    Yogynaaythiru neethiyaal--
  4. Raajanaay vaazhuka nin neethiyaal bharikka nee
    Raajitha mahessezhum naadhane! thava
    Daasane bharamelkka nee--
  5. Ninthiru prabhaavaminn enthu njaanurackkunnu!
    Ninthiru munnaracher veezhume sthuthi-
    Paadume madiyenniye--
  6. Kaazhchakalodu thiru vaazhchayilaver vannu
    Veezhcha koodaathe vanangidume
    Muzhangidume sthuthigaanavum--
  7. Paathakanmaar thirumun vedanayoduzhari
    Khedamodudan virachidume
    Olichidume tharamaakukil--
  8. Theeyodu mezhuku polaamaver neeyo nithya
    Sthaayiyaay param vasichidume
    Bharichidume yugakaalamaay. K.V.S
