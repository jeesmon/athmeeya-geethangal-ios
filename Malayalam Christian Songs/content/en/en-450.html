• Yeshu rakshithaavenikku nalla snehithan
    Ettam vilayulla moorin kettenikkavan
  1. Onnukondum kaivediyaathe thannaatmaavaal
    Ennum koode paarthidunna maakrupaaluvaam-
  2. Than mahathwa sannidhiyil enne-yeppozhum
    Kanmanipol kaathidunna kaarunyavaanaam-
  3. Ee lokathil koodeyulla yaathrayilente
    Kaaladikale kshanamprathi nadathunna-
  4. En prayaasangal sakalavum satatam than
    Mumbil kondu chelluvathinnu kshanikkunna-
  5. Mediniyil neridunna khedangal moolam
    Vedanappettidumbol aamodam nalkunna-
  6. Than niravil ninnanudinam kshanam thorum
    Ennaavashyangal akhilam theerthu tharunna-
  7. Mruthyuvin nerathumellaa shathruvil ninnum
    Kaathu nithya bhaagyaloke cherthukondidum. T.D.G
