• Baabeladimayin kastathakal
    Ninacheedukilaayathinattamillaa
    Shobha thingeedunna daiva janangalaa
    Deshathadimakalaayirunnu
  1. Paattukal poyavar veenakal vaayippaanottum
    Kazhiyaathirunnu poyi chinthaavivasharaay
    Seeyoneyorthavaranthamenye kanneer vaarthirunnu-
  2. Daivaalayathile bhangiyum bhakthiyu
    Mulloru sevakal nastamaayi
    Yaagavum yaaga mrugangalumillini
    Devaalayam chuttu chaambalaayi-
  3. Bhaasha marinjupoy vedam grahikkuvaan
    Sheshiyillaatheyaay theernnithalloo
    Baabel kuzhappathil naavum nayanavum
    Theereppishakeedum nischayame-
  4. Munthiringaappazha mathippazhangalum
    Thinmaan kidachilladima naattil
    Swantha deshathile swaadulla bhojanam
    Chinthayil polum ruchichathilla-
  5. Swaamikalaay nija naattil vasichavar
    Daasaraayoozhiyam cheythidunnu
    Aanja koduthavaraanjakal paalippaan
    Kaathu ninnidanamennu vannu-
  6. Santhosham kondiru kankal vikasicha
    Sundara roopikalanya naattil
    Kanneerolippichu kankal kuzhinjangu-
  7. Yaavinnadiyina sevichirunnoru
    Daiva janangalilaayirangal
    Bel vigrahathinu mumbil vanangunna
    Kaazhchayo chanku thakarthidunnu-
  8. Vellappalunkupol swachathayaandoru
    Daiva pramaanam marannivide
    Kallappurohitha thanthram sravikkunna
    Thullamashesham porichidunnu-
  9. Njaanaarthdakamaayoraaraadhana kramam
    There marukruthiyaakiyaho!
    Vasthumayamaayoraaraadhana kramam
    Theerthu nadathunnadima naattil
