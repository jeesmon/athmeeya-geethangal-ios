• Daivamaam yehovaye sthuthikka thante mandire-Oo
    Vaanagola samvidhaanameni kandu vaazhthuka
  1. Dhanymaaya thante vela mannil raksha thannu haa!
    Unnathante van krupacku munnil nammal vaazthanam
    Vanmahimayulla thante naamamethra vandiyame-
  2. Kaahalangaluchanaada bhangiyil muzhanganam
    Veenameettippaadoo nammal modageethamangane
    Kinnarathinulla kambiyeenameki nilkkanam-
  3. Thappu vaadyamoppamulla chittakal chamackkanam
    Thanthri naadamennu venda venunaadamodeyum
    Nruthagaanamodu nammal sthothra meki vaazhthanam-
  4. Karangal kottiuchanaada bhangiyil pukazhthanam
    Vallabhante nalla naama mennumennum unnatham
    Jeevanullothokkeyum yehovaye vananganam. K.J.G
