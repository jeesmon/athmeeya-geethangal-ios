• Devanandanaa! vandanam! jeevanaadhanaam
    Devanandanaa! vandanam!
    Devanandanane! pi-thaavin valabhaagathil
    Mevikkond-anudinam divya sthuthikalelkkum
  1. Kanyaanandana! vandanam! bhoothale vanna
    Unnathaadhipaa! vandanam!
    Mannil duritham poonduzhannu parithaapappe-
    Dunna manujarekka-ninju veendukondoru
  2. Ghorasarppamaam saathaante shirassu-
    Chathachoru naadhane! vandanam!
    Kroora vedenayettu kurishil marichuyirthu
    Paaram bahumaanam poond aarohonamaayone!
  3. Karuna niranja karthaave ashudhi neekkaan
    Urava thuranna bharthaave!
    Durithamozhichengale arikil vilichu krupaa-
    Varangal thanniduvaan nin karalalinjidaname
  4. Vedakaarana karthane! sarvvalokangalkk
    Aadikaarana vandanam!
    Dootharkkum manujarin jaathikkum adhipanaay
    Neethiyodu bharanam cheytharulunnavanaam. J.J
