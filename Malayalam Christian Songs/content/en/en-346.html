1. Mahonnathaneshuve nisthulanaam naadane
    Stuthichu stuthichu paadaam
    Aaraalumavarnnyamaam athishaya naamathe
    Vandichu vandichu vaazhthaam
  2. Kanyakayil jaathanaay mannil vanna naadane
    Stuthichu stuthichu paadaam
    Kaalathilathulyanaay avathaaram cheythone
    Vandichu vandichu vaazhthaam
  3. Vellathe veenjaakkiya athishaya naadane
    Stuthichu stuthichu paadaam
    Marichathaam laasare uyarppichoreeshane
    Vandichu vandichu vaazhthaam
  4. Moonnaam naalil kallara thakarthuyuirtheshuve
    Stuthichu stuthichu paadaam
    Paapathinte shambalamaam maranathe jaichone
    Vandichu vandichu vaazhthaam
  5. Veendum varaamennura cheythu poya naadane
    Stuthichu stuthichu paadaam
    Innum karuthunnavan namukkaayittakayaal
    Vandichu vandichu vaazhthaam. P.K.A
