• Daivathin kunjaade sarvva
    vandanathinum yogyan nee
    Njaanavum shakthiyum dhanam balam
    Sthuthi bahumaanam ellaam ninakke
  1. Ghorapishaachin nukam neengaan
    pora swayathin shramangal
    Chorayin chorichilaal yeshuve ee van
    porine theerthavan nee
  2. Nyaayapramanathinte shaapam
    aayathellaam theerkkuvaan
    Praayaschithaarthdamaay paapathinnaayi
    Nin kaayathe elppichu nee-
  3. Mruthyuve jaippaan nee daiva
    bhrthyanaam ninnethanne
    Nithya daivaavyaal arppichathaalee
    marthyarkku jeevanundaay
  4. Daivathin koottaayma njangal
    chaavilum aaswadippaan
    Daivathaal vidappettu krooshinkal nee
    Nin jeevane elppichappol
  5. Kuttam chumathunnathaar ninte
    shathru varggam evide?
    Yudhamozhinju samaadhaanamaayi
    Vishudhamaam rakthathinaal
  6. Halleluyya paadin kristhu
    nallavanenn aarkkuvin
    Vallabhamaam thirunaamathil srushti
    ellaam vanangidette. V.N
