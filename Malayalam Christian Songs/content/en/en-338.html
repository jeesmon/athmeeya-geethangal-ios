• Chollaame sthuthi vallabhaneshuvine
    Vallabhante nalla naamamettu chollu nammalinnu
  1. Sarvvavum chamachavan pullilkkidannu
    Keerathunithundil maathaavin chaare
    Daivathin sunuvekkandu vidwaanmaar
    Kaazhchakal arppichu thansthuthi cheythello
  2. Dwaadasha praayathil eshu maheshan
    Veda velivodangaalayam pooki
    Vedathin saarathe jnjaanathodothi
    Vedajnjaraadaram kaattiye thanmumbil
  3. Yeshuvin snaanam nadannorunerathaakaashe
    Ninneesha shabdamuthirnnu
    Daivathin preethi than puthranil maathram
    Evarum thanmozhi kelkkuka yogyamaam-
  4. Neethiyin nervazhi paapiyekkaattaan
    Paapam vahicheshu krooshinmeleri
    Shaapathilaandorin veendeduppaayi
    Jeevanil thaanuiyarthunnathe vazhunnu-
  5. Paapathin shiksha kodupporu daivam
    Mochanam thannatho than ninam moolam
    Jeevan pakarnna than chorayaal ennum
    Shudhi labhichavar thansthuthi cheyyanam-
  6. Thaathan thanvaasasthdalathu namukkaay
    Veedukal theerthittu vegam varunno-
    Reshuvin munnilannevarum veezhum
    Karthan thaaneshuvenn-ellaarum chollume. K.J.G
