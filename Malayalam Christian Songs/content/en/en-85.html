• Tune: Yeshuve! krupa cheyyane


    Vaazhthuvin! param vaazhthuvin!
  1. Raathrikaalam muzhuvan daivaalayathil nilkkum
    Daasanmaare bhavaanmaar vaazhthidin-
  2. Shudha mandirathinkal kaikaluyarthi ningal
    Vaazhthuvin yehovaye vaazhthidin-
  3. Aakaasham bhoomiyiva nirmmicha devadevan
    Seeyonil ninnu ninne vaazhthette-
  4. Eken threeyekanaakum sneha swaroopiyennum
    Ekatte mangalangal menmelaay. K.V.S
