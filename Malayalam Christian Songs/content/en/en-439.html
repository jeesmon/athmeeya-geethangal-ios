• Yeshuve ninthiru vachanamippol
    Aashishamodu nalkidaname
    Mariyayeppol nin thiruppaadathil
    Irunnukond aashayil ninne nokkunnu
  1. Thiruvezhuthukalodu divya shakthiyum
    Aryaanjaal adiyangal pizhachidume
    Karunayode nee budhi thurannu
    Maraporulaayathu thelivaakkuke-
  2. Anusarichidunnoru manassulloraay
    Manujantethallithu paran vachanam
    Ennellaaperum anubhavippaan
    Vannu nin shakthiyaal aruliduka-
  3. Nin thiru sathyathe hrudayamathil
    Pinthiriyaathe kaikondiduvaan
    Indriyangalellaam kramappeduthi
    Nin vishudhaathmaavaal bharikkename-
  4. Vedathin kaathalaam ninnil njangal
    Bhedamillaathe vasichiduvaan
    Khedamulla ellaa vazhikaleyum
    Bodham varuthuka parishudhane!
  5. Vinnil ninthirumukham kaanum vare
    Mannil jeevikkum naalennum njangal
    Kannaadiyaam ninte suvisheshathil
    Ninnekkandaashayil aanandippaan. V.N
