• Nithyaananda daivame! nin polkoodaaram ramyame
    Yaahwayin praakaarathe vaanchichu murchikkunne
    Eenmanam dehavum jeevannuravayaam
    Daivathe nokki praghoshichidunnithaa-
  1. Veedonnu kurikilum koodonnithaa meevalum
    Thedipparam nedinaar avvannami ssadhuvum
    Ennude bhupathiyum daivavumaayavane!
    Nin mandirathile yaagapeetdathe njaa-
    Ninnu kandathinen bhaagyaperumayaal-
  2. Sarvveshaa! nin mandira vaasikalaam sarvvarum
    Nirvvedamillaathoru dhanyaathmaakkal aana ho!
    Ninne manoharamaam vaanikalaalavarum
    Varnnichu vaazhthi sthuthikkum, balam ninni-
    Lulla manushyano bhaagyavaan bhaagyavaan-
  3. Idhanyarinn ullathil swarppoorathin paathakal
    Vyakthamaayittullathaal thettillavar maarggathil
    Kannuneer thaazhvarayil aayavar sancharikke
    Thaazhvara-yashruvaal neerkkulamaakilum
    Munmazhayaalathu santhoshapoornnamaam-
  4. Shakthippedumee vana sanchaarikal melkkumel
    Aarum nashikkaathavar chennidume daiva mun
    Sainyaadhipan yehove! kelkkuka praarthanaye
    Israayelin paraa! yaachanaketteni-
    Kutharamekana-mulkkanivodu nee--
  5. Njangalkku phalakame! nokkuka nee njangale
    Ninnabhishikthan mukham naadhaa! kadaakshikkuka
    Ninnude praakaarathil paarkkunnorakadina-
    Maanymaamaayiram vaasarathil bahu-
    Maanyamaayi thava daasan ganikkunnu-
  6. Dushta sadanangalil paarppathekkaal ninnude
    Shishtanivaasathile dwaasthatha kaikkolvathu
    Ishtamaaneeyenikku yaahwayaam devan mama
    Shathruvinnasthram thadukkunna khedavum
    Mithramaayulloru mithranumaanaho-
  7. Devan krupa nalkunnu devan druthiyekunnu
    Nerullavarkkennume nanma mudakkillavan
    Sainyangalin yehove! dhanyaraam nin bhruthakar
    Vandithanaam threeyekannu nithyam mahimonnathiyundaay
    varatte nirantharam. K.V.S
