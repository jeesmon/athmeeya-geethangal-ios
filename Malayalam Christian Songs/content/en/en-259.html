• En naadhane, nee paadukaletto!
    Nin thaathan ninne vitto mruthikkaay
  1. Ottikkoduthu yudaasu ninne
    thallipparanju pathrosu polum
  2. Ninpriya shishyar polumanneram
    vittodi ninne mruthyu bhayathaal
  3. Threelokanaadhan ezhayeppole
    ee lokaraale bandhithanaay-
  4. Krooshum vahicha kaalvary thannil
    klesham sahichu keri pareshan-
  5. Vanpaapamettu jeevan vedinju
    en shokabhaaramonnaay ozhichu. T.M.D
