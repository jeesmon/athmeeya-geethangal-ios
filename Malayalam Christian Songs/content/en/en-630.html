• Kristhuvil vasikkum enikku
    eppozhum santhoshame
  1. Enthellaam kashtam vannaalum
    Enthellaam nashtam vannaalum
    Aarellaam pazhichennaalum njaan bhayappettu pokayilla
  2. Shathrukkal chuttum ninnaalum mithrangal hasichennaalum
    Gaathramellaam kshayichaalum
    Njaan bhayappettu pokayilla-
  3. Sooryan irundu poyaalum thaarakangal thaazhe veenaalum
    Bhoothalam venthazhinjaalum njaan bhayappettu pokayilla
  4. Mannilen vaasam theerumbol vinnilen veettil cherumbol
    Nindakal theernnu paadum njaan
    En kannuneer thornnu vaazhum njaan. G.P
