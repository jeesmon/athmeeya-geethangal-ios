• TUNE Enthathishayame daivathin
  1. Innu pakal vinayoronnaay vannennaal karthane nin chirakin- keezhil
    Bhadramaay sharanam nalkiyathorthu njaan vandikkunnaadaravaay-
  2. Shathruvinnasthram parakkum pakalilum raavin bhayangalilum- ghora
    Maariyo peedayo leshamezhaathenne paalichenthatbhuthamaay!-
  3. Kanneerilaandu valanju niraashayil lokar njerangidumbol-devaa
    Ethrayum modamaay paaduvaan nee thannu
    Bhaagyamee saadhuvinnu-
  4. Ghoramaam koorirulerunnu paarithil naadhane! nee velicham thukum
    Pon prabhaatham varum vela vare ninnil vishraamam thannidenam. P.P.G
