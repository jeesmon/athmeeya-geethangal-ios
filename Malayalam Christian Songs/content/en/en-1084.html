1. Paaduvin sodarare! jaya geethangal
    Oothiduvin vellikaahalangal
    Veezhatte vairikal, thaazhattavar bhujam
    Vaazhatte yudayin baalasimham muda
  2. Yuda gothrathile veeranaam kesari
    Mumbil nadakkunnu chanthamode
    Daivamavarude madhye koodaarathil
    Aathmaavu meethe kodukkunnithu thanal
  3. Megham kodukkunna swargeeya deepthiyaal
    Shobhitharaamivar andhathayil
    Seeyon puriyathilethuvaan saadaram
    Deepamithu vachassathre mahaatbhutham
  4. Gambheera naadam muzhakkiduvin athi-
    Gaambheeryamode nadanniduvin
    Sambhramikkendari samkham virachidum
    Vibhramam poondavar thaazthum thalakale. C.T.M
