• Vallabhanaam yeshu-paran-
    nalla sahaayakanaam
    Sukham balavum samaadhaanavum
    Sakalavum namukkavanaam
  1. Aadhiyaalullam kalangidilum
    Vyaadhiyaalettam naam thalarnnidilum
    Uttavarellaarum kaividilum
    Muttilum kaathavan nadathidume-
  2. Lokathinnimbam thyajichidaam naam
    Krooshinte nindakal vahichidaam naam
    Yeshuvin saakshiyaay poyidaam naam
    Kleshangal sahichu poruthidaam naam-
  3. Snehathin deepam koluthidaam naam
    Jeevante sandesham urachidaam naam
    Paapikalinmanam thirinjiduvaan
    Praarthanayil sadaa unarnnidaam naam-
  4. Aatmaavil niranju balappeduvin
    Daiva-hitham nammal-arinjiduvin
    Unnatha jeevitham nayichiduvin
    Mannavan varavinaay-orungiduvin- G.P
