• Enikk-eshuvundee maruvil ellaamaayennu-mennarikil
  1. Njaan-aakulanaayiduvaan manameyini kaaryamilla
    Dinavum ninakk-avan mathiyaam--
  2. Kadum shodhana velayilum paadi-enmanam-aashwasikkum
    Nedum njaan-athil-anugrahangal--
  3. Paaril-ennude naalukalee paran-eshuve sevichu njaan
    Karanjinnu vithachidunnu--
  4. Onnu-maathramenn-aagrahame enne veendedutha naadhane
    Mannil evideyum keerthikkanam--
  5. Neerum-ennude vedanakal maarum njaanangu chennidumbol
    Maaril cherthu kanneer thudackkum. C.J
