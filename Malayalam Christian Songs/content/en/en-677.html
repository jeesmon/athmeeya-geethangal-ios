• Sevikkum njangal yehovaye ennennum
    Senayin adhipanaam naayakane
    Sevikkum njangal yehovaye ennennum
    Jayathin veeranaam yeshuvine
  1. Nammude daivam jeevikkunninnennum
    Naalthorum bhaarangal perunnavan
    Unnathan nammude balavum geethavum
    Ennum nallavan immanuvel-
  2. Nin thiru dayayaal anudinam njangale
    Ee maruvin choodil pularthiyallo
    Bheethiyum aadiyumeriya jeevitham
    Modamathaakki theerthathinaal-
  3. Parithil naam paradeshikal nammal
    Paarkkunnu nin krupayonnathinaal
    Thirinju nokki stuthikaluyarthi
    Vanna vazhikale orkkuka naam. I.M
