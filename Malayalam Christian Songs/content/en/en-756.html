• Karuthidum karuthidum karuthidum naadhan
    Karathalathil cherthanachu kaathidum naadhan
    Kashtanaalil kaividaathe thante pakshangal-
    Kkullilabhayam thannu saadhu enne karuthidum
  1. Manassuruki neerum neram thazhukidum
    Manassalinju ezhayenne karuthidum
    Kannuneerellaam karthan thudachidum
    Enteyullam kuthuhalathaal niranju kavinjidum
  2. Marana nizhalil vazhikalil thuna varum
    Ariganathe jaichidaan krupa tharum
    Sarvva bheethiyum akale akattidum
    Enneyennum paricharichu naadhan nadathidum
  3. Sugandha thailam ente thalayil pakarnnidum
    Puthiya krupakal naalthorum thudarnnidum
    Sthothra geethamen naavil uyarnnidum
    Ennum naadhan prinjidaathenn arikil paarthidum
  4. Parama seeyon puriyil naadhan orukkidum
    Puthiya veettil chennu njaanum chernnidum
    Dukhamillini maranamillini Nithya modam
    anubhavichu nithyam vaazhum njaan. G.P
