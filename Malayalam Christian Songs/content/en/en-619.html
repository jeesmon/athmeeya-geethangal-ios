• Psalm 137

    Ariyabaabilon nadikkarike chennirunnengal
    Thiruseeyon puramorthoralavettam karanjupoy
  1. Karathaaril cheruveena karuthiyenkilum paadaan
    Aruthaath angalarimelava thookkiyudan njangal-
  2. Urumodamezhum seeyon thiru ganangalilonnu
    Parichil paaduvaan prerichudamakkar chilarannu
  3. Parama devanin geetham paradesham athiloru
    Vidhavum paaduvaan melaanjadiyaar maunikalaayi
  4. Purikalkkokkeyumettam thalayaam
    shreeyerushalem Purame!
    ninne marakkaanaruthe yingoru naalum-
  5. Paramaananda poorame! thava naamam marakkunno-
    Ralavilen valankaramathine njaan marakkatte-
  6. Amithaananda dayaay njaan bhavathiye
    ganikkaanjaal
    Mama naavennude thaaluphalake samkhadikkatte-
  7. Adiyolam bhavathiye podiyaakkaan
    shramichedom
    Kudilar nin jayashree kandathi lajja kalarette-
  8. Athinaashamananjulla hathabaabelsuthe!
    ninte Kruthipol nalprathikaaram
    thava cheyvaan param dhanyan
  9. Kulade! durbhage! ninte cheriya makkale thookki
    Shilamel aanjadipponar avanennum
    mahaa dhanyan. K.V.S
