• Nallorushassithil vallabha sthuthicheyvaan unaru nee
  1. Shalyamaamirulakann alloyee bhoothalam
    Nalloli veeshi prakaashikkunn aashakal
  2. Kaarirul thiraneekki kathiravanithaa vannu
    Karunayaa kadaakshikkum kaalam nee kalayaathe
  3. Nokkukee prabhaathathin kaazhchakalathi ramyam
    Aakkunna para shakthi orkkethenn akame nee-
  4. Thannilam kathirinaal mannine shishusooryan
    Ponnin kadalil mukkunnenn eshuvumivvannam
  5. Pushpangal vidarunnu sadgandham thudarunnu
    Shashpangalilam pachappattengum virikkunnu
  6. Pakshikal paadunnu shikshayil koodunnu
    Rakshitha ganam sthuthi keerthanam thedunnu
  7. Yisrayel himamaamen karthane smarippikkum
    Muthani himabindhu dhaathrimel lasikkunnu
  8. Raavu kazhivaaraayi pakalettam aduthenna
    Daivaatma vilambaram bhuvengum muzhangunnu
  9. Raavin vilangukeezhaay kidapporkkithaa
    Yovel kaahalam nithya swaathanthryam dhwanikkunnu. K.V.S
