  1. Bhoothala naadanithaa nijathaathano-daadi thudarnnithuthe
    Modamodothina naadamathaadara-neeya mathaamithu ke-
    Ttirikkaam neevittirikkaam bhaavam
    Dharikkaam neethikarikkaam gunam
    Mathikkulithumathi chathikkilo
    Thyajikkumavanithu dharikka nee
    Thava vidhiyum rathiyum mathiyum gathiyum
    Vidhi sameepmathiladi pedumithu shari-
  2. Sodomithedanu bhadamillaathe kandaayathile kadannaar
    Saadamenye jaganaadanethirthavan
    Doshavazhikkuyarnnaar kaariyam marannaar desham marannaar
    Vaanam thurannaaneeshan thudarnnaanagni
    Chorinju chara charayerinju mel
    Karinju kadtinamaay karanjavar
    Kariyo? Karayo? Thirayoyidamura varum vidhathiloru
    Kadal nirayuyarnnithu-
  3. Maanujasuthakalaam maaninikalil bahumaanamiyannavare
    Senayin paran suthar sevichu nijahitham
    Saadichu thadavasare
    Naadan therinjaar bhaavam thirinjaar
    Manam thirinjaar jalam chorinjaan
    Kaduthu kadumuzha vidarthidaa thaduthu
    Thaduthavar paduthwavum
    Nilavum phalavum balavum kulavum
    Jalathinadiyathil thaazhthithu viravodu-
  4. Levyin suthar daathaan korahoddabiraa me-
    Nneeyivar paran hithathe
