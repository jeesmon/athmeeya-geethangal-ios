• Paahimaam devadevaa!-paavana roopa!
    Paahimaam devadevaa!
  1. Mohavaaridhi thannil kevelam valayunna
    Dehikalkkoru rakshaa navkayaam paramesha!-
  2. Lokavum athilulla sarvvavum nija vaakkaal
    Chaalave padachoru devanaayaka vandhe!
  3. Kshaama sangadom neekki praanikalkkanuvelam
    Kshemaajeevitham nalkum prema harmyame!deva
  4. Paapamaam valayil njaan apathich uzhalaayvaan
    Thaapanaashana! nin kaiyyekidename nithyam-
  5. Dharma rakshanam cheyvaan urvvyilavathaara
    Karmamenthiya sarvva sharmmada namaskaaram-
  6. Neethiyen galathil melongiya karavaalam
    Veethamaakkiya jagadthraathaave sharanam nee-
  7. Jeernamaam vasanathaal chaadhithanaayorenne
    Poorna shubhramaa manki thoornam dharippichone
  8. Nithya jeevenunnullil sathyamaayulavaakkaan
    Sthuthyamaam puthu janmam datham cheythoru naadhaa!-
  9. Deenaril kaniverum praananaayaka potti!
    Thaanu njaan thirumumbil
    veenithaa vanangunnen. K.V.S
