• Tune: Yeshu naadhaa nin krupackkaay


    Praana naadhaa! ninne njangal vandikunnippol
    Paavanamaam nin naamathe orthu saadaram
  1. Nithyajeevan marthyarkkaayi daanam cheyyuvaan
    Mruthyuvin bhayankaratha nee sahichathaal
  2. Nin maranathaal akhila paapavum pokki
    Ninne nithyam vaazhthiduvaan thanna krupackkaay
  3. Paapathinu daasanmaaraay jeevichavare
    Nin rakthathaal veendedutha snehamorthithaa
  4. Nin prayathnathin phalamaam nin daasarippol
    Nin kalppana pole ninneyorthu bhakthiyaay
  5. Nithyathayil ninmukhathe kaanum nerathum
    Nithymaam nin snehamathre sthothra sangeetham
  6. Sarvva bahumaanam sthuthi sthothram shakthiyum
    Sarvvada nin naamathilarppichu bhakthiyil. P.I.K
