• TUNE: Akkarackku yaathra cheyyum
  1. Aanandam aanandam aanandame
    Aarum tharaatha samaadhaaname
    Aruma naadhan ente arikilunde
    Athu mathi adiyanee maruyaathrayil
  2. Thann-arikil ennum modamunde
    Aanandathin paripoornnathayum
    Maan aruvi thiranjidunna pol
    Njaan avan sannidhi kaamkshikkunnu
  3. Nallavan thaanenne ruchichharinjaal
    Illoru bhaaravu-meeyulakil
    Than chumalil ellaam vechidum njaan
    Thaan chumadaake vahichiduvaan
  4. Anthyam vare enne kaivediyaathanthike
    ninnidaa-mennu chonna
    Than thiru maarida-mennabhayam
    Enthin-enikkinee lokabhayam. G.K.
