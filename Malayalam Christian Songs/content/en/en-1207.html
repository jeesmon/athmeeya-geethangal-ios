• Karunaakaraa! Daivame!
    Varamekane yeekshanam
    Thiru vedavaakkevarum
    Shariyaay grahichiduvaan-
  1. Madhupoleyallethilum rasamerumee bhojanam
    Sudha thanneyaam ponnilum
    Vila koodumaasaadanam-
  2. Thiruvaakyamaarullilorthathu
    Nalla pol kaathidunnavaneppozhum divyamaa
    Molipoonda ho! Ninnidum-
  3. Bahudooramaam yaathrayil pishakaathe yee daasare
    Vazhikaattuvaan nin vachassuthakum padikkekanam-
  4. Ripusanchayam porinaayilakunnithaa shaanthiyum
    Bhayavum kalarnnullathaam
    Prathivaada mothuvathinnu nee-
