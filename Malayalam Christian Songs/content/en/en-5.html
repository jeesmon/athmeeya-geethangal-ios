• Krishthuvin naamethe sthuthikka naam dinavum
    Sthuthikka naam dinavum sthuthikka naam dinavum
  1. Shathruvin sakala balatheyum thakarthu
    Nithyamaam jeevaniluyerthezhunnavanaam-
  2. Karunayin bhujathin balathaalinnarare
    Durithangal neekki paripaalichidunna-
  3. Paapathin bhaarathaal valayunna janangal
    Daivathodanayuvaan vazhi thurannavanaam-
  4. Naadhane naaminnu sthuthippathu kettu
    Modamodavan thejassevarumarivaan-
  5. Paavana suvishesha padavikalengum
    Kevalamarinjeesha pada-thalir vanangaan. K.V.S
