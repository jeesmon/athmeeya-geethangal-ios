• Paapikku maravidameshu rakshakan
    Paarithil vannu jeevan thannavan
    Paramonnathan kurisholavum
    Thanne thaazhthiyenneorthavan
  1. Ulakathil paapathe neekkuvaan
    Udaleduthuzhiyil vannavan
    Uyir thannavan moonnaam dinam
    Uyirthezhunnu vaanil chennavan
  2. Ennumullavan sarvvavallabhan
    Mannum vinnumellaam undaakkiyon
    Unnathaadhipan heena paapiya-
    Menne thedi vannathatbhutham
  3. Vazhi sathyam jeevanum aayavan
    Vazhi pishakaathe nadathidum
    Pozhiyum sadaa krupa maaripol then-
    Mozhikal thuki thaangidum
  4. Paapa bhaaram peri valanjini
    Shaapa theeyil veeneriyaathe naam
    Krupayeridum kristheshuvin
    Kurishil vishraam nedidaam. M.E.C
