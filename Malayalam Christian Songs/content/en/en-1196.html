• Thiruppaadam thediyithaa varum saadhu njaan yeshuve
    Krupa thannanudinavum enne nadathaname
  1. Dukha bhaarathaalen hrudayam thalaraathirunniduvaan
    Kanivolum karathalathaal kanivodenne thaanganame
  2. Samaadhaanamillaathu-lakam sadaa bheethi poondidunne
    Thava saanthwana nukamettu thangu-vorkk-enthaashwaasame
  3. Jadamohangal vedinjadiyan jeevapaathayil nadappaan
    Jagatnaadhaa! balam tharika jayajeevitham cheythiduvaan-
  4. Chuduchora krooshil chorinjen kodum paapam theertha para!
    Ninakkaayen jeevithathe nikhilam samarppikkunnu njaan. G.P
