    Devaadhidevane raajaadhiraajaave
    vaazhthi vanangidume
  1. Athbhutha nithya sneham ennil
    Santhatham thannidum daivasneham
    Ennum maaraatha divya sneham
    ennil vasikkum sneham
  2. Jyothiyaay kaanum sneham ullil
    Jeevan thannu enne veenda sneham
    Thyaagam sahicha krooshinsneham
    Divyamadhura sneham
  3. Maayalokathin moham thedi
    Shaapametta enne veenda sneham
    Ennethediya divya sneham
    ennil perukum sneham
  4. Jeevanekiya sneham sarvva lokathin
    Shaapathe neekkum sneham
    Jeevashakthiyaam krooshin sneham
    ullam kavarum sneham
  5. Vaakkumaaraatha sneham thiruvaagdatham
    Thannenne nirthum sneham
    Sarvva vallabhanaamen naadhaa!
