• Tune: Kaanuka neeyee kaarunyavaane


    Kaanukaneeyaa kaalvary thannil kaarirumbaanikalaal
    Kaalkarangal bendhithanaayi karthanaameshuparan-
  1. Paapathin shaapam neekkiduvanaay paarithil vannavanaam
    Praana naadhan paapikalkkaay praanan vedinjidunnu
  2. Mannavanaakum yeshumaheshan maanavanaay dhariyil
    Vandanathinu yogynaayon nindithanaay theernnu
  3. Aakulamaake neekkiduvaanaay vyaakulanaay theernna
    Aayiram pathinaayirangalil sundaranaam naadhan
  4. Veedukalorukki vinnathil cherppaan veendum varunnavanaam
    Veendeduthoru thanjanathinu vishramam nalkiduvaan. P.M.J
