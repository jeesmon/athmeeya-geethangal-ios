• Daivame ninakku stothram paadidum
    Oraayiram stuthikal njaan karettidum
    Santhaapa kaalathum santhosha kaaalathum
    Eppozhum ente naavu nine vaazhthume
  1. Ninneyarinjidaathe poya paathayil neeyenne thedi vanna
    Snehamorkumbol en naavathangane mindaathirunnidum
    Sthothra yaagamennum arppichidum njaan
  2. Enneyanudinam nayichchidename
    Veezhaatheyannu ninnadukkaleththidaan
    Aalambamaayidum aathmaave thannathaal
    Sthothra yaagamennum arppichidum njaan
  3. Paapa cheliyil ninnu veendeduthenne
    Paarayaam kristhanil sthirappeduthi nee
    En naavil thannu nee navya sankeerthanam
    Sthothra yaagamennum arppichidum njaan
  4. En kannu neerellaam thudachidunnu nee
    Kanmani pole nithyam kaathidunnenne
    Van krupayorkkumbol ennullam thullunne
    Sthothra yaagamennum arppichidum njaan
