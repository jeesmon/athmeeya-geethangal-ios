• Varika vegam devaa thava
    Thirujanangalkkulla durithangal theerkkuvaan-vari
    Vaika nee dharaniyil vegam vannu
    Tharika nin thirukrupasekam divya
    Bharanam varanam karuna-kkadale kaanthaa nee-
  1. Yudha shruthikal kaathil muzhangunne marthyar
    Krudha vairikalaayi poruthunne
    Idharaneethalam haa! nadungunne kshaamabhadharaay
    Marthyarellaam kuzhangunne-ninte
    Varavathallaathoru gathiyilla divya
    Bharanam-varanam-karuna-kkadale kaanthaa nee-
  2. Kollakkaar peedanangal thudarunne ninne-
    Thallipparanju palar piriyunne
    Kalla pravaachakanmaar perukunne palar
    Kkullathilezhum sneham kurayunne-ninte
    Varavallaa-thoru gathiyilla divya
    Bharanam-varanam-karuna-kkadale kaanthaa nee-
  3. Athivruksham thalirthu thudangunne venaladuthu
    Poyennunjangal karuthunne
    Atthal theeruvaan njangal kothikkunne-neeyingadukkal
    Vaathilkkal aayennariyunne-ninte
    Varavallaa-thoru gathiyilla divya
    Bharanam-varanam-karuna-kkadale kaanthaa nee-
  4. Shokathaal hrudayam haa! kalangaathe njangal
    Lokathin madithattil mayangaathe
    Dushtadaasaraay njangal bhavikkaathe krupaa-
    Vrushti varshikkanam nee kurayaathe- ninte
    Varavallaa-thoru gathiyilla divya
    Bharanam-varanam-karuna-kkadale kaanthaa nee. M.E.C
