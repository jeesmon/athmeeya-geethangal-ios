• Swarggapithaavin madiyil
    paarkkum naadhane!
    Swarggam vitteebhoomiyil nee
    vannathengane?
  1. Ethrayo mahathwamerum nin padaviye
    Marthyalokathinuvendi vittozhinju nee
  2. Mruthyu kaippariya nithya naadhane!
    Shathruraksha cheyvathinnaayathra vannu nee-
  3. Bhakthi heenaraayirunna njangalkkuvendi
    Mruthyuve sahippaan madi kaattiyilla nee-
  4. Sathya daivathinnarivaal marthyareyellaam
    Shudheekarich eduppaanaay mruthyuvettu nee-
  5. Drushyamallaathulla daiva thathwam ashesham
    Vishwamathil theliyicha vishwanaadhan nee-
  6. Eka jaathanaayavante thejassaayi njangal
    Devasuthaa thiru thejassinnum kaanunnu-
  7. Deepathullya shobhayodu jeevippaan ninte
    Jeeva vazhi theliyikka jeevanaayakaa! K.V.S
