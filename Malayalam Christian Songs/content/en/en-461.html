• Neeyallo njangalkkulla divya sambatheshuve
    Neeyallaathilla bhuvil aagrahippaan aarume
  1. Neeyallo njangalkkaayi mannidathil vannathum
    Neecharaam njangalude paapamellaam ettathum-
  2. Kaalvary mala mukaleri nee njangalkkaay
    Kaalkaram chernnu thoongi marichuyirekiya
  3. Annannu njangalkkullath ellaam thannu pottunnon
    Innum ennum koodeyundennulla vaakku thannavan
  4. Shathruvinn-agniyasthram shakthiyod-ethirkkunna
    Maathrayil jayam thannu kaathu sookshichidunna
  5. Janakanude valamamarnnu nee njangalkkaay
    Dinamprathi pakshavaadam cheythu jeevichidunna
  6. Lokathil njangalkkullath ellaam nashtamaakilum
    Lokakkaar nithyam dushichidilum ponneshuve
  7. Nithya jeeva mozhikal ninnilundu parane
    Ninne vittadiyangal engupoyi vasikkum! P.V.T
