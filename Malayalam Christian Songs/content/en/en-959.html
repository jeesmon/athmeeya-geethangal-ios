• Tune: En priya rakshakane

    Njaanennu kaanumente bhavanama-maananda mandhirathe
    Heenamaayulloree lokavumennude
    Deenatha yerumee dehavum vittini-
  1. Idharayil vasikkum dinamellaam karthanil ninnakannu
    Paarthidunnennu thanne enikkithaa
    Vyakthamaay thonnidunnu
    Itthirasheelayakannu velichama-
    Ngujjwalikkum puram kaanmaan kothikkunnu
  2. Daiva thejassu thingi vilangidum divya nagaramathil
    Ethinokkiduvaanum irulinnu shakthi yundaakayilla
    Ippuri thante manohara kaanthiyil
    Nithyam nadannidum jaathikalevarum-
  3. Paapam eduthidaatha puramathil paavana-maanavanmaar
    Paarile maalozhinju suvishramam paaramiyannidunnu
    Paaparin dweshamam asthrangal ingulla
    Paarathri-kaanandam bhanjikka yillallo-
  4. Kanneeravideyilla-kalushatha kaanuvaan polumilla
    Durnnaya mennathilla-duraashayaal dooshithar aarumilla
    Poornna sukhaprada maamee nagarathil
    Pookuvorkk illoru dukha vichaaravum-
  5. Rogamellaamakalum vishistamaam deham abhi labhikkum
    Theere yozhinju pokum mruthi nithya jeevanenikk udikkum
    Daivapithaavine vaazhthiyanudinam
    Mevumavanude sannidhiyilaham. K.V.S
