• Chenniname! en kanmasham neekkiya chenniname!
  1. Bhoomiye vaakkinal nirmmicha deva nee
    Bhoomiyil vanneduthulla shareerathin-
  2. Nischayamaayumen shikshaavidhi neekkaan
    Rakshithaavaam daivam shikshayettoottiya-
  3. Paathakar kaikalaal paavana dehathil
    Paadukalettathil ninnumozhukiya-
  4. Ponvelliyaadi yazhinjupom vasthukkalen
    veendeduppinn-aakaykayaal chinthiya-
  5. Bhandramaay theernnente jeevitham swarggathil
    Mudrayaayennum angeekarikkunnathaam-
  6. Kuttam chumathum apavaadi maunamaay
    Kuttamattulla kunjaadin vilayerum. T.K.S
