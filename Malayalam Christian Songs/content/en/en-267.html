    Narare rakshicheeduvaan
    Urvvi naadhan eshu devan
    chorinja thiru rakthame
  2. Yeshuvodee lokar chytha
    thorkka ee poongavane
    Vedanayood eshu devan
    chorinja thiru rakthame
  3. Kaattu chennay koottamayor-
    aadine pidichapol
    Koottamay dushtar-adicha-ppol
    chorinja rakthame
  4. Mullukondullor mudiyaal
    Mannavan thiruthala-
    Ykkullilum purraththumaayi
    Paanjnja thirurakthame
  5. Neenda erump-anikondu
    dusharaa kaikalkale
    Thondiya neram chorinja
    rakshithavin rakthame
  6. Vanchaka saathane bandhi-
    chandhakaaram neekkuvan
    Anchu kaayangal vazhiyay
    paanja thiru rakthame
