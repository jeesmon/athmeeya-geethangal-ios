• Parane! thirumukha shobhayin kathirennude hrudaye
    Niravaan krupayarulenamee divasaarambha samaye
  1. Irulil balamakhilam mama nikade ninnangozhivaan
    Paramaananda jayakaanthiyen manathaarinkal pozhivaan
  2. Puthujeevanin vazhiye mama charanangalinn urappaan
    Athishobhitha karunaaghana mihamaam vazhi nadathaan
  3. Hrudaye thirukaramekiya paramaamrutha jeevan
    Prathi vaasaram valarnnettavum balayukthamaay bhavippaan
  4. Paramaaviyin thirujeevanin mulayee-ennil valarnni-
    Ttarisanjchaya naduvil ninte gunashakthikal vilangaan
  5. Maranam vare samaraankanam athil njaan nila ninnitta
    Amar cheythente nila kaakkuvaan thava saakshiyaay irippaan
  6. Amithaananda sukha shobhana nilaye vannanganavaan
    Avidennude priyanodothu yugakaalangal vasippaan. K.V.S
