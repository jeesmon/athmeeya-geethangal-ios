• My faith looks up to Thee
  1. Nin raajyam daivame! engum varaname- maashakthiyaal
    Saathaante vaazhchayum aadaamin thaazhchayum
    Maattukellaadavum nin puthranaal
  2. Chintha illaatheyum antham orkkaatheyum-lokamithaa
    Therunn upaayathil vaazhunn anyaayathil
    Deva nin nyaayathil vilangi vaa
  3. Nin sathya bhakthanmaar aayore shakthanmaar-aakkiduke
    Paapathe shaasaippaan neethikkaay vaadippaan
    Veeryavum kaattuvan unarthuke
  4. Snehithan abrahaam praapicha vaagdatham orkkename
    Shudhaatma daanamaam puthran anugraham
    Bhuvaasikalkellaam aakename
  5. Bhoomiyil okkeyum nin naamam khoshikkum-veerare thaa
    Yeshuvin bhakthiyaal aathmaavin shakthiyaal
    Saakshikkaay naalkkunaal nirackkuke
  6. Aayiram aayiram aathmaavil vandanam cheyyum vare
    Oduvin doothare, paaduvin koottare
    Praarthippin shudhare eppozhume
  7. Thaatha suthaathmaavaam threeyeka daivamam yehovaye
    Raajaadhi raajane karthaadhi karthane,
    Vaazhuka ennume krupaaluve! V.N
