• Ente yeshuve! naadhaa! swarggeeya thaathaa!
    Ennum-abhayam nee ennum-abhayam nee
  1. Ellaam neeyariyunnuvallo enneyum neeyariyunnuvallo
    Ninne njaan snehichidunnuvennu nannaayi nee-arinjidunnu
  2. Thiru-hitham-arinju kondee maruvilen jeevitham njaan
    Pularthiduvaan krupayekidaname alivulla naayakane!-
  3. Aashritha vatsalane! aananda-daayakane!
    Agathikal adiyangalkkennum-abhayam arulaname sadayam. G.K
