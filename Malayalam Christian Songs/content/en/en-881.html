• Ellaam ariyunna naadhaa enne nadathunna naadha
    Enthoru bhaarangal vannidumpozhum enne karuthunnavan nee
  1. Chaarath ullavarellam doorath maaridumpozhum
    Thaangunnavan nee kanneer thudappaan nee
    Aasrayippaanum neeye
  2. Thumpangaleri vannaalum bhaagyavanaanu njaaninn
    Mannadhi manna nin kaavalinaal
    Allalillaathe njaan vaazhvoo
  3. Nin kaahalanaadam kelkkum nerathth njaanum parakkum
    Vallabha ninte savidhaththilethi
    nin paade veenu namikkum. A.K.W
