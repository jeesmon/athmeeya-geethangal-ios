• Sakaleshajane vediyum-
    Narasamkham chuvade podiyum
  1. Sankadam niranjingamarum than-
    janangale kaatharulum
    Thungathejassaa vaanil varum-
  2. Saaramaaya than vaakkukal
    nissaara-mennu thaane karuthi
    Neruvittu nee pokukayo?-
  3. Nashtamelkkil nooru gunam
    kittumennalla raajyamathil
    Nithyajeevanum kittumennaal-
  4. Daiva daasorodingidayum
    paapikal kidann angalayum
    Kopamett ashesham valayum-
  5. Choolapole eriyunna dinam
    kaalumagniyaay vannidave
    Thaaladikku thulyam nararaam-
  6. Verukombiva sheshichidaa
    chaaramaa-mavar kaalkkadiyil
    Nerukaarathi shobhitharaam-
  7. Yoodar kristhane thalliyathaal
    Khedamenthu vannorkkuka nee
    Bhedamilla neeyaakilumee. K.V.S
