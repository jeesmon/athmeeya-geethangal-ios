• Enthoru santhosham haa! haa! enthoru samaadaanam
    Anantha saubhaagyam anubhavikkunn
    Anugraha jeevithamaam
  1. Ennum karuthum than karathil
    Onnum kurathinnihathil
    Khinnatha theerkkum sannidhau cherkkum
    Enne kaakkum nallidayan-
  2. Thellum enmanam kalangukilla
    Alum pakalum nal thuna thaan
    Vallabhaneshu lokaanthyatholam
    Ellaa naalum koodeyunde-
  3. Marana nizhalin thaazhvarayil
    Sharanam tharuvaan varumarikil
    Njaan oranarthdavum bhayappedukilla
    Avanen koodeyullathinaal-
  4. Nanmayum karunayum pinthudarum
    Thinmakalonnum enikku varaa
    Vinmaya veettil cherthidum vegam
    Vinnil vasikkum deerghayugam-
