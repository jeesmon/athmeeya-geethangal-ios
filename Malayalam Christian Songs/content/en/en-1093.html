• Paaduvin sahajare kooduvin kuthukaraay
    Theduvin puthiya samgeethangale
  1. Paaduvin ponveenakaleduthu
    samgeethangal thudangiduvin
    Paaril illithupoloru rakshakan
    paapikalkaasryamaay
  2. Desham deshamaay thejassin suvishesha-
    Kaahalam muzhakkiduvin
    Yeshu raajan jaikkatte yeriho
    mathilukal veenidatte!-
  3. Omanapputhu pulariyil naamini
    cherum than sannidhiyil
    Komalamaam thirumukha kaanthiyil
    Theerum santhaapamellaam-
  4. Ee daivam innumennekkum
    nammude daivamallo
    Jeevakaalam muzhuvanumavan namme
    nal-vazhiyil nadathum. M.E.C
