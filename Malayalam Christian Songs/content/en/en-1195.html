1. Parane nin thiru-mumbil varunnoree samaye
    Shariyaay praarthdana cheyvaan krupa nalkidaname
  2. Njerengi njangalin perkkaay irannidum parane
    Varikinnee adiyaaril chorika nin varangal
  3. Anugraha muriyin poottukal thaane thurappaan
    Kanivolunnoreshuvin manassennil tharika
  4. Unarthikkum varamellaam kshanam thannidaname
    Thuna neeyenniye veraarumillennorkkaname. T.J.V
