1. Nithyavandanam ninakku sathyadaivame
    Sthothravum jayavum yogyam athyunnathane
  2. Marthyakula srashtakane nithya pithaave
    Sathya vishwaasikal cheyyum sthothram ninakke
  3. Ethrayo manaoharam nin kruthyangalellaam
    Chithramathi chithramavayethrayo shreshtam!
  4. Cherubukal mandhye vasikkum sarvvashakthane
    Urvviyengum vyaapichidum ninakkennum sthothram
  5. Maanava kulathil paapam mochanam cheyvaan
    Heenamaay kurishil shaapametta parane!
  6. Ninnil vishwasikkunnavan ennekkum moksham
    Thannarulaan unnathil chernna parane!
  7. Sarvva bahumaanam sarvva mahathwam sthuthiyum
    Sarvveshwaranaaya yehovacku thaan amen. M.V
