• Kaalvaryil kaanum snehamatbhutham
    Varnnyamallaho athente naavinaal
  1. Paapathil ninnenne veededutha snehame
    Paavan ninam chorinja krooshin snehame
    En manam kavarnnu nee athulya snehame
    Maattamillaa divya sneham ethra atbhutham-
  2. Neengippokum parvvangal kunnukaliva
    Kaanumee prapachavum dhanam mahimayum
    Marthya sneham maaridum kshanathilenkilum
    Maattamillaa divya sneham ethra atbhutham-
  3. Mruthyuvinte bandhanam thakartha snehame
    Shathruvinte shakthiye jaicha snehame
    Marthyaril marana bheethi neekkum snehame
    Maattamillaa divya sneham ethra atbhutham-
  4. Vaana mekhe swarppoore karerippoyavan
    Innum ennum koodeyull nalla snehithan
    Enne vegam aanayikkum swargga gehathil
    Maattamillaa divya sneham ethra atbhutham-
