• Tune: Paramapithaavine paadi

    Pakalulloru paripaadikal ellaam kazhinju

    Thiruvadanam kaanunnor lajjikkayilla
    Thiruvachanam paalipporkkoru bheethiyilla
    Sharanamavarkk ennum nee thanne naadhaa

    Pakalil thava kaiyenne thaangunnu nithyam
    Akathaliril sukhamodu njaan paadunnee neram
