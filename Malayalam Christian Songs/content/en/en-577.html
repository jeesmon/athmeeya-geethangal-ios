• Ennennum paadi njaan vaazhthidum en rakshakanaam yeshuvine
    Thannude naamathe keerthikkum njaan
    Ente aayussil naalellaam
  1. Enne than thanka chorayaal veendeduthenthor-atbhutham
    Enne nithyavum kaathidum thannude sneham ha varnnyamo
  2. Koorirul-erum paathayil than mukhathin shobha kaanum njaan
    Ee maru yaathrayil chaaruvaan karthan-allaathe yinnaarullu
  3. Allal-eridumbol thaanguvaan nalloru koottaali yeshu thaan
    Njaan sadaa thannude chaarathu mevidum naal-akalamalla. A.T
