• Meghathil vannidaaraay vinnil
    veedukolorukki naadhan
    Vagam vannidum than janathinte
    aadhikal theerthiduvaan
  1. Lokam namukkinnekum avan
    naamathaal-apamaanangal
    Krooshin nindakal sahikkunnathu
    naam dhanyamaay ennidunnu
  2. Vithum chumannu nammal karanjinnu
    vithackkum mannil
    Veendeduppin naalukal varumbol
    aarppodu koythidum naam
  3. Veettil cherum vareyum avan
    kaathidum chirakin maravil
    Bheethiyenniye namukkeeyulakil
    adhivasikkaam dinavum
  4. Cherum puthiya shaalem puriyil
    naam thannarikil haa!
    Theerum vinakalakhilam varavil
    tharum prathiphalam namukke. C.J
