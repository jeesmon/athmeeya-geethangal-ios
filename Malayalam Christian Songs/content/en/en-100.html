• Yeshu deva! yeshu naayaka! shreeyeshu naadhaa!
    Yeshu deva! vaazhka naayaka!
  1. Daasarinn azhalozhichullil aanandamekum
    Yeshuve krupayinnazhake!
    Naasha lokathil manushya-naayudichu mey velicham
    Veeshumathi shobhayulloru
    Velichathinneeshane yeshu naayakane!
  2. Aayiramaayiram vaanaver vanangum pathinaayiram
    perilu-thamane!
    Kaayameduthaad ipaap-mettathine maattuvaanaay
    Maayamattulloru kunjaadaay-mannil vanna
    Vaanavane! yeshunaadhane!
  3. Ulakilulla pizhakaleyellaam chumenneduthu
    Kalavathinnu gathasamanenna
    Thalamathil poykatdinamulppaa-danubhavichu parithapichittudal
    viarthu thiru shareerathil
    ninnu chorinju rudhiramippaapiye-kkaninju
  4. Irulamarnnu parajanam vannu ninneppidippaanarikilanayumalavil
    oruvante
    Cheviyaruthu thdhadithipethranavanu
    thrukkai sukhamatheki
    Katdinkayyer kaikalilayyo ninne nee vittu
    Katdinanaamennil kanivattu-
  5. Kolavidhichu thalavarelppichu ninneppilaathan
    Vilamathichu kurishinelppichu
    Palavidha paadetta pinpu-kurishilechaavanubhavichu
    Puthu shava kallarayiladakki paathaalathilethi
    moonam naaliluyirthu-
  6. Galilayaavin kadalilekkarayil shishyarkku moonnaamthavana
    nee prathykshanaayappol
    Iravilokkeppanikal nokki viphalaraayi vivasharaayi
    Maruvumavarodaruli veeshuvaan-veeshinaareshi
    Valayil valare valya malsyangal-
  7. Eliya shishyarkkamalanaatmaave-nalkuvaan swarggasthalapithaavin
    valamamarnnone!
    Puzhuvathaameeyadiyanu nin kazalinathaara bhayameki
    Karalinju krupayil nadathi nee varunneram
    Kanakalokam cherthukolluka. J.J
