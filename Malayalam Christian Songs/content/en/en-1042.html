• Prathyaashyodithaa bhaktharangunarunne
    Vannudikkum ponnushasse
    Orkkumthorum ramyam
  1. Lakshymengum kaanunnallo karthan than varavil
    Nithyamaaya rakshaye than pakshamaay nalkidum
    Lakshathil sundaran akshayanaam rakshakan
    Ethrayum kshanathil namme akshayaraakkidum-
  2. Rajaneshu vannidum nee orungeettundo?
    Naaluthorum nee avante saakshiyaakunnundo
    Malpriya sodaraa ninakkuvendi thaan sahicha
    Kashtathayil pank innu nee vahikkunnundo-
  3. Ennayundo nin vilakkil nee orungeettundo?
    Nirmmalamaam neethivasthram nee dharichittundo?
    Snehathinaazhavum neelamathin veethiyum
    Thyaagavum sampoornnathayum nee grahichittundo?-
  4. Paarilaarum paadidaatha paattu nammal paadum
    Paarilaarum choodidaatha vaadaamudi choodum
    Jeevante naadhanaay thyaagam sahichathaam
    Snehamanavaalanode seeyonpure vaazhum.
