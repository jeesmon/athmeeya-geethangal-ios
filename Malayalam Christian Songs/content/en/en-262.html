• Thungaprathapamaarnna shreeyeshu naayakane!
    Njangalkku nanma cheytha kaarunya vaaridhiye!
    Vanangidunnadiyaar-thava-padangalaashrayame
  1. Nirmalamaaya raktham sharmmadaa! nee chorinju
    Kanmasham pokki dushta karmma phalathil ninnu
    Viduthal cheythathinaal-njanga-ladi vangidunne
  2. Gathasamenayenna thottathilethi bhavaan
    Raktham viarthadhika dukhamanubhavicha
    Charithamorthidumbol-mana-murukidunnu paraa
  3. Hannaasu kaiyyaaphaavum herodu mannu ninne
    Nindichu peeda cheythathellaam sahichuvallo
    Marutha thilla thellum-roma-governor mumbilum nee
  4. Peshippulambi dushter krooshichidum pozhuthum
    Vaashikkadheenamaayi theernnilla nin hrudayam
    Vimala kaanthi chernnu mukham vilangi shaanthiyaarnnu
  5. Nin saumyamaam swabhaavam nannaay padichadiyaar
    Van praathikulya mandye mumbottu yaathra cheyvaan
    Thiru mukha prakaasham njangal-kkarulka nee sathatham
  6. Lokaika sadguruve! swarjeevanakkaruve!
    Daasarkkabheeshtamekum mandaaramaam tharuve!
    Thiruvadi niyatham njangal-kkarulanamabhayam
  7. Thathwa vithaam muniye! dushtalokashaniye!
    Sathya vedadhwaniye! jeevaa gamakkaniye!
    Karunayin dhuniye! njangal varunnithaa thaniye. K.V.S
