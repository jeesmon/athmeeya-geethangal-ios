• Tune: Yeshu maheshane njaan



    Sthothramenneshu paraa nin naamethe
    Maathram pukazhthunnu njaan
  1. Aarthikal theerthenne-cherthiduvanaayi
    Paarthalam thannil vanna
    Karthaavine maathram pukazhthunnu njaan-
  2. Jeevane neeyeni-kkaayvedinjennude
    Jeevane veendathine
    Ninachu nin naamam pukazhthunnu njaan-
  3. Paavana lokayen - jeevaneyum kondu
    Raappakal vaanidunna
    Sarvvesha! nin naamam pukazhthunnu njaan-
  4. Jeevane neeyennil- raappakal nalki nin
    Aaviyil kaathidunna
    Krupaalo nin naamam pukazhthunnu njaan-
  5. Thaatha suthaathmana-naarathavum sthuthi
    Neethiyin sooryane nee
    Prakaashamaay vaanidunnennilathaal. T.J.V
