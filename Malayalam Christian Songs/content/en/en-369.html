• Paramadevaa! ninnaathma krupa
    Choriyenami ssabhayil
    thirunaamam vilangidaan
  1. Ninn arul anusarichinnarar nadappathi-
    Ninnarulka ninnaashisham nee-
  2. Vishwaasa jeevithinn aashwaasam theriyaatha
    Samshayakkaar ninnodaduppaan-
  3. Lokamohanga laathma dehathe kshayippikkum
    Kaalamithil nin kaarunyathaal-
  4. Durkghada yugamathil ookkamay nilaninnu
    Bhakthiyennum njaan abhyasippaan
  5. Sathya suvisheshathin nithya smaadhaanamaay
    Bhaktharil nee prathyakshanallo. K.V.S
