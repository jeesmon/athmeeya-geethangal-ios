• Tune: Daivathin kunjaade


    Unnatha nandanane! devaa! vandanam vandaname!
    Nandiyode thirunaamethe njangali- nnaarthu khoshichidunne
  1. Maanava rakshakanaay neeyo deenatha poondadhikam
    Vaana bhoo paathaala mennivackeka manohara naayakanaay
  2. Nee maranam sahichu thejass aayathinaal dharichu
    Maamahathwam nin simhaasanathi-nnalankaaramathaay bhavichu
  3. Shathru samhaaramathum ninte mithrarin rakshanavum
    Thathra labhichivid-aayiramaandini vaazhunna daiva suthaa!-
  4. Anya janatheyum nin keezhil thannu pithaavathinaal
    Annavar ninte prasaadamanweshichu vannidumaasthdayode-
  5. Raajarum prabhusutharum sarvva maanava gothrangalum
    Poojikkum ninneyavar mahathwam ninte paadathilarppichidum-
  6. Mooppanmaar jeevikalum swargga senakalaasakalam
    Raappakal vaazhthum manohara naamathe vaazhthidunniyadiyaar-
  7. Njangalo ninnadiyaar ninte nanmayo vanmadhuram
    Mangalamaay anuvaasaram aaswadikkunnu paraaparane! C.T.M
