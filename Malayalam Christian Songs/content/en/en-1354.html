• Tune: Vandanam vandanam naadhaa



    Mangalamaayini vaazhum nammalellaarum
  1. Ingu shaapam niranja bhoo-neengi puthuthaayidum
  2. Ivvishaanu kkalakannu - vaanam shudhamaayidum
  3. Kannu neerum muravili-thellumillaathaamini
  4. Jeeva jala nadiyannaa-lodum punya bhoomiyil
  5. Jeeva vruksha mavidennum-kaayichidum pushtiyaay
  6. Aayathin tha naliliru-nnaashwasikkaam vatsare!
  7. Ennumennum nrupanmaaraay vaazhum nammal bhoomiyil
  8. Ningalodu koodiyenne kaanum njaanen vathsare
  9. Kaanumenne ningalumaa-shaalem raajadhaaniyil
  10. Ningalude ponmukham chumbikkum njaanaasthale
  11. Poothigandham nirayumee deham thakarnneedume
  12. Puthanaakum shareerathe datham cheyyum naayakan. K.V.S
