• Yeshuve bendhikkenne
    Nin krooshodu vishwaasathaal
    Aashayaal vishwaasathaal
    Aarthiyode kenchunne (2)
  1. Nin krooshil janicha njaan
    Nin krooshil vasikkatte
    Ente sankethamathe
    en prashamsayumathe-
  2. En prashamsa nin krooshil
    Maathramaaninnen prabho
    Karthane vasikkennil
    Ennum raajaraajanaay
  3. Krooshen bhaagya sanketham
    Yeshu ennaashaa kendram
    Avide chennethuvaan
    jeevanauka nayikke-
  4. Ninnodoppam uyirppaan
    Ninnodoppam marippaan
    En jadam marikkane
    nin jeevan ennil vaazhane-
  5. Paapa bendhanam neengaan
    Paapa shareeram chathu
    Enna sathyam grahikkaan
    enne nee sahaayikka-
  6. Ihathilen jeevanaal
    Mahathwam ninakkaakaan
    Saastaangam nin paadathil
    vandichu vanangunne-
  7. Mannine vedinju njaan
    vinnilethuvolavum
    Kanmani pol kaakkane
    Enne nin karangalil. T.T.M
