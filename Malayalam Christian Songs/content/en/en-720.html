• Mahimayezhum parameshaa!
    paahimaam yeshumaheshaa!
  1. Nisthula sneha saagarame, haa
    Prasthaavyame thirunaamam
    kristho nee thaanen vishraamam
  2. Kaarmukil bheekaramaay varunneram
    Kaanmatho niyamathin villaa-
    naayathil theerumen bhaaram-
  3. Nin mukha-kaanthiyen mlaanatha neekkum
    Nin madhuraamrutha vachanam
    khinnathayaakave pokkum
  4. Sangathi-yillilakiduvaan sneha-
    Changalayaal thiru-maarvvod-
    enne-yinachathu moolam
  5. Thaavaka sannidhi chernnathikaale
    Jeevanil nirenj-ezhunnelkkum
    paavana chinthakalaale. E.I.J
