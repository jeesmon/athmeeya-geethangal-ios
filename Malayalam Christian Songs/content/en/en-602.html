1. Daivamente nanmayorthu cheyvathaamee sarvvavume
    Divya kaikal cheyvathallaathurvi thannil nanmayilla
  2. Ottanekam kashtathakal ettiduvaaneshu naadhan
    Ishtamaakum enkilum njaan ottume niraashanaakaa-
  3. Paarthalamen swantha naado paarthiduvaanotha veedo
    Alla thellumennarivaan allalellaam nallathallo-
  4. Shikshakal thannidumenkil rekshakan kaiyund-athinkal
    Icchapole cheyyumenkil thucchamaayonnill-avankal
  5. Thejassilen vaasamorthaal aayussilinnulla khedam
    Haa! nissaaram ennarinj-ennaayussellaam paadidum njaan. T.K.S
