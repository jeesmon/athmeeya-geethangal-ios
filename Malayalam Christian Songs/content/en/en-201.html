• Manuvela vandanam! mannilekanaay
    Manujaathikkaayi vannathaa munnathaadhipaa!-
  1. Jnjaanam dhanavum maanavum sweekarikkuvaan
    Vaanam bhoomi-aakeyum yogynaam bhavaan-
  2. Sankadamere ettu nee en kadangale
    Van kurisheri veetti nee shankayenniye-
  3. Nindithanaay nissaaranaay nissahaayanaay
    Vandithanaam nee ninnathinn orthu nandiyaay-
  4. Thaazhchayilenneyorthathaal kroosilolavum
    Thaazhthukayaay nee ninneyennorthu nandiyaay
  5. Kuppayil ninnuyirthi nee shreshtaraam narar
    Koppamiruthi enneyenth althbhutham para!
  6. Ee upakaaram cheyyuvaan yogyanalla njaan
    Nee krupa kaatti naayakaa - jeevadaayakaa!
  7. Ninnude paadam thannil njaan veenu vandanam
    Cheythidumallaathenthu njaan thannidunnu haa! T.K.S
