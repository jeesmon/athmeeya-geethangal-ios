• Kurisheduthen yeshuvine
    Anugamikkum njaananthyam vare
  1. Bhaarangal neridumbol karangalaal thaangidunnu
    Maranathin thaazhvaray-athilu-menne
    Piriyaatha maadhurya nalla sakhi thaan-
  2. Avan-enney-arinjidunnu avaniyil karuthidunnu
    Aavashya bhaarangal-ananjidumbol
    Avalambamaay enikkavan maathramam-
  3. Maaridum manujarellaam marannidum snehitharum
    Maattamillaath-avanen manuvel
    Mahimayil vaazhunnuinnum enikkaay
  4. Theeranam paarilen naal thiruppaada sevayathaal
    Cherum njaan-oduvilen priyann-arikil
    Arumayod-avanenne maarodanackkum. C.J
