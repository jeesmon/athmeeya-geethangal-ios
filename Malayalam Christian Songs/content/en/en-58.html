• Ennaalum sthuthikkanam naam - naadhane
    Ennaalum sthuthikkanam naam
    Vandanam paadi mannan mun koodi
    Mandatha yakannu thiru munnilabhayamirannu
  1. Modamaay kooduka naam-paranu bahu
    Naadaamaay paaduka naam
    Geetha ganam thedi naadhanu naam paadi
    Naadhanamavante thirunaamame gathiyaay thedi
  2. Shreshta guna daayakan -avan ninackkil
    shishta jana naayakan
    Spastam thiru daasarkkishtamaruluvon
    Kashtathayil ninnavare dhrushtanaayudharippavan
  3. Thanne sthuthichidunnu janangal padam-
    thannil pathichidunnu
    Maanava mannar prasannaraay vaazhthunnu
    Nandiyodavare varu munnathane vanangunnu
  4. Devakalin naadhane samasthaloka
    jeevikalin thaathane
    Jeevannuravaayi mevum pareshane
    Jeevanulakinnudippaan sunuve kkoduthavane
  5. Thannaama keerthanam naam thudarnnu cheykilennum
    divyaanandamaam
    Unnathan thannude sannidou ninnu naam
    Mannavane puthugaana vandanangalodanisham. K.V.S
