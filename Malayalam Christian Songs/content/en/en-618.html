• Maname vaazthuka! naadhane
    thannupakaara morthu nee
    Anudinam bhakthi niranjakame!
    aathma naadhane vanangi
  1. Ninnaghangal kshamichidunnu
    nin vinakal akattidunnu
    Nanmayum karunayum nirantharam thaan
    Niravadhiyaay ninnil pakarnnidunnu-
  2. Manam kalangi thalarnnidumbol
    manassaliyum daivamavan
    Arikil varum ninakkabhayam tharum
    Alavillaa krupakal avan arulum-
  3. Thiruvadanam darshikkukil
    oru kuravum varikayilla
    Maranam vare karunayezhum
    Karathalathil ninne karuthidum njaan-
  4. Nallavanaam daivamanne
    ellaa naalum ruchicharivin
    Vallabhane sthuthi cheythiduvin
    Halleluyya sthothram paadiduvin. G.P
