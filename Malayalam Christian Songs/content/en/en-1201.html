• Psalm 130 TUNE:Ninte hitham pole


    Aazhathil ninneeshanodu yaachikkunne daasanippol
    Kelkkaname yaachana nee- shradhikkuka praarth anaye
  1. Neeyakruthyam ormma vechaal-aaru nilkkum? deva!deva!
    Ninne bhayannidum padi-mochanam nin pakkalunde
  2. Kaathirikkunn eesha! ninne-kaathirikkunn ente ullam
    Nin vachanam thannilathre ennude prathyaashayennum
  3. Prathyushasse kaathirikkum-marthyarekkaal athyadhikam
    Kaathirikkunn innadiyan nithyanaamen yaahine njaan
  4. Yaahilennum aasha vaypin-van krupayundaayavanil
    Yisraayele! ninnakruthyam pokkiyavan veendedukum

  5. Thaatha suthaatmakkalaakum -aadi daivamaayavannu
    Aadimuthal innumennum-haa! mahima kaivaratte. K.V.S
