• Tune: Yeshu maheshaa nin sannidhiyil
  1. Ennu nee vannidumeshu maheshane!
    Innulla thunpangal theernniduvaan-priya!
    Ninnodananju njaan vaaniduvaan
  2. Vinnil haa veedukal theerthittu vegam njaan
    Vannidaamennura cheythavane- naadhaa!
    Vannidaan thaamasam enthiniyum?-
  3. Mannile jeevitham klesha bhooyishtamaam
    Ninnajangalkkiham yogyamalla-vegam
    Vannu ninnanthike cherthidane
  4. Bhinnathayaalinnu ninjanam thaanidaathunnatha
    Shakthiyaal thaangidane-ellaa
    Mandathayum neekki kaathidane-
  5. Mannane sundara! nin mukham kandidaan
    Ennullam vaanjchichu mohikkunne-ninte
    Ponnu mukham kandonnaanandippaan-
  6. Unnatha nandanaa! en priya kaanthane!
    Vannu nin raajyathil cherthidane -njangal
    Onnaayittangennum vaaniduvaan. G.P
