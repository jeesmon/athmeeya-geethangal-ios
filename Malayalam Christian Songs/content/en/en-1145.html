• Vanniduka yeshu paade
    thannidum thaan nithya jeevan
  1. Eka moksha vaathil loka rakshakaneshuvathre
    Vazhiyum sathyavum jeevanumavane
    Varumo neeyinnavan chaare?-
  2. Pullin pookkal poleyallo ninnude jeevithame
    Vaadikkozhiyum maranam varumbol
    Nediyathellaam aarkkaakum?-
  3. Lokam oduvil ninne shoka-kkadalil thallidume
    Jeevitha naukayil avannidameki Povuka
    jeeva vazhiyil nee-
  4. Ethra valiya raksha innu thyajichu pokukayo?
    Iniyum samayam labhichidumenno? Ithu nin rakshaa dinamallo. C.J
