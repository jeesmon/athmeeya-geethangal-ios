• Dinamanu mangalam devaadhideva
    Devaadhideva devaadhideva
  1. Divi maruvidum jeevikalaake
    Dinavum ninnadiyina paniyunnu naadhaa!-
  2. Ninthiru theja-ssanthara menye
    Chanthamaayadiyangal kaanmathinnarulka-
  3. Thirukkaram thannilirikkuma chenkol
    Bharichidunnakhilavum vichithramaam vidhathil-
  4. Ethoru naalum- ninthiru kaiyyal
    Chethana labhichengal modamaay vaazhvoo-
  5. Nithiyamaam jeevan-puthraniloode
    Marthiyaraam-adiyarkku thanna maheshaa! K.V.S
