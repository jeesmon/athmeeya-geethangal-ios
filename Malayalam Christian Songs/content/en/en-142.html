• Deva-vandanam vandaname-kristhu naadhaa vandaname
    Yeshu raajaadhi raajaave karthaadhi karthaave
    Vandanam vandaname-
  1. Aadi kaarananaayavane-dootha vanditha vallabhane
    Nithya jeevanum, sathyavum maarggavumaaya shree-
    Yeshu mahonnathane-
  2. Vinnin mahima vittirangi paapa lokathil vannavane
    Marthya paapavum shaapavum ellaam chumannozhichunnathanaatmajane-
  3. Krooshil maricha rakshakane, Moonnaam naaliluyirthavane
    Innu vinnilum mannilum karthaadhi karthaavaay
    Vaazhum athyunnathane. G.K
