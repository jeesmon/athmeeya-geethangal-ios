• TUNE: O God, Our Help In Ages Past
  1. Inneyolam thunachone iniyum thunacka
    Iha dukha rakshayum nee eeyen nithya gruham
  2. Nin simhasana nizhalil nin shudhar paarkkunnu
    Nin bhujam mathiyavarkku nirbhayam vasippaan
  3. Parvvathangal nadumumbe pandu bhoomiyekkaal
    Parane nee anaadiyaay paarkkunnallo sadaa
  4. Aayiram varsham ninakke aakunninnaleppol
    Aadithyodaya mumbile alpayaamam pole
  5. Nithya nadi pole kaalam nithyam thanmakkale
    Nithyathwam pookippikkunnu nindrapoleyathre
  6. Inneyolam thunachone iniyum thunacka
    Iham vittu piriyumbol eeyen nithya gruham-
