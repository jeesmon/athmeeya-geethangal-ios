1. Devagana naayakane! paapabhaya naashakane!
    Deena jana paalakane! dinamennum sthuthi thava!
  2. Unnathathe vittuvannu mannidathil shaapamettu
    Paapikale veendedutha parameshaa! sthuthi thava!
  3. Sukham balamiva thannu durithangal thulachinnu
    Thirumukham anachoru thirusuthaa sthuthi thava!
  4. Manamathil samaadhaanam dinanthorumadiyaril
    Niravaayi pakarunna nikhileshaa! sthuthi thava!
  5. Varavinaayorungi njaan anudinamirikkuvaan
    Orukki maam nadathidum oruvane! sthuthi thava!
  6. Dinavum ninnadiyina sharanamennurachu njaan
    Maruvuvaan arulenam manuvela! sthuthi thava!
  7. Krupayenmel choriyuka puthushakthi nirackkuka
    Chirakin keezmarackkuka chiranthana! sthuthi thava! K.V.S
