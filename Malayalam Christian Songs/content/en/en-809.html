    Enne-thirukkarathil tharunnu
    Thava maaril njaan chaarunnu
    Mama bhaaramellaam theerunnu
  1. Kaalvary kurishathil-enikkaayi raktham nee vaarthallo
    Thaazhchayilenne orthallo-
  2. Veenu njaan paapa chelikkuzhiyil
    kenu valanjallo
    Thaanu nee vannu karam thannu-
  3. Nin mahat-sneha madhura swaram
    enmanam kavarnnallo
    Ninmakanaay njaanum theernnallo. M.E.C
