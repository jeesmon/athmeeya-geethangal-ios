• Vazhthi stuthikkumennum njaan
    Ente thaazhchayil ortha eeshane
  1. Varnnichidaanenikkente naavu poraaye!
    Ennitheerthidaamo avan cheythathe
    Aayiramaay stuthichidunne
    Aanandahasthangale uyarthi-
  2. Papashaap rogamaayathenete bheethiyaal
    Naasha garthathil pathikkum nerathil
    Sneha hastham neettiyenne
    Nin thiru raajyathilaakkiyallo-
  3. Chettilallayo kidannathorthu nokkiyaal
    Naattamallayo vamichathen jeevitham
    Maattiyallo en jeevithathe
    Maattamillaatha ninte krupayaal-
  4. Paapikalethedi vanna yeshu rakshakan
    Paapamillaa shudharkkaay ithaa varunne
    Varavin dinam athi sameepam
    Varavin prathyaasayaal niranjidaame-
  5. Allal thingum jeevithathil
    njaan vasichappol
    Vallabhaa nin snehamennil oottiyallo
    Jayageetham paadiduvaan nin jayam
    Nee enikkekiyallo-
