• Ente jeevanaayakaa! yeshunaayakaa!
    Jeevadaayakaa! ente jeevanaayakaa!
  1. Marthyarakshackkaay mruthi varikkuvaan
    Nithyathayil nee nirnnayikkayo!
    Ippaapiyen paapathin bhaaram nee vahikkayo!
  2. Parama dushtaraam drohikal njangal
    Parishudhan ninne kurishil thookkiyo!
    Ippaathakarkkennum nin krupayonneyull aashrayam
  3. Paapathe sada verukkum daivam nee
    Paapiye muda snehikkunnathaal
    Nin chankile chorayaalenne veendedukkayo!
  4. Ekayaagathaal vishudharaakuvorkkennum
    salgunapoorthi nalki nee
    Nalputhuvazhi thurannu thannenikkumul praveshanam
  5. Pareekshithanaay nee kashtamelkkayaal
    Pareekshitharkku nee nalsahaayiyaam
    Thenmozhikalen hrudayathin vinakal theerkkumeppozhum
  6. Varunnu njaanithaa kurishin paathayil
    Marichu njaanum nee ennil jeevippaan
    Eeyenikkineeyulakathin mahima venda nee mathi
  7. Swantha puthrane yaadarichidaathennum
    njangalkkaay thanna daivame
    Nee sakalavumavanil thannenne nithyam pottidum. M.E.C
