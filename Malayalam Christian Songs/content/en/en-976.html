• Karthan varunnue vegam varunne
    Kaathirikkum bhaktharkkaay vaanil varunnu
  1. Unaruka naam orunguka naam
    utsukaraayirikkaam
    Ulakilengum namukkiniyum
    Uyartheedaam kristhuvine-
  2. Nindakalum ethirukalum
    naaminne sahichukonde
    Ninneeduka anthyam vare
    Nalkidum prathiphalam thaan-
  3. Saakshikalaay ikshithiyil
    rakshakan kodikkeezhil naam
    Aninirakkaam jai muzhakkaam
    Athidhairyam namukku nilkkaam-
  4. Mannavaril mannavanaay
    mannidam bharichiduvaan
    Thannidathil iruthiduvaan
    Vannidum iniyumavan. C.J
