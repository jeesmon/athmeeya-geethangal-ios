• Seeyon sanchaaryyai
    yeshuvil chaari njaan
    Pokunnu kurishinte paathayil
  1. Moksha yaathrayaanithe njaan nadappathe
    Kaazhchayaaleyalla vishwaasathaalayaam
    Veezhchakal thaazhchakal vannidum velayil
    Rakshakan kaikalil thaangidum-
  2. Lokamethum yogyam allenikkathaal
    Shokamilla bhaagyam undu kristhuvil
    Naadhanu mulmudi nalkiya lokame
    Nee tharum perenikkenthinaay?-
  3. Saakshikal samooham ente chuttilum
    Nilkkunn-aayirangal aakayaale njaan
    Bhaaravum paapavum vittu njaanodume-
    Nneravum yeshuve nokkidum-
  4. Enne nedunna santhoshamorthathaal
    Nindakal sahichu maricha naadhane
    Dhyaanichum maanichum savichum pokayil
    Ksheenamenthenn arikilla njaan-
  5. Baala-shiksha nalkumenn appanenkilum
    Chelezhum than sneham kuranju poyidaa
    Nanmaye thankaram nalkumenn eeshanil
    Enmanam vishramam nedidum. M.E.C
