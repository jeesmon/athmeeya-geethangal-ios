1. Anpode yeshu vilikkunnu ninne
    Enneyum ninneyum thaan vilippu
    Shreeyeshu rakshakan deerkhakshamayode
    Enneyum ninneyum kaathirippu
  2. Paapathil modikkum matsnehithaa! nee
    Paapathe vittudanodi varu
    Snehamodeeshan vilikkunnu ninne
    Paapi! nee odi vaa! thannanthike
  3. Krooshilozhukunna chenninam kaanka
    Ponmukham vaadithalarunnallo
    Nin paapameshu vahichillayenkil
    Enthinnavanee vidham marichu?
  4. Yeshuve karthaavennettu nee cholka
    Vishwasichiduka thannuyirppil
    Thaazhmayod eevidhamettu nee chonnaal
    Thalkshanam daiva kumaaranaakum
  5. Yeshu ninakkaayi kaathirikkunnu
    Vegamavan chaare vanniduka
    Nin paapamellaamavan paadeyarppiche
    Mochithanaayi neeyaanandikka. K.D.W
