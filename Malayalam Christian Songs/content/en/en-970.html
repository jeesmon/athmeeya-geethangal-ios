• Yeshu maheshan thaan vaanavithaanathil
    Vanniduvaanithaa samayamaay priyare
    Orungidaam naam vegam
  1. Karuthalodini naam kaathirikkaam
    Karunayin sambannanaayavane
    Karangalaal nadathiya krupayorkkaam
    Kannuneer thookidaam
  2. Laukika sukhangal vittodaam
    Lokathin laabhangal veruthidaam
    Yosephin dheeratha kaattidaam
    Miryaamin gaanam paadaam
  3. Marannidaam misrayimin choolakale
    Pirupiruppokke akattidaam
    Snehathin kodikkeezhil aninirakkaam
    Onnaayi munneraam
  4. Chenkadal randaay pilarnnidum
    Puthuvazhi munnil thurannidum
    Vairikalellaam amarnnidum
    vijayam naam nedum. W.P.G
