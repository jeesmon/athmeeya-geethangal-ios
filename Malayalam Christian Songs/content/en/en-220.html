• Sundara rakshakane!
  1. Sundara rakshakane!
    enikkaananda kaaranane!
    Innaleyum innumennum ananyene!
    vandanam-vandaname
  2. Raajaadhi raajaavu nee ennum
    karthaadhi karthaavum nee
    Unnatha devaa! neeyenneyum
    snehichath Athbhutham athbhuthame-
  3. Shaaronile rosa nee eni
    kkaaromal snehithan nee
    Enmel viricha nin snehakkodikkeezhi-
    lennumen vishraamame-
  4. Aadiyumanthavum nee paapa
    vyaadikku vaidyanum nee
    Neethiyin sooryanum yudayin simhavum sarvvavum nee parane!-
  5. Munneyirunnavan nee maaraath-
    ennumirippavan nee
    Vannavan neeye, varaanullavan neeye
    vanditha vallabhane! M.E.C
