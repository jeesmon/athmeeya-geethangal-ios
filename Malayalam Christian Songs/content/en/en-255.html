• Chumbichidunnu njaan ninmurivukale
  1. Immaanuvele! paraa immedinie naayakaa!
    Nin murivukalaham unmayaay kandaayathaal-
  2. Paani randilum nallirumb aanikal tharachaayathum
    Praanaadhaa! nee sahichee praaniye prathi shaanthamaay
  3. Mullukondu chamachathaaulloru kireedam dharichulla
    nin shirassineyum ullapol darshichenaham
  4. Maaridam thulachangu van dwaaramaakki nin vairikal
    Chorayum jalavumoru neerurava polozhuki
  5. Varnnyamalla deva thava dandanagal bhubhaashayil
    Poornnamaay grahippaanumee mannilillayaarum drundam
  6. Kevalam pishaachin suthanaay vasicha paapikku nin
    Jeevanaaya raktham chorinjevameki nithya jeevan. E.I.J
