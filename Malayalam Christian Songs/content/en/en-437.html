• Karunaa nidhiyaam thaathane! nin vachanathin
    Mahimaavanantham-eeshane!
    Paraloka lipikalaal parishobhichidumithu
    Smaranam cheythadiyangal thirunaamam vaazhthidunnu
  1. Kaniverum rakshithaavinte swaagathaswanam
    Vitharunnu swargga shaanthiye
    Azhivillaa jjeevanum angalavillaa pramodavum
    Anayunnu ninte divyadhwani kelkkunnathuneram
  2. Suraloka rekhakalithu sarvva neravum
    Mama modamaayirikkanam
    Dinavum puthya bhangi valarum velichamiva
    Ithilninnu kandiduvaan thunacheyka paramaathman
  3. Parama guruvaam naadhane! daya niranja
    Parane! sakalanaalum nee
    Arikeyirunnu ninte thiruvaacham abhyasippaan
    Arulenam krupayennu thava daasar urackkunnu. K.V.S
