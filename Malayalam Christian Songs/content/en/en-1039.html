1. Kashtangal saaramilla kannuneer saaramilla
    Nithya thejassin ghanamorthidumbol
    Nodi nerathekkulla kashtangal saaramilla
  2. Priyante varavin dhwani muzhangum
    Praakkale pole naam parannuyarum
    Praanente priyanaam manavaalanil
    Praapikkum swarggeeya maniyarayil
  3. Bhaagyavaanmaaravar vasippor aalayathil
    Nithyam ninne stuthikkum
    Kanneer thaazhvarayo jalaashayamavarkk
    Melkkumel balam praapikkunnu
  4. Eka divasam nin praakaarathil vaasa-
    Aayiram dinangalekkaal
    Athyuthamamallo athimahathallo
    Sooryanum parichayum neeye
  5. Yudhavum kshaamavum bhookambangalum
    Yudhathin shruthiyum kelkkunnillayo
    Yisrayelin daivam ezhunnellunne
    Yeshuvin janame orunguka naam. W.C
