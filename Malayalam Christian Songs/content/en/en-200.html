• Vandanaameshu devaa!
    vandanam jeeva naadhaa!
    Vandanam mannidathil
    vanna dayaaparane!
  1. Thanka ninathil paapa
    pankam kazhukiyente
    Sankadom theerthavane
    nin kazhal kumbidunnen
  2. Neechanaamen perkkaayi
    nindakaletta devaa!
    Nisthula krupaa nidhe!
    nin sneham nisseemame!
  3. Kroora vedanayettu
    krooshil marichuyirthu
    Ghora marana bhayam
    theere thakarthavane!
  4. Nindya saathaanya nuka
    bendhithar aayavarkku
    Nithya swaathanthryam naadhaa!
    nee vilambaram cheythu
  5. Paapiye thedi vanna
    paavana roopaa devaa
    Paadam paniyumenne
    paalanam cheyka naadhaa! M.E.C
