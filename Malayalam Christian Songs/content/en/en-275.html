      Manushyaril enthu nanma kandu nee rakshaakara!

    Veendeduppaan ethra kashtam sahichu nee shaanthamaay

    Paapikalkku jeevan nalki rakshikkunnee rakshakan
  4. Krupayaale rakshappetta paapiyaaya njaanithaa
    Hrudayathil daivasneham erivaan vaanjchikkunnu
  5. Paapiyil pradhaaniyaayirunna enne rakshippaan
    Shaapa mruthyuvetta ninne nithyakaalam vaazthum njaan. V.N
