• Nee ente karthaavum nee ente survvavum
    Ee jeevitha yaathrayil (2)
  1. Daivame nee enikkaay karuthunnathaakayaal
    Ninnilarppichidunnu yen aakulangalum
    Nin karangalil thaanirikkumbol
    Nin krupayaalenne thaangidunnallo (2)
  2. Daivameyen bhaarangalum kastathayellaam
    Nin chumalil thaangiyenne nadatheedunnu
    Ninte dayayil njaan aasrayikkunnu
    Ninte rakshayin njaan aanandikkunnu (2)
  3. Daivame nin vaagnathangal orthe paarth njaan
    Nandiyode vaazhthidum nin naamathe ennum
    Prathyaasayin dinam vannidum vegam
    Nin mukham neril kandaraadikkum njaan (2) C.J.P
