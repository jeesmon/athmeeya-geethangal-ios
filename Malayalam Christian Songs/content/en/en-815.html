• Kaividall-enne-yen karthaave! nee
    Kaivittaal pinneyonn aashrayippaan vere
    Aarumill-oozhiyil karthaave!
    Eni-kkaarumill-oozhiyil karthaave!
  1. Kaividukilla njaan-oru naalumennulla
    Vaakk-enikkund-athil vishwasikkunnu njaan
    Vaanavum bhoomiyum maarum-ennaakilum
    Vaanava vaagdatham maarunnathallallo-
  2. Ennude vishamangal viakthamaay ariyuvaan
    Ninne-ppol-illenikk-aarumen daivame!
    Uttavaraayaalum uthamaraayaalum
    Thettidharippathee marthya swabhaavamaam-
  3. Marthyarenn-aavashyam-enthinn-ariyunnu?
    Nithya dayaanidhe! neeyarinjaal mathi
    Arinju nee nalkunnath-imbamo thumbamo
    Anugrahamaan-enikkathu mathiy-eppozhum-
  4. Onninu melonnaay vannidunnen mumbil
    Kunnupol van thiramalakal kleshangal
    Nee kaninjaal mathiyonnu chonnaal mathi
    Bheekara durithangal-aake-yamarnnupom-
  5. Ethra nissaramaam kaaryamaan-enkilum
    Enne kondaavilla saadhippaan daivame!
    Ethinum nin thuna venamenneravum
    Ezhayen balamellaam ninnilaaneshuve!
