• TUNE: Aanandamaanandamaanandame


    Aarumillen priyaa, neeyozhike
    Chaaruvaanee bhoovil nin daasane
    • Anudinavum ninte kaarunyathe
      Anusmarichidumathu yogyamallo
  1. Ninkrupaye orthu thiru sannidhau
    Nandiyaal kumbidumen priyane
    Paavananaam thava thirumozhikal
    Maanasathil madhu pakarnnidunnu-
  2. Aashrithavalsala en karthaave
    Aashrayippore nee thyajikkayilla
    Thirusavidhe nithyam modamunde
    Aamodathin paripoornathayum-
  3. Lokathilinnu njaan paradeshiyaam
    Naakanaadan enne pottidunnu
    Anthyatholam karthru kaaladikal
    Nokki njaan paarthale anugamikkum- K.J.S
