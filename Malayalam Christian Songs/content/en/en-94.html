• Sthuthichu paadam mahipanavane
    Mahathwapoornanaam threeyeka naadhane
  1. Rakshayude prathyaashayu-maninju
    Rakshakaneshuvin naamavum dharichu
    Athma varangalaalamkrutharaayi naam
    Aathma naadhane paadi sthuthikkaam-
  2. Nandi niranjavaraayavan cheythathaam
    Nanmakaloronnaay orthu nirantharam
    Aadipithaakkanmaar aaraadhichathupol
    Modamodaatmaavil paadi sthuthikkaam-
  3. Rakshakanaay paripaalakanaayaatma
    daayakanaay saukhyamekum yehovayaay
    Nithyavum namme vazhi nadathidunna
    Sthuthyanaam yaahine paadi sthuthikkaam-
  4. Swargga santhoshathinnuravayil ninnum
    Nithyavum paanam cheythaanandippathinnaay
    Avan nammilum naam avanilumaayathaam
    Nisthula bendathinnaayi sthuthikkaam
