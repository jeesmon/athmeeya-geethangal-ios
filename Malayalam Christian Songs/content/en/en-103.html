  1. Shreesurasevitha paadajalaja- yuga
    Paavana bhaasura bhaavasujana
    Sarvvesha saundarya! gunasaaramaadurya!-
  2. Aadiyiledanilaadi manujer-cheytha
    Paathakamaakave aahanichidaan
    Ulkkrushtayajanam cheytha thushtasujana-
  3. Paapavan bhaarama paakarichidaan-athi
    Shaapakaramaamee bhoothalathinkal
    Krooshil mruthimoolam jaicha nethaave!-
  4. Khoravisham kudichengal than jeevan-bhahu
    Saaramaay kaathoru dharmma sudheeraa!
    Salbhudhi tharanam
