• Vanniduka snehamaay vilichidunnu yeshu
    Mannidathil maanavar samastharum-
  1. Unnathathil ninne cherthiduvaan-yeshu
    Ulakithil baliyaayi theernnu
    Kannathiladikal mushtiyaalidikal
    Kashtangalum nindakalum sahichu-
  2. Paapiyaay paaril nee marichaal-angu
    Paathaalathil chernnidum sunischayam
    Paapangal kshamichidum swargga lokam cherthidum
    Paapi vanniduka yeshu sannidhau-
  3. Alppakaalam maathramulla jeevitham- bhoovil
    Kristhuvinnaay nee samarppichidukil
    Dushtane jaichidaam pushtamodam vaanidaam
    Shreshtanaam shreeyeshuve namichidaam. P.M.J
