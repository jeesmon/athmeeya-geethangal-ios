    Krupaye marannidaathe-
    krupaye marannidaathe
  1. Papikalaay naa-maadhikalarnnum
    bhaaviyeyorthu bhayannum
    Paarthoruneram nammale thediya
    Parane dinavum sthuthicheythidaam
  2. Irulin bheekara vazhiyil ninnum
    nirupama thajassil vannu
    Nija guna mahima ghoshanam cheyvaan
    Nirmmala janamaay namme karuthiya-
  3. Neecharaam nammale mochitharaakki raajapurohitharaakki
    Aathmika balikal kazhipathinaayi
    Yogyarenenni athbhuthamaamee. T.K.S
