• anthatham vandanamen parameshane-
    yanuvaasaram
  1. Hantha! jeevadayaanidhe! thava
    swanthasunuvilennude
    Bendutheckkoru maarggamekiya
    ninkrupack abhivandanam-
  2. Jeevalokamithinkal ninnathi
    shaapameriya bhoothale
    Jeevapoorthi varuthuvaan thava
    divyasoonuve nalki nee-
  3. Paapamettu valanjalanjathi
    bharamarnn uzhalumbozhen
    Shaapamaakeyozhicha nin krupa
    jeevanaay vilasunnitha-
  4. Ninsuthankalirunna vankrupa
    enkalekku pakarnnu nee
    Nin sabhaanga nilackenikkava-
    kaashameki athorthithaa-
  5. Devaraajyamanantha shakthiyilee
    jagathiludikkave
    Jeevanaayaka sammukham mama
    kaanuvan krupa cheyyane. K.V.S
