1. Sahodarare, pukazthidaam sadaa paraneshuvin krupaye
    Mahonnathanaamavan namukkaay marichuyire dharikkukayaay
    Mahaatbhuthamee mahaadayaye-marakkaanaavatho priyare
  2. Bhayankaramaaya van-narakaa vakaashikalaayidum nammil
    Priyam kalaraan mukhaantharamaaya than dayayenthu nisthullyam
    Jayam tharuvaan balam tharuvaan- upaadhiyumee mahaadayayaam
  3. Nijaajnakale anaadaricha janaavaliyaakumee namme
    Niraakarikkaathe van dayayaal pularthukayaayavan chemme
    Niraamayaraay vimochitharaay-vishudha vamshamaay nammal
  4. Sahaayakanaay dinamthorum sameepamavan namukkundu
    Manam kalangaathirunnidaam -dhanam kuranjaalumee bhoovil
    Samaadhaanam sadaa modam-namukkundaayathum krupayaal. T.K.S
