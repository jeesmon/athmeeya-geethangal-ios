• Rock of Ages
  1. Pilarnnoru paaraye! ninnil njaan marayatte
    Thuranna nin chankile raktha-neer en paapathe
    Neekki sukham nalkatte muttum rakshikkayenne
  2. Thikavaay kalpanaye kaappaan aalalla ee njaan
    Vairaagyam eriyaalum kannuneer chorinjaalum
    Vannidaa paapa-nasham nee thaan rakshikka venam
  3. Verum-kaiyaay njaanangu krooshil maathram nambunnu
    Nagnan njaan nin vasthram thaa
    heenan njaan nin krupa thaa
    Mlechhanaay varunnithaa swatchhanaakku rakshakaa!
  4. Ennilodunnee-shwaasam vitten kanmangu-nneram
    Ajnjaatha lokam chernnu ninne njaan kaanu-nneram
    Pilarnnoru paaraye! ninnil njaan marayatte. T.K
