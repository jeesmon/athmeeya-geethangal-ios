• Sthuthigeetham paaduka naam
    uyarthuka jayanaamam
  1. Sthuthikku yogyanavan
    sarvvashakthan yehovayavan
    Namme snehichu namme veendeduthu
    Swantha janamaay theerthathinaal-
  2. Rogikku vaidyanavan
    sarvvashakthan yehovayavan
    Saukhyam nalki thaan shakthiyekidum
    Ennum aashwaasam pakarumavan-
  3. Senakalin naayakan
    sarvvashakthan yehovayavan
    Sthuthi sthothravum ellaa pukazhchayum
    Avanennennum aamen-
  4. Rajadhi raajanavan
    sarvaa shakthan yehovayavan
    Sthuthi stothravum ella pukazhchayum
    Avanennennum aamen
