• Naadan varavu kaathu bodhamaarnnirunnithu
    Chaathuryamode pathu kanyakamaar
    Kaatharyameshaathavar
  1. Budhimathikalenna kruthyamaay karuthinaar
    Budhiyillaathoranchu kanyakamaar
    Nishtayillaathirunnithil-
  2. Nindritharaayangardha raathriyil varanude
    Prasthaanaghosham kettu-kanyakamaar
    Uthsitharaayaar sheekhram-
  3. Deepangal thelippathinnaayavar thudanginaar
    Moodakalaamanchu-kanyakamaar
    Aadalaal pinvaanginaar-
  4. Snehamillaaykayaale deepangal polinjithu
    Aakave thelinjanchu-kanyakamaar
    Shobhayaay kathichava-
  5. Ennayin prabhaavathaalanneram manavara
    Thannullil kadannaho! Kanyakamaar
    Thurnamaay varanodum-
  6. Budhiheenaraamavar kathippaanenna vaangi
    Yethiya nerathekku-manavara
    Kruthyamaayadachaho!-
  7. Karthane njangalkkum nee perthum thurakkaname
    Praarthicheevannamayyo-kanyakamaar
    Moorchikkayalle phalam-
  8. Ningalaarennariyunnilla njaanorungaatha
    Kanyakamaare! Vegam poykkolluvin
    Kanneerin sthaanathekke-
  9. Ennurachidum varan-mannavanaakumeshu
    Thanneyaanaayavante varavinnu
    Poornnaraay kaathirippin. K.V.S
