1. Ninnodu praarthippaan priya pithaave!
    Vanna nin makkale chevikkondaalum
  2. Yeshuvin naamathil vannithaa njangal
    Aashisham tharika nin vaagdatham pole
  3. Parishudhaathmaavin sahaayathe nalki
    Shariyaayi praarthdippaan abhyasippikka!
  4. Lokathin chinthakal leshamillaatha
    Ekamaam maanasam thannarulenam
  5. Shudhamaam kaikaluyarthuvaanaayi
    Shudhanaam naadhane! nee krupa cheyka!
  6. Chodippin nalkum njaanenna nin vaakkil
    Modamodaashrayam vechu nin makkal
  7. Ennamillaatha nin krupakalkkaayi
    Nandiyum sthothravum ennekkum aamen! V.N
