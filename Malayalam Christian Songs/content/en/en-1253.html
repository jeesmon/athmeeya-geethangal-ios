• Managalam mangalame-puthu
    Vadhuvararivarkkinnumennekkum
    Managalam mangalame!
  1. Aadiyiledanil nee -bhavya-
    dambathikalkk aashissekiyathu vidham
    Aadiyiledanil nee naadhaa
    anugraham nalkidenam sarvva-
    Saubhaagyathodennum vaazhuvaanaayivar
  2. Jeevitha poovalliyil-nalla
    Komalamaam varnnappookkal choodi jeevithappoovalliyil Modamiyannu
    saurabhyam pakarnnivar
    Mediniyil shubham paarkkuvaanaayidam
  3. Kristhuvum thansabhayum-enna-
    polivarekashareemaay chernnu
    Kristhuvum thansabhayum vaazhanam verpiriyaathanthyam vare
    Vedanayerunna neravum snehamaay-
  4. Daivika raajyatheyum-athin
    Neethiyeyum munbe thediyennum
    daivika raajyatheyum Paarilengum
    nalla maathruka kaatti
    swarggeeyapuri nokki yaathra
    cheyvaan mudaa. G.P
