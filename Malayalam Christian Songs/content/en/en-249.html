• Devesha! yeshupara! jeevanenikkaay vedinjo
    Jeevanatta paapikalku nithyajeevan koduppanaay nee maricho!
  1. Gathasamana poovanathil adhikabhaaram vahichathinaal
    Athivyadayil aayittum thaathanishtam nadappathin anusarichu-
  2. Annaasin aramanayil mannava! nee vidhikkappettu
    Kannangalil karangal kondu manna ninne adichavar parihasichu-
  3. Peelathosenn avanum vilamathichu kurishelppichu
    Thalayil mullaal mudiyum vechu palar pala paadukal cheythu ninne-
  4. Balaheenan aaya ninne valiya kola maram chumathi
    Thalayodida malamukalil alivillaathayyo yuder nadathi ninne-
  5. Thirukkarangal aanikondu marathodu cherthadichu
    Iruvashavum kurishukalil irukallar naduvil nee maricho para-
  6. Katdina daaham pidichathinaal kaadivaangaan idayaayo
    Uduppukoodi chittiyittu udambum kuthi thuranno rudhiram chinthi-
  7. Nin maranam kondente van narakam neeyakatti
    Ninmahathwam thediyini enkaalam kazhippaan krupa cheyyaname. J.J
