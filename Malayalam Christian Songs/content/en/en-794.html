• Art thou weary
  1. Yeshuve! en aashrayam nee eka aashrayam
    Ninnilunde poornna bhaagyam saujanyam
  2. Ninnilunde paapathinnaay poornna mochanam
    Ninnil ellaa bedhanmaarkkaay swaathanthryam
  3. Ninnilunde poornna shudhi krooshin rakthathaal
    Vendumpol nee kazhukidum ente kaal
  4. Ninnilunde jeeva vellam daaham theerkkuvaan
    Ini venda ingum angum oduvaan
  5. Ninnilunde aatma-daanam paripoornnamaay
    Kaviyunnor paanapaathram enikkaay
  6. Ninnilunden vazhi ellaam poornna thelivaay
    Valanjathu nereyaakkum nin thrukkai
  7. Ninnilunde poornna praapthi enne kaakkuvaan
    Kuttamillaathor samaapthi nalkum thaan
  8. Ninnilunde daivathinte sarvva niravum
    Ninnil theernn-enikkum-ellaa kuravum. V.N
