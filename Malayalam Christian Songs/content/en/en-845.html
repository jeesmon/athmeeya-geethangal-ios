• Thiru rakthathaal vahichu enne
    Thiru hithampol nadathename
    Kushavan kayyil kalimannu njaan
    Anudinam nee paniyename
  1. Nin vachanam dyaanikkumbol
    En hrudayam aaswasikkum
    Koorirulin thaazhvarayil
    Deepamathaay nin mozhikal
  2. Aazhiyathil olangalaal
    Valanjidumbol en padakil
    Ente priyan yeshuvunde
    Chernnidume bhavanmathil-
  3. Avan namukkaay jeevan nalki
    Orukkiyallo valiya raksha
    Drushtikalaal kaanunnu njaan
    Swargga kanaan deshamathe. J.V.P
