• Vaazhthuka nee maname-en parane
    Vaazhthuka nee maname
  1. Vaazhthuka than shudha naamethe perthu
    Paarthivan thannupakaarathe orthu-
  2. Ninnakruthyam paranokkayum pokki
    Thinnamaay rogangal neekki nannaakki-
  3. Nanmayaal vaaykavan thrupthiye thannu
    Navyamaakkunnu nin yauvanaminnu-
  4. Makkalil kaarunyam thaathanannonam
    Bhaktharil vaatsalya vaanavan noonam-
  5. Pullinu thullyamee jeevitham vayalin
    Poovenna polithu pokunnithulavil-
  6. Than niyamangale kaathidunnorkkum
    Thannude daasarkkum thaan daya kaakkum-
  7. Nithya raajaavivanorkkukil sarvva
    Srushttikalum sthuthikkunna yehova. K.V.S
