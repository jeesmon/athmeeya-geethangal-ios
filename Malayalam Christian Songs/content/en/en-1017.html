    Thamburaante thanka mukha shobha kaanum naam
    Enthoru mohanam aa divya darshanam
    Chinthackkatheethamaam kolmayir kondidum
    Thanka meni thannil bahu paadukalum kandidum
    Panka makattuvaanetta khora denddanam-
  2. Divya vilikettu vanna shudhanmaare kaanum naam
    Divya bhoovil thejassaayitt ennumennume
    Thamburumeettiyum thappukal kottiyum
    Anpu pozhikkunna gaanangal paadiyum
    Nithyamaayi nruthamavar cheythukondu muthidum
    Nithya rakshithaavinnude paada padmangal-
  3. Rakshackkaayi kaamkshikkunnavare vilichidunnu
    Rakshakanaa meshuv-allaathaarum-allathe
    Santhoshamodathu kelkkumo sodaraa
    Santhatham mevidaam thaathante sannidhau
    Sathya neethi paathayavan nithya rakshithaavum thaan
    Nithya raksha ninakkinnu saadhyamaakume. K.V.J
