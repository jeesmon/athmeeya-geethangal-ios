1. Kristhumoolam daivaraajyam lokathil prathyakshamaay
    Vishwasikkathakka vaakyam ithu sarvva marthyarkkaay
    • Daivaraajyam samaadhaanam
      Santhoshamathu neethiyum shudhiyum
      Puthran moolam saujanyadaanam
      Thaazhmayull ellaavarkkum
  2. Saathaan raajan sevainkal illayoru laabhavum
    Aathma nashtam ihathinkal pinne nithya shaapavum
  3. Aadaam moolam vanna saapam puthranmoolam theernnellaam
    sarppam lokam jadam paapam ivayellaam jayikkaam
  4. Paapashakthi azhinjidum puthran raktha shakthiyaal
    Ullamellaam niranjidum daivaathmaavin snehathaal
  5. Raajyakkaarin gurunaadhan athu daivaathmaavu thaan
    Yeshuvile jeevapaatha evanum kandarivaan
  6. Sathyaprajakalellaarum rajaakeeya kulamaam
    Daivasannidhiyil vaazhum ivarkke paurohithyam
  7. Thurannirikkunnu swarggam thaathanodum adukkaam
    Daivadoothanmaarin varggam sevackkaay orukkamaam
  8. Nithyajeevan ihathinkal dehikk anubhavamaam
    Deham karthen varavinkal praapikkum roopaantharam
  9. Daivame! nin swarggaraajyam vannathaale vandanam
    Ninte janathin saubhaagyam paranju theeraathathaam. V.N
