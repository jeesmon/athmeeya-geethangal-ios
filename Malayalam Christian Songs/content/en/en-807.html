• Ninnekkaal snehippaan ennude aayussil
    Onnum undaakalle karthaave!
    Vandippaan vaazhthuvaan paaduvaan khoshippaan
    Ennum ninne mathi karthaave!
  1. Enneyumorthu nee khinnanaay kaalvary-
    Kkunnile krooshilen karthaave!
    Mannile mohangal onnumen kanninu
    Munniluyaralle karthaave!
  2. Ennazhal neenguvaan nin-kazhal-aashrayam
    Nin-nizhal sheethalam karthaave!
    Ennirul neengidum nin-mukha shobhayil
    Ninnarul saanthwanam karthaave!
  3. Ennu nee vannidum ninnudal kaanuvaan
    Ennini saadikkum karthaave!
    Vannidum njaan vegam ennura cheythapol
    Vannidane yeshu-karthaave! T.K.S
