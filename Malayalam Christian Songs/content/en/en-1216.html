    thilakkaming udikkunnu
    Velichamaam masheehayee-

  4. Thirumozhi divasavum
    puthiyathaay patdikkukil
    Sharivare vazhithettaa
    thirunnidaa masamshayam
  5. Dinamathin thudassathil
    manuvelin mukhaambhujam
    Dharishicha naranoru
    durithavum varikilla
  6. Manamathi thelivino
    dirunnidum samaadhaanam
    Divasathinnoduvolam
    bharichidum manassine
  7. Samasthamaam pareekshayum
    jaichidaam krupa moolam
    Orikkalum ilakaatha
    puramathil kadannidaam
  8. Gathiyillaa janangalil
    kaniyunna maheshane!

    vazhikalil nadathuka. K.V.S
