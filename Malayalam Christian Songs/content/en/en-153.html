• Tune: En maname dinam vaazhthuka


    En maname sthuthi paadiduka
    En praana rakshakanaam- shreeyeshuvinu
  1. Swargeeya dootharin nal sthuthigeethavum
    Swantha pithaavinte laalanayokkayum
    Anthamillaathulla than mahimaavathum
    Hantha! vedinjiha vannu pirannu
    Enthoru thaazmayennorthadhikam-
  2. Sakalathin laakkum kaaranabhoothanum
    Sakala prapachathinn-ekaavakaashiyum
    Daivathejassinte poorna prabhaavavum
    Daivika thathwathinnekamaam mudrayum
    Sakalavum vachanathaal vahippavanum-
  3. Thannude sannidhou vannavarkkevarkkum
    Thakka prathiphalam nalkaathirunnilla
    Dukhitharkk-aashwaasam vishannorkk-aahaaram
    Kurudarkku kaazhchayum moodarkku jnjaanavum
    Kodukkuvaanavanottum madichathilla-
  4. Naamellaam nammude swantha vazhikalil
    Naanaavidha sukhabhogangal thedi
    Aamayam neekkuvaan kaalvari thannil
    Aa marakkurishil paathakar naduvil
    Maamaranam ninakkaay sahichu. E.K.G
