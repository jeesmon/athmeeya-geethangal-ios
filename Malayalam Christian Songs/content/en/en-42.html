• Sthothrameshuve sthothrameshuve -ninne
    Maathram nandiyodeyennum
    vaazhthipaadum njaan
  1. Daasanamente naashamakettaan- nara-
    Veshamaayavatharicha daiva jaathane-
  2. Paapathinude shaapashikshayaam- daiva
    Kopatheeyil ventherinja jeeva naadhane-
  3. Shathruvaamenne nin puthranaakkuvaan-ennil
    Chertha nin krupackkanantha sthothrameshuve -
  4. Aarthikal theertha karunaa samudrame!- ninne
    Sthothram cheyvaanenneyennum paathramaakkuka-
  5. Jeevanaadhane devanandana-ninte
    Jeevenennil thannathinnaay sthothrameshuve
  6. Naasha lokathil daasana menne- salprakaashamaay
    nadathidenam eshu naadhane. P.V.T
