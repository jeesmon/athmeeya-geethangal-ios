• Paadum ninakkum nithyavum-parameshaa! paadum..
    Kedakattunna mama needaarnna naayaka!-
  1. Paadum njaan jeevanulla naalennum naavinaal
    Vadaathe ninne vaazhthume parameshaa!-
  2. Paadavamulla sthuthi paatdakanennapol
    Thedum njaan nalla vaakkukal parameshaa!-
  3. Pookunnu vaadiyoru poovalli thoomazhayaal
    Orkkunnu ninte paalanam parameshaa!-
  4. Gandham parathidunna pushppengalennude
    Anthikam rammyamaakkunnu parameshaa!-
  5. Shudharil vyaaparikkum swargeeya vaayuvaal
    Shudhamee vyoma mandtalam parameshaa!-
  6. Kashtathilum katdina nashtathilum thudare
    Thushtippeduthiyenne nee parameshaa!-
  7. Snehakkodiyenikku meethe virichu priyan
    Njaanum sukhena vaazhunnu parameshaa!-
  8. Aayavan thanna phalam aake bhujichu mama
    Jeevan samurdhiyaakunnu parameshaa!-
  9. Daivaprabhaavamente munnil thilangidunnu
    Chollaavathalla bhaagyamen parameshaa!-
  10. Ennullamaakum mahaa daivaalayathil ninnu
    Pongum ninakku vandanam parameshaa! K.V.S
