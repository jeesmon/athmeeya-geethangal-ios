• Aarumilla neey-ozhike chaaruvan-oraal
    paarilen priyaa
    • Neeri neeri khedangal moolam eriyunna maanasam
      Ninthiru maaril chaarumbozhallaath-
      Ashwasikkumo aashwasikkumo?
  1. Eliyavar thanmakkalkkee lokamethum
    Anukoolamallallo naadhaa!
    Valiyavanaam nee anukoolamaanen
    Balavum mahimayum nee thaan-
  2. Priyarennu karuthunna sahajerennaalum
    Priya lesham-illaatheyaakum
    Priyane nin sneham kurayaathe ennil
    Niyatham thudarunnu mannil
  3. Girikalil kankalu-yarthi njaanothum
    ‘Evide-yaanente sahaayam?’
    Varumen sahaayam ulakam-aakaasham-
    Ivayulavaakkiya ninnaal-
  4. Maruvil than priyanodu chaari varum sabhayaam
    Tharuneemani bhaagayavathi thanne
    Marubhoomi vaasam tharumorum klesham
    Ariyunneyillaval lesham- T.K.S
