• Tune: Paaduka paaduka
  1. Swarggeeya raajaavin paithangale!
    Yaathrayil paaduvin modamode
    Than kriyayin mahimaa vezhunna
    Rakshakannu sthuthi paadiduvin!
  2. Abraamya puthrare! santhoshippin!
    Kristhuvil naamellaam onnaayithaa!
    Maanusharin jadam thaan dharichu
    Aatmaakkale swayam veendeduthu
  3. Deepthiyin makkale! kannuyarthin
    Seeyon nagaram aduthuvallo
    Nammude nithyamaam veedathathre
    Rakshakane-kkaanum naamavide
  4. Rakshithaavinte chuvadu nokki
    Swargga yaathra cheyyunn-ezhakal naam
    Bheekaramaaya pareekshanathil
    Yeshuvin kaaladi sookshikkuvin
  5. Ha! bhayam venda sahodarare!
    Modamaay yeshuvil chernnu nilppin
    Puthranaam daivamothu-nnivannam
    Munnottu pokuvin nirbhayaraay
  6. Naadhane! sarvvavum thalli ninne
    Santhosha poorvvam anugamikkaam
    Vaazhthappettor-abhishikthane! nee
    Njangalkku naayakanaayirikka. K.V.S
