1. Maanavarkku raksha nalkaan swarggalokam -vitta
    Vaanavane! yeshunaadhaa! namaskaaram
  2. Deenaril kanivu ninneppole yaarkkum-kaanmaa
    Naavathalle moovulaku thediyaalum
  3. Sathya paathe yenthannariyaathe ninte-pala
    Puthrarayyo! marubhuviluzhalunnu
  4. Nithyakaikal kondavare thaangi daiva-loka
    Methuvolam nadathuka nasrinaadhaa!
  5. Andhakaaram chuzhannoree bhoovanathil -ninte
    Bendhuramozhiyaaloli veeshumello
  6. Ksheenaraakum adiyangal shaktharaavaan-swargga
    Bhojanamarulka deva dinam thorum
  7. Ninthirumukhathin kaanthi theliyikka-njangal
    Kkantharangam kulirkkuvaan thakkavannam
  8. Swaadezhunna paalinodu padayelkkum-thava
    Geerukal kelppathinnaasha valarunnu
  9. Sradhayodu ninmozhikal kettu njangal ninnil
    Sakthiyuktha raakuvathin arulenam
  10. Ullilettamanalthatti jadashaithyam neengaan
    Vallabhane! chorika nin aathmikaagni. K.V.S
