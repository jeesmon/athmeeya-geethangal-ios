• Nallidayaneshu thani-kkullajangal kkaayi vanna
    Snehame snehame! -
    nallidayan aadukalkkaay Jeevaneyum nalkidunna- snehame snehame
  1. Alayunnoraadukalkkaay nilavittu thaanirangi
    Pala mattu maaliyanna snehame!
    Vilayerum thanka ninam choriyaanum thaan kaninja- thaaraalum varnnyamaaka snehame snehame!
  2. Oru naalum kaividukill athinaaleeaadukalil
    Bhayamilla thante mahaa snehame!
    Kanivolum than karathaal thaalolichee maruvil
    Chelodu pottidum than snehame snehame!
  3. Koorirul thaazhvarayil koode nadannidaya-
    Nnaadalakattidum than snehame!
    Aavashya velakalil aaharam nalkiyava-
    Ckkaashwasam ekidum than snehame snehame!
  4. Shathru bhayamakattum santhaapam theere maattum
    Santhosham ullilettum snehame!
    Sayaahnam aayidumbo-laalayam thannilekke
    Aahwaanam cheythidum than snehame snehame! T.K.S
