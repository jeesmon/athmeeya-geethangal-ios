• Karunayin saagarame!
    Ennennum paadi pukazhthum njaan
    kaalvariyin sneham!
  1. Neechanaam enneyum thedi
    vankrooshu chumanno nee
    En paapthin phalamaam maranam sahich-
    enneyum veendedutho?
  2. Mulmudi choodi neeyen shaapam vahichitho
    Aa paavana paanikal
    aanikalil krooshil tharachitho!
  3. Paapathin shikshakal neekki
    enikkaashrayam nalkidaan
    Nin paavanahrudayam pilarnnuvenno!
    paavana snehathaal
  4. Maranathin shakthiye thakarthu
    sathaane jaichu nee
    Ee adimayaamenne viduvichathaal
    ennum nin adima njaan. P.T
