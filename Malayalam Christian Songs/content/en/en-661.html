• Ente naavil navagaanam
    Ente naadan tharunnallo
    • Aamodaalennume avane njaan paadume
      Uyirulla naal vareyum halleluyya-
  1. Enne thedi mannil vannu swantha jeevan thannavan
    Onninaalumezhayenne kaividaathavan-
  2. Paapachettilaandirunna enne veendeduthallo
    Paapamellaam pokkiyenne shudhi cheythallo
  3. Illa bheethiyennilinnumethra modamullathil
    Nalla naadaneshuvinte paatha vannathaal-
  4. Halleluyya stothra geetham paadi vaazhthumeshuve
    Ellaakkaalam nandiyode ente naalellaam. C.J
