• Varuvin! ee nalla samayam-
    vrudaavaakkaathe
    Ninne yeshu vilichidunnu
  1. Bhoothalavaasam kannuneeril
    bhaarathode nee kazhikkukayo?
    Vannudan paadam thedidukil
    vinnathil ninne cherthidume
  2. Ulakam maaya azhakum samam
    athu nambaathe mayakkum ninne
    Maranam orunaal vannidume
    marakkaathe nin rakshakane
  3. Vaanathin keezhe bhoomi meethe
    vaanavaneshu naamamenye
    Rakshippaan maarggam vereyilla
    rakshakaneshu thaan vazhiye-
  4. Sathya vachanam kettu nee vaa!
    nithya jeevan ninakkekidume
    Ninperum jeeva pusthakathil
    unmayaay innu ezhuthidume. K.V.J
