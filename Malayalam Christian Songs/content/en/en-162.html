• Yeshuvin naamam en yeshuvin naamam
    En jeevithathilekayaashrayame
    Njaanennum stuthikkum njaanennum vaazhthu
    Yeshuvin naamam enikkethrayaanandam
  1. Paapiyaayirunnenne rakshippaanaay
    Yeshu krooshileri thante jeevanarppichu
    Yeshu ethra nallavan yeshu ethra vallabhan
    Pathinaayirathil athishrestanavan-
  2. Nallidayanaaya yeshu naadan
    Nirantharamaayenne vazhi nadathum
    Avanenne shasikkum avenenne rakshikkum
    Kodikkeezhilenne nithyam nadathumavan-
  3. Samaadaanmillaathe njaan alanju
    Yeshu samaadaanamaayente arikil vannu
    Avanenne anachu avanenne thaangi than
    Bhujabalathaalenne nadathumavan-
