• Ente perkkaay jeevan vedinja ninte swantham njaanini
    Antharange-maam vaazhuka neeye
    Santhathameshu naayakaa!
  1. Mama kodum paathaka shikshakaletta
    Thiruvudal krooshil kanden njaan
    Hrudi valarunne priyam ninnil mathiyini paapajeevitham
  2. Swantha ninamathaal en mahaa paapa
    Vankadam theertha naadhane!
    Enthekidum nin krupackkaayi-tten jeevitham pulpoleyaam!
  3. Krupayezhumenge viliyekettu
    varunnithaa njaanum naayakaa!
    Arulicheythaalum anusarichidaam adima ninakkennaalume
  4. Kadalil meethe nadannavane!
    lokakkadalin
    meethe nadathenamenne
    Kadalil thaazhum pethreneyuyarthiya karamathilenneyum ettane
  5. Alanjuzhalum shishuvakaathe njaan
    alakalin meethe odidum
    Balavumenikken jeevanun neeye
    mathiyavalambam naayakaa! P.A.J
