• What a wonderful Saviour
  1. Kristheshuvinte naamame athi chithramaam naamam
    Aaakaashathin keezhengume athi chithramaam naamam
    • Athi chithramaam naamam en yeshuvin naamam
      Athi chithramaam naamam en rakshakante
  2. Thanshudha raktha thalippu athi chithramaam naamam
    Varuthi daiva yojippu athi chithramaam naamam
  3. Ha! paapa vaazhcha kazhinju athi chithramaam naamam
    Pishaachin bendham azhinju athi chithramaam naamam
  4. Ashudhi paartha hrudayam athi chithramaam naamam
    Vishudhaatmaavin paarppidam athi chithramaam naamam
  5. En daivathinnu jeevippaan athi chithramaam naamam
    Njaan swathantran njaan bhaagyavaan
    athichithramaam naamam
  6. Pareekshakan sameepichaal athi chithramaam naamam
    En jayam krooshin rakthathaal athi chithramaam naamam
  7. Ennekkum enne kaakkuvaan athichithramaam naamam
    Kaiyetten praana naayakan athi chithramaam naamam
  8. Ha yeshuve! nin swantham njaan athichithramaam naamam
    Ennekkum ente swantham thaan athichithramaam naamam. V.N
