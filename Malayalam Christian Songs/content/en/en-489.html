• Yeshunaayakan samaadhaana daayakan
    Ninakkennum dhaname
    Enthinaakulam kalarunnen maname?
    Nin sahaayakanavan shaktha-naakayaal ninakku nirbhayame
    Loka porithil anudinam jayame
  1. Ninte nikshepam avanennu karuthaamenkil
    sakshemamavaniyil amaraam
    Ithra shreshtanaam oruvan nin koottinaa yarikilund athinaal
    Enthinaakulam kalarunnen maname?
  2. Neeyinnadhikam dhaniyaakaan nidhiyaam
    Theeyil oothikkazhicha ponmathiyaam
    Bhoovil kristhuvullavaneppo-lithra maa dhanikanillarika
    Enthinaakulam kalarunnen maname?
  3. Lokadhanam saukhya marggamaay karuthi
    Pokum nararkkulla vinackkilloraruthi
    Ennaal kristhuvil samaadhaanam
    Nithyamaam sukhadhaanam arulum
    Enthinaakulam kalarunnen maname? T.K.S
