• Jagal guru naadhaa! yeshumahesha!
    Jaya jaya thava namaskaaram
  1. Aadiyil vachanam aayirunnavan nee
    Aadiyanaadiyum neeye
  2. Aadamanedanil-aadiyil cheytha
    Paathakam theertha maheshaa!
  3. Paapanivaarana kaaranan neeye
    Paalayamaam parameshaa!
  4. Vazhiyum sathyavum jeevanum neeye
    Vazhiyil thanalum thunayum nee
  5. Pazhaya puthiya niyamangal randum
    Niranju nilppavan neeye
  6. Thaavaka naamam paapikku sharanam
    paavana devakumaaraa!
  7. Jaya jaya janathathi vanangum ninpaadam
    Jaya jagadakhilam nin naamam. M.E.C
