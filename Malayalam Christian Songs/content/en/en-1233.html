• Tune: Vandanam yeshupraa
  1. Poya raavil muzhuvan
    karunayodenne sookshicha naadaa
    Nandiyode thiru snehathinaayi
    njaan vandanam cheythidunnu
  2. Andhakaaram niranju
    palapala thinmakal vaanirunna
    Raavil thava dootharin
    kaaval thannu enne sookshichathinaal
  3. Mannithil marthyaganam
    palavidha kastanastam sahikkum
    Neramenikku thann
    sukham samaadaanamiva ninachu-
  4. Dukhangal vannidilum
    thava daasan rogiyaay theernnidilum
    Dukham sahicha naadaa!
    Thirukrupa thannu paalikkaname-
  5. Innu pakal muzhuvan
    thiruhithamen modamaayirippaan
    Swargeeyamanna thannu
    adiyanu shakthi pakarnnidane-
  6. Innente kriyakalum
    maathramalla vaakmanobhaavangalum
    Shudhamaay sookshikkuvaan
    shudhaatmaavaal enne nayikkaname
  7. Paapaandhakaarathinaal
    niranjathaam paarithil jyothissupol
    Paaram prakaashameki paarthiduvaan praapthanaay theerthidane. P.J.A
