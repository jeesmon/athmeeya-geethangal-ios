• Praanesha! Yeshunaadhaa!
    Neeyenikku mathiyennekkum
  1. Thava-mukhathin saundaryamen
    kannukal kandidum thorum
    Bhoovanamathil-aagrahippaa-
    nonnumillenn-ariyunnu njaan
  2. Anpu-thingum nin swaramen
    karnnangalil pathiyum thorum
    Imbamathil valarunneni-
    kkimbamalla annya-shabdam
  3. Van-krupakal moolam-ente
    sankadangal theerthidunna
    Ninkalallaath-aasha bhoovi-
    yinkal-illayee-enikku
  4. Shakthiyeridunna nin
    saannidhyam-athilulla vaasam
    Thrupthiyum-aanandavumen
    hruthinekidunnu paaram
  5. Ninnilulla premam-ennil-
    ninnakattidunnathinnin-
    Onninum kazhinjuvennu
    vannidunnathalla noonam. E.I.J
