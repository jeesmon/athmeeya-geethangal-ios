• Tune: Ihathile durithangal
  1. Nadakkanam priyare naam parishudhaatmaavine
    Anusarichanudinam samarppanamaay
    Jadatheyanusarichu nadakkukilorikkal naam
    Kanatha shikshaavidiyilakappedume
    Aatmaavine anusarikkukil praapikkaam vijayam dinavum-
  2. Vilikku yogyamaay nammal vilichonaam vishudhante
    Vishudhikkanusarichu nadannidenam
    Ashudhiyumethuvida durnnadappumorikkalum
    Porukkukillavanathi vishudhanathre-
  3. Jadathinte pravruthikal marippikkanam naam
    Jagathinte mohangal thyajichidenam
    Jayichidukil nammal priyanothu nadannidum
    Labhichidum prathiphalam namukkorun naal-
  4. Nanmayum poornnavumaya daivahithamaraangidaam
    Velichathilullavaraay nadannidaam naam
    Sakala salgunangalum aneethiyum sathyavumallo
    Velichathin phalamennatharinjidanam-
  5. Dharikkanam daivathinte sarvvayudhavarggam nammal
    Periya shathruvinodu poruthiduvaan
    Samayam durllabhamennatharinjavan
    Vruthareyum chathikkuvaan sramikkumennarinjidanam-
  6. Pimbilullathu marannum mumbilullathine nokki
    Parama viruthinaayittodidenam naam
    Vishwaasathin naayakanaameshuvin karathil ninnum
    Labhyamaakum neethiyin kireedamorunaal. C.D.C
