1. Ethra nalla mithram-enikkeshu-bhaara-
    Mathrayum vahippathinnu praapthan-enne-
    Ethrayum snehichu shaapa mruthyuven perkkaay
    Sahicha mithram- aatma mithram!
  2. Ethrayo santhosham than thruppaadam-thathra
    Athrayum theerunnu matsanthaapam-ullil
    Ethrayum kauthukam nalkidunna than thiru vachanam
    Othaal -athichithram!
  3. Bhaktharude yoga-menikkimbam- athu
    Shakthiyerum daivathin kudumbam-athil
    Kathru meshayod-anayum neramullil ninnozhinju
    Pokum-sarvva thunpam
  4. Sankadathaal ksheenichu njarangi-dumbol
    Than karangal moolamenne thaangi-thante
    Van krupakal-oronnenne kaanikkum neramullil
    Santhosham thingidunnu. E.I.J
