• Kaalvary krooshil kaanunna roopame!
    Kanmashakkoorirul neekkum pon deepame!
    Enthukanivo! swantha ninavum
    Chinthi mahaamruthi yenthiyathaami-
  1. Ennude sakhiyaayi maruvidaa-
    Nanyaraal nindithanaayi theerenamo?
    Aarum thunayillaathe valaya-
    Thagathiyaam enne karuthuka thanne-
  2. Nanma cheythu nadanna paadangalil
    Van murivekidunn aanikal munayaal
    En nadappu nannaakuvathinnaa-
    Yonnumilleyanya maarggamithenye!-
  3. Aanikal nijapaani yugangale
    Shonitha poorithamaakki kanunnitha
    En kriyakalin van kalushatha
    Neekkukayaanithu nokkiya hethu-
  4. Mulmudiyathu moolam murinjithaa!
    Than mukhathoodey-ozhukunnu thanka ninam
    En ninavile thinmayakhilam
    Shirassilettiduvaan manassalinjone-
  5. Maa marakkurisshaayi balipeetdom
    Vaanavan eeshwaranaayi yaagamrugam
    Marathil thoonguvon shapickappettavan
    Likhithamitheevidhamaayi nirvvaadam. T.K.S
