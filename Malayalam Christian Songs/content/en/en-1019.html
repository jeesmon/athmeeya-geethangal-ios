1. Kunjaattin thiru rakthathaal njaan shudhanaay theernnu
    Than chankile shudha rakthathaal njaan jayam paadidum
    Mahathwam rakshakaa sthuthi ninakkennum
    Chettil ninnenne nee veendeduthathinaal
    Sthuthikkum ninne njaan aayussin naalellaam
    Nandiyod adivanangum
  2. Aarppodu ninne khoshikkum ee seeyon yaathrayil
    Mumbottu thanne odunnu en viruthinaayi
    Labhikkum nischayam en viruthenikke
    Shathrukkal aarume kondupokayilla
    Praapikkum annu njaan raajan kayyilninnu
    Doothanmaarude mandhyathil
  3. En bhaagya kaalam orkkumbol ennullam thullunnu
    Ee loka sukham thalli njaan aa bhaagyam kandappol
    Nithyamaam raajyathil annu njaan paadidum
    Raajan mukham kandu ennum njaan khoshikkum
    Rakthathin phalamaay vaazhume swarggathil
    Kodi kodi yugangalaayi
  4. Manoharamaam seeyonil njaan vegam chernnidum
    En kleshamaake neengippom avide ethumbol
    Nithyamaam santhosham praapikkum annu njaan
    En shathru vinnathe eduppaan paadilla
    Aanandam koodidum saanandam paadidum
    Shreeyeshu raajan mumbaake. M.K.V
