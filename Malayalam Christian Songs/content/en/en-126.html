• Sthothram paadidum njaan
    ennaayussil naalellaam
    Nin daya orthennennum
    ninne sthuthichidum njaan
  1. Paapathinn adheenathayil kidanna
    Dushkkarmiyaamenne viduvichu
    Khoravairiyin shirassine thakarthenikkaay
    Keerthanam paaduvaan krupa thannu-
  2. Nin krupakal ethra mohaname
    Nin kriyakal ethra agaadhame
    Ennamillaatha swargeeya daanangalaal
    Ezhaye anugrahichu kaathathinaal-
  3. Santhoshathodennum vannidume
    Sangeethathode nin sannidhiyil
    Dainamdinam nadathunna vankrupackkaay
    Nandiyodennum njaan sthuthichidume. S.V
