• Kristheshu naadhante paadangal pinthudarum
    Naamenthu bhaagyamullor! priyare naamenthu bhaagyamullor!
    Naadhante kaalchuvadu naalthorum pinthudaraan
    Maathrukayaayi thaan nalla maathrukayaayi thaan
  1. Paapathin shiksha neekki bhaavi prathyaasha nalki
    Bhaarangal naalthorum sarvva bhaarangal naalthorum
    Thanmel vahichukondu chemme nadathidunna-
    Thaananda-maanandam para-maanandam aanandam
  2. Bhudhi paranju thannum shakthi pakarnnu thannum
    Mumbil nadakkunnu avan mumbil nadakkunnu
    Than naadam kettukondu pinpe gamichidunna-
    Thaanandam-aanandam para-manandam aanandam
  3. Kristhu enikku jeevan mruthyu enikku laabha-
    Mathreyennaanallo thannil prathyaasha vechullo-
    Rethum niraasha koodaathothunnath aakayaale-
    Nthaanandam-aanandam para-maanandam aanandam
  4. Lokathil aashrayichum bhogathilaasha vechum
    Pokunnavarellaam innu pokunnavarellaam
    Vekunna theekkadali laakunna naramathilaanandam-
    aanandam nammal-kkaanandam aanandam. T.K.S
