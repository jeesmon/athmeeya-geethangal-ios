• Paapa lokam thediyippaaril vannu devan thaan
    Paralokam paapikalkkekiduvaan
    Suralokamahima sakalavum vedinju
    Kurisholam thaanavan thaan-
  1. Ulakathinuyirekaan unnathan meni
    Thakarunnu kaalvary maamalayil
    Choriyunnu kurishinmel chenchoradaara
    Manuvel mashiha maanavarkkaayi
    Akhilaandam chamachon paapikku badalaay
    Baliyaayi swamanassaal-
  2. Irulunnu ulakaake pilarunnu paara
    Uyiraarnnu bhaktharin mruthadeham
    Keerunnu maraneekkaan thirasheela neele
    Kurishil mashiha uyir vitta neram
    Paavana rudhiram paapikalkkaruli
    Paraloka maarggamathre. G.P
