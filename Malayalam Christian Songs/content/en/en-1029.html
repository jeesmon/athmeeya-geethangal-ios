• Karthaavu vaanil vannidaaraayi
    Prathiphalam namukku thannidaanaayi
  1. Kashtathayonnumilla pattini thellumilla
    Dushtajanamaarum avide varikilla
  2. Innihe vannidunna khinnatha bhinnatha
    Onnumavidilla onnallo naamellaam
  3. Vaazhum naam mannidathil annaalil mannavar naam
    Thaazhumariganam aa nalbharanathil-
  4. Puthuvudal dhariche prathiphalam praapiche
    Puthushaalem puriyathil kaanum namellaarum-
  5. Kaalangalereyilla naalukal neelukilla
    Kaanthanavan varum orungidaam priyare. C.J
