1. Aashrayam yeshuvilennaal maname nina-
    Kkaashwaasamaayidum aayussellaam
    Aashrayichidunn-avarkk-anudina-abhayamaa-
    Navaganichidukayill avanavare-
  2. Manushyanil-aashryichaal-anisham niraashayillaa-
    Thoru sukham manassin undaayidumo?
    Yeshuvil aashrayichaal ethu vishaadamee bhu-
    Vaasathil vannaalum niraashayilla--
  3. Avane nee ruchikkuka sharanamaay karuthuka
    Dinavum nin chumadukal-avanmel veckka
    Avanude chuvadukal pathinjidam nokki ninte
    Chuvadukal pathichu nee nadannu kolka--
  4. Marichu manmarayunna manujante mahimayil
    Mayangumo mahiyithil mathi-ullavar?
    Marichuyirth-eshuvinte mahima nee kandu kolka
    Maduthu pokill-avanod-aduthu kolka--
  5. Avanude valippavum mahathwavum-innanekar
    Ariyunnillenkilum thaan varumoru naal
    Aadarichavaruman-aadarichavaruma-
    Rennathu velippedumaa dinathil. T.K.S
