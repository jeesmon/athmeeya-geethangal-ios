• Let us with a gladsome mind
  1. Daivamaam yehovaye! jeevanuravaayone!
    • Ninakkente vandanam!-Ninakkennum mahathwam
  2. Aadyam anthavum illa- bhaagyavanaay vaazhunna
  3. Nithyam shreshta doothanmaar-sthothram cheyyum naadhan aar ?
  4. Sarvva srushtikkum sadaa- savvavumaam vallabha!
  5. Puthran krooshin rakthathaal- shudham cheyyunnathinaal
  6. Ninne snehippathinaay- ennil thanna krupackkaay
  7. Thaanidunna hrudaye- vaanidunnen raajaave!
  8. Anyer seva vyarthdame- dhanyer ninte bhakthare
  9. Ninneppole daivam aar?- onnumillill anyanmaar
  10. En prakaashamaayone!- than santhosham thannone!
  11. Thanthru naama rahasyam- thaatha puthran aathmaavaam. V.N
