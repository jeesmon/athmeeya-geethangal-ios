• Snehanidhiye jeevadhuniye
    Nee maathramaanen gathiye-naadhaa
  1. Bheethiyaam koorirul paathayilum
    Vedanathingum velayilum
    Aadhi kalaraath aashrayippaan
    Naadhaa! neeyallaathaarumilla
  2. Dukham niranjathaam paanapaathram
    Bhruthyanaamen kayyil thannidukil
    Stothram cheythu njaanettu vaangi
    Keerthanam paadumen jeeva naadhaa-
  3. Theeyil nee shodhana cheythennaalum
    Thellum paribhavamilla naadhaa
    Ponnilum thejassenikku nalkaan
    Ennil kaninja nin snehamallo-
  4. Mannilen yaathra thikachu ninte
    Sannidhi thannil njaan vannidumbol
    Ennude dukhavum kannuneerum
    Minnum kireedangalaayi maarum. G.P
