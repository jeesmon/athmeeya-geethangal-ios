• Njaanente karthaavin swantham swarakthathaal
    Thaanenne vaangiyathaal
    Daya thonniyennethan makanaakki theerthoru
    Sneham marakkaavatho?
  1. Krupayaale daivam kristhuvilenne
    kandu yugangalkkumunne
    Anne baliyaakaan daiva kunjaadine karuthiyenikkaay avan-
  2. Thanmakkalethan kanmani pole yunmayil kaakkunnathaale
    Kalangaatheulakil njaan kulungaathe dairyamaay
    Anudinam vaazhunnu haa!-
  3. Karthaavennethan koodaaramaravil kaathidum kashtatha varikil
    Chuttumethirkkunna shathrukkal mumbil njaan
    Muttum jayam nedidum
  4. Onneyennaasha than sannidhaanam chernnennum aananda gaanam
    Paadippukazhthithan mandirathil dyaanam
    Cheythennum paarthidanam. M.E.C
