• Devadevannu mangalam mahonnathanaam
    Devadevannu mangalam
  1. Devadootharaakaashe divya geethangal paadi
    Kevalaanandathodu mevi sthuthi cheyyunna
  2. Sakala lokangalile sarvva ganangaleyum
    Sukhamudane padachu sakala naalum paalikkum-
  3. Naraganangalinnathi duritha mozhippathinnaay
    Thirumakane naranaay dharaniyinkalayacha
  4. Paapabodam varuthi paapiye shudhamaakkaan
    Paavanaatmaave nalkum jeeva jalaashayamaam-
  5. Aadaravodu thante vedavelivumanu-
    Jaathikalkkaruliya aadinaadhanaakunna. J.J
