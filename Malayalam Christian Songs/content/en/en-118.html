• Vandanam vandanam vandanam naadhaa
    Vandanam yeshuparaa
    Vandithanaam vallabhanaam yeshuparaa-
  1. Aadiyugangalkku munname ninnil
    Thiranjeduthenno naadhaayenne
    Nirupama sneham thava thirusneham
    Anudinavum pukazhthidum
    njaan-Sthuthichidum njaan--
  2. Swargga mahimakal vittu neeyenne
    Neechajagathil thediyo? naadhaa!
    Nikarillaa sneham thava thirusneham
    Anudinavum pukazhthidum
    njaan-Sthuthichidum njaan-
  3. Raktham chorinjenne veendeduppaanaay-
    Mruthyu varicho? Kurishilen naadhaa!
    Alavillaa sneham thava thirusneham
    Anudinavum pukazhthidum
    njaan-Sthuthichidum njaan--
  4. Ithra mahaadaya thonnuvathinnay -
    Enthullu nanmayee saadhuvilorthaal
    Athirillaa sneham thava thirusneham
    Anudinavum pukazhthidum
    njaan-Sthuthichidum njaan. G.P
