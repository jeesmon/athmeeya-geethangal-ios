• Haa! varika yeshunaadhaa! njangal aavalodirikkunnithaa
    Nee varaaykil njangalkkoru jeevanille nikhileshaa
    Chaavilaanda manushyarum jeevikalaam ninnadukkal haa!
  1. Mruthyumakkal sthuthikkilla-ninne-chathavar pukazhthukilla
    Idharayil mankattakal-thinnu-thrupthiyadayunnaayavar
    Uthamane! nin charithramithiri dyaanichidumbol
    Ethrayum aanandamullil prathyaham vardhichidunnu
  2. Ninte vishudhaavipandu-jala-thin mukalil nilayaandu
    Aa mahathaam sthithi kondu-mrutha jeevikalunarvu poondu
    Jeevaraashi theruthereyaa valiya pralayathil
    Kevalam peruki vishwamaakave punarbhavichu
  3. Swantha vellikkaahalam-nee-yoothi mandathayakattiduka
    Yaahwayude pakshathullo-rathu-saadaram prathidhwanikkum
    Yisrayelil nadakkunna vigrahathinnarchanaye
    Vidravippichiduvaanaay sathwaramorungumavar
  4. Vaanaloka jeevamanna-njangal-maanamaay bhakshichidatte
    Thenozhukum ninmozhikal-njanga-laadaraal shravichidatte
    Daiva vaakkaam vaaleduthu daivaraajya prasidhikkaay
    Vaibhavamaay pporutheettu kaivarette vijayashree. K.V.S
