• Nokkil-aarkkum mathivaraa mandiram
    Bhaagya naadhante divyamaam aalayam
    Poka namavidennu chonnala-venthu modamudichu me!-
  1. Saaleme! njangal kaalukal ninnude
    Vaathilinn akameyithaa sthithi cheythidunnathi chithramaay
    Thammil cherthinakki-ppanithulloru
    Harmmyame thava bhangi vaazhthuvath-
    Ethoruthanu saadhyamaam?-
  2. Iisraaye linnu saakshyathinaay thathra
    Sthothraseva nadathuvaan akhilesha gothra samuchayam
    Ethunnavide daaveedu raajante
    Shasthamerina dharmmapeetda mirunnidunnu vidhikkitha!
  3. Aashams-kkevam shaalemin shaanthiye
    Swairamaay nivasikka ninnodu kurezhunnavar evarum
    Ninnara-mana kothalam enniva
    Nirnnayam sukha shaanthikondu niranjirikkana mennume-
  4. En sahodaranmaarum sakhaakkalum
    Ninnilaanu vasippathennoru kaaranathe ninachu njaan
    Ninnil shaanthiundaakennu khoshikkum
    Mannavaalaya mullathaal thava nanma njaan karuthidume
  5. Vandanam pithaavinnu nirantharam
    Vandanam naravargga rakshakan eshuvinnumathe vidham
    Vandanam paripaavana rupikkum vandanam-
    Yuga kaalamaay thudarette maanavar evarum. K.V.S
