• What a friend we have in Jesus
  1. Ethra nalla mithram yeshu khedabhaaram vahippaan!
    Ethra swaanthryam namukku sarvvam bodhippikkuvaan!
    Nashtamaakki samaadhaanam, bhaaram chumennethra naam
    Yeshuvodu parayaayka-moolamathre sarvvavum
  2. Shodhanakal numukkundo klesham-ethilenkilum
    Leshavum niraasha vendaa yeshuvodu parayaam
    Kashtatheyil panku kollum shreshta mithram yeshuvaam
    Namme muttumariyunna-thanneyariyikka naam
  3. Bhaaram moolam njerungunno? ksheenam vardhikkunnuvo
    Yeshu allayo sanketham-thanmel sarvvam vechidaam
    Snehithanmaar hasikkunno? yeshuvodu paraka
    Thante ullam-kaiyyil namme paalichaashwasippikkum. E.I.J
