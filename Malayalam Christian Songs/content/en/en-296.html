• Snehathin deepamaam yeshuvennil
    Vannath-enthabhuthame!
    athyagaadham athullyamaane
    Assaadhyam varnnichidaan
  1. Keerthikkum njaanennum sodararodothe
    than thirunaametheyum
    Orthu sthuthichidum aarthiyaalezhaye
    Cherthathan snehathinnaay-
  2. Paapiyaamennude paathakam pokkuvaan
    paapam aakkappettone
    Paarithil paarkkilum paadi sthuthichidum
    Bhaarangal orthidaathe-
  3. Pollayaan oozhiyil ullathellaameni- kkillathilaasha thellum
    Vallabhan thaanennullil undaakayaalilla
    niraasha thellum--
  4. Mekhathil vannidum naadhanin
    chaarathu vegam njaanethidume
    Neerumen maanasam maari njaan modamaay
    Vaazhum than sannidhiyil. S.K
