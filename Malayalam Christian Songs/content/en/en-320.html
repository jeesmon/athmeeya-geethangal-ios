• Ethrayo shreshtanaayan kristhu thaan ennurachu paaduvin
    Nithya naasham neekki bhaagyam
    Marthyan ekuvaan kazhinja shakthanaamavan
  1. Shreshtaraam manujarokke manmaranjupom
    Mruthyuve jaichuyirthatheshu maathramaam
    Nithyaraajan nisthulaabhan
    Kristhu naadhan ethrayethra shreshtanaam mahaan!
  2. Ekanaay athishayangal cheythidunnavan
    Evanum vanangidenam thante paadathil
    Ethunaavum ettuchollum
    Yeshukristhu karthanennu daivamahimackkaay
  3. Thullyamaay illa mattu naamamonnume
    Thellume yalaasaraayidaathe thanjanam
    Nalla naamam kristhu naamam
    Chollanam thanvallabhathwam bhoovilariyatte. T.K.S
