• Pass me not, O! gentle Saviour
  1. Pokalle kadannenne nee priya yeshuve!
    Mattullore darshikkumbol nokkukenneyum
    • Yeshunaadhaa! ennapeksha kel
      Mattullore darshikkumbol nokkukenneyum
  2. Nin krupaasanathin mumbil veenu kenjchunne
    En vishwaasam ksheenikkumbol nee sahaayikka
  3. Ninte punyam maathram ente nithya sharanam
    Ninte krupayaale maathram ennudhaaranam-
  4. Jeevanekkaal ere nannu neeyen karthaave
    Bhoomi swarggam thannilum nee maathram aashrayam.
