• Divyaraajaa ninne vaazthum - ninte
    Bhavyamaam naamam njaanennum pukazhthum
    Naalthorum njaan thirunaamethe vaazthi
    Naadhaa thudarnnini ninne sthuthikkum
  1. Yaave neeyo mahaan thanne-athaal
    Everumennekkum vaazhthidum ninne
    Devaa nin kaikalil shreshta karmmangal
    Kevelam chollume kaalangal thorum-
  2. Nin prathaapathin mahathwam-thingum
    Van bahumaanathe yoonniyurackkum
    Unmayaay ninnatbhuthangalodengum
    Pongum nin shakthiyum thejassumothum-
  3. Nin nanmayinnormmayengum- kaatti
    Nin neethiyekkurichennum njaan paadum
    Nin kriyakal thanne ninne sthuthikkum
    Nin shudhimaanmaar thaan ninne pukazhthum-
  4. Mannaa! nin raajyamennekkum-nilkkum
    Ninnaadhikaaramo-ennumirikkum
    Kannukalokkeyum nokkunnu ninne
    Nalkunnavacku theen thalsamaye nee
  5. Sathyamaay nokki vilikkum-narar
    Kethrayum chaarave neeyirikkunnu
    Bhaktharinnichaye saadhichavarin
    Praarthana kettu nee raksha cheytheedum-
  6. Paalikkum nee snehippore-ennaal
    Moolachedham cheyyum doshavaanmaare
    Chelodu njaan sthuthi cheythidumellaa
    Kkaalavum jeevikal vaazthum nin naamam. K.V.S
