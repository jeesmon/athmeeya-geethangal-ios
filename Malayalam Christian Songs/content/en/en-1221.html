1. Innum raavile vannu njaan thiru sannidhi thannil naayaka
    Ennum nee thanneyenne kaaval cheyyunna van paripaalakan!
  2. Poya raathriyil njaan samaadhaana-thoduranguvaan nin krupa
    Naayakaa! nee chorinjathaal sthuthi-gaanangal paadidunnithaa!
  3. Raavakannoli veeshi bhoothalam shobhitham aayidunnithaa!
    Maamaka andhatha maaruvaan thava kaanthi vannathorkkunnithaa
  4. Innalem innumennum neeyenikk anyanallathu moolamaay
    Munnilaay ninne kaanunnethrayo dhanyamaay mama jeevitham!
  5. Vannidum pular kaalamonnini-yennu kaathirikkunnu njaan
    Mannidam thava ponmukham moolam minnidum neethisuryane! T.K.S
