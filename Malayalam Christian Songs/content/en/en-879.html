    Ente karthaavum en daivavume
    Ninnil aashrayikkunnu njaan innum ennum
  1. Theeyin naduvilum vellathilum
    Ente jeevitha naalukal aayidilum
    Venthupokaatheyum thaanu pokaatheyum
    Thaangi niruthidum nin karangal-
  2. Rogathilum athivedanayil nin
    Saannidhyam enikkenthoraashwaasame
    Rogikale thotta nin karangal enne
    Thaalolichaanandam nalkeedatte-
  3. Santhaapathil athi santhosham nalkum nin
    Saanthwana shabdam njaan kettidatte
    Nin roopam kaanuvaan nin swaram kelkkuvaan
    Nin vachanam enikkaanandame-
  4. Innaleyum innum ennum ananyanaam
    Ninnil njaan aayathaal ethra danyan
    Ee loka jeevithakaalam kazhiyumbol
    Ninnodu cherum njaan nithyathayil. P.T
