• Parmasuthanaam mashiha bhuvane manujaa! -ninne
    Thiravaan kanivaay vannu-
    karuthenamithu manassil
  1. Thava paapabhaaram neekkaan manohara
    Roopan thaan-yaagamaay
    Thiru maranamathaal labhyam-vimochanam evarkkum
    Vishwaasamathil vechaal- ithu thaan suvisheshamaho!-
  2. Thiru vedamathothidum vilayerumee vaartha-sodaraa!
    Karuthaatheyini poyaal-vannidum theeraatha
    Shikshaavidhi ennorkka- oru maattavumillaathe. T.M.D
