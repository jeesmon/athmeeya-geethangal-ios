1. Yeshuve ninte roopamee
    yente kannukalkkethra saundaryam
    Shishyanakunna enneyum ninne
    ppoleyaakkanam muzhuvan
  2. Snehamaam ninne kandavan
    pinne snehikkaathe jeevikkumo?
    Dahippikkanam enne ashesham
    sneham nalkanam enprabho!
  3. Deenakkaareyum heenanmaareyum
    aashwasippikkaan vannone
    Aanandathodu njaan ninneppole
    kaarunyam cheyvaan nalkuke
  4. Daasaneppole sevaye cheytha
    daivathin ekajaathane
    Vaasam cheyyenam ee nin vinayam
    ente ullilum naadhane
  5. Paapikalude vipareethaththe
    Ellaam sahichcha kunjnjaate
    Kopippaanalla kshamippaan nalla
    Sakthi enikkum nalkuke
  6. Thante pithaavin hithameppozhum
    Modamodudan cheythone
    Ente ishdavum daivaishdaththinn
    Anuroopamaakkename
  7. Thiruvezhuthu shaishavam thottu
    snehichaaraanja yeshuve
    Guru nee thanna vachanam nanne
    grahippikka nin shishyane
  8. Raathri muzhuvan praarthichunarnna
    bhakthiyulloru yeshuve
    Praarthippaanaayum unaraanaayum
    shakthi tharenam ennume
  9. Lokasthaanangal saathaan maanangal
    verukkum daiva veerane
    Ekamaam manam thannitten dhanam
    daivam than ennorppikkuke
  10. Kaushalangngalum upaayangngalum
    Pakakkum sathyaraajaave!
    Shishuvinnulla paramaarththhatha
    Ennilum nithyam kaakkuke
  11. Ihalokathin chinthakal ottum
    Illaathaashritha valsala!
    Mahal shakthiyaam nin daivaashrayam
    Kondennullam urappikka
  12. Manushyarilum doothanmaarilum
    Athisundaranaayone!
    Anudinam nin divyasaundaryam
    Ennaamodamaakkename. V.N
