    Marachidum-avenenne chirakadiyil
    Marannidaath-ividenne karuthiduva
    Maaraathe-avanente arikilunde
  1. Anudinavum anugamippaan
    avan Nalla maathruka yaakunn-enikke
    Aananda jeevitha vazhiyilinne
    Aungarahamaay-enne nadathidunnu-
  2. Pala vidhamaam ethirukalen
    paathayil-Adikkadi uyarnnidumbol
    Paalikkum parichode paramenenne
    Patharaathe nilkkuvaan balam tharunnu-
  3. Vilicha daivam vishwasthanall
    Vazhiyil valanju njaan-alayaan
    Varikaiyillavan-enne pirikayilla
    Valathu kai pidichenne nadathidunnu-
  4. Ithaa vegam njaan vaana-viravil
    Iniyum varumennu aruli-cheytha
    Ee nalla naadane kaanuvaanaay
    Iravum pakalum-enni vasichidunnu. C.J
