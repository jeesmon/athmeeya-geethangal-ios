• Kankaluyarthunnu njan- ente
    Sankethamaakunna seeyon giriyilen
  1. Aarude kaikaleeyaakashabhoomikalaakruthi
    cheythavan thaan sahaayamen-
  2. Thettukayillayen paadangal paathayil
    Thellum urangukayill ente naayakan
  3. Paarilen paalakan israayel naayakan
    Paarkkilavanenn arikiloru thanal-
  4. Raappakal doshangal eshaatheyee vidham
    Aapathakann-enthoraananda jeevitham!
  5. Ente gamana agamanangal sarvvavumennumavan
    kaathu nannaay nadathidum. M.E.C
