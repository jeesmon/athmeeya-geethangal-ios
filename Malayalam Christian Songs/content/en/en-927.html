• Nanmayin bhaavamaay naam dinavum
    Thinmaye cheykayallo chemme kulippathinu
    Poyudan chaale than meyyil cherananinju
    Varunnathupole-
  1. Kayyilirippathu daivapramaanam
    Kaaryamaay kothippathu lokathinmaanam
    Shayyayil kidakkave deva samaanam
    Sangathiyodaduthaal bhedamanyunam-
  2. Laukikarodulla verpaadanukoolam
    Lokabendhamozhiyumbol prathikoolam
    Sakalavumorthu kandaalaakave jaalam
    Saukaryam poleyethum cheyyumikkaalam-
  3. Parane stuthippathupol naavinaal paadum
    Pala vishayangalilum manassulanjodum
    Parichodu yogangalil deham poykkoodum
    Purame manogathangal thirinjalanjidum-
  4. Janaprasidhikkuvendi koduppathuhithamaam
    Janamariyaatha daanam thudarukil mithamaam
    Parasya japaalayathil paayvathu druthamaam
    Paramannarikil thanichirippathu bhayamaam-
  5. Parajanangalodokke divyamaay parayum
    Pathivaay cheyvathu kandaalavarkkaandhiyam nirayum
    Parayumbol keerthi moham vaakkinaal kurayum
    Tharamothu varumbolaayavayellaam marayum-
  6. Vachanam pramaanamennu parayumeppozhum
    Bhajanam cheyvatho marthyavidhikalkkuchoozhum
    Ashanamashippathinum praarthanayezhum
    Vyasanakaramaayavackkivayellaam thaazhum-
  7. Manassinnashudhi neekkaanilloru moham
    Maranajadam vedippaakaanjathishokam
    Minusamullaabharanthodathiraagam-
    Manamellaam nirayunnu paapamaam rogam
  8. Bahumathanaayirikkumethoru dhaniyum
    Sukhamallaathirunneedilevanum kaniyum
    Anuvivashatha kaattumennu vendiniyum
    Panamalppam labhichidilethinum thuniyum-
  9. Prathimaa poojakanmaare naam sadaa pazhikkum
    Prathimayaam naanayathin munnil naam namikkum
    Chathiyanaam yoodaavine sarvvadaa shapikkum
    Gathi tharum naadane naam cheruthinum thyajikkum-
  10. Parane mathathilaakkaan shramamottu valare
    Viravodu nammude durnnadappithinnethire
    Kuravillennurachu naamirunnidunnuyare
    Maravillaa parannithin vidhiyo thannadhare- K.V.S
