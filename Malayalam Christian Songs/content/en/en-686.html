• En naadhane nee maathrame
    Ennaathmaavin sangeethame-en naadhane
  1. Neerthodu thedi odunna maanpol
    Thedunnu njaan nin sannidhaanam
    En praananaadhaa! ninnodu cheruvaan
    En praanan vaanjchichu paarthidunnu-
  2. Nin thiruvaasam mohanam thanne
    Nin snehamethrayo maadhuryamaam
    Nin yaagavediyen vishrama sthaanamaam
    Nin paada sevayenn aanandamaam-
  3. Innelem innum ennum ananyanaam
    Ninnilee saadhu njaan dhanyanallo
    Ennodu koode nee ninnodu koode njaan
    Ennum piriyaatha jeevithamaam. G.P
