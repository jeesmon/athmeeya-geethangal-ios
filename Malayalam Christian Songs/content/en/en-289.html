• Yeshuve snehavaaridhe!
    Sthothrame-sthothramennum ennume
    Vaanalokam vittu neeyi
    Paaril ezhayaamenikku vendi vannitho
  1. Thaathanodothu nithyamaayi mevi nee
    Aadiyil vaanam bhoomiyaadiyaayava
    Srushti cheytha naalkku mumbe
    Kandu nin pramodam annumingee marthyaril
  2. Vaanava vrundamokke paadi vaazhthidum
    Vinnavar naadhan paapiye ninackuvaan
    Ethumilla kaaryamennaal
    Thannu ninte praananente veendeduppinaay-
  3. Ambare kaanum aayirangalaayidum
    Golangal srushti cheytha naadhane! neeyi-
    Keedathulyar marthyareyangorthu
    ninte makkalaakkiyenthor athbhutham!
  4. Thaanu nee krooshilolam maheshane!
    Thejassil aakkidaan aneka puthrare
    Daivaputhran enkilum nee Kashtamettu
    poorthiyaakki thaathanishtathe. T.M.D
