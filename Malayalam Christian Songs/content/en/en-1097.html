• Jaikkume! suvishesham lokam jaikkume!
    Peyude shakthikal nashikkume-
    Sakala lokarum yeshuvin naamathil vanangume,
    Thala kunikkume - athu bahu-santhoshame
  1. Kooduvin! - sabhakale! vannu paaduvin-yeshuvin keerthi-
    Kondaaduvin suvishesham chollaan
    Oduvin, nidra vittunarnniduvin, manam-
    Othellaarum ninniduvin vegam-
  2. Idikkanam, peyin kotta naam idikkanam- jaathibhedangal
    Mudikkanam, snehathin kodi pidikkanam
    Yeshu raajante suvisheshakkodi
    Khoshathoduyarthidenam vegam-
  3. Marichu thaan, namukku mokshathe varuthi thaan
    Velicha maargathil iruthi thaan- eliya koottare
    Uyarthi thaan, yeshu devante rakshayin kodi
    Khoshathoduyarthidenam nammal-
  4. Chelavidin sukham balatheyum chelavidin
    Budhi jnjaanatheyum chelavidin- vasthu sambathukal
    Chelavidin- yeshuraajante mahimayin kodi
    Evarum uyarthidenam nammal-
  5. Yogyame, ithu namukkathi bhaagyame-rakshithaavinte
    Vaakyame, swarggalokarodaikyame
    Yeshu raajya prasidhikkaayi naam
    Evarum prayatnichidanam. M.V
