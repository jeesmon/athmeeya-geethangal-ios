• Paapee! unarnnu kolka nee nidrayil ninnu
    Paapee! unarnnu kolka nee
  1. Valiya naasham vannidum-kalippaan samayamilla
    Chelavu cheyyaruthe nee-vilayillaathathinnaayi-
  2. Kadalin irachal pole idimuzhakkom poleyum
    Vidhi naalin bhayankaram aduthaduthu varunnu-
  3. Ninte vazhikaleyum anthar bhaagangaleyum
    Thante thulaasil daivam santhatham thookkidunnu
  4. Sathyamaarggathilekkum nithya rakshayilekkum
    Marthyanaakunna ninne karthan vilichidunnu-
  5. Narakaagniyin jwaalackku irayaay pokaathirippaan
    Paranodu yojikkuka karuna kandethuvaanaay
  6. Urakkam thungaruthini thurakka vegam nin kankal
    Veruthe kalayaruthe churukkamaam rakshaakaalam. V.N
