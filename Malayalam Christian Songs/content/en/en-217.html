• Vaazhthuvin shreeyeshukristhuvine
  1. Daivathin puthranavan-
    vedathin saaramavan
    Vaanavar raappakal veenu
    vanangunno-runnatha devanavan
    Halleluyya paadi vaazhthiduvin-
  2. Paapathin bhaarathaal naam-
    paaram valanja neram
    Ghora kurishathil chora
    chorinjavan veendeduthallo namme
    Halleluyya paadi vaazhthiduvin-
  3. Ithra nal rakshakane-
    yethray-aaraadhichaalum
    Bhaktharinn aagraham theerukillalppavu
    methrayo Nisthulan thaan
    Halleluyya paadi vaazhthiduvin-
  4. Karthaadhi karthaavu thaan-raajaadhiraajaavu thaan
    Vinnilum mannilum sarvvaadhikaaravum
    Praapicha naayakan thaan
    Halleluyya paadi vaazhthiduvin-
  5. Kristhuvin thruppaadathil-
    bhakthiyodeppozhume
    Sthothravum sthuthiyum maanam
    mahathwavum Arppichu kumbiduvin
    Halleluyya paadi vaazhthiduvin. G.P
