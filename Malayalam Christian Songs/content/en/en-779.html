• Kannuneeril kaividaatha karthaavunde
    Ulluruki karayumbol thaan koodeyunde
    Amma thante kunjine marannidilum
    Njaan marakkaa ennuracha karthaavunde
  1. Nenchurukum neramavan thancham tharum
    Anchidaathe nenchilenne cherthanackkum
    Chanchalamilleshuvente nallidayan
    Vanchanayo thellumilla thante naavil
  2. Uttavarottikkoduthaal khedamilla
    Uttu snehikkunna naadan koodeyunde
    Maattamilla thante sneham nisthulyame
    Mattu sneham maarippokum marthya sneham
  3. Alpanaalee bhoomiyilen jeevithathil
    Alpamillaa shodhanakal neridukil
    Alpavum thalarukilla bheethiyilla
    Chilpurushan maattumellaam nanmackkaayi
  4. Karthru naamathil sahikkum kastathakal
    Karthanu prasaadamullathennarinje
    Stothra geetham paadi nithyam paarthidum njaan
    Karthru paada seva cheythee paaridathil. A.V
