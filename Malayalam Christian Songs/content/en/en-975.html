• Ennu vannu kaanumenneshuvine
    Ponnu thirumeniyaam vallabhane
  1. Vinnilaanente pithrubhavanam mannilullatho vittupiriyum
    Ulakithennarinju uyarunnen manassil
    Mama priyan varavine kaathiduvaan-
  2. Swargga geham pookiduvaan vembalerunnen hrudayam
    Thankamukha prabhayil anthamilla yugathil
    Mama priyan koode njaan vaaniduvaan-
  3. Yeshu raajan vanniduvaan bhakthar vaanil paranniduvaan
    Kaalamereyillallo kanneerellaam thuvaraan
    Aananda gaanangal paadiduvaan. G.P
