• Nee karuthukayaal oru vidhavum parithaapamilla para!
    Nin thunayallo thanalallo neeleyenikkabhayam
  1. Manam kaninjenikkaay-ninam chinthukayaay
    Nee kaalvarikkurishil-enikkoru viduthal
    Labhichu nin karuthal-kandaashwasippaanaay
    Krupayaruliyathe marannidumo?
    Maaraatha mahal sneham- nin thuna...
  2. Dhanam kuranjirikkum-manam thalarnnirikkum
    Aa neramen nere-thava mizhi thirikkum
    Thirumozhiyurackkum-santhaapavum theere-
    Mama karumanayinn arikanayaa-
    Naaromal snehithanaam- nin thuna...
  3. Amithamaay karuthu-llamalan nee varuthu-
    Nnen kaalukalkkurappe-mahimayil niruthu-
    Nnathu vare nadathum-veezhaathe nee kaathu,
    Nal vazhi nirathum athil nadathum
    En bheethiyakattum- nin thuna....T.K.S
