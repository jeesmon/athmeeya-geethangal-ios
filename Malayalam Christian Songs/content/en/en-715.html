• Shasahwathamaaya veedenikkunde
    Swargga naadathilunde
    karthaav-orukkunnude
  1. Paapama-nnaattililla oru shaapavum-avideyilla
    Nithya santhosham shirassil vahikkum
    Bhaktha janangalunde- halleluyya!-
  2. Iravu pakalennilla avid-iruloru leshamilla
    Vitharidum velicham neethiyin sooryan
    Athumathi-aanandamaay- halleluyya!--
  3. Bhinnathe avideyilla kakshi-bhedangal-onnumilla
    Oru pithru sutharaay orumichu vaazhunnanugraha
    bhavanamathaam-halleluyya!-
  4. Vazhakkukal-onnumilla panimudakkukal varikayilla
    Manushyaril daridrar dhanikarennilla
    Eka shareeramavar- halleluyya!-
  5. Kanner avideyilla ini maranamundaakayilla
    Arumyod-eshuvi-nnarikil naam nithyam
    Orumichu vaazhukayaam- halleluyya! M.E.C
