• Paadum paramanu parichodu njaan
    Varumavanu sathatham jayam-
    Odum vaajiye saadiyodu koodave kadaloode
    thalli than thejassode prabalapettaan
  1. Maamakabalam sthuthiyaayavan thaan yehova
    Aayavanenikkulla-rakshayum daivavumaam
    Aakayaalavannoru vaasasthalam chamayeppen
    Ha! pithrudevanavan njaanavane pukazhthi-
  2. Yudha manushyanavan yaahennavante namam
    Sathwaram pharavovin sainyam radangaleyum
    Abdhiyil thalliyittu yudha nethaakkal mungi
    Guptharaay chenkadalil athalodavar thaanu-
  3. Aayavare aazhangal moodi kallupolavar
    Aayamodadiyilottaayavar thaanu yehovaye
    nin valathukai aayodhane balappettaayathu
    shathrukkale maayamenye nurukki-
  4. Ninnethirikele ninnunnatha thejassinaalonnichu
    thallikkalanjunnathane ninnude
    Minni jwalikkum krodham ninnil ninnayachu nee
    Chinnum thaaladiyeppol thinnithavareyathu-
  5. Nin mookkile dwaarangalil shwaasathaal vellangal
    Onnichu koodapettu neerottangal koottampol
    Ninnithaazhangal kadal thannullilurachupoy
    Vandithane! parama mannava! nin krupayaal
  6. Shathru paranju: njaan pinnaale chellum, pidikkum,
    Vidruthamapahrutham bhadram njaan bhaagichidum;
    Thrupthippedumenn aatmaavathraye kkondumuden
    Udharichen vaalaal njaan nigrahikkumavare-
  7. Nin kaattukondoothi nee; sindhuvavare moodi
    Chengadalileeyam pol shankayenye thaanavar
    Ninnathbhutha kriyakal panka heena sthuthikal
    Thankalethrayum maanyan shankaneeyan nisthullyan
  8. Neetti valathukai nee thatti vizhungi bhoomi
    Koottikkondu vannu nin koottathe krupayaa nee
    Vaattamillaa sthalathu kettaal janam pedikkum
    Spashtamaay phelisthyaro thattukedaay dukhikkum
  9. Edom prabhukkalellaam khedamode bhramikkum
    Movaabile balavaan maaraakave virackkum
    Kanaan prajakalellaam saavegamay dravikkum
    Ha! bhayavum njettalum aayavare pidikkum
  10. Nin bhuja vallabhathwa sthambhitharaayaver nin
    Sambaathitha janam kadannupokum vareckkum
    Kambanamillaathulla kallukal pole thanne
    Kumbhinee naadhane! nin mumbilirikkumaver-
  11. Ulpraveshippichu nin salpurathilavare
    Thwalpaarppinaay sthdaapicha shudhasthdalathu thanne
    Kelppulla ninnavakaa shaalpetharaadri thannil
    Nilpaanarulum bhavaan salpaalakanaay vaazhum. K.V.S
