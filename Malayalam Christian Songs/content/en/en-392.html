• Yeshu ente swantham aayathinaal
    Njaanethra bhaagyavaan
  1. Ente thaazhchayilorthavanaam
    Enne thedi vannavanaam
    Swatha jeevanum thannavanaam
    Ente sankadam theerthavanaam
    Enthu thyaagamithaacharyam
    Enthu snehamithavarnnaneeyam-
  2. Bandhumithrangal marannidilum
    Enthu vannini bhavichidilum
    Enne snehichayen priyane
    Ennum pin chellumanchidaathe
    Mannilenikkulla nimishangal
    Thannilaanandam thedidum njaan-
  3. Anthya naal vare karam pidichu
    Enne nadathuvaan shakthanavan
    Kashtanaalukal vannidukil
    Karthan kaathidum chirakadiyil
    Ennum krupa tharumezhalil
    Enikkathu mathi jeevithathil. G.P
