• Anjchaam raajitham undaame param-anjchaam-
    Thunjchidaathe kelkkilinnu
    nenjchilaanandam valarthum-
  1. Aadaaminn ulppathi naalathil evam
    Othinaan eeshanee bhoovin prabhaavam
    Nee vaanadakkukenn aakilo paapam
    Cheythaatma raajyam vinashtamaakkeedinaal-
  2. Simhaprathaapiyaam baabilon raajan
    Saamraajya moorthithan pon shirassaayon
    B.C.arunoottiyaaril swaraajyam
    Sthaapichu thannichhapole nadathinaan-
  3. Velli saamraajyamaam pershybhallukam
    Anjootti muppethiyettil thudassam
    Baabel thakarthirunoottettu kollam
    Vaanithu vallaatha himsra swabhaaviyaam-
  4. Pullippulikkotha bhangi veeryangal
    Thullikkalikkunna greekku sainyangal
    Maasidonil ninnudichezhu kollam
    Nisthula maahaatmya mothathra vaanumel-
  5. Shreemaan alexander bhoopathi veeran
    Seemaathiriktha paraakramasaaran
    Bhooviyogam cheythapothu naalaayi
    Bhaagichu thaamramaam thandraajyamaayavar-
  6. B.C. dashathraye romayin kaisar
    Naashamaakki greekku raajitham romar
    Saamraajyamonnu samsthaapanam cheythaar
    Kaalaayasathinte kaatdinyamullathaay-
  7. Munnoottarupathu naalu kristhaabde
    Randaayi pirinjithu roma saamraajyam
    Appozhe mannaal dushichirumbaake
    Kelppukettu chhinnabhinna raajyamaae roppathannil
  8. Unnathamaaya girikkumel ninnu
    Vannidum kallu paadaanthike chennu
    Choornneekarikkumee raajyangalannu
    Vannakallu vanmalackku thullyamaay niranju vaazhum
  9. Loka raajaakkalin kaalathu daivam
    Bhoovil parathunna nithya raajathwam
    Anyajaathikkaay vidappedaa sathyam Unnathente
    nandanarkku thanneyaana thennumorkkil K.V.S
