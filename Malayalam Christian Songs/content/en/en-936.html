• Paramaanandam-aanandamaay paraneshuve-
    Ppukazhthidaam
    Avanaasanna bhaaviyil haa! bhuviyaagathan aayidum
  1. Namukkaakulmakattidaan-veraarumilleeyulakil
    Manashaanthi sadaa tharuvaan-manuvel mashihaa mathiyaam-
  2. Pithrubhavana mathil namukkaa-yavan veedukal orukkukayaam
    Ee bhuvanamathil athinaal-namukkanyaraay paarthidaam
  3. Deva kaahalanaadam haa!-mandhya vaanil muzhangidum
    Ee manmaya deham haa!-maari vinmayaraakum naam
  4. Dharaneethalam aakeyum haa!-sarvva shaapa vimochithamaam
    Marubhoomiyilum puthuthaay-panineer kusumam viriyum. M.E.C
