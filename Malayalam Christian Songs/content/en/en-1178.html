• Adhwaanikkum snehithare
    Bhaaram chumappavare
  1. Aashwaasam ningalkkingekiduvaanaay
    Rakshakaneshu vilichidunnu
    Aashayodeshuvin chaare varu
    Aashwaasam nalkumavan
  2. Ningal than kleshangalethaakilum
    Paapamo rogamo mattenthaakilum
    Vishwaasathode kadannu varu
    Kleshangal neekkumavan-
  3. Nanma cheyvaan ningalkkaashayundenkilum
    Thinmayathallo cheythidunnu
    Paapamathinnude kaaraname
    paapathe neekkumavan
  4. Vyartda vichaarathil veenulayaathe
    Karthanaameshuvil aasrayippin
    Nithya niraamaya swargga sukham
    nischayam nalkumavan

  5. Ithu suprasaada dinam priyare
    Ithu thanne karunaa samayam
    Kaalvaryil poorthiyaakkiyathaam
    Rakshackkaay vanniduka-
