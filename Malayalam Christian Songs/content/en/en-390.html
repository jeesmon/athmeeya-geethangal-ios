• Shreemanuvel marijaathaathanaam
    Sura thaathanaam- naadanaam
  1. Sithaniramodatha chuvappum
    Chithamodu kalarnnoru purushanithaa
  2. Ayuthanara-rathilumaho!
    Athi gunamezhunnoru pramukhanivan
  3. Karayakanna-kanaka thulyam
    Parasuthanivanude sirassathulam
  4. Avanudaya mudi niramo
    Dhavalamallathu karuppadhikamaho!
  5. Ovukalinnarikeyulla
    Praavukalude samamavante kankal
  6. Paalilaho-snaathamava
    Chelodu parthippikkappettathu thaan-
  7. Kavilukalo-misithadam pol
    Kamalthulyaadarangal pozhikkunnu moor-
  8. Pacha ratnam pathicha ponnin
    Swatcha mothirangal pol karangalaho!
  9. Neela ratnamamizhnna dantham
    Pole thannudaramangathiramanam-
  10. Venkalthoonathu samam kaal
    Thankamaam chuvadinkal urachathupol
  11. Libanthulyam- mukhaswaroopam
    Drumamathu daarukmithinnu samam-
  12. Vadanmatho-maduramaho!
    Athuvumallivana thiramaniyanaam. K.V.S
