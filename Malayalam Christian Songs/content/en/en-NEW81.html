• Tune: ‘amazing grace’
  1. Enthaashcharyam van kurpayaal nikrushtanaam enne
    paapandhakaarathil ninnum veendeduthathorthaal
  2. van bhayam neengi en manam ninne bhayannidaan
    nin krupa ethra modamaay njaan vishwasikkayaal
  3. enthaapatho van keniyo kashtamo vannaalum
    ninkrupa enne nadathum nithyathayolavum
  4. van nanma karthan vaagdatham en jeevanil balam
    en pankum nal parichayum ee jeeva naalellaam
  5. en deham dehi maanjupom ee jeevithaanthyathil
    en swanthamaakum swarggeeya santhosha jeevitham
  6. oraayiram samvalsaram pon soorya prabhapol
    ninne stuthichu paadeelum nithyatha theerilla. S.V
