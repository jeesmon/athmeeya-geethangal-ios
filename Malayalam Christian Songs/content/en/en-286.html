• Fairest of all the earth beside
  1. Unnathanaamen daivame mannithin sthaapanathinum
    Munname enne kanditho mannavaneshu naadhanil
    • Athbhutha snehame ennennum paadum njaan
      Enne veendeduthathaam athbhutha snehame!
  2. Kaalithozhuthil heenanaay kaalvary krooshil ekanaay
    Kaalkaram kaarirumbilaay kaanunnithenthor aascharyam!
  3. Iprapanchathin naayaka! enpraayaschitha yaagamaay
    Nin praanan krooshil nalkiyo ipraaniyenne neduvaan!
  4. Athbhutham athyagaadhame! apprameyam avarnnyame!
    Immahaa snehamennume nithyayugam njaan paadume. P.T
