• Karthaavil eppozhum santhoshikkum njaan
    Kristheeya jeevitha yaathrayathil
    Kanneerin velayo kashtangalereyo
    Varikilumavanil njan santhoshikkum
  1. Aakula chinthakal eriyennullam
    Vyaakulamaay muttum theernnidumbol
    Ekunnu thanmozhi saanthwana menikke
    Aakayalilloru chanjchalavum-
  2. Kaalamellaam than kaikalaalenne
    Kaathiduvaan vazhi nadathiduvaan
    Karuthanaa-mavanennum karuthidunnavanaam
    Karthanil maathramenn aashrayamaam-
  3. Arinjidunnenne yakhilavum daivam
    Avan hitham pole nadathidunnu
    Annannu thaan tharum thumbamo imbamo
    Athumathiyenikke anugrahamaam-
  4. Parishodhanakal pala kleshangal
    Paaricha bhaarangal sahichidumee
    Paarile naalukal theernnidum vegam
    Pirinjangu pom njaan priyannarikil. C.J
