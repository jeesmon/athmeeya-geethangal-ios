• Divya janeshwara! bhavya gunaatmaka!
    Bhaagya mrutha daayaka! thiruppaadam gathi naayaka!
  1. Divya jadam vedinja vyaya loke
    ninnurvviyil vanna paraa! paradeva vihathaashara!
    Poorvvodithaatma nirvyaaja vaakkathra
    Nirvvahippaan vanna sarvveshwaraa! sundara!
    hruthagarvva janatheshwara-
  2. Dushta saathaan adippettethrayum mahaa-
    Kashta manubhavichen-athaalettam parithapichen
    Ishtanaadhaa! thiru vishtamiyannenne
    Kashtathayil ninnu meettatharinj-urachen krupa
    Thottennu nischayichen-
  3. Manna! bhavaann-uyirimmanma-yannu nee
    Thannidu mennaakilo-bahu dhanyanaay theernniduven
    Vinnavar-ennaalu mannyaa-drusha sneha-
    Nandi kalarnneesha! ninne sthuthikkum vidham
    Njangalennekkum vaazhthum drundam. K.V.S
