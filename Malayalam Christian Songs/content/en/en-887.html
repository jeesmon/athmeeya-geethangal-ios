• Tune: Omanathinkal kidaavo


    Nallaaril sundaree! ninte priya-nenthu visheshathayullu?
  1. Ente priyan chuvappodu-nalla venma kalarnnoru veeran
  2. Aayiram pathaale nokke-athil enneshu mukhyanaayunde
  3. Ponninte kattaye nokke-athil enneshuvin thalayunde
  4. Akkarin-kaakkaye nokke-athil enneshuvin mudiyunde
  5. Praakkalin kannukal nokke-athil enneshuvin kankalunde
  6. Nanmana-ppunthadam nokke-athil enneshuvin kavilunde
  7. Thaamara-ppoovine nokke-athil enneshuvinte chundunde
  8. Pacha-pathicha pon nokke-athil enneshuvin kaikalunde
  9. Neela-kkal danthathe nokke-athil enneshuvin vayarunde
  10. Thankathin venkalthoon nokke-athil enneshuvin thudayunde
  11. Devadaara maram nokke-athil enneshuvin gaathramunde
  12. Sarvvaanga sundaran thanne-enne veendeduthoru kumaaran
  13. Shaalemile mankanmaare!-ivar ente priyathamen noonam. K.V.S
