• Para paramehsaa varamaruleesha
    Neey-athreyen rakshaa sthaanam
  1. Ninne kkaanum janangalukku pinne dukham-onnumilla
  2. Ninte ella nadathippum ente bhaagya niravallo
  3. Aadiyinkal kaippaakilum anthyamo madhuramathre
  4. Karmeghathin-ullilee njaan
    minnum soorya-shobha kaanum
  5. Sandhyayinkal vilaapavum santhosham-ushassinkalum
  6. Ninnodonnichulla vaasam ente kanneer thudachidum
  7. Ninte mukha shoba moolam
    ente dukham theernnu pokum. K.V.S
