• Yordhaan nadee theeram kaviyumbol maname
    Olangal kandu nee kalangendaa thellume
  1. Vellam perukiyaalullam patharenda
    Vallabhaneshu nin arikilundallo
  2. Nallor vishwaasathil swarlloka paathayil
    Kallola medukalothungi ninnidum-
  3. Anpulla rakshakan munbil nadakkave
    Thumba varikilennum thunayavanallo-
  4. Bheethe vendottume munpottu poka nee
    Ethu vishamavum yeshu theerthidum-
  5. Paal thenozhukidum paavana naattil naam
    Paarthidum aanandageetham paadidum. M.E.C
