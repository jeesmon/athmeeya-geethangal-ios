    Enthumen jeevitha paathayil vannaalum
    Eppozhum santhoshikkum
  1. Maanasa dukhangal maaridum
    Maanuvel thanmaaril chaarum neram
    Maaraathe nithyavum koode varum
    Mithramenneshu maathram-
  2. Ennaalum njaanavan swanthamaam
    Enthoru saubhaagya bandhamaam
    Mruthyuvo jeevano saadhyamalla
    Ee bandham verppirippaan-
  3. Snehakkodiyente meetheyum
    Shaashwath bhoojangal keezheyum
    Swargeeya dootha ganangal chuttum
    Santhatham undenikke-
  4. Kanneerin naalukal theernnidum
    Karthaavin veettil njaan chernnidum
    Kaalangalenni njaanoro dinam
    Kaathidunnente manam. C.J
