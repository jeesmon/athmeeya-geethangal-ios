• Kandaalum kaalvaryil
    Kurishil shirassathum chaanju paran
    Kandeeduka priyane! Ninakkaay
    Thoongidunnu moonnaanikalaal
  1. Shirassil mulmudy aninjavanaay
    Hrudaym nindayaal thakarnavanaay
    Vedanayaalettam valanjavaneey
    Than jeevane vediyunnu swayam ninakkaay-
  2. Lokastaapanam muthal arukkappetta
    Kalankamilla daiva kunjaadinithaa
    Lokathin paapangal chumannukonde
    Vaaninum bhoovinum mandye thoongidunnu-
  3. Samrudhiyaay jeeva jalam tharuvaan
    Paaneeya yaagamaay theernnu vanne
    Kaipu neer daahathinekeedave
    Ninakkaay avanaayathum paanam cheythu-
  4. Paathakarkkaay kshama yaachichavan
    Paathaka lokam vedinjidumbol
    Nivruthiyaay sakalamennothiyaho
    Swantha praanan pithaavineyelppikkunnu-
  5. Thanthirumeni thakarnnathinaal
    Thanka ninam chinthiyathinaal
    Nin vilayallo nalkiyavan
    Ninne swargeeya samboornaakkiduvaan-
