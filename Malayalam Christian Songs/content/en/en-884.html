• Shaalomiye! varikente priye!
    Chelezhum swarloka sundariye!
    Maalozhikkum ninte premarasam vazhi-
    Njaalapippikkunnen chundukale mama-
  1. Thaathanaho thanikkullavare lokaril ninnu pirichidunnu
    Aakayaal thannude vaachamanusari-
    Chaayavaril ninnu vegam akannu nee-
  2. Devakalodulla sakhyamichhi-chaanokku vittu than snehithare
    Dyovilekkaayavan pokunnathin munpu
    Daiva-pumaanenna saakshyam labhichithu-
  3. Noha muthalaaya saathwikanmaa-rekamaay ninnu poruthathinaal
    Lokamavarkkingu yogyamaay vannathillaaykilum
    Divya sambath-avarkkundathaal-
  4. Maathaa, pithaa, nilam, bandhu-janam,
    Sodaree, sodarar, bhaarya, makkal
    Aadukal, maadukal-aadiyaam swathodu
    Jeevanum kaivedinjaadal koodaathe nee-
  5. Maalika veedukal, gopurangal, melamezhunna devaalayangal
    Collegukalodu universityu-
    Methume nokkaathe poriken naayike!-
  6. Bankukal, chittikal, companikal, theenku-cheyyum-kali, shodathikal
    Jimneshyamodada naadaka-shaalakal
    Enniva-yorthu nee thaamasichidolla-
  7. Veshavisheshangal-aabharanam jaathikalkkotha duraacharanam
    Dureekarikka nee swodara-puranam
    Saadhichidum kapad-aatmika-dhaaranam-
  8. Kannukal-moham, jadathin-moham, jeevanathinte prathaapamiva
    Onnum pithaavil-ninnallithu lokathil
    Ninnu thanne-yivayellaam-ozhinjupom-
  9. Vaame! lebaanone vittudane kshemamaay porika naattilekke
    Premamulloramaa naamukalum, sheneer
    Hermmon-mudikalum vittu-varika nee-
  10. Loka vailaari theernnidumbol murin malamel njaan vishramippaan
    Simha guhakalum pullippulikalin
    Parvvathavum vittu porika nee shubhe!-
  11. Divyamaayulla manavaraye daivam namukkaay orukkumaye!
    Dyovil labhikkumee vaasasthalathu naam
    Mevum-anavadhi modamod ennume-
  12. Kettiyadachulla thottameyen mudrayittulla jalaashayame
    Vattidaathulla nin prema-vellangalil
    Muttum layichu ramippen sadaapi njaan. K.V.S
