• Yeshunadhaa nin krupackkaay
    Sthothra-mennekkum
    Eeshane nin naamamente kleshamakettum

  1. Naashamayenayorennil jeevanarulaan-van
    Krooshine sahichapamaanam vahichoru-
  2. Paavanamaam neethiyil njaa- nennumirippaan- ninte
    Jeevanilo ramsha menikkekyathinaal-
  3. Nin hrudhayam thannilenne munkurichoru
    Van karunekkinnumiva-nnarhathayille-
  4. Than jada shareera maranam nimitham nee
    Nin pithaavodenne nirappichathu moolam
  5. Ethra kaalam nin krupaye vyarthdamaakki njaan
    athranaalum andhakaaram thannilirunnen-
  6. Jeevalathe aaya ninnil njaan nila nilppaan- ninte
    Jeevarasa mennilennum thannu paalikka-
  7. Vishrama deshathilee njaanethum varekkum-ninte
    Vishrutha krupakalenne pinthuderenam. K.V.S
