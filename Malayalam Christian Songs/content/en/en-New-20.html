• Pranapriya yeshu natha nin maha-
    Sneham marakathenum padum njan
    Jeevan thanu veendedutha nin maha-
    Sneham marakathenum padum njan
  1. Enikayi manushnayi bhovil vano
    Etavum daridranayi thernuvano
    Papam ilathavanayi jeevichuvo
    Daivathin vishudhi theliyichuvo
    Ente papam muzhuvanum vahichavano
    Kadina papiyenapol marichuvano
  2. Enikkayi jeevan thana yeshuvinayi
    Ente jeevitham njan samarpikkune
    Yeshuvin balathale yudham cheythum
    Papathil vezhathe kathukondum
    Nithyatha muzhuvan nilanilkum
    Katakale theyil ninum koythedukum
  3. Enikayi bhavanam thaan orukedunu
    Vegathil vannu enn cherthiduvaan
    Nithyatha muzhuvan en priyan mukham
    Darshichu swargathilum aradhichidum
    Anipadula than padangalil
    Anandabashpangale arpichukondum. M.P
