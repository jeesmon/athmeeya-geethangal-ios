• Kristhu nammude nethaavu veenu kumbidaam
    Mruthyuve venna jethaavu veendum vannidum
  1. Bethlehemil jaathanaay nammi-
    laareyum pole aayathinaale
    Naal thorum nammude bhaaram chumakkum
    Nalla snehithanaam ennumeshu-
  2. Paapam vahichu paadu sahichu
    Krooshil marichu vijayam varichu
    Thaane uyirth saathaane thakarthu
    Vaazhunnunnathathil inn-enneshu-
  3. Mannavan vannaal annavanonnaay
    Kannuneer thornn-aanandamaay nannaay
    Thanmakkal chernnaalasyangal theernnaa-
    Modamaay vaazhum naam ennu-mennum
  4. Ennum sthuthickkaam veenu namikkaam
    Je je jaya kaahalangal muzhakkaam
    Nammude nethaavu nithyam jaikka
    Amen halleluyya-halleluyya. M.E.C
