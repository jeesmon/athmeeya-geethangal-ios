• Tune: Daivamakkale nammal


    Maanya snehithaa, ninte jeevayaathrayil
    Enguchennu cherumennu chinthacheyka nee
  1. Paapa bhaaram peri bhoovil jeevikkunnavar
    Thaapamerum nithyatheeyil veenerinjidum
    Paapamethra ghoramennathorthu snehithaa
    Yeshu naadan chaare vannu raksh nedukaa-
  2. Veethiyerum paathayil charichidunnavar
    Jeevanatta naasha pattanathilethidum
    Modamaarnna swarggamandiram ananjidaan
    Kristhu enna paathayil nee yaathra cheyyanam-
  3. Marthya paapamettu krooshil jeevanarppichu
    Veendeduthu marthyare shree yeshu vallabhan
    Shrestamaamee snehamorthu thaazhmayode nee
    Krooshinodananju paapamettu cholluka-
  4. Yeshu moolam paapamochanam labhichuvo?
    Nithyajeevanettu daivaputhranaayo nee?
    Illayenkil ghoramaam narakamathre nin
    Nithya gehamennathorthu kezhukaa sakhe. K.D.W
