• Ente samrakshakan ente paripaalakan
    Sarvva daathaavum karthan thane
    Ee marubhoo yaathrayil
  1. Aanjadichidunna kodunkaatin shakthiyum
    Aazhiyin olathin muzhakkangalum
    Amaruka enna prapancha naadante
    Aanja kettudanadangeedunnu
    Allal thellumillaathen padakum neengidunnu-
  2. Ihathile durithangaladikam aanankilum
    Isabelin bheeshani erukilum
    Ivide yaathonnum shakthamennude
    Ee daiva snehathe verthirippaan
    Ithil thanneyente dehi aatma uracheedunnu-
  3. Kaahalam dhwanicheedum
    Kaanthan vannidum vegam
    Kodaanukodi doothar mandhyathil
    Kandidum njaanente kaanthante ponmukham
    Karumana theernnidum ennekkumaayi
    Kazhal random ee ezha njaan muthidume. A.N
