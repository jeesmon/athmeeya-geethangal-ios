• Vaazhthidunnu naadhaa
    vaazhthidunnu deva
    Vaazhthidunnu bhoovaanangal
    srushticha daivaathmajaa
  1. Aadi yanaadiyum nee
    alpha omegayum nee
    Aayiram pathinaayirangalil
    sarvaanga sundaran nee
  2. Unnathadevan nee
    vandyamahonnathan nee
    Lokathin paapam chumennozhippaan
    vannoru kunajaadu nee
  3. Swantha ninam krooshil
    chinthi muzhuvan nee
    Nonthu ninpraanan vedinju-
    yirthu vaazhum aachaaryan nee
  4. Ekamandhyasthdan nee
    evarkkum rakshakan nee
    Innale innu mennum maaraatha
    vallabha devan nee
  5. Sthothram halleluyya
    maa mahathwam sthuthiyum
    Saadaram sarvvavum arppichu naadhaa
    paadam kumbidunnu. G.P
