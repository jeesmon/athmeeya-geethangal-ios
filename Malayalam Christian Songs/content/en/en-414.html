1. Edukka en jeevane ninkkaayen yeshuve
    Anthya shwaasatholam thaa-nenjathil halleluyya
  2. Edukka en kaikale cheyvaan sneha velaye
    Kaalukalum odanam nee vilichaal thatkshanam
  3. Edukka en naavine sthuthippaan pithaavine
    Swaram adharangal vaaye-nilkkunnu nin dootharaay
  4. Edukka en karnnangal kelkkuvaan nin marmmangal
    Kanninum prakaasham thaa-ninne kaanman sarvvada
  5. Edukka en budhiye grahippaan nin shudhiye
    Manashakthi kevalam ninakkaayeriyanam
  6. Edukka en hrudayam athu nin simhaasanam
    Njaan alla en raajaave nee athil vaazhaname
  7. Edukka en bhavanam ninakkeppol aavashyam
    Yogathinnum shishyarkkum athu thurannirikkum
  8. Edukka en sambathum ente ponnum velliyum
    Vendaa dhanam bhoomiyil en nikshepam swarggathil
  9. Makkaleyum yeshuve edukkenam ninakke
    Aver ninne snehippaan patdichaal njan bhaagyavaan
  10. Edukka en yeshuve ennethanne priyane!
    Ennennekkum ninakku enne njaan prathishtichu. V.N
