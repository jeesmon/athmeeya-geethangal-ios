• Jesus I my cross have taken
  1. Krooshumeduthini njaanen-yeshuve pinchelkayaam
    Paaril paradeshiyaay njaan-mokshaveettil pokayaam
    • Jeevanenperkkaay vedinja naadhane njaan pinchellum
      Ellaarum kaivittaalum krupayaal njaan pinchellum
  2. Maanam dhanam lokajnjaanam sthaanam sukhamithellaam
    Laabhamallenikkini van-chethamenn arinju njaan
  3. Dushtar enne ppakachaalum kashtamenthu vannaalum
    Nashtamethra nerittaalum ishtamaay njaan pinchellum
  4. Klesham varum neramellaam krooshilen prashamsayaam
    Yeshu koodeyundennaakil thumbamellaam imbamaam
  5. Nithya raksha daanam cheytha divya snehamorkkukil
    Ethu kashtatheyum thaandi anthyatholam poyidaam
  6. Daivathin parishudhaathma vennil vaasam cheykayaal
    Kleshamenthin avanenne bhadramaayi kkaathidum. M.E.C
