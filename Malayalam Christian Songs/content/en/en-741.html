• Uyarthidum njaan ente kankal thunaarulum van giriyil
    En sahaayam vaanam bhoomi akhilam vaazhum yehovayil
  1. Yisraayelin kaavalkkaaran nidra bhaaram thungunnilla
    Yehovayen paalakan thaan illenikku khedamottum
  2. Shathru bhayam neekkiyenne maathra thorum kaathidunnu
    Neethiyin sadpaathakalil nithyavum nadathidunnu
  3. Shobhayerum swarppuriyin theeramathil cherthidunnu
    Shobhitha purathin vaathil en mumbil njaan kandidunnu
  4. Vaana sena gaanam paadi vaanidunnu swargga seeyon
    Dyaanichidum neramente maanasam modichidunnu. K.V.D
