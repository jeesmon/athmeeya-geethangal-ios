• Nimishangal nimishangal jeevitha nimishangal
    Ozhukunnu thirike varaathevannam
    Kanakakkinaavukal manathaaril kandu nee
    Mathimaranneeshane marannidalle
  1. Manassinte maniyara vaathil thurannu
    Karunaaprakaashame vazhithelikku
    Kanivaarnna rakshakan karavallarikal neetti
    Virivaarnna krupayaal anackkum ninne-
  2. Irulinte vazhithaaril snehathin deepam
    Thirukkaikalaal theliyicha devan
    Puthusrushtyaakkidum kalankangal pokkidum
    Kurishinte nizhalil nayikkum ninne
