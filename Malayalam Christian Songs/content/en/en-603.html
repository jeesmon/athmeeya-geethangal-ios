1. Unnathane! vannadiyaare veendedutha naadhaa!
    Ninney-allaathonnu-mengal kkethu-mishtamaakaa
    Ninnuyire thannariye vennu veendeduppaan
    Nee kaninjathorthu njangal keerthanangal paadum
  2. Nin mahimayothu-vathin immaanavarkk-assaadhyam
    Vaanavarkku-mathbhuthamaam nin pravarthanangal
    Nin deyayo sindhu samam-enthatheetham naadhaa!
    Chintha cheyyunthoru-methum-anthamilla-yonnum
  3. Ninnarike vannoruvarum lejjitharaayilla
    Ninkalekku nokkiyor prashobhitharaayallo
    Nin mukhamo sundarame kandu kankulirthum
    Nin vachanam kettu kondum naal kazhikkumengal
  4. Kanmani pol karuthi dinavum kaathidunnu naadhaa!
    Nin jenamaa-mengale nee yethra bhadramaayi
    Nee varume annuvare-ninneyorthu paarkkum
    Pinne ninnodonnu chernnu vaazhu-mengalennum
  5. Halleluyya cholliyadiyaar vaazhthidunnu naadhaa!
    Allalellaam theernnu, thellumilla chanchalam haa!
    Nallavane! vallabhane! ulla-kaalamellaam
    Ninnil-engal-ullassichu halleluyya paadum. T.K.S
