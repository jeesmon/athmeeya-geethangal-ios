• Nee maathramenn-aashrayam- naadhaa
    Nee maathramenn-aashrayam
    Paarithil kleshamaam seeyon yaathrayil
    Nee maathramenn-aashrayam - naadhaa
  1. Neerunna nerathe uttavar polume maarunnu paaridathil
    Maaraathe thallaathe thalleyeppole nee
    Perunnath-enthaascharyam!-devaa-
  2. Vishwaasa yaathrayil enthu bhavichaalum aashrayippaanayum
    Aashwasippippaanum en chaareyundu nee
    Nishwaasam-enthinnini- thaathaa?-
  3. Nandiyaal paadum njaan nanma niranjone!
    Ninmahaa snehamorthu
    Vandithanaayone! ninne sthuthikkuvaan
    Nin krupa nalkidane-ennum. A.K.W
