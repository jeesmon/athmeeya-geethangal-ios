• Daivamethra nallavanaam avanilathre ennabhayam
    Anugrahamaay atbuthamaay anudinavum nadathunnenne
  1. Karuneyezhum than karathil karuthidunnee maruvidathil
    Karumanayil arikilethum tharum krupayil vazhi nadathum
  2. Kaarirulin vazhikalilum karaluruki karayumbozhum
    Koode varum koottinavan kanneerellaam thudackkumavan
  3. Lokam tharum dhanasukhangal-kkekiduvaan kazhinjidaatha
    Aanandamee kristhuvil njaan anubhavikkunninnu mannil
  4. Orukkunnavan puthubhavanam orikkelenne cherthiduvaan
    Varum viravil praanapriyan tharumenikke prathiphalangal. C.J
