• Ithratholam yehova sahaayichu
    Ithratholam daivamenne nadathi
    Onnumillaaymayil ninnenne uyarthi
    Ithratholam yehova sahaayichu
  1. Kannuneerum dukhavum niraashayum
    Poornnamaay maaridum dinam varum
    Annu paadum doother mandhye
    aarthu paadum shudharum
    Ithratholam yehova sahaayichu
  2. Haagaarineppole njaan karanjappol
    Yaakkobineppole njaan alanjappol
    Marubhoomiyil enikku jeevajalam thannenne
    Ithratholam yehova sahaayichu
  3. Ekanaay nindyanaay paradeshiyaay
    Naadum veedum vittu njaan alanjappol
    Swantha naattil cherthukollaam ennurachu naayakan
    Ithratholam yehova sahaayichu
