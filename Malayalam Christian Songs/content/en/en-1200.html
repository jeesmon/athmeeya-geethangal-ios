• Praarthdana kelkkaname!
    karthaaveyen Yaachana nalkename!
  1. Puthrante naamathil chodikkum kaaryangal-
    Kkutharam thannarulaa-mennulloru
    vaagdathampol dayavaay-
  2. Thaathanum maathaavum neeyenikkallaathe
    Bhoothalam thannilille-veraarumen
    Aathankam neekkiduvaan-
  3. Nithyathayil ninnullathyantha snehathaal
    Shathruthaye akatti-enikku nee
    puthrathwam thannathinaal
  4. Bhruthyaranekarin praarthdana kettu nee
    Utharam nalkiyathor-thathyaadaram
    thruppaadam thedidunnen-
  5. Kallante yaachana kettullalinja nin
    Thulyamillaa dayayor-thithaa vannen
    nallavane sabhayam
  6. Yeshuvin moolamen yaachana nalkume-
    Nnaashayil kenjchidunnen-allaathennil
    leshavum nanmayille. T.V.J
