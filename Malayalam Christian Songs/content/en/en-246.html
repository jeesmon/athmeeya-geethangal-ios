• Tune: Nin mukham kaanmaan kothi
  1. Nin paadathil njaan vandikkunne
    Nin mahathwam njaan varnnikkunne
    Athyunnathan nee nisthullyanaam
    Athyaadaram njaan namikkunne
  2. Thaathan savidham vitteebhoovil
    Thaanirangi nee krooshilolam
    Thaazhchayilenne orth ninne
    Thaazmayode njaan namikkunne
  3. Neechanaam enne veendedutha
    Nin maha sneham avarnnyamaam
    Nalkidunnenne njaan ninakkaay
    Nandiyode njaan namikkunne
  4. Rajaadi raajan unnathan nee
    Devaadi devan vandithan nee
    Stothram stuthikal sweekarippaan
    Paathram nee maathram innumennum. C.J
