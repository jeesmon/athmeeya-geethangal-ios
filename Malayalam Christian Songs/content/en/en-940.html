• Thaamasamaamo naadhaa! varaanaay thaamasamaa-mo?
    Thaamasamaamo naadhaa varaanaay aa aa
    Bhoovaasa morthaal ayyo! prayaasam thaamasamaa-mo ?
  1. Vegam varaam njaan veedangorukki vegam varaam njaan
    Vegam varaam njaan veedangorukki O-O-O
    Ennu nee aruli-cheythapol varuvaan thaamasamaa-mo ?
  2. Peedakalaale valayum nin makkal peedakalaale
    Peedakalaale valayum nin makkal O-O-O
    Veedonnu kandu vishraamam varuvaan thaamasamaa-mo?
  3. Paadukaletta paanikalaale paadukaletta
    Paadukaletta paanikalaale O-O-O
    Bhaktharin kannee-ranpil thudappaan thaamasamaa-mo?
  4. Theeraa vishaadam nee vannidaathe theeraa vishaadam
    Theeraa vishaadam nee vannidaathe O-O-O
    Nee raajyabhaaram elkka vaikaathe thaamasamaa-mo? M.E.C
