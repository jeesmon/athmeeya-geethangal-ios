• Eravinnirul nira theeraaraay pakalin kathiroli kaanaaraay
    Puthiyoru yugathin pulari varum neethiyin kathironoli vitharum
    Adhipathi yeshu vannidum athumathiaadhikal theernnidum
  1. Unarin unarin sodarare! urangaanulloru neramitho?
    Uyirthannonaay jeevippaan undo veroru neramini?-
  2. Tharishu nilatheyuzhaanaayi thiruvachanathe vithackaanaay
    Darishanamullavar-ezhunnelppin kurishin ninda vahikkaanaay-
  3. Innu karanju vithackkunnu pinnavaraarppodu koyyunnu
    Innu vithackka madiyanmaar annu karanjaal gathiyenthe?-
  4. Kathitheernnoru kaithiripol poothupozhinjoru pooveppol
    Ethithirike varaathe pom karthavyathin naazhikakal
  5. Sneham nammude adayaalam thyaagam nammude kaimuthalaam
    Ikyam nammude nalla balam vijayam nammude anthyaphalam
  6. heeyil nammude velakale shodhana cheyyum vela varum
    Maram pullu vaickkol eva venthu-poyaal baakki varum enthe?
  7. Inniha nindithar bhaktha ganam annu nadathum bhoobharanam
    Kezhum khinnatha theernnavaraay vaazhum nammal mannavaraay- M.E.C
