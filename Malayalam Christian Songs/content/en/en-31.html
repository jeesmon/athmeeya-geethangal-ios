• Paraloka bhaagyam paapiyennullil
    Pakarunnoru devene-nin
    Pukazhpaadi vaazhthidum njaan-
  1. Oru naalumen naavil thirunaamam cholluvaan
    Anupolum arhathayillaatha paapi njaan
    Thirunaama keerthanam rakshayin puthugaanam
    Urumodam paadunnu santhosha sthuthi gaanam-
  2. Aayussinnaruthiyil akkare naattil njaanaananda
    kannuneer thooki nilkkum neram
    Arikil varum naadhan kanneer thudackum than
    Thirumeyyazhakin noliyil muzhukum njaan-
  3. Krupayude paripaadi theerumbozho manappulari
    vidarumbol nithya yugam thannil
    Azhiyaatha vaadaathoravakaasham karthaavumorumichu
    pankittu vaazhum njaanennaalum. M.E.C
