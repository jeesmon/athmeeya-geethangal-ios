• Aakaashame kelkka-bhoomiye chevi tharika
    Njaan makkale potti valarthi
    avarennodu matsarikkunnu
  1. Kaala thante udayavare-kazhutha thante yajamaanente
    Pulthotti ariyunnallo-en janam ariyunnilla
  2. Akruthya bhaaram chumakkum janam-
    Dushpravruthikkaarude makkal
    Vashalaayi nadakkunnavar-daivamaarenn ariyunnilla
  3. Aakaashathil perinjaarayum-kokkum meevalppakshiyum
    Ava thante kaalamariyum-en janam ariyunnilla. A.K.T
