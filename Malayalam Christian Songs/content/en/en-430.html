• Break Thou the Bread of Life
  1. Daivathin puthranaam kristheshuve
    Jeevante vachanam nalkename
    Aashrithar mandhyathil paarkkunnone
    Daasare sathyathil nadathuke
  2. Pandoranchappavum meen randume
    Kandappol vaazthi vardhippichone
    Ingulla praapthiyum athyalppame
    Ange thrukkaiyyaal ellaam vaazhthuke
  3. Jeevanillaathavar jeevikkuvaan
    Daivathin bhakthar shakthar aayidaan
    Ekuka yeshuve nin vaakkinaal
    Ekuka krupaye nin aathmaavaal
  4. Daiva rahasyangal minniduvaan
    Evanum nandiyode vandippaan
    Moodalum mangalum maattiduke
    Doothukal velicham aakkiduke
  5. Sathyathin swaathanthryam vishudhiyum
    Nithyamaam ieshwaryam subudhiyum
    Salgunam okkeyum nalkiduke
    Sathyathin paalakanaam yeshuve!
  6. Nin sannidhaanathin aashwaasangal
    Nin thirunaamathin sugandhangal
    Vyaapichu veeshatte nin aalaye
    Vaazhuka mahathwathin raajaave! V.N
