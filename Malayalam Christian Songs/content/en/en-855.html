• Ente balamaaya karthanen
    Sharanamathaakayaal paadidum njaanulakil
    Ettamurappulla maravidamaanenikken
    Priyan chaaridum njaanavanil-
    • Haa-halleluyyaa geetham paadidum njaan
      Ente jeevitha yaathrayathil
      Ente allalakhilavum theerthidum naal
      Nokkippaarthidum njaanulakil-
  1. Ellaakkaalathum aasrayam vechiduvaan
    Nalla sanketham yeshuvathre
    Petta thalla than kunjine marannidilum
    Kaanthan maattam bhavikkaathavan-
  2. Thirukarathil van saagarajalamellaam adakkunna
    Karuthezhum yaahavan thaan
    Oru idayaneppolenne avaniyil karuthunna
    Snehamenthaacharyame-
  3. Ullam kalangunn nerathe priyan than vaagnatham
    Orppichunarthumenne ullam karathil varachavan
    Urvvikkadheeshan thaan ennude aashwaasakan-
  4. Maarum manujerallaam mahithalamathu
    Theejwaalackirayaayi maarukilum
    Thiruvaagnathangalkkethum maattam varillavan
    Varavin naalaasannamaay-
