• I am coming to the Cross
  1. Njaan varunnu krooshinkal sadhu ksheenan kurudan
    Sarvvavum enikkechil poornna raksha kaanum njaan
    • Sharanamen karthaave! vaazthappetta kunjaade!
      Thaazhmayaay kumbidunnu rakshikka-yenne-ippol
  2. Vaanjchichu ninneyethra dosham vaanennil ethra?
    Imbamaay chollunn eshu njaan kazhukidum ninne
  3. Muttum njaan tharunnitha bhoonikshepam muzhuvan
    Deham dehi samastham ennekkum nintethu njaan
  4. Vaagdatham ennaashrayam raktham upayogichu
    Poozhiyil thaazhthunn-enne yeshuvodu krooshichu
  5. Yeshu vannenn-aatmathe nirackkunnu poorthiyaay
    Sukhamundu poornnamaay-mahathwam kunjaattinnu
  6. Ennaashrayam yeshuvil-vaazthappetta kunjaattil
    Thaazhmayaay kumbidunnu rakshikkunnippoleshu. T.K
