• Krooshinte vachanam daivashakthiyum
    Daivajnaanam praapichavan kaamyavum
    Ennennum jeevashakthi nalkidum jeevithaththe danyamaakki theerthidum (2)
  1. Aadiyil vachanamaayirunnavan jadameduthu
    Bhoovil vannu paarthavan
    Paapikale thedi vanna rakshakan
    Paapam neekkaan yaagamaayi theernnavan
  2. Alpajnaanikale jnaaniyaakkuvaan
    Sathyamaam vachanamonnu maathrame
    Sathyavum krupayum niranje vannavan
    Sathyathil vazhi nadathi paalikkum (2)
  3. Koorirul niranja lokayaathrayil
    Koodeyundennaruli cheytha naadane
    Krooshinte vachanathaal prakaashichum
    Ethu paathayilum namme karuthidum (2) C.J.P
