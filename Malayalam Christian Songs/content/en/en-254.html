  1. Immaanuvel than chankathil ninnozhukum raktham
    Paapakkara neengumathil mungi theernnaal aarum
    • Enperkkeshu marichennu njaan vishwasikkunnu
      Paapam ennil ninnu neekkaan
      Yeshu raktham chinthi
  2. Kallan krooshil paapashaanthi kandiee uravayil
    Avaneppol njaanum doshi kanden prathishaanthi
  3. Kunjaattin vilayeriya rudhrathin shakthi
    Veendu kollum daiva sabha aake visheshamaay
  4. Than murivil rakthaneer njaan kandannumuthal than
    Veendeduppin sneham thaanen chintha innumennum
  5. Vikkulla ente ee naavu shavakuzhikkullil
    Maunam ennaal ennaathmaavu paadum unnathathil. T.K
