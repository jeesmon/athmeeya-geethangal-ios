1. Manavaaleneshu varunnithallo
    Manavaatti vegam unarnnidatte
  2. Lokamengum lakshyam kandu thudangi
    Vegam varum yeshu loka rakshakan
  3. Atthivruksham poothu thalirthu kaanmin
    Veendeduppin kaalamaduthithallo
  4. Yudhavum pakarcha vyaadhikalellaam
    Kristhu varavinte sathya lakshyangal
  5. Kallanennapol njaan vegam varunnu
    Vella vasthramellaam kaathukollatte
  6. Kanyakanmaar pathum urangidunnu
    Paathiraathri thannil priyan varume
  7. Dasarellaam nithyam jaagarikkatte
    Yeshu varum secondarinju koodaa
  8. Varika yennaavi othunnathupol
    Manavaatti koode paranjidatte
  9. Gopurathil koodi akathupovaan
    Vasthramalakkunnor bhaagyamullavar
  10. Perumeenudichu vaanaviravil
    Ushakaalam vanningaduthuvallo
  11. Vegam varunnennu mozhinja paraa!
    Varika meghathil njangale cherppaan. K.V.S
