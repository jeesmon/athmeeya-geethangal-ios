• Shreenarapathiye!- seeyon manavaalane!
    Naashapaathramaya lokamaakeyudharippathinnu
    Kanyakayil jaathanaay vanna- shreenarapathiye!
  1. Aattidayar koottamaayi vanna neram
    Keettu sheelayil kidanna yeshu devaa
    Pattinulla moolavasthu shrushti cheytha daivame yee-
    Klishtamaam kidappu kandu njaan- namikkunnen--
  2. Vannu maagar ninnadukkal bhakthiyode
    Ponnu mooru kundurukkam kaazcha vechu
    Unnathaadhikaaramulla mannavan neeyennathanyar
    Sannamichu ghoshikkunnithaa-nisshankamaay--
  3. Panthirandaam vayassinkaleshuve! nee
    Thantha thallayodurumicheesha geham
    Sambrathi pathichavidekkanda shaastrivaryarodu
    Darmmabhaashanam nadathiye- modichu nee--
  4. Muppathaam vayassil snaanamelppathinnu
    Thwalpuram vedinju yoodya thannil vannu
    Swalppavum madichidaathe yurddinaann ozhukkinullil
    Alpparaakum marthyarannapol- majjichoru--
  5. Naalppathu naalupavasichidave nee
    Thaalppariyathodaduthu vairiyaayon
    Dushtamaam pralobhanathin onnume vazhippedaathe
    Nishtayaalaghora vairiye- tholppichoru--
  6. Kushtarogikalkku nalla saukhiya meki
    Drushti poyavarkku bhavaan kaazcha nalki
    Nashta jeevanaaya laasar naalu naal kazhinju yirthu
    Pushtaraay ayyaayiram janam- anchappathaal-
  7. Dushkruthamaam visha bhaadha neekkuvaanaay
    Thakka yaagamaay marichu kurishil nee
    Ikkatdina paapiyude-yakkramathinulla shiksha
    Nishkalangam nee vahichathaal- namikkunnen-
  8. Ethra kaalam njaanivide jeevichidum
    Athranaalum nin krupaye njaan sthuthikkum
    Athridiva vaasikalkkum chithramaaya ninte naamam
    Utharotharam ninackkuvaan- arulka nee--
  9. Kaashu polum vilayilla vishayathil
    aashayenikkiyalaathe yeshuve! nin
    Naashameshidaatha desha maashayodu nokki vishu-
    Dhaashayathod eeshane ninnil- veshikkanam. K.V.S
