    eppol njaan vannu cherum?
    Dharaniyile paadum kedum
    eppol ingozhiyum?
  1. Bhaktharin bhaagyathalame
    parimalasthalam neeye
    Dukham vichaaram prayatnam
    ninkalangille-
  2. Raavum andhakaaram vail
    sheethavum angille
    Deepa thulyam shudharangu
    shobhichidunne
  3. Ratnangalallo ninmathil
    ponnum maanikyangal
    Panthrandu nin vaathilkalum
    minnum muthallo-
  4. Yerushalemen imba veede!
    ennu njaan vannu cherum?
    Parama raajaavin mahathwam
    arikil kandidum
  5. Shreshta nadakkaavukalum
    thottangalum ellaam
    Kaattuvaan-inayillaatha
    kootta marangal-
  6. Jeeva nadi imba shabdam
    mevi athiloode
    Povathum eeraaru vruksham
    nilppathum modi-
  7. Dootharum angaarthu sadaa
    swaramandhalam paadi
    Naadhane kondaadidunna
    geetham maamodi-
  8. Yerushalemin adhipaneesho!
    thirumun njaan sthuthipaadaan
    Varumvareyum arikil bhavaan
    irikkanam naadhaa! M.V
