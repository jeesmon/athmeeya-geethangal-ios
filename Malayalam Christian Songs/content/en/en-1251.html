• Paraneshuve karunaanidhe! varamekuka dambathikal-
    Kkarulename krupaye dinamprathi maaripol daivajaathaa
  1. Thava daasaraamivar-aikamathyamode vasichiduvaanum
    Avasaanakaalam-ananjidum vare preethiyil mevathinnum-
  2. Paramaaviyaalivare nirackka mahonnathane dinavum
    Thirunaama keerthi sadaa ninachu thangal vasichiduvaanum
  3. Paraneshu than priyanaay thirusabhaye varichaayathinnaay
    Maranam sahichathupoleedaasan than patniye cherthukolvaan
  4. Thava daaseeyaamivalum anusarichidanam nithyavum than
    Dhavane puraa saarayum abraamineyennapol modamode
  5. Pala maattavum marivum niranja lokeyivar nithyavum nin
    Alivettamullavayaam chirakadi chernnu sukhippathinnum. T.J.A
