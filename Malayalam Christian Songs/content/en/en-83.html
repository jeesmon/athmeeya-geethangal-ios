• Sthuthicheyvin eshuvine-
    athivandithanaam avane
    Daivamakkal ellaavarume-
    divya bhakthi niranjakame
  1. Avan mediniyil vannu-
    puri bethelehem thudangi
    Giri kaalvariyil vareyum-
    athivedanakal sahichu-

  2. Thirujeevane aadukalkkaay-
    tharuvaan manassaayavanaay
    Oru nallidayan dayaye -
    karutheeduka naam hrudaye
  3. Hithamaay avane thakarppaan-
    sutharaay narare ganippaan
    Pithru neethiyidam nadappaan-
    suthan vanniva aaswadippaan
  4. Sthuthi sthothrangal sweekarippaan-
    avan maathrame moovulakil
    Oru paathramaayullarikil-
    sarvvagothravume varuvin. T.K.S
