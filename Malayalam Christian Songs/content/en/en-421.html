• Yeshuvin saakshiyaay
    pokunnu njaaninnu
    krooshin paathayil
    Ha!ennikkethrayo
    yogyamathaakayaal
    Njaan mahaa bhaagayavaan!
  1. Vishwaasathaalinnu pokunnu njaan
    Swantha shaashwatha naattil
    Mannil njaananyan kristhuvil
    dhanyan ennathu nirnnayam
  2. Mannin mahimakal maana
    dhanaadikal ennivayalla
    Kristhuvin ninda nithya
    dhanamenn enni pokunnen
  3. Aashwaasadaayakan vishwaasa-
    naayakan satprakaashakan
    Paathayilennum nalloli
    thannu nadathidunnenne
  4. Puthanaam shaalemilethiyen
    raajane kaanum vegathil
    Nithya santhosha geethangalode
    than paadam cherum njaan. C.J
