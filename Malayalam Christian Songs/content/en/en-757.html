• Yeshu sannidhaanam enthoru samaadhanam
    Azhalum manassine arulum sukhadaanam!

    shokathaal veenidume
    Kristhuvin sannidhi chernnidunnor-
    kkennum athyaanandame
  2. Vedanayerunna neravum
    sodarar maarumbozhum
    Maaraatha snehithanaameshuvin
    maaril njaan chaaridume!-
  3. Verilloar aashwaasa sthaanavum
    verillor aashrayavum
    Mruthyuvilum samaadhaanamente
    kristhuvin sannidhaanam
  4. Kannuneer poornnamaay thornnidum
    karthaavu vannidumbol

    thannil maranjidum njaan. G.P
