• Free from the Law

    Kristhuvililla nyaayapramaanam
    Krooshinmel vannathinnavasaanam
    Nammude paapam vahichu thaan
    Nammude shaapam sahichu thaan
  1. Ekayaagathaal vannu raksha
    Ekayaagathaal theernnu shiksha
    Krooshikkappetta mandhyasthanaam
    Yeshuvil vannu puthrathwam
  2. Daivathin makkal madhura naamam
    Puthranil vannee swarggeeya sthaanam
    Dathinaalalla jananathaal melil ninnulla shakthiyaal
  3. Evideyundo akshara vaazhcha
    Avideyundu jadathin thaazhcha
    Nanmaye cheyvaan aagrahikkum
    Thinmayathre nivarthikkum
  4. Nyaayapramaanathinnu marichu-
    Uyirtha yeshuvine dharichu
    Parishudhaatmaavil jeevippaan
    Vilichu namme daivam thaan
  5. Mosheyin seva ayyo! prayaasam
    Yeshuvin seva enthorullaasam
    Jadam aatmaavinmel bharanam
    Cheyyaatha divya-swaathanthryam
  6. Rakshackkaay pravarthikkaennalla
    Raksha sammaanichathre than nalla
    Hitham cheyyunnullil daivam thaan
    Ithil nilkkunnon bhaagyvaan
  7. Pravarthikkunnon aayathu daivam-
    Nivarthikkunnon aayathum daivam
    Sarvvaril sarvvam than nirmmitham
    Sarvvavum than krupaavaram. V.N
