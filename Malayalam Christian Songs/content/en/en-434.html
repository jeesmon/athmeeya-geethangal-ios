1. Nalla devane! njangal ellaavereyum
    Nalloraakki nin ishtathe chollidename
  2. Pachamechil njangal menjiduvaanaay
    Mechamaay-aahaarathe nee nalkidename
  3. Andhakaaramaam ee lokayaathrayil
    Bendhuvaayirunnu vazhi kaattidename
  4. Imbameriya nin anpulla swaram
    Mumbe nadannu sadaa kelppikkename
  5. Vedavaakyangal njangalkk aadaayamavaan
    Veda naadhane ninte jnjaanam nalkuke
  6. Santhosham sada njangal chinthayil vaazhaan
    Santhoshathe njangalkkinnu daanam cheyyuke
  7. Thaathan aathmanum priya nithya puthranum
    Saadaram sthuthi sthothram ennum chollunnen
