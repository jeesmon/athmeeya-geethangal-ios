• Vaazhthidum yeshuve njaan-
    en jeevanee Moorthi vidumvareckkum
  1. Aarthikal neekkumee
    paarthdivan thannude
    Vaarthayin vaibhavathe-dinam thorum
    keerthanam cheythiduvan-
  2. Shreshtathayulla than veettine vittiha
    Dushta nararkkuvendi-maranathil
    petta maheshanivan-
  3. Paapavashaalulavaaya maranathe
    Kevalam neekkiyenne-than
    jeevanil bhaagiyakkid unnathaal-
  4. Swantha kashtangalaal en durithangale
    Chanthamaay neekkiyatho duyarnnathaam
    chinthaye thannenikku-
  5. Loka dukhangalil onninu mennude
    Shokamillaa nilaye-marippathi-
    nnaakukayilla thellum. K.V.S
