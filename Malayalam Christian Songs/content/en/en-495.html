• Yesu nallavan enikku yesu nallavan
    Nalla rakshakan than naamam
    vaazhththippaadum njaan
    Yesu nallavan athe en yesu nallavan
    Yesu nallavan

  1. Chengkadal pilarnnu nalvazhi nadathidum
    Adimayin nukam thakarthu veendeduthavan
    Chirrakukal vidarththi marravil kaathidunnavan
    Yesu nallavan

  2. Munnilum pirrakilum nadannu kaavalaay
    Meghamonnenikku mel virichchu snehamaay
    Neenda marubhoovil ullangkaiyil thaangngidum
    Yesu nallavan

  3. Kottakal thakarthidaan balaththe nalkidum
    Neettiya bhoojaththaalenne thaan nadathidum
    Veettil eththuvolam ponmukhathe nokkidaam
    Yesu nallavan

  4. Kshaamakaalathenne kshemamode kaathidum
    Pachchappulthakidiyil kidathidunnavan
    Vendathellam nithyameki pottidunnavan
    Yesu nallavan
