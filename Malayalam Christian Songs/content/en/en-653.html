• TUNE: Aaswasame enikkere

    Aanandamundenikk aanandhamundeni-
    Keshu mahaa raja sannidhiyil
  1. Loakam enikkoru shaswathamallennu
    Sneham niranjeshu cholleettunde
    Swarloka naattukaarkk ikshithiyil pala
    Kashta sankadangal vanneedunnu.
  2. Karthave neeyente sankethamaakayal
    Ullil manaklesham leshamilla
    Vishwasa kkappalil swarpuram cheruvan
    Chukkan pidikkane ponnunaadha
  3. Ennathmave ninnil changalyamenthihe
    Bakhayin thaazhvara yathrayithu
    Seeyon puri thannil vegam namukkethi-
    Tananda kkannuneer veezhthidame
  4. Koodara vaasikalaum namukkingu
    Veedenno naadenno cholvanundo
    Kaikalal theerkkatha veedonnu thathan than
    Meethe namukkai vechittundu
  5. Bhaaram prayaasangngalerum vanadesa-
    Thaakulamaathmaavil vanneedukil
    Paaram karunayulleesan namukkaayi-
    Tetam krupa nalki paalichidum
  6. Karthave nee vegam vanneedane njangal
    Kkorthalee kshoniyil maha dukham
    Ennalum nin mukha shobhayathin moolam
    Santhosha kaanthi poondanandikkum
