• Entre karthaavin paadathinkal
    Stothra yaagangal arppikkum njaan
    Ente paapathin shaapathe
    Neekkiya karthane vaazthi vanangidum njaan
  1. Ente shikshakal neekkiduvaan
    Yeshu rakshakanaay vannihathil
    Ente lamkhanangal ellaam kshamichu thannu
    Thante kaalvary rakthathinaal-
  2. Roga shokangal vannidumbol
    Saukhya daayakanaay arikil vanne
    Ente aakulangal ellaamakattiyennil
    Ennum aashwaasam pakarnnidum thaan!

  3. Ente ethirikal nira nirayaay
    Chaare ananjidum samayamathil
    Loka rakshakanaameshu naadanavan
    Maarvvathil cherthenne kaathidume! P.U.T
