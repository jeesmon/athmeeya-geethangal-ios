• Ezhunnettu prakaashika deepam thelichiduka
    Yeshuvin naamathe vaazthiduka
    Naalthorum paadi pukazhthiduka-
  1. Krooshumeduthini poyidaam
    Jeeva maarggamurachidaam
    Puthen veediyorukkidaam
    Suvisheshakkodikal uyarthidaam-
  2. Sathyathin paatha kaattidaam
    Sneha saamraajyam orukkidaam
    Neethisooryante keezhil naam
    Ullaasamode vasichidaam-
  3. Halleluyya geetham muzhakkidaam
    Naadan yeshuvin varavithaa
    Vaanathil vannidum vallabhan yeshuve
    Naaminnu padi pukazhthidaam. J.M
