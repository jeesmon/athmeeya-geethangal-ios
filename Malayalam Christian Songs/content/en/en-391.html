1. Kristheshuvinte senayil njaan chernnu nilkkunnu
    Than rakthamaam kodikarathilenthissa dhairyamaay
    Jagathin naayakaa! Daridranaaya njaan
    Irikkum naalellaam ninnude kaarunyam
    Pukazhthum sarvvavum ninakkaayarppikkum
    Namikkum thruppaadangalil-
  2. Lokathinaashissekidum nin krooshin vachanam
    Aavunna mattilothuvan nistharkkamaayengum
    Enikkaay peedakal sahicha naadane
    Smarikkum nerathin durkhadamethilum
    Ksheenikkunnilla njaan
    yeshuvin karabalathaal-
  3. Vishwaasapporil mumbanaay njaan nilkkum daivathin
    Nishwastha vaakkaam vaalinaal hanippen vairiye
    Shaktharaam veeranmaar jeevane thyajichum
    Sathyathekkaathoripporkkala staanathil
    Rakthathinnanthyamaam thulliyum chinthi njaan kristhane anukarikkum
  4. Ketteedume arackku sathayamenna kacha njaan
    Dharikkume suneethiyaam kavachavum sadaa
    Than suvisheshathin yatnamen cherippum
    Raksha en chaaruvaam thalakkorikayum
    Vishwaasakhedavum dharichu poruven
    Ugramaam aatma yudhathil-
  5. Ottalla vannu thattidum van pattiniyilum
    Vittoduvaan thininju koodaa kastangalilum
    Kastamundaakilum karthane nokki njaan
    Shathuvin koottathe vetti munnottu thaan
    Thusti poonderanam sainya naadan tharum
    Aajnakal kaathu jeevippaan-
  6. Ullinnu dukhamettolaa kudumba bhaarathaal
    Kallinu shathruvaakanam subhodam paalippaan
    Bhaaramellaamen raajanedukkunnu
    Njaanathu chumannaal bhoshatham bhoshatham
    Jeevan vedinjaalum shathru en raajyathu
    Kaaloonnaanidavarallo-
  7. Yudhangalozhinjethume njaan raajadhaaniyil
    En karthanppolekumen viruthu sarvvavum
    Thalkshanam thannudeyomana thrukkaram
    Muthi njaan vaangumeyen viruthokkeyum
    Nithyamaay shobhippaan sathyamaay modippaan
    Swargikal chutti nilkkave. K.V.S
