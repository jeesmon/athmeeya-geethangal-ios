1. Mahathwathil vasikkum deva-
    mahathwam ninakku-parane
    Sthuthi ninakku uchitham thanne -
    sthuthi njangal karettidunne
  2. Lokangale srushtichavane -
    lokathe nee snehichathi-naal
    Puthraneyayacha nin sneham-
    ethrayo athbhutham thaathane
  3. Marthyare rakshippaan vannonaam-
    Nithyavachanamaam-karthaave!
    Sthuthi sthothram ninakku njangal-
    athimodaalarppikkunne
  4. Puthu jeevan nalki njangale
    puthu srushtiyaakkum-aathmaave
    Vandichidunnu njangal ninne-
    nandiyaal nannaay vaazhthidunne
  5. Vandithane, thriyeka deva!
    unnathanaam swargga thaathane!
    Ennum njangal ninne vandikkum
    nandiyode aaradhichidum. T.T.M
