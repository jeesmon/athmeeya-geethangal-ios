• Paramaatmaavura cheyyum mozhiyellaa
    Sabhakalum shravikkanam
    Sthiranaam saakshiyum vishwasthanumaaya
    Srushtiyinnaadya nila labichoru vimalanothina-
    Mozhi dharikkukil shubhamezhum thava
  1. Sheethamall ushnavumalla thavagunam ethennu nijavumilla
    Ee vidhaminiyum nee vaadiya jalam pole
    Naal kazhikkukil ninne njaan mama
    Vaayil ninnu purath umizhnnidum-
  2. Njaanoru dhanithanne-enikkilla deenatha lavaminnu
    Maanamodidam cholli heenanaay kurudanaay
    Neeyirippathu kaanka paranude
    Munnil veenariyikka neeyathu-
  3. Sambannaavathinnunde thanithankam
    Venvasthram dharippaanundu
    Nin kannu theliyuvaan lepavum vilackkunde
    Vannu vaanguka shikshayaal priyare
    Njaanunarthunnith arika nee-
  4. Maansaantharappeduka erivodu
    Nee maanasam thuranniduka
    Vaathililaham ninnu muttunnaayathu kettu
    Yaathoru naran thurannu tharikila-
    Pputhanodu njaan virunnu kazhinjidum-
  5. Sathyamaay jayam kollum
    Bhadannu njaan othiripparulidum
    Kruthyamaayaham mevum bhadraasana mathinkal
    Nithyamaayavan vaazhum lavudikya
    Madhyatho jaichenna viruthinaal. K.V.S
