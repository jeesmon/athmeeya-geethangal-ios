• Enni enni sthuthikkuvaan
    ennamillaatha krupakalinaal
    Innayolam than bhujathaal
    ninne thaangiya naamame
  1. Unnam vecha vairiyin
    kannin mumbil patharaathe
    Kanmanipol kaakkum karangalaal
    ninne moodi marachille?
  2. Yorddaan kalangi mariyum
    jeevitha bhaarangal
    Eliyaavin puthappevide
    ninte vishwaasa shodhanayil?
  3. Ninakk-ethiraayi varum
    aayudham phalikkayilla
    Ninte udayavan ninakk-avakaasham
    Thante daasarin neethiyavan. J.V.P
