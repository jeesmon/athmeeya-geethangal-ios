• Aashrayam yeshuvilennathinaal
    Bhaagyavaan njaan! bhaagyavaan njaan!
    Aashwaasam-ennil thaan thannathinaal
    Bhaagyavaan njaan! bhaagyavaan njaan!
  1. Kaarirul moodum velakalil
    karthaavin paadam chernnidum njaan
    Kaarirumbaaniyin paadulla paaniyaal
    Karuna niranjavan kaakkumenne- kaakkumenne-
  2. Thannuyir thanna jeeva naadhan
    enn-abhayam en-naal muzhuvan
    Onninum thannidam enniye verengum
    Odenda thaanguvaan thaan mathiyaam-thaan mathiyaam-
  3. Kaalvary naadhan en rakshakan
    kallarackkull odungiyilla
    Mruthyuve vennavan athyunnathan vinnil
    Karthaadhi karthaavaay vaazhunnavan- vaazhunnavan-
  4. Ithra saubhaagyam ikshithiyil
    Illa mattengum nishchayamaay
    Theeraatha santhosham kristhuvil-undennaal
    Thoaraatha kanneere mannilullu- mannilullu. M.E.C
