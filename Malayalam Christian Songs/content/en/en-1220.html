• TUNE: Ennodulla nin sarvva
  1. Inneeyushassil ninte vanmahathwam kaanmaan
    Thanna krupackkanantha vandaname-kaanmaan
  2. Doshamezhaatheyenne poornnamaay kaathu nee
    Raathri muzhuvanum van kaarunyathaal-ghora
  3. Ellaa vazhiyilum nin aatma saannidhyavum
    Divya prakaashavum nee nalkidenam-innu
  4. Samkhyayillaa janangal kanneer pozhikkunnu
    Njangal paadunnu divya paalanathaal-innum
  5. Mumbe nin raajyavum nin neethiyum theduvaan
    Ennaatma naadhane! nee paalikkenam-ennum
  6. Mruthyuvin naalvareyum nin mahathwathinnaay
    Bhakthiyil poornnanaayi kaathidenam-enne
  7. Dushtabhuvanamithin kashtatha okkeyum
    Theerthutharum nin raajyam kaamkshikkunnen-vegam. P.P.G
