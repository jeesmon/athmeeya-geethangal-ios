1. Ennodulla nin sarvva nanmakalkkaayi njaan
    Enthu cheyyendu ninakkeshupara- ippol
  2. Nandi kondente yullam nanne nirayunne
    Sannaahamode sthuthi padeedunnen-devaa
  3. Paapathil ninnu enne koriyeduppanaay
    Shaapa shikshakaletta devaathmaja-maahaa
  4. Enne anpodu dinam thorum nadathunna
    Ponnidayannanantham vandhaname-ente
  5. Anthiyam vareyumenne kaaval cheythiduvaan
    Anthikeyulla mahal shakthi neeye- naadha
  6. Thaathan sannidhiyil enperkku sadaa-paksha
    Vaadam cheyyunna mama jeevanaadha-paksha
  7. Kuttam kudaathe yenne thejassil mumbhaake
    Muttum niruthaan kazhivullavane-enne
  8. Mannidathiladiyan jeevikkum naalennum
    Vandanam cheyyum thirunaamathinu-deva. P.V.T
