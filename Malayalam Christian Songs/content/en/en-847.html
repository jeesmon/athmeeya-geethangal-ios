• Ennaalum enne karuthunna karthaave
    Nee maathram en nalla mithram
    Ippaaril neeyallaa thillenikkaashrayam
    Eppozhum paadum njaan sthothram
  1. Aakula nerathil aavashya bhaarathaal
    Akam-nonthu karayunna neram
    Ariketh-anenjennil aashwaasam-arulunnu
    Alivulla mithram nee maathram-
  2. Aapath-anarthdamen jeevitha yaathrayil
    Athidukham vitharumbol naadhaa
    Aviduthe thirumukham darshikkum nerathen
    Azhalellaam akalunnu doore-
  3. Alayaazhi thannilen vishwaasa naukayil
    Alayaatheyen yaanam thudaraan
    Alakalin meethe nadanna nin paadamaan-
    Abhaya-mennaalumen naadhaa-
  4. Akhilaandamulavaakum athinellaam munname
    Akhileshaa! enne nee orthu
    Athinaal ninnadi-malar chumbichu njaaninnu
    Athimodam paadunnu sthothram. G.P
