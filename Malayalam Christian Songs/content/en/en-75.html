• Sthothram sree manuvelane,
    mamapriya yeshunaathhaa
    vinmayanaam nee manmayanaay
    mama kodumpaapaththe neekki
  1. Azhamulla vankuzhiyil
    kuzhachetil veenavan njaan
    thaanirrangngi karaketi
    paarrayil kaalukal nirruthi
  2. Navagaanam paadiduvaan
    gamanaththe sthhiramaakki
    dehamithaa samarppikkunne
    lokaththin mohangngal vedinju
  3. Daarunamaay adiyetu
    murrivukalaal ninamaninju
    nindakalaal manamthakarnnu
    kurishathil nee thalachaayichu
  4. Udayunne mamahrudayam
    nirrayunne nayanangngal
    varnnikkuvaan ninvyathhakal
    neerrumen manassinnaaville
  5. Snehikkum sevikkum
    vandikkum kaalamellaam
    keerththikkume paadidume
    nisthulasneha dayaaparane. K.D.W
