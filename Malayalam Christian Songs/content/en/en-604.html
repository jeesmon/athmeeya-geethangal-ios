• UNE: Vandaname yeshu rekshakanen

    Rekshakane! ninte pakshamaay njaan
    ikshithiyil
    Nischayamaay nishchayamay
    Nilkumennaayussin naalellaam
  1. Sathyavum jeevanum maarggavumaaya nin
    Pakshamaay nilkkunnor lejjitharaakumo?-
  2. Chathu mannaayidum marthya-nethaakkalodothu
    vasichavar orthu niraasharaam-
  3. Ozhiyum aakashavum bhoomiyum enkilum
    Ozhinju pokaatha nin mozhikalaanen belam-
  4. Vairiyin porukaleri varumbozhum
    Dhairyam enikku nee tharumathu nishchayam-
  5. Pala vidha dukhangal ulakil-undaayaalum
    Alayaathathennum ninnarikil njaanaakayaal-
  6. Nithyatha thannil njaanethunna nerathum
    Nilkkum nin chaarathu muthum
    nin paadathil. T.K.S
