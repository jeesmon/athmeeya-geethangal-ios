• Dinam dinam dinam nee vaazhthuka
    Yeshuvin paithale nee
    Anudinavum paadi vaazhthuka
  1. Kaalvary rakthame yeshuvin rakthame
    Kaalvaryin yeshu thaan swantha raktham chinthi nin
    Paapathe shaapathe neekki thante rakthathaal-
  2. Rogam sheelichavan paapam vahichavan
    Kaalavary malamukal kaikaalkal virichavan
    Rakshikkum yeshuvin paadathil samarppikka-
  3. Enneshu sannidhi enikkethrayaashwaasam
    Kleshamellaam maattidum rogamellaam neekkidum
    Vishwaasathaal ninne yeshuvil samarppikka-
  4. Njaan nithyam chaaridum enneshu maarvvathil
    Nallavan vallabhan enneshu ethra nallavan
    Enneshu ponneshu enikethra nallavan-
  5. Aatmaavin jeevitham aananda jeevitham
    Aatmaavil nirayuka aananda nadiyithu
    Paanam cheythiduka yeshu vegam vannidum
