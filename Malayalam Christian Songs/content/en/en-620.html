• Divyanilaye diganthavalaye deva!
    Bhavyamezhum ninkarunaarasamen nilaye
  1. Daasarkkekinnilaye deenajala shilaye
    Daanamarulkeshu naadhaa! thirukaarunyamen
  2. Saadhu janangalin ghedamozhikkum naadhaa!
    Saadamakannennum ninnodanavaan thuna chey
  3. Neethimaanaaya nin divya maranathinaal
    Neethi nalki paalicha ninkrupa ennabhayam
  4. Paapamillaatha nin jeevane nalki pari-
    Paavanamaam rakthathaalen duritham neekkiya. K.V.S
