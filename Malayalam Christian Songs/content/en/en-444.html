• Thiruvedathin porule mama guruvaaya maheshajane
    Arulenamee samayam thava paramaamruthamaam vachanam
  1. Parishudha vedamathil ninnathbhutha kaaryangal kaanmathinaay
    Thurakkename mama kankal nee krupayodu paraa parane
  2. Paramaaviyin varamekanam viravodeyee daasanu nee
    Paramaananda karamaam mozhi parichodu grahippathinaay
  3. Ulakamathin pala chinthakal balamaayi vannen manassinnalambal
    cheyyaathirikka anadiyane kaathu sookshikkename
  4. Madhuram thava vachanam enikku then kattayekkaal adhikam
    Sathatham mahaa nidhiyaamithu sadayam tharanam parane
  5. Ulakamathil vilayeriya pala muthu rathnangalekkaal
    Alankaaramaay vilasidunnu balameriya nin mozhikal
  6. Maravaayulla mahaa marmmangal manathaarin pathivathinnaay
    Marakkaathe njaan karuthiduvaan tharanam krupaye yadhikam. P.V.T
