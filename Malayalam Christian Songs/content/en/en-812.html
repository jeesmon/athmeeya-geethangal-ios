1. Ente daivam mahathwathil aardravaanaay jeevikkumbol
    Saadhu njaanee kshoni thannil kleshippaan-ethum
    Kaaryamallennente ullam chollunnu-
  2. Vaishamyamullethu kunnum kara-keri nada-kolvaan
    Rakshakanen kaalukalkku vegamaay-theernnen
    Paathayil njaan maanine-ppolodidum
  3. Aarum-enikkillenno njaan ekanaayi theernnuvenno
    Maanasathil-aadhi-poondu khedippaan-saadhu
    Andhanaayi theernnidalle daivame!
  4. Ente nithya snehithanmaar daiva dootha samkham-athre
    Ippolavar daiva mumbil sevayaam-enne
    Kaaval cheythu shushrushippaan vannidum
  5. Dukhithanaay-odippoy njaan marubhoovil kidannaalum
    Enne orthu daiva doothar vannidum-ettam
    Snehachoodod appavumaay vannidum
  6. Naalaye-kkonden manassil lava-lesham bhaaramilla
    Oro naalum daiva-menne pottunnu-thante
    Kaikalil njaan dinam thorum chaarunnu
  7. Kaakkakale vichaarippin vithayilla koythumilla
    Daivam avackkaayi vendathekunnu-lilli
    Pushpangalkkumavan shobha nalkunnu
  8. Patmos dweepil ekanaay njaan vasichaalum bhayamilla
    Swarggam thurannente priyan vannidum -mahaa
    Darshanathaal vivashanaay theerum njaan
  9. Haa! maheshaa! karunesha! ponnu thaathaa! neey-enikkaay
    Vendathellaam dayathonni nalkumbol -ente
    Dehi vrudaa kalangunnath-enthinnaay? M.K
