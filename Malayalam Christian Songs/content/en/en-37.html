• Yeshu ente rakshakan
    ethrayaanandam
    Thante naamam vaanam
    bhuvilethra shrestamaam
  1. Paapikale thedi yeshu paarithil vannu
    Paavana ninam chorinju paarithin mandhye
    Kandu daivasneham krooshil puthrane konnu
    Thanna raksha enthoraaschariyam mahaathbhutham!
  2. Mruthyuve thakarthuyarthezhunnu karthanum
    Marthyathackkaruthi vannu thannuirppinaal
    Mruthyuvinnadimayilirunnathaam janam
    Nithyamaam prakaashathal prashobhitharaayi-
  3. Ithra shresta naayakan enikku karthanaam
    Thante shresta naamamente keerthanam sadaa
    Paarthalathil parathidunna kaalamathrayum
    Aarthiyoden naadhane vanangi vaazhthidum-
  4. Pooki yeshu vaanaloke naadhan pakshathil
    Mevidunnaachaaryanaayi than janathinnaay
    Paramathinkal puramorukki arikilaakkuvaan
    Viravileshu vannidum thirujanathinnaay. K.V.I
