    Jeevanaadhaneshuve
    Ithupol swajeevan thannoraathma
    Snehithan veraarini ?

    khoranaam pishaachine
    Marnathinaale neekki mruthiyu
    Bheethi theertha naadhane-
  2. Bahu maanyanaam-achaaryanaayi
    vaanilavan vaazhkayaal

    Cherthu kollumaakayaal-
  3. Dinavum maname thalsamayem
    vankrupakal praapippaan
    Athidhairyamaay krupaasanthin-
    nanthikathil chennu nee-

    vaazahthidunna naadhane
