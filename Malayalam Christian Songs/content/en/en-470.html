• Kaathidum paranenne kaarunayod avanennum
    Karalalinju than thirukkarangalaal
    Bharichu kaakkum thaanennekkum
  1. Charama naal vare maattamenniye
    Parama nischayam moolam
    Parama neeyenne paripaalichukond
    Irunnidumennum nirnnayam-
  2. Thiru mukhamenne nokkiyirippathathi
    Dhairyathinu hethuvaam
    Marana nizhalin vazhiyil koodi njaan
    Nadannalum thellum bhayappedaa-
  3. Azhiyathulloru jeeva shakthiyil
    Puthu jananathin makkalay
    Thiruvachanathal janippichatmeeka
    Puthukkam thannenne kaakkunnu
  4. Aare vishwasikkunne-nnariyunnundu njaaninne
    Avanenikkullorupanidhi param-
    Avasaanam vare sookshikkum. K.V.S
