• Maanuvel manuja suthaa!-ninte
    Maanamerum thruppaadangal vanangi njangal
    Mangalamothidunnithaa-nithyam
    Mahimayundaayidette ninakku naadhaa!
  1. Edanilaadi manujar- cheytha
    Paathakam pariharippaan bhoothale vannu
    Krooshathil marichuyirtha-ninte
    Peshalamaam charithamenthathi vipulam!-
  2. Vanparumanu nimisham-paadi
    Kumbidunna gunamezhumadhipathiye
    Chempakamaler thozhunna-paada
    Manpinode namikkunnu namikkunnithaa-
  3. Neecharaay ganichirunna-pethra-
    Naadiyaaya dheevarare divya krupayaal
    Sheshi kondalankarichu-param
    Prekshanam cheythavaniyil gurukkalaay nee-
  4. Vandanam paramaguro!-ninte
    Nandaneeyamaam gunangalurappathaamo?
    Chandanam puzhukivaye kkaalum
    Thonnidunnu nincharitham surabhiyaayi-
  5. Alppamaamupakaranam-kondu
    Nalpezhunna mahathaaya velakal cheyyum
    Shilpikalkkudayavane! neeye
    Chilppurushan chiranthana! namaskaaram-
  6. Kashtathayude naduvil-njangal
    Pettuzhannu valayunnundaakayaal deva!
    Thottu ninnomanakaiyyaal-param
    Chuttuneerum manassine thanuppikkane-
  7. Spheethamaam karimukile! sadhu
    Chaathakangalaanu njangal nee tharunnoru
    Sheekaramanubhavichu-sarvva
    Shokavum shamippikkuvaan krupa cheyyane-
  8. Aashayumanusruthiyum-sneha
    Paashabandham vinayavum vimalathayum
    Daasaril valarthaname-nithyam
    eshu naadhaa! namaskaaram, namaskaarame. K.V.S
