• Tune: Ente bhaaviyellaamente
    (Psalm 136)
  1. Sthuthippin naam yehovaye-
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Sthuthikkanamavane naam-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  2. Devadevane sthuthippin-
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Karthrukarthane sthuthippin-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  3. Thanichatbhutham cheythavan-
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Chamachaakaashangalavan-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  4. Bhumel vellam virichava-
    navan nalla naadhanallo
    Jyothissukal chamachava-
    navan krupa nithyamallo
  5. Pakal vaazhum sooryneyum-
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Raathri chandraadikaleyum-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  6. Misrem kadinjool konnava-
    navan nalla naadhanallo
    Yisraayele veendu kondo-
    navan krupa nithyamallo
  7. Visthruthamaam kaiyyookkinaa-
    lavan nalla naadanallo
    Chenkadal pakuthathavan-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  8. Athiloodavare nayi-
    chavan nalla naadhanallo
    Ethirikale amizthi-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  9. Maruvil janathe nayi-
    chavan nalla naadhanallo
    Arachare nashippichaa-
    navan krupa nithyamallo
  10. Shruthippetta raajarethaa-
    navan nalla naadhanello
    Seehonamor raajaavine-
    avan krupa nithyamallo
  11. Bashan raajavogineyum -
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Avar desham pakuthava-
    navan krupa nithyamallo
  12. Than janathinnava nalki-
    avan nalla naadhanello
    Than janathin thaazhnilayor-
    thavan krupa nithyamallo
  13. Shathru kaiyyil ninnum veendaa-
    navan nalla naadhanallo
    Pottunnavanellaatteyum-
    mavan krupa nithyamallo
  14. Swargga devane sthuthippin-
    avan nalla naadhanallo
    Nithya raajane vaazhthuvin-
    avan krupa nithyamallo. K.V.S
