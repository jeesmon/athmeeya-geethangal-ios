• Jaya jaya jagal guruve
    Varunnithaa thirusavidhe
    Anudinamanavadi thiru krupakal thanne
    Adiyaare nayikkename
  1. Kristhuvil njangale jayotsavamaay
    Nadathunna daivathil aasrayippaan
    Naada nin shakthiyaal nirackename
    Nithyam jayaththode munnerruvaan
  2. Kristhuvin suvishesha saakshikalaay
    Njangalennum saurabhya vasanayaay
    Naada nin pathramaay velippeduvaan
    Aatma daanangal nalkidane-
  3. Kristhuvin kastathil koottaaliyaay
    Njangal dukhithare muttumaashwasippan
    Naadaa nin aashwaasam anugrahamaay
    Krupayaalennum choriyename-
  4. Kristhuvin thejasse kannadipol
    Njangal shobhikkunnavaraayiduvaan
    Naada nin thejasse praapichennum
    Prathiroopamaay maattidane. P.B.S
