1. Enthoru snehamithe! ninam chorinju marichiduvaan
    Daivanandananee nararekkaruthi
    Jadameduppathinaay manassaay
    • Avan thaazhchayil nammale orkkukayaal
      Than padavi vedinjithu haa!-
  2. Athbhutha snehamithe! namukkaagrahikkaavathilum
    Avanappuramaay cheytha salkriyaay
    Marakkurishathil kaanunnu naam-
  3. Nithyamaam snehamithe! avanaadyam namme snehichu
    Avasaanatholam avan snehichidum
    Orunaalum kuranjidumo-
  4. Nisthula snehamithe! daivam puthrane kaivedinju
    Thante shathrukkalkkaay thakarkkaan hithamaay
    Ithupoloru snehamundo!-
  5. Daivathin snehamithe! daivam puthraneaadariyaathavane
    tharuvaan madikkaanjathinaal
    Tharum sakalamineem namukkaay-
  6. Divyamaam snehamithe! narar kaattidum snehamathil
    Pala maalinyavum kalarnnennuvaraam ennaal
    Kalankam illaathathithe. T.K.S
