• Kristheshu naamamaho! niranthara vishraamame enikku
  1. Paaram paribhramappettu valanjulla paapikalkk-aasakalam parithoshavumaathma
    vimukthiyumekiya
    Naamamithe sathatham
  2. Paapam vahichavan shaapam sahichu van
    Crodhavumettathivan deva-
    Neethikku shaanthatha nalkiyarishtarin shokavum periyavan
  3. Thaathante maanasamaaril prasaadikkumaayavanaaninimel
    enikkaanandamekunna
    divyapurushane-nnaayathu njaanarivu-
  4. Shakthiyarulunna kristhuvin saannidhyameppozhumathy
    adhikam mama
    Vyakthamariyunnu thrupthi nirayunni thuthamamaan-enikke-
  5. Deva savidhathil ninnu kavinjozhukidunna
    van krupakal anuvaasaram
    aaswadikkunnithu kristhuvil aavasipporadhikam
  6. Thannude ponnukarangal ellaattilum
    Kaanuvathere sukham thante-
    Unnathanaamathil aashrayipporathi dhanyarellaa dinavum
  7. Onnukondum punareeyulakam mama
    Vaanjchithamaayivaraa-para
    monnatha nandanenn abhilaashama-thaayi vasippathinaal. C.T.M
