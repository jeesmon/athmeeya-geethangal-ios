• Tune: Ithuvare enne karuthiya


    Stuthikku nee yogyan stuthikalinmeethe
    Vasichidum parane yeshumahesha
  1. Avaniyilamitham modamaay vasippa
    Narulename ninkrupakalapaaram
    Parma saubhaagyam paapikalkekaan
    Puthuvazhi thuranna sneha swaroopa!
  2. Narakula paapachumadu nee vahichu
    Kurishil kareri praanane vedinju
    Parishudhane thiruppadaanthikathil
    Parichodu stuthippaan krupayarulenam-
  3. Thava thirunaamam jaikkatte menmel
    Stothra yaagangal samarppikkunnadiyangal
    Thirukkaram thannil vahicha anudinavum
    Nadathuka daasane nin hithampole
  4. Ajaganathe nallidayaneppole
    Dinamprathi nadathum snehamapaaram
    Harithapulmaaliyil vishramamekum
    Swatcha jalaashayam thannil nee nadathum-
  5. Maranathin thaazhvara thannil nadannaal
    Preethiyodidayan saanthwanamekum
    Saha vasichidum nee anishavum senha-
    Thrukkaram thannil vahikkum nallidayan-
  6. Parama sambannan daridranaay theernnu
    Daridra janaavali sambannaraakaan
    Darithriyil paapathil marichavar jeevan-
    Darichu kristheshuvin dasaraay bhavichu. K.J.S
