• Yeshu thaanunnathan aarilum athivandithan
    Nandiyaay hrudi thingum aadaraal
    Cheyaka naam stuthi naavinaal
  1. Daiva nandanunnathe-ninnu Vannavan kanyaa jaathanaay
    Klishtamaam pashushaalayil shashpa Shayyayil pallikolvonaay
    Munnarulpole bethelem puri thannil vannu pirannu thaan
    Thannude peril vannathellaame munname arul cheythapol-
  2. Bhakthi badhanmaar budhanmaar
    Ponnu kunthurukkavum moorumaay
    Kaazhchayarppichu sannamichavarunnatha sutheneshuve
    Vedanjaanikal baalaneshuvil kandu vedyavedaanthiye
    Amma kaanaavil chonnille sutha-
    nnaanja polellaam cheyyuvaan
  3. Daivaputhranaam yeshu than thaatha
    Nennume priyamullavan
    Than mozhikelppaanallayo daivam unnathe ninnurathathum
    Thante vaakkinaal bhoothamodiye
    Vyaadiyokkeyadangiye
    Thannadikkadi vankadalthira panjipoleyamarnnaho!
  4. Alpamaayulla bhakshanam balanarppicheshuvin kailaay
    Thuptharaayi ayyaayiram janam pushtabhojanam bhakshiche
    Daivamaayavan thannorappamaay
    Dyovil ninnu thaan vannathaay
    Kaanuvorkkavan jeeva poopamaay
    Jeevan thannoru rakshakan-
  5. Mruthyuvin pinup uyirthezhunnathi Shakthiyodihe vannonaay
    Kristuvenniye veraarumilla nithyamaam jeevanaanavan
    Vaanileriyon thaan varum vegam
    Than vrutharo than koodeyaay
    Jeevanil vaazhum khedamo pinne
    Yethume kaanukil ho!. K.J
