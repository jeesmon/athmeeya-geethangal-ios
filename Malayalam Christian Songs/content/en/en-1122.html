• Paapi varika! paraneyarika! thava
    Paapamellaam ozhinjettu paraka, thirika!-
  1. Paapamethennarika aake ninkennerika
    Paapa shiksha maranamennarika, thirika-
  2. Jeevanetheduka vegamaayodiduka
    Jeevan nalkum kaalvaryodanaka, thirika-
  3. Aavalodikshanam krooshodananjidukil
    Jeevan-avan-audaaryamaay-arulum sthiramaay-
  4. Puthranil vishwasichidunna nararkkinnu
    Nithya raksha labhikkuvaan thadavilludane-
  5. Sathyaathma parayunnu kelkkuvonum parayaam
    Ichhipporkku jeeva vellam veruthe vaangidaam-
  6. Kristhu kaanthayumithaa shakthiyaay vilikkunnu
    Swastha labhippathinu vazhiyithonnu thaan
  7. Jeeva-jalathinn-urava-yozhukum ninnil
    Jeevanengum vyaapippathaal maranam neengidum-
  8. Andhakaaram neengiyenthu velichamaakum!
    Swaanthamellaam prabha thingi nirayum druddamaay
  9. Mooddabodamozhiyum kedakannu thiriyum
    Gaaddamaay jnjaanamullil kalarum valarum-
  10. Iddinaminnu nee nashtamaakkidumenkil
    Mattoru dinam ninakku tharamaay varumo? K.V.S
