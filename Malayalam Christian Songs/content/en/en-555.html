• Ponneshu thampuraan nalloru rakshakan
    Enne snehichu than jeevan vechu
  1. Swargga simhaasanam thaathante maarvvathum
    Doothanmaar sevayum vittenperkkaay
    Daasaneppolavan jeevichu paapiyen
    Shaapam shirassathil ettiduvaan-
  2. Thalleyeppol namukkulloru rakshakan
    Kollakkaaran pole krooshil thungi
    Ullamurukunnen chanku thakarunnen
    Kannu nirayunnen rakshakane!
  3. Enthoru snehamee saadhuve orthu nee
    Santhaapa saagaram thannil veenu!
    Enne vilichu nee enne eduthu nee
    Ninnomana ppaithalaay theerthuvallo
  4. Paapam perukiya sthaanathu krupayum
    Ettam perukiyath aascharyame!
    Paapyil pradhaaniyaayirunna njaanum
    Snehathin puthrante raajyathilaay-
  5. Bhooloka maayayil moham pathichente
    Kaalam njaan paazhil kalanjidaayvaan
    Swarlloka raajyathil thanka kkireedathi-
    Lullaasamekane ponneshuve-
  6. Paapam cheyyathenne kaaval cheythiduvaan
    Sarvvesha! thrukkailelppikkunnen
    Raappakal neeyenne veezhchayil ninnente
    Swapnathil koodeyum kaakkename!-
  7. Karthaavu vegathil meghangalil kodi
    Doothanmaar aarppumaay vannidumbol
    Ennil kaninjenne maarvvodanachente
    Sankadam theerkkanam rakshakane! M.K
