    Yeshuve enne nee.
  1. Ardhapraananaay kidanno-renne
    raksha cheythiduvanaay aa aa
    Thanirangi bhoovil nee vannitho!-
  2. Keettu sheelayil pothinja-
    ponnumeniyangu shayippoo aa aa
    Pulthozhuthilallayo naadhane!-
  3. Gatshamana poovanathil-
    paapachashakam kandoruneram aa aa
    Mannil veenupoyavan aarthanaay-
  4. Ninamaninja meniyil van-
    kurishumenthi paadukalettu aa aa
    Veenu veenu pokayaanen paran!-
  5. Kurishil kaikaalkal viriche-
    shirassil mulkireedamaninju aa aa
    Thala chumalil chaayichavan kezhunnu-
  6. Sakalarum kaivitta neram-
    Praanan kurishil yaagamaay theernnu aa aa
    Veendeduthen praanane snehavaan. K.D.W
