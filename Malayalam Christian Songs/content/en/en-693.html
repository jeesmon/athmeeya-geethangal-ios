• yeshuve njaan ninne snehikkunnu
    En yeshuve njaan ninne snehikkunnu (2)
  1. Ninne njaan thediyilla en yeshuve
    Ninne njaan thediyilla
    Neeyaanallo swargga bhaagyam vittirangi
    Ezhaye thediyathe-
  2. Ninne njaan snehichilla en yeshuve
    Ninne njaan snehichilla
    Neeyaanallo aadyamezhaye snehichu
    Saubhaagyam nalkiyathe-
  3. Ninne njaan orthathilla en yeshuve
    Ninne njaan orthathilla
    Neeyaanallo enne thaazhchayilorthathum
    Swarggathiliruthiyathum
  4. Ninne njaan stuthichidunnu en yeshuve
    Ninne njaan stuthichidunnu
    En deham dehiyum aatmaavumorupol
    Nandiyaal stuthichidunnu. K.V.H
