    Sthothramanantham sthothramanantham-
    sarvva Karthaa! nin naamathinennum
    sthothramanantham
  1. Nithya daivame! sathya naadhane! ninna-
    Pathyathayin dathinaayi
    Sthothramanantham
  2. Nin niyamitha vrundamathil nee-yenne
    Mun niyamichennathinaal
    sthothramanantham
  3. Aadipithaavil haa! mruthanaaya-ente
    Jeevaninnuyirppinaayi
    sthothramanantham
  4. Than jadamaaya puthuvarnna vazhiyaayi- swargge
    Enneyumiruthyathaal
    sthothramanantham
  5. Shudhamaam nilayil nithya jeevani-linnum
    Kaatha nin krupackkuvendi
    sthothramanantham-
  6. Ninnude thirusannidhi moolam-ippol
    Enne nee shudheekarikka
    sthothramanantham
  7. Ninnude balamennil vannu niravaan- ippol
    Enne nee anugrahikka
    sthothramanantham-
  8. Nin krupayil njaanen mruthiyolam-nilpanenne
    nee aashirvadikka
    sthothramanantham
  9. Than hitham pole sarvvavum cheyyum- nithya
    Mannavane! nin krupackkaayi
    sthothramanantham
  10. Unnathamaaya ninnude naamam- sarvva
    Sannuthamaay bhavikkatte
    sthothramanantham. K.V.S
