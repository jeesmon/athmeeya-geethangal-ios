1. Daivathin paithal njaan yeshuvin koode njaan
    Mevunnu mannithil modamaay
    Jeevitham dhanyanaay sankadam theernnupoy
    Ee vidham ennum njaan bhaagyavaan-
  2. Aakula velayil eppozhum thaangidum
    Aakayaal vyaakulamillineem
    Aashrayam nalkidum anpezhum nenjchathil
    Asahwasippikkum than kaikalaal-
  3. Jeevitha paathayil eridum bhaarathil
    Bheethanaakilla njaan dhairyamaay
    Naadhanaam eshuve nokki njaan poyidum
    Aananda gaanangal paadi njaan-
  4. Uttavar kaividum nerathum maaraathe
    Muttum than snehikkum nischayam
    Ithra nal naadhane snehichum sevichum
    Maathramen naalukal theeranam-
  5. Meghathil vannidum priyanaam eshuve
    Vegathil kaanum njaan thejassil
    Mruthyuvum dukhavum nindayum theernnu ha!
    Nithyamaay vaazhum thankoode njaan. C.J
