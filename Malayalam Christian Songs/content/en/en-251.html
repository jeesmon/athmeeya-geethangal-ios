• Enthoranpithappane! ippaapimel enthoranpithappane!
    Anderkone! neeyi-chandaala drohiyil
    Kondoranpu parayendunnath engane!-
  1. Anpolum thampuraane! ninte mahaa anpulloru makane
    Imbam niranjulla nin madiyil ninnu
    Thumpam niranja paarinkal ayachathum-
  2. Kanmaniyaam nin makan poonkaavinkal mannil veenirannathum
    Ponnin thirumeni thannil ninnu chora
    Mannil veenathum nin kannengane kandu!-
  3. Karunayatta yoodanmaar ninmakante thirumeniyaake naadhaa
    Koradaavu kondadichuzhutha nilamaakki
    Kurishippathinaay kurisheduppikkunnu!-
  4. Daaham vishappukondu thalarnnu kai-kaalkal kuzhanjidunnu
    Dehamazhalunnu dehiyuzhalunnu
    Sneham perukunnippaathakanodayyo!-
  5. Shathrukkal mandhyekoode pokunnitha kuttamatta kunjaade
    Kashtam erushalem puthrimaar kandu maarathadichayyo
    vaavittalaridunnu-
  6. Karuna niranjavan thankaikaalkale kurishil virichidunnu
    Karunayatta dushtar kroorakaikalaale
    Kurishodu cherthaani vechidunnayyayyo!-
  7. Aakaashabhoomi mandhye ninte makan haa! haa! thoongidunnayyo!
    Kaalkarangalude chorayozhukunnu
    Vekunnu nin kopatheeyinkal veenavan-
  8. Daivame, en daivame, enthukondu kaivedinjathenne nee
    Evamithaa ninte eka suthan thante
    Vaavittalarunnu nee kelkunnillottum!
  9. Chanku thurann-ozhukiyathaam raktha thinkalenne kazhuki
    Ponkaram kondu nadathi pputhusaaleminkal
    cherkka yeshu sankethame enne. J.J
