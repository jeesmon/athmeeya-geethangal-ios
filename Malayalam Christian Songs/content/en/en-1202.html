• Enne snehikkum ponnesuve!
    ennum paalikkum en naathhane!
    ee marubhoovil kaividalle
    thiruchirrakenne pothiyename
  1. ennil vannupoy thetadhikam
    ellaam kshamikkane karththaave!
    enne venmayaakkename
    vannidunnezha nin savidhe
  2. ullam aake thakarum neram
    utavar vittupiriyum neram
    enne vittangngu pokaruthe
    neeyallaathillenikkabhayam
  3. engngum aapaththolichchirikkum
    velayil nindaasanaamenne
    ullam kaiyil vahichchidane
    kanmanipole kaaththidane. A.V
