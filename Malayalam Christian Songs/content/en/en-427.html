• Oru cheru thaakam pol oru cheru kaithiripol
    Vilangenam ninakkaayen naalukal theerum vare
  1. En kuravukal orkkaathe en veezhchakal kanakkidaathe
    Nin krupakal chorinjenne nin paathe nadathidane
  2. Pala vidhamaam shodhanayil
    Valayil njaan veenidumbol
    Valanjidaathe ninniduvaan balam enikkekidane-
  3. En thaalanthukal akhilam en maanavum dhanavumellaam
    En jeevitham sampoornnamaay
    Nin mumbil samarppikkunne-
  4. Perum thaapathaal alanjidumbol
    Verum naama maathramaay theerumbol
    Iru karangalaal thaangiyenne thirumaarvvodanachidane. G.V
