• Paapamillaa naayakan yeshudevan maathram
    Paapamellaam neekkiyathum yeshunaadhan thaane
  1. Paathakaraay naamirunna kaalamathil namme
    Neethimaanmaar aakkuvaanaay yeshu thaan marichu
  2. Bhedamillaa reethiyil naam vaaniduvaanaay
    Thaathanodu yaachanam cheythidunnavaninnum
  3. Bhoothalathil ninnakhila baadhakalum neekki
    Neethiyode vaazhuvathinneshu thaan varunnu
  4. Neethiyin theerppathinulla kaalamathivegamaay-
    Anayunn-aayathinaal naamunarnnirikkaam. K.V.S
