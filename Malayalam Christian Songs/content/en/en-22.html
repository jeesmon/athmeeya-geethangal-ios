1. Sthothram cheyka en maname nee-avante kaaval
    Orthu nithyam paadi sthuthikkaam
    Sthothram cheythu vaazhthiduvaan yogynaay mattaarumilla
    Chora chinthi shudhiyaakki shaashwatha santhoshameki-
  2. Ithra nalla rakshithaavinte vankrupakal nee
    Ethra nannaayanubhavichu!
    Ethra marthyer mruthyuvinna dheenaraayi theernnidunnu
    Mithramaayitteshu ninte koodeyundaayirunnello-
  3. Dushtaraaya koottukaarumaay kazhicha kaalam
    Nashtamaayi poyathorkka nee
    Shaapayogynaaya ninne saukhyamaakki shudhanaakki
    Krooshileri jayam nedi paapi ninne veendeduthu. V.C.V
