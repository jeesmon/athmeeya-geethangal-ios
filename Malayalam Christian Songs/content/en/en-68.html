• Deva! threeyeka nee krupa cheythathinaal
    Nin paadaashrithanaayi veenu vanangunnen
  1. Nin priya suthaneya krooshinmel thakarthen
    kadamakhilavum theerthallo
    Sankadam sakalavum theernnu prathyaashayil
    Enmanam aashwasikkunnallo-
  2. Mattoruvanumeihu cheythilla-kani-
    Vuttavarithupol mattilla
    Maanava paathakamettu marichathu-
    Mannilithenniye mattilla-
  3. Mannassaliv agathiyil kaanichu-mama
    Malinathayaakave maayichu
    Maarilanachu nin mahimayezhunnoru
    Mandire cherthenne maanichu-
  4. Parishudhan parishudhanennum cholli
    Thirumumbil doother veezhunnenkil
    Paapathil jeevichor enneyorthathin
    Nandi njaanengane kaanikkum! T.K.S
