• Yeshuvin snehathaalennullam pongunne
    Than snehamaaduriyam chinthaatheethamathre
    Ha ethra aazhame yeshuvin snehame
    Aayathin dyaanamen jeevitha bhaagyame
  1. Lokastaapanam mumbenneyum kandallo
    Lokathil vannu than jeevane thannallo
    Ethrayo shrestamaam swargeeya viliyaal
    Enneyum yogyanaayenniya snehame-
  2. Karthaavaam kunjaattin kalyaana naalathil
    Kanthayaam thanmumbilenneyum nirthuvaan
    Ghoramaam paadukal krooraraam yoodaraal
    Kaaranamillaathe sahicha snehame-
  3. Seeyonilenikkaay moolakkallaakuvaan
    Seeyonilenneyum cherthu paniyuvaan
    Swargeeya thaatha nin veleyum thikachu
    Swargeeya shilpiyaam yeshuvin snehame-
  4. Atbutha snehamaam swargeeya daanathaal
    Sampoornnanaakkidum enneyum thanneppol
    Shathruvaamenneyum than swanthamaakkiya
    Snehaswaroopa nin athulya snehame-
  5. Geevakireedavum jyothiyaam vasthravum
    Neethiyin chenkolum dharichu vaazhuvaan
    Mulmudy dharichu nindayum sahichu
    Mannadi manna nin maaraatha snehame
  6. Veendeduppin gaanam paadum njaan seeyonil
    Vindootharkkum paadaan assaadhyameyathe
    Kaalvary giryil kaalkaram thulacha
    Mannadi manna nin maaraatha snehame-
