1. Marubhoomiyin naduve
    nadannidum daasane viravil
    Thirusaannidhyam nirayum megha-
    mathinkeezhil nee marackka
  2. Kaduthorushnam varuthum rashmi paratheettenmelulakam
    Vanathil choora chediyennapo-
    lunakkaan yathnichidunnu
  3. Athi sheethala tharamaayulla
    libanaadriyin himame
    Hrudi vannettam thanuppikkuvaan
    krupayundaayidaname
  4. Parane thava mukhaming-
    anucharikkunnill ennirikkil
    Oru kaalathum purappeduvaa-
    naruleedaruthayi! nee
  5. Priyane thava paramaamrutha-
    manisham swarggamathil ni-
    Nnuyarum modakaramaayente
    hrudayam thannil chorika
  6. Thava thejassin dhanamorthula
    kithilanyanaay vasippaan
    Parane ee njaan paradeshiye-
    nnuracheppozhum irippaan
  7. Thelivaarnnulla mukham thannil ni-
    nnoli praapichittathinaal
    Thelivin desham athilennum njaan
    nilanilkkuvaanarulka! K.V.S
