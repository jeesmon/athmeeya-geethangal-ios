• Ninte hitham poleyenne nithyam nadathidename!
    Ente hitham poleyalle en pithaave en yahove!
  1. Imbamulla jeevithavum- ere dhana maanangalum
    Thunpamatta saukhyangalum-chodikkunnille adiyan
  2. Andhakaaram bheethikalo-appane! prakaashangalo
    Enthu nee kalppichidunno-ellaam enikkaashirvaadam
  3. Ethu gunam ennarivaan illa jnjaanamennil naadhaa!
    Nin thirunaamam nimitham neethi maarggathil thirichu
  4. Agnimegha thoonukalaal-adiyane ennum nadathi
    Anudinam koode irunnu-appane! kadaakshikkuke. M.V
