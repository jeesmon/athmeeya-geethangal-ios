1. Nin thiru vachanathil ninnathbhutha kaaryangal
    Chanthamaay grahippathinnu ninthuna nalkeesho
  2. Andhakaara raajanente chintha kalakkaathe
    Kaanthaa! nin chinthayennil tha-nnaadyantham paalikka
  3. Sathya vachanathil ninnu nithya jeevavaakyam
    Chithe pathippichiduka shudhaathma naayakaa!
  4. Vishwaasathodennullil nin nishwaasamozhikal
    Nishchayamaay kalarnnenikk
    aashwaasamundaavaan. T.J.V
