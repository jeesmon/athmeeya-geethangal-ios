• Kroosheduthu njaan yeshu rakshakane
    Kleshangal vannaalum pinchellume-en kroosheduthu njaan
  1. Dukhathin thaazhvarayil kashtathil kaarirulil
    Thru-kkaikalal thaangi karthaavu thaan kaathu
    Ekkaalavum-enne nadathume-
  2. Ullam kalangidilum uttavar maaridilum
    Venda vishaadangal yeshuvin maaridam
    Undenikku chaari vishramippaan
  3. En jeeva-vazhikalil aapathu naalukalil
    Enne karuthuvaan ennennum kaakkuvaan
    Enneshu rakshakan mathiyallo-
  4. Lokam veruthaalumen-deham kshayichaalume
    Mruthyuvin naal vare kristhuvin deepamaay
    Idharayil kathi theernnenkil njaan-
  5. Karthaavin sannidhiyil ethum prabhaathathil njaan
    Kanneer-ellaamannu poornnamaay theernnennum
    Nithyathayil kristhan koode vaazhum. G.P
