1. Narare! vanniduvin paranodu yojippin
    Maranam sahicha than puthranaal ippol
  2. Gathiyillaa marthyarkkaay udicharunodayam
    Athikramam neekkiya kristheshu thanne-
  3. Japichaalum naamangal thapassode raappakal
    Labhikkuyillathaal rakshaye paapi!
  4. Oru naamameyullu karuna kandethuvaan
    Thiruveda saakshyangal kettaalum ningal
  5. Kurishinkal nokkiyaal durithangal neengippom
    Murivetta kunjaattin rakthathaal ellaam
  6. Hrudaya vishudhiyum krupayaale praapikkum
    Sadayam than aalayam aakkidum daivam
  7. Vasikkuvaan daivathil rasichidaan sathyathil
    Nashikkaatha jeevane nalkum thaan ullil
  8. Maranam vannidumbol sharanam ee rakshakan
    Parudeesainkal thaan cherthidumappol
  9. Oduvil thaan neethiyil vidhikkumathin mumbil
    Eduppin than saujanya rakshaye ningal. V.N
