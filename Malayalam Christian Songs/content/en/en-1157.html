• Yeshu maheshan aashritharkkellaam
    Aashwaasamarulaan vallabhanallo
    Naashathinnirayaay theernnoreyellaam
    Eeshanin rudhiram veendidumallo
  1. Maranathin bheekara vaazhchayil lokam
    Sharanamillaathe valayuvathenthe?
    Kurishile maranam moolamithaa van-
    Narakathil bhayamellaam asthamichallo-
  2. Aashrayaheenaraay valayunnore,
    Yeshuvinnarike anayuka vegam
    Mattoru maarggam mannithilundo?
    Mattoru rakshakan vannittundo?
  3. Thannude sannidhau vannidunnorkku
    Khinnatha theernnu mannithilennum
    Mannavan paade veenu namikkaam
    Pinnavan cheyyum nanmakalothaam. E.K.G
