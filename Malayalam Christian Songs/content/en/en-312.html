• Enthorathbhutha purushan kristhu-
    Thante mahima nisthulam
    Ithra mahaanaay uthamanaakumoruthane
    yulakil kaanumo
  1. Unnatha daiva nandananulakil vannithu kanyaa jaathanaay
    Innolamoraal vannillithupol
    thannavathaaram nisthulam
  2. Thala chaayppanaay sthdalamillaathon ulakamahaanmaar mumbilum
    Thala thaazhthaathe nilathettaathe
    Nalamodu jeevichathbutham
  3. Kurudar kandu thriudar virandu shaanthatha poondu saagaram
    Thellirakondu bahujanamundu
    Mrutharuyir poondu kristhanaal
  4. Kalushatha lesham kaanunnillee manujanilennura cheythu haa!
    Maranamathin vidhiyezhuthiyathivane
    Prathi maathram bhooviathbhutham
  5. Paara pilarnnu, paarilakunnu, paavana mrutharuyiraarnnu, haa!
    Keerukayaay thirasheelayum than mruthineram
    sooryanirundupoy
  6. Bhoothalanaadhan thannude maranam kaanuka durvvahamaayatho
    Bhooribhayam poondilakukayoyee-
    Prakruthikal akhilamith athbhutham!
  7. Mruthiye vennavanuyirthezhunnettu
    Ithinethiraarinn-othidum?
    Hrudibodam lavamullorellaam adipaniyum thansannidhou
  8. Olivennothum malayil ninnum thirujanamarikil nilkkave
    Charanamuyarnnu gagane gathanaay
    Thaathan arikilamarnnu thaan
  9. Jaya jaya nisthula kristhuraajan jaya jaya nirmmala naayakan
    Jaya jaya ghosham thudaruka janame
    Jayam tharum naadhanu sthothrame. T.K.S
