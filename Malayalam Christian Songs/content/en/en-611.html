• Tune: Velichathin kathirukal
  1. Kathru naamam moolamellaa-thinmayum ningale-kkonde
    Kalavaayi chollidumbol ningalettam bhaagyavaanmaar
  2. Aatmaavil dridraraayi theernnavaraam bhaagyavaanmaar
    Aadhi venda swargga raajyam nithyam avarkk-ullathallo
  3. Dukhikkumbol bhaagyavaanmaar labhyamaakum aashwaasavum
    Saukhyavum sampraapthamaakum sathya naadhan saameepyavum
  4. Saumyatha-ullavar ennum santhushtanmaar bhaagyavaanmaar
    Bhoomiavar-avakaasham aakkiyennum vaazhum noonam
  5. Neethikku vishannu daahikkunnavaro bhaagyavaanmaar
    Neethiyaal samthruptharaakum neethimaan nimithamaayi
  6. Kaaruneekar bhaagyvaanmaar kaarunnyamavarkku kittum
    Kaaranikan kaakkum muttum bhaaram lavalesham venda
  7. Shudhiyulla hrudayamaam sidhiyullor bhaagyavaanmaar
    Bhakthiyode paarthaal nithyam parishudhimaane paarkkam
  8. Samaadhaanam saadhyamaakkum sodararo bhaagyavaanmaar
    Sarvveshan thannude sutharenn-avar vilikkappedum
  9. Neethikkaayi peeddanangal elkkuvore santhoshippin
    Daiva raajyam ningalude divyamaayor-avakaasham. P.P.K
