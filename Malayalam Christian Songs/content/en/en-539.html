• Anudinamenne pularthunna daivam
    Anavadi nanmakal nalkidunnu
    Ananthamaam thirukrupa mathiye
    Anugraha jeevitham nayichiduvaan

  1. Avaniyile anarthdangalaal
    Alayuvaan avanenne kaividilla
    Akame thaanarupiyaa yullathinaal
    Aakulamilleni kkaadhiyilla

  2. Jeevithamaam en padakil
    vanthiramaala vannaanjadichaal
    Amarathen abhayamaay naadhanunde
    Amarum van kaattum thiramaalayum

  3. Balaheenamaam en shareeram
    Ee mannil mannaay theerumennaal
    Tharum puthudeham thandeha samam
    Thejessezhunnoru vinshareeram. A.V
