• Yeshu naadaa yeshu naadaa
    Ennu nee vannidum praana naadaa?
    En klesha barangal theerthiduvaan (2)
  1. Lokathin maayakal mohichu njaan
    Ordiya neramen chaare vannu
    Kaipidichenne than thejassin maarggathil
    Aakkiyen karthaavu halleluyya-
  2. Ee lokathil njaanoranyanathre
    Ee maru jeevitham kleshamathre
    Swargga kanaanente swantha stdalamathre
    Swarggeeya jeevitham nithyamathre-
  3. Karthan varavine nokki nokki
    Paarthale paarthidunnashayaal njaan
    Prathyaashayaalullam thingi nirayunne
    Amen karthaave nee vannidane. K.V.H
