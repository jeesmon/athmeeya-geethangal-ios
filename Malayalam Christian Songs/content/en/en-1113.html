• Poyidaam namukku daiva janame
    Yeshuvin naamathil poyidaam
    Jeevan thannu veendedutha naadane
    Lokamengum ghoshikkaan poyidaam
  1. Ethrayo aayirangalinnihe
    Paapathaal andharaay mevunnu
    Rakshayin vazhiyavarkku cholluvaan
    Poyidendavar nammalallayo-
  2. Andhakaaram bhoomiye mooduvaan
    Kaalamereyillennu naam orkkuka
    Kaalamottum paazhilaakkidaathe naam
    Poyidaam yeshuve ghoshikkaan-
  3. Naadan thaan varunnathinu munname
    Mannile vela nammal theerthidaam
    Vannu nammecherthu thaan prathiphalam
    Thannidunna naalinaayorungidaam. A.K.W
