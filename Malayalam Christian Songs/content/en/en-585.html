• Yeshumaheshane njaan chinthippathenn-
    Ullathinnaanandame
  1. Eeshanude thiru sannidhi chernnini
    Vishramam cheythu thante
    Thiru mukham kaanmath-athyaanandame--
  2. Vaana sainyangalum maanusha jaalavum
    Oonamillaathe ninne
    varnnikkuvaan saadhyam athaakayilla
  3. Maanusha rakshakaa ninnude naamam pol
    Vaanilumbhoomiyilum
    illeyoru naamam ninappathinnu
  4. Ullam nurungiyor-kkulla prathyaashayum
    Nalla prasaadavum nee
    sarvveshwara saumyathayullavarkke
  5. Ethra dayaa paran veezhunnavar-kkeshu
    Ethra nalla gunavaan
    thedidunna marthyaganangalkku thaan
  6. Kandidum maanavar-kkenthor-aahlaadam nee
    Undo naavum penayum
    varnnikkuvaan aa nalla sandarbhathe
  7. Yeshuvin sneham-athenthennu cholluvaan
    Aaswadichor-kkallaathe
    mattaarkkuma-saadhyamarinjiduvaan. T.K
