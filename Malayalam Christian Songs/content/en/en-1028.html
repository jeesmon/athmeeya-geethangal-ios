• Varum praanapriyan viravil thante
    Kaanthaye cherthiduvaan
    Thante rakthathaal veendedutha priyaye
    Than koodennennum vaaniduvaan
  1. Loka raashtrangal-aakaveyilakum
    Athin shakthiyo balaheenamaakum
    Karthan varavine kaathidum shudharo
    Avar puthukkidum shakthiye dinavum-
  2. Kashtam nindakaleri varikilum
    Kastam sahichavan koode undennum
    Thirusaannidhyam aanandam nalkum
    Thirukarangalal thaangi nadathum-
  3. Manavaalen than varavu sameepam
    Unarnniduka naam athivegam
    Theliyichiduka nammal deepam
    Annu chernnidum naam than sameepam-
  4. Vaattam maalinyam leshamillaatha
    Swargga naadathil vaanidum modaal
    Thejassode naam yeshuvil koode
    Vaazhum shobha paripoornnaraayi. M.E.C
