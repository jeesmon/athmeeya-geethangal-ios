    Enikkenthaayaalum en yESu mathi
  1. Oru naalum akalaatha sakhiyaanu thaan
    Thiruppaadam thedum agathikku thunayaanu thaan
    Varumoro dukhangngal bhaarangngalil
    Thellum paribhramam ventenikesu mathi
  2. Dinam thorum karuthuvaan aduthundu thaan
    Manam kalangngaathe avanilennavalambamaam
    Kaniverum karangngalaal kaathidum thaan
    Enne paalippaanithupole veraarulu?
  3. EruL moodum vazhiyil nalloliyaanu thaan
    Pakal marubhoovil chuduveyilil thanalaanu thaan
    Varalunna naavinu jalamaanu thaan
    Ennil puthubalam tharum jeevavachassaanu thaan
  4. Orikkalen perkkaayi murivettathaam
    Thiruvudal neril darsichu vanageedum njaan
    Mama kanneer thullikaL thorumannaal
    Mannan masihathan deepthiyil nithyam vaazhum njaan
