• TUNE: Manujanivan bhaagyavaan

    Arumanaadhane! thava parama jeevane mama-
    Duritha parihaaramaay kurishil vechethorkkunnen
  1. Paapam cheythathoyi njaan shaapamaayatho bhavaan!
    Thaapam neekkuvaan devakopam thooki nintemel
  2. Manujaraakave mruthi vasharaay kopapaathraraay
    Manujanaayi nee jeevan arulaan deva sunuve-
  3. Chathu krooshathil ninno dothu marthyanaamivan
    Puthan jeevane nee thaan datham cheythathaal mama
  4. Puthan kallarayathil bhakthar vechu ninneyum
    Sathyam ninnudeyada kkathilenneyum param-
  5. Neeyo chaavine jaichaarambham aayuyirppin-
    Jeevanil enneyum uyarthi ninnodukoode
  6. Aarohanamaayi nee thaathan valabhaagathu
    Mevunnenneyu mavi daakki bhaagyavaanaham
  7. Kallara yenikkithaa! vellam thanne snaanathil
    Nalla saamyamun dada kkathinnum uyarppinum.CTM
