1. Devaadhi devan nee- raajaadhi raajan
    Doothanmaar raappakal vaazhthidunnon
    Mannilum vinnilum aaradhyanaam nee
    Unnatha nandanan-nee yogyanaam
    • Nee ennum yogyan nee ennum yogyan
      Daivathin kunjaade nee yogyanaam
      sthothram, sthuthi, bahumaanangngalellaam
      sveekarippaanennum nee yogyanaam
  2. Swargga sukham vedinjen paapam theerppaan
    Daivathin kunjaadaay bhoovil vannu
    Nee arukkappettu nin ninam chinthi
    Veendeduthenneyum -nee yogyanaam
  3. Krooshila koorirulilekanaayi
    Daivathaal kaividappettavanaay
    Nee sahichu daiva crodhamathellaam
    En paapam moolamaay-nee yogyanaam
  4. Paathaker mandhyathil paathakaneppol
    Paapamaay theernnu nee krooshathinmel
    Nee marichu ente paapangal pokki
    Enthoru snehame! -nee yogyanaam. P.T
