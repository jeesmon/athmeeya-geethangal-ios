• Jayam jayam kollum naam- jayam kollum naam
    Yeshuvinte kodikeezhil -jayam kollum naam
  1. Naayakanaay yeshu thanne nadathunna sainyam
    Maaya lokam pedikkenda-jayam kollum naam
  2. Sarvva loka sainyangale saathaan koottiyaalum
    Swargga naadhan chirikkunnu-jayam kollum naam
  3. Kaushalangal thathwanjaanam yeshuvinnu vendaa
    Vachanathin shakthi mathi-jayam kollum naam
  4. Kristhan krooshil rakthathaalum nithya jeevanaalum
    Vishudhaathma shakthiyaalum- jayam kollum naam
  5. Kleshikkenda halleluyya! daivathinnu sthothram
    Yeshukonda jayathaale-jayam kollum naam. V.N
