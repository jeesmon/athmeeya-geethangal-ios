• En sankadangal sakalavum theernnupoyi
    Samhaara doothan enne kadannupoyi
  1. Kunjaattinte vilayeriya ninathil
    Maranju njaan rakshikkappetta rakshanathil
  2. Pharavonu njaanini adimayalla
    Parama seeyonil njaan annyanalla
  3. Maruvilen daivamenikk adhipathiye
    Tharumavan puthumanna athumathiye-
  4. Maaraye madhuramaakki theerkkumavan
    Paaraye pilarnnu daaham pokkumavan-
  5. Manoharamaaya kanaan deshame
    Ate, enikkazhiyaathor avakaashame-
  6. Aanandame paramaandame
    Kanaan jeevithamenikk aanandame-
  7. Ente balavum ente sangeethavum
    En rakshayum yeshuvathre halleluyya. M.E.C
