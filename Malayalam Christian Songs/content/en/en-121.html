• Yaahe sthuthichiduvin-
    en maname nandiyodanudinavum
    Vishudha naamathe vaazhthi sthuthikka
    Thankaruna marannidaathe
  1. Akruthyam okkeyum mochikkunnavan
    Rogasaukyam ekidunnavan
    Naashathil ninnen jeeveneyum
    Ennekkum veendeduthathaal-
  2. Dayayum karunayum aniyikkunnu
    Nanmakonde thrupthiyekunnu
    Yauvvanavum kazhukaneppol
    Puthukkidunnaayathinaal-
  3. Maname aayussin naalukalellaam
    Maanjidume poovin samamaay
    Than dayayo bhakthanmaarkkaay
    Ekunnu thalamurayaay
  4. Avenen prakruthi ariyunnallo
    Manmayanenn orthidunnallo
    Aakayaal njaan paadidume
    Nandiyod-anudinavum. T.V.S
