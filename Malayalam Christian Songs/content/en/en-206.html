• Vandanam yeshupara! ninakkennum
    vandanam yeshupara!
    Vandanam cheyyunnu ninnadiyaar thiru
    Namathinnaadaravaay
  1. Innu nin sannidhiyil adiyaarkku
    vannu cheruvathinnay
    Thanna ninn unnathamaam krupackabhi
    vandanam cheythidunnu-
  2. Nin rudhiramathinaal prathishticha
    jeevaputhu vazhiyaay
    Ninnadiyaarkku pithaa vin sannidhau
    vannidaame sathatham-
  3. Ithra mahathwamulla padaviye
    ippuzhukkalkkarulaan
    Paathratha yethumilla ninte krupa
    yethra vichithra maho!-
  4. Vaana dootha ganangal manohara
    gaanangalaal sathatham
    Oonamenye pukazhthi sthuthikkunna
    vaanavane ninakku-
  5. Mannaril mannavan nee
    manukulathinu rakshaakaran nee
    Minnum prabhaavamullon pithaavinu
    sannibhan neeyallayo!-
  6. Neeyozhike njangalkku suraloke
    yaarullu jeevanaadhaa!
    Neeyozhike ihathil mattaarumilla-
    Agrahippaan parane! P.V.T
