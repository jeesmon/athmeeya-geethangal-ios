• Nin daanam njaan anubhavichu
    Nin sneham njaan ruchicharinju
    Yeshuve en daivame nee ennum mathiyaayavan
  1. Yeshu enikku cheytha nanmakal orthidumbol
    Nandi konden manam paadidume
    Stothra gaanathin pallavikal-
  2. Daivame ninte sneham ethra naal thalli neekki
    Annu njaan anyanaay anaadhanaayi
    Ennaal inno njaan danyanaay-
  3. En jeevan poyennaalum enikkathil bhaaramilla
    Ente aatmaavinu nithya jeevan
    Yeshu enne orukkiyallo-
  4. Nithyathayorthidumbol en hruthadam aanandikkum
    Swargeeya santhosha jeevitham
    Vishwaasakkannaal njaan kandidunnu. W.C
