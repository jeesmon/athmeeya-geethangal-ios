1. Kannuneer thaazhvarayil njaanettam valanjidumbol
    Kannuneer vaarthavanen kaaryam nadathi tharum
    • Nin manam ilakaathe nin manam patharaathe
      Ninnodu koodeyennum njaanunde anthyam vare
  2. Koorirul paathayatho krooramaam shodhanayo
    Koodidum neramathil krooshin nizhal ninakkaay-
  3. Theechoola simhakkuzhi pottakkinar marubhu-
    Jailara eerchavaalo maranamo vannidatte-
  4. Kaalangal kaathidano kaanthaa nin aagamanam
    Kastatha theernniduvaan kaalangal ereyilla-
  5. Daahichu valanju njaan bhaarathaal valanjeedumbol
    Daaham shamippichavan daaha jalam tharume-
  6. Chenkadal theeramathil than daasan kenathupol
    Chankinu nere varum van bhaaram maarippokum-
