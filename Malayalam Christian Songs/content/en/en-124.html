    Jnaanamaay vaazhum karthaave!
    Vandyanaam nithya jeevamarggame vandanam jayamangalam
  1. Kaalkarangalilaaniyettu nee kaalvary krooshil yaagamaay
    Paavana raktham chinthi njangale veendathaal jayamangalam
  2. Swantha puthraneyaadariyaathe swanthamaay njangalkekiya
    Swarggathaatha! nee sarvvavum thannil thannathaal
    jayamangalam
  3. Saakshaal munthiri valliyaam ninnil kombukalaakki njangale!
    Mechamaam phalam kaayckkuvaan chethi-
    Swachamaakkayaal mangalam-
  4. Kaarunyamaarnna kaikalil vahi-chaalambhaheenar aayore
    Van durithangal neekkiyanpinaal pottidum naadha! mangalam-
  5. Saanandam sthuthi keerthanangalaal vaazhthuvaaninnu njangale
    Praaptharaakkiya devanandana! vandanam jaya mangalam!. T.O.C
