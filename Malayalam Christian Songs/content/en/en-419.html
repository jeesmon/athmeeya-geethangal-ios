• Have Thine own way Lord
  1. Ninnishtam deva! aayidatte
    njaano manpaathram ninkarathaal
    Nin paadathil njaan kaathirikkum
    ninnishtam pol nee maattukenne
  2. Ninnishtam pole aakaname
    ennullam novum velayilum
    Nin karam thottu thaalolikken
    karthaave! njaanum shakthanaavaan
  3. Ninnishtam pole aakaname
    ennishtarenne thalliyaalum
    njaan kaividillayennu chonna
    naathhaa ninvaakkenthaasvaasame!
  4. Ninnishtam pole aakaname
    nithyavum njaannin daasan thanne
    ennullil vaazhum suddhaathmaavaal
    ennum nirranjnju sobhippaan njaan
  5. Ninnishtam pole aakaname
    nin sannidhau njaan thaanirikkum
    Nin vachanamaam thanneerinaal
    enne kazhuki shudhi cheyka. G.P
