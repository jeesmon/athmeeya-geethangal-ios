• All the way my Savior
  1. Njaan karthaavinaayi paadum jeevichidum naalellaam
    Daivamahathwam kondaadum keerthikkum than vaatsalyam
  2. Bhaaramullor manamalla daivaathmaavin lakshanam
    Saakshaal abhishiktharkkellaa kaalathum santhoshikkaam
  3. Daivamukhathin mumbaake veenayaale sthuthippaan
    Yeshuvinte rakthathaale enne praapthan aakki thaan-
  4. Kelkka doothanmaarin gaanam bethlehemin vayalil
    Nokkuka pithaavin daanam cheruka sangeethathil
  5. Paalum thenum ozhukidum nallor raajyam entethaam
    Aashwaasangal niranjidum kristhan maarvven paarppidam
  6. Paadum najan santhoshathaale ullam ellaam thullumbol
    Paadum enne agniyaale shodhana cheythidumbol
  7. Athivruksham vaadiyaalum munthiringa valliyum
    Onnum nalkaathirunnaalum njaan karthaavil pukazhum
  8. En nikshepam swarggathinkal aakayaal njaan bhaagyavaan
    Lokarude dukhathinkal enikkundo dukhippaan
  9. Daivathinkale santhosham aashritharin balamaam
    Aashayattupoya klesham dooratheriyuka naam
  10. Halleluyya daivathinnum halleluyya puthrannum
    Halleluyya aathmaavinnum innum sarvva kaalathum. V.N
