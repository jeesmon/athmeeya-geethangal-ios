1. Bhoovil vannavan jeevan thannavan
    Puthiya maarggam ulakilaarkkumaay thurannavan
    Than sneham mahaatbhutha sneham
    Than naamam mahonnatha naama-vaanilum bhoovilum
  2. Avan daivamaam anaady anthanaam
    Avaniyum thann arulappaadilulavaay vannathaam
    Veshathil manushyanaayi thaan
    Krooshinmel marichuyirkkayaal-mokshathin vaathilaay
  3. Mahabhaagyamaay avan pakshamaay
    Maruvilenkilum vasippathamitha modamaay
    Sevippaa-navannu thulyamaay Snehippaan mattaarumillathaal-than paadam kumbidaam
  4. Avanmoolamaay sukham nithyavum
    Anugamichidum janangal anubhavichidum
    Aaraalum akattuvaan bhooviy-
    Aakaatho radutha bendhuvaam-aanandadaayakan
  5. Jayam nischayam jayam nischayam
    Vijaya veereneshuvin nimithamidhare
    Khoshippin avan janangal sa-
    Nthoshippi-navante naamathil-nithyathayolavum. T.K.S
