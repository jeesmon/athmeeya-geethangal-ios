• Krupayaal krupayaal krupayaal njaan daivamakanaay
    Albhuthamalbhuthame, albhuthamalbhuthame
  1. Shaapam nirayum dharaniyil neecha
    Paapiyaay piranna drohee njaan
    Enneyum snehikkayo thamburaan enneyum snehikkayo!
  2. Kaanmeen naam nijasutharaay varuvaan
    Daivam nalkiya snehame
    Ithra mahaa sneham dharayil verenthithupole!
  3. Azhiyum loka janangalin sneham
    Pozhiyum pullin pookkalpol
    Vaadaatha sneham kurishil
    kaanunna sneham! M.E.C
