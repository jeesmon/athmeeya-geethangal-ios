• When I survey
  1. Nisseemamaam nin snehathe prakaashippikkum krooshine
    Darshicha neram naadhane ninakku njaanadheenanaay
  2. Ee bhoomiyil nikshepamaay njaanennivanna sarvvavum
    Ganichidunnu nashtamaay ee darshanam mughaantharam
  3. Pramodamaay ennaayussil snehicha vyarthda kaaryangal
    Nikrushtamenn arinjaham vedinjidunn asheshavum
  4. Nin krooshil njaan nirantharam prashamsichidum rakshaka
    Mattonnilum enmaanasam mahathwamaa grahikkola
  5. Agaadhama prameyamaam ee snehamarhikkunnidam
    En deham dehi maanasam sampoornnamaay samasthavum
  6. Saashtaamgam veenu paadathil vanangidunnu bhakthiyil
    Ninakkum nin pithaavinum mahathwam daivaathmaavinum. E.I.J
