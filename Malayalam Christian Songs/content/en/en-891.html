• Amba-yerushalem ambarin kaazhchayil
    Ambare varunna naalenthu manoharam!
  1. Than manavaalanu vendi-yalankarichulloru
    manavaatti thanneyikkanyaka--
  2. Nalla pravruthikalaaya suchelaye
    Mallamizhi dharichu-kond-abhiraamayaay--
  3. Baabilon veshyaye-ppolivale maru-
    Bhoomiyilalla kaanmu maamalamel drundam--
  4. Neelavum veethiyum uyaravum saamyamaay
    Kaanuvath-ivalilaan-annyayilallathu--
  5. Ivalude sooryachandrar oru vidhathilum vaanam
    Vidukayillival shobha aruthi-illaathathaam--
  6. Rasamezhum sangeethangal ivalude kaathukalil
    Sukhamarulidum geetham swayamival paadidum--
  7. Kanakavum muthu-ratnam ivayanikillenkilum
    Sumukhiyaamival kantdam bahuramaneeyamaam- K.V.S
