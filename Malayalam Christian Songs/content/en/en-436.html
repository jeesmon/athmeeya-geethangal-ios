1. Sathyavedamonnu maathramethra sreshthame!
    Kristhane velippeduthidunna saakshyame
    Nithyajeevamannayaamathente bhakshyame
    Yukthivaadamokkeyumenikkalakshyame
  2. Vaanilum dharithrithannilum pradhaaname
    Thenilum sumaadhuryam tharunna paaname
    Ponnilum vilappedunna daivadaaname
    Mannilanyagranthhamillithin samaaname
  3. Aazhamaay ninappavarkkithathyagaadhame
    Ezhakalkkumekidunnu divyabodhame
    Paathayil prakaashamekidunna deepame
    Saadamerridunnavarkku jeevapoopame
  4. Sangkadathilaashvasikkaththakka vaakyame
    Santhatham samaadarikkilethra saukhyame
    Samshayichidendathellumathrayogyame
    Sammathichchanusarippavarkku bhaagyame
  5. Sathruve jayichchadakkuvaan krupaaname
    Sathyamaadarikkuvorkku sathpramaaname
    Nithyavum samastharum padhichidename
    Sathyapaatha kristhunaathhanennu kaanume. T.K.S
