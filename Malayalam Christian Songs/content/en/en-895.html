• Thirumukham kaanumthorum-orumayin
    Kathiraalenn aravindam vidarunnu
    Arumayinn uyiraalaa! azhakerum manavaala!
    Karimukil kuzhalaala! karunayin maanuvelaa!
  1. Aadiyil thaathan ninnodina chaan enneyennaalum
    Paathakan ninnil ninnu pirichithenne
    Aadivachanam jadamaayone nin jeevane
    Yaathanayilaakkiya paapi njaane!
    Paathivruthya-bhangathaal puthippoozhiyaam ennil
    Neethiparimala neeruthi vaayaalozhicha-
  2. Panjcha paathakiyaamen nanjchunda vaayil ninnu
    Punjchiriyodu thookum kenjchum-mozhi
    Nenjchu podiyichoorum ninjeevana thaalennil
    Chenjchamme cheyyunnabhi shinjchanathe
    Anjchumurivil ninnu panjchambanbaanametta
    Ninjchedi njaanaakayaalen chinthanamellaam nin-
  3. Kaantha! nin kanakavaay kaanthikalarukayaal
    Poonthenuruvaaya nin swaanthamulla
    Ninthen cholkkuriyaal sugandham
    Nalkidunnoru kaanthaaravinda maamee kaanthaye nee
    Santhoshathod ulkkondu santhatham thirumaarvvil
    Chinthaamaniya nee cherkken chinthanamellaam ninte
  4. Mannaa! nin ponnin karam ennil nizhalichathaal
    Ninnuruvamennullil minnunnithaa
    Ninnil maathramozhike mannilullonnilume
    En naavinnilla ruchi thellupolum
    Ninnuyir niranja nin vahnibeejamaam mannaa
    Thannuposhippikkenne vinnolikal niranja. J.J
