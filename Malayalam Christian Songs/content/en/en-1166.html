• Ninakkaayen jeevane mara kkurishil
    vedinjen makane!
    Dinavum ithine marannu bhuvi nee
    vasippathenthu kanmaniye?
  1. Vedinju njaanente paramodangal-akhilavum ninnekkaruthi ninte
    Katdina paapathe chumannozhippathinnadima
    vesham njaaneduthu-
  2. Parama thaathante thirumumbaake nin
    Duritha bhaarathe chumannu-kondu
    Paravashanaayi thalarnnen viyarppu chora thulli polozhuki
  3. Periyoru kurish eduthu kondu njaan kayari kaalvary mukalil-udan
    Karuthezhunnavar pidichizhachenne-kidathi vankurish athinmel-
  4. Valichu kaalkaram pazhuthinayaakki pidichirumbaani cheluthi-ottum
    Alivillaathadi chirakkye raktham-therikkunnente kanmaniye!-
  5. Parama daahavum vivashathayum kondadhikam
    Thalarnna ente-naavu
    Varandu vellathinniranna nerathum tharunnethenthu neeyorkka-
  6. Karunayillaatha padayaaliyoru periya kunthamang eduthu-kuthi
    thurannen chankineathil ninnozhuki jalavum rakthavum udane
  7. Orikkalum ente parama snehathe marakkaamo ninakkorthaal ninmel
    Karalalinju njaaniva sakalavum
    sahichen jeevane vedinju. P.V.T
