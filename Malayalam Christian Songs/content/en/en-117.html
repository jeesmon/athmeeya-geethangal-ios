• Parama karunaarasaraashe! O!
    Parama karunaarasaraashe!
  1. Paarithil paathakiyaamenikkaayi nee
    Parama-bhavana-mathine-vedinja
    Karanayoru pozhutharivathinn idararuvathinn arulina
    Karanamathu thava charanamaam
    Mama sharanamaam bhava tharanamaamayi!-
  2. Naadha! ninnaaviyen naavil vannaakayaal
    Navamaa-yudikkum-sthuthikal-dhwanikkum
    Nalamodahamura-cheythidum-mama cheythidum ninkrupa
    Kalitha sukha mihamaruvidum
    Sthavamuruvidum daya perukidunnoru-
  3. Shaapamee bhoovil ninnaakave neenguvaan
    Sakalaadhipa-vaanoliyaal niravaan
    Sakala manujaril amithamaam sukhamuyaruvaan saadaram
    Pakarukaru-lathi sulabhamaayathi
    vipulamaay behu saphalamaamayi! K.V.S
