• Enthoranaandamee-kristheeya jeevitham
    Daivathin paithalen jeevitham
  1. Bheethiyum illenikk aadhiyumilla
    Bhauthika chintha bhaaravumilla
    Mama thaathanaay swargga naadhanun-
    Davan mathiyenikkethoru velayilum-
  2. Vyasanamilla niraashayumilla varuvathenthenn aakulamilla
    Enneshu than thirukkaikali-
    Eenne santhatham anpodu kaathidunnu-
  3. Manushyanil njaan aashrayikkilla
    Dhanathilen manam chaayukayilla
    Uyirpom vare kurishenthi njaan
    Ulakil manuvelane anugamikkum
  4. Aarilenn aashrayamennenikkariyaa-
    Mavanenn upanidhiyoduvolam kaakkum
    Thannanthike varumaareyum avan thallukailloru velayilum
  5. Koodaara vaasam bhoovilen vaasam
    Paaridamo paarthaal paradesham
    Paran shilppiyaay paniyunnoru
    Puramundathu kaathu njaan paarthidunnu. M.E.C
